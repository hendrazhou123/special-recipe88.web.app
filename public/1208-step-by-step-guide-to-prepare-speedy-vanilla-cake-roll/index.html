Instructions to make Vanilla Cake Roll:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray a 15 ×10 by 1 inch jelly roll pan with non stick spray. Line pan with parchment paper and spray paper with non stick spray
  2. Have egg whites in one large bowl and egg yolks in another large bowl and have them at room temperature
  3. Whisk flour. baking powder and salt in a bowl, set aside
  4. Beat egg yolks and buttermilk until light in color and increased in volume. Beat in 1 cup of the sugar and vanilla until well mixed
  5. Stir in flour mixture. Making sure it's mixed well but don't overmix
  6. With clean and dry beaters beat egg whites and cream of tarter until soft peaks form, slowly add remaing 1/2 cup sugar and beat until glossy peaks form
  7. Fold 1/4 of egg whites into egg yolk mixture then fold in remaining in 3 more additions.
  8. Spread batter evenly in prepared pan.Bake 16 to 18 minutes until golden and not sticky to touch and it springs back when touched
  9. Cool 5 minutes on rack in pan. Run a knife around edges of cake to unstick any edges if cake
  10. After cake cools 5 minutes invert onto confectioners sugar dusted kitchen towel. Carefully peel off parchment paper
  11. Roll cake up in towel, into a roll. Cool in towel on rack
  12. MAKE FILLING
  13. Beat cream until soft peaks form, add strawberry jan, confectioner's sugar and vanilla and beat until stiff peaks form. Add drops of food color now if you want a darker pink color.
  14. Unroll cooled cake and spread strawberry cream over cake
  15. Roll cake up, using towel as a guide to tightly roll.
