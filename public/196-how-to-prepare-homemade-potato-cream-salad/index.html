The ingredients needed to make Potato Cream Salad:
  1. Take 4-5 potatoes
  2. Take 2 carrots
  3. Prepare 1 cup corn
  4. Get 1 cup cabbage
  5. Take 1 cup peas
  6. Make ready 1 cup cream
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp milk
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp sugar
  9. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Take 1 tbsp black pepper
  11. Get 1/2 tsp butter

Instructions to make Potato Cream Salad:
  1. Cut potatoes, carrots and cabbage in cube form.
  2. Now boil potatoes and peas with salt as much as required and black pepper.
  3. Now take a bowl and add 1/2 sugar add milk in it, then mix it well, now add cream and mix well.
  4. Then, add carrots, peas, potatoes, corn (boil), cabbage in that paste. And mix well add butter, black pepper and salt in it.
  5. Now, enjoy it.
