The ingredients needed to make Butter chicken (indian Style):
  1. Take 1 kg chicken ( cleaned and cut to small pieces, preferably breast)
  2. Make ready For Marination
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp Pepper powder
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  5. Take 1 tsp salt Yoghurt, 2tbsp
  6. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  7. Take For Gravy
  8. Take 4 nos savala (cut to small pieces)
  9. Get 3 tsps ginger grated

  11. Make ready 1 tbsp cashew-nuts
  12. Prepare big-3 Tomatoes
