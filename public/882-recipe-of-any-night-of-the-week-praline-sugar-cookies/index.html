The ingredients needed to make Praline Sugar Cookies:
  1. Take For Cookies
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 white sugar
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
  5. Prepare 1 stick butter
  6. Take 1 egg
  7. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Take For Pralines
  9. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
  11. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Praline Sugar Cookies:
  1. For cookies: melt butter and cream together with sugar (after its been given a chance to cool—don’t use hot melted butter!) Then add egg and vanilla and mix well. Slowly add flour and mix by hand until a semi-firm dough forms.
  2. For pralines: melt butter on medium-high heat, add sugar and simmer for 5 minutes stirring constantly. Once it turns a light caramel color and looks fairly smooth and creamy, pour onto parchment paper (I did mine on a plate) and let cool and harden for about 5 minutes.
  3. Create balls of dough for cookies and about a handful size and pay flat in your hand. Place approx 15 cookies on the parchment paper.
  4. Top each cookie with a chunk of hardened praline and bake on 350°F for 15 minutes and then let cool for at least 5-7 minutes before indulging!
