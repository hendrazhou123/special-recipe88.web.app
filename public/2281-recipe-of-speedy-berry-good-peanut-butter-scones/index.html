The ingredients needed to make Berry good peanut butter scones:
  1. Make ready 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup packed light brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Get 1 teaspoon cream of tartar
  5. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  6. Take 1 cup natural unsalted creamy peanut butter
  7. Prepare 1 package (5 ounces) mixed dried berries (about 1 cup)
  8. Take 3/4 cup plain greek yogurt
  9. Get 1 large egg

Steps to make Berry good peanut butter scones:
  1. Preheat oven to 400° line a heavy baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Combine the flour, brown sugar, baking soda, cream of tartar, and salt in a food processor. Pulse to combine (i dont have a food processor i used my kitchen aid and it worked fine)
  3. Add the peanut butter by spoonfuls to the flour mixture. Pulse until the mixture is combines and looks like sand. Transfer to a mixing bowl and stir in the berries.
  4. Stir in the yogurt and egg togrther in a small bowl and add to the flour mixture. Stir with a spoon until combined. Use your hands if necessary, to ensure that all of the flour is incorporated.
  5. Transfer the dough to a lightly floured work surface and pat into a circle about 1 " thick. Cut the dough into 8 equal wedges. Arrange wedges on the baking sheet and bake for 15 minutes ornuntil lightly browned. Let cool slightly and serve warm.
