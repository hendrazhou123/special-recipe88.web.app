The ingredients needed to make Cheese balls:
  1. Get peeled, boiled, grated potatoes
  2. Prepare chopped green chillies
  3. Take pepper powder
  4. Take salt
  5. Prepare grated garlic
  6. Make ready chilli flakes
  7. Prepare corn flour
  8. Take besan/gram flour
  9. Prepare oregano
  10. Make ready cheese cubes as required
  11. Make ready bread crumbs
  12. Make ready oil for deep fry

Instructions to make Cheese balls:
  1. In a mixing bowl take mashed potatoes, add in all the other ingredients except bread crumbs and cheese cubes,mix well everything together,
  2. Now take little mixture and stuff cheese cubes inside make balls, roll them in bread crumbs, when all the balls are prepared keep them the fridge for about 10 minutes
  3. Take them out,deep fry them on medium flame, serve hot..
