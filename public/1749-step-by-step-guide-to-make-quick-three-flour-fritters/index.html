The ingredients needed to make Three flour fritters:
  1. Take 1 cup diced half mushrooms wash in warm water
  2. Take 1 tablespoons ginger garlic paste
  3. Make ready 1 scoop gram flour
  4. Get 1 scoop corn flour
  5. Prepare 1 scoop wheat flour
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Take 1 teaspoon red chilli powder
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon turmeric
  9. Make ready 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  10. Get 1 teaspoon chaat masala
  11. Make ready 1 teaspoon garam masala
  12. Get 1 teas curd homemade optional
  13. Make ready Oil to fry

Steps to make Three flour fritters:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together without water first.now add warm water gently and slowly
  2. Make thick mixture and let mix mushrooms inside and keep it for 5 minutes
  3. Let coated the mushroom with mixture well
  4. Make oil completely hot and turn the gas very low
  5. Now start putting single piece one by one gently and just put only 4 to 5 pieces in one time
  6. Let it deep fry and make golden
  7. Sprinkle chaat masala is optional.yummy delicious and super healthy fritters are ready
