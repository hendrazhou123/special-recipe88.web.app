The ingredients needed to make My Chocochip Cookie:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp Butter
  2. Take 2 tbsp Brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1 Egg yolk
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup Flour
  5. Get 1 pinch Baking soda
  6. Get 1/4 cup Chocochips

Steps to make My Chocochip Cookie:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 The whisk the butter and sugar till creamy
  2. Add the egg yolk and whisk again
  3. Add the flour and the baking soda and combine well
  4. Fold on the chocochips
  5. Take coffee mug or ramekin and grease it
  6. Put the cookie dough into it and sprinkle some more chocochips
  7. Bake for 22 to 25 mins or till a skewer inserted comes out clean
  8. Treat yourself with this amazing single chocochip cookie
