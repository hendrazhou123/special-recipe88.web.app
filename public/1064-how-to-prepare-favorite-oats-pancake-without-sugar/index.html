The ingredients needed to make Oats Pancake Without Sugar:
  1. Get 1 Cup Oats (instant Oats)
  2. Take as needed butter
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp desiccated coconut powder
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp jaggery powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  7. Prepare 2-3 green cardamom
  8. Make ready As needed dry fruits

Instructions to make Oats Pancake Without Sugar:
  1. Take a blender jar, Now add all ingredients like oats, desiccated coconut powder, jaggery powder, milk and blend till they resemble a flour like consistency.
  2. Set mixture aside for 5-8 minutes to thicken up. Now place a pan over a low-medium flame and let it warm. grease pan with butter and ladle 2-3 tablespoonfuls of mix onto pan
  3. Cook 2-3 minutes on each side, till it's golden colour. Pancakes are ready to Serve… enjoy!
