The ingredients needed to make Cranberry Cookie:
  1. Get 350 g All purpose powder
  2. Prepare 10 g Milk powder
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon Double action baking powder
  4. Prepare 225 g Unsalted butter
  5. Prepare 1 Egg
  6. Prepare 150 g Mixed Berry
  7. Prepare 110 g Icing sugar
  8. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon salt

Instructions to make Cranberry Cookie:
  1. Beat unsalted butter with salt until soft
  2. Add icing sugar in the mixing bowl with high speed until rise
  3. Add egg in mixing bowl with high speed
  4. Add sifted flour with low speed and then add cranberries
  5. Use scoop to lap finished flour and mixed berry on the tray
  6. Bring it in the oven with 160 celsius 15 minute
