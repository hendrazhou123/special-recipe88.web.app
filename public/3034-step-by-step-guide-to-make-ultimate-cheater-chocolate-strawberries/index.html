Instructions to make Cheater Chocolate Strawberries:
  1. Wash & dry strawberries well, place on a plate or cookie sheet lined with parchment or wax paper
  2. To prepare your frosting, remove the lid, the foil, & the label
  3. Microwave the frosting for 15 seconds, or 20 if want a runnier consistency
  4. Dip your strawberries into the frosting & place on the paper
  5. Sprinkle on your choice of toppings
  6. Either chill them for half an hour or enjoy them now :)
