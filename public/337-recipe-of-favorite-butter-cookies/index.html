  2. Make ready 3/4 cup refined flour
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup corn flour
  4. Make ready 5 tbsp powdered sugar
  5. Get As required choco chip and tutti futti

Instructions to make Butter cookies:
  1. Firstly, beat Butter into a mixing bowl until becomes soft.
  2. Further add powdered sugar and beat again becomes fluffy.
  3. Now add sieve refined flour, cornflour, and mix in better.
  4. Now pour batter in piping back.
  5. And place into a greased baking tray. Garnish with choco-chip and tutti Frutti.
  6. Bake preheated oven at 170 degrees Celsius for 10 minutes.
  7. Cool down cookies before serving. And keep for 5-6 days in air tight container.
