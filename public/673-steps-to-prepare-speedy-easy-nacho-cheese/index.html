Steps to make Easy Nacho Cheese:
  1. Melt the butter into a pan and whisk in flour over medium heat. Allow to cook until bubbling. Once bubbling continue to cook about 30 seconds.
  2. Add milk to mixture and change heat to medium high. Bring mixture to a simmer. Once simmering mixture will thicken quickly. When it is thick enough to cover a spoon remove heat.
  3. Add cheese to mixture and stir until melted. Then add remaining ingredients. Let rest about 3 minutes to allow ingredients to properly blend. Then serve while warm.
