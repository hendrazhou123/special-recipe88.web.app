Steps to make SF, Gluten Free, Vegatarian, Cashew flour Choc. Chip cookies:
  1. In a large bowl add all wet ingredients and blend well with spoon
  2. Add dry ingredients and mix thoroughly until thick. Stir to activate the thickening of the xanthan gum, the more you stir the thicker it will get.
  3. Place tbsp full dollop on silicone mat and bake at 375° for 7 mins until bottoms are light brown.
  4. Cool on cookie rack.
