The ingredients needed to make Blueberry lemon cake:
  1. Take 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 salt
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Take 1 sugar
  6. Prepare Zest of one lemon
  7. Get 1/3 oil
  8. Get 1/4 yoghurt
  9. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  10. Make ready 1 cup frozen or fresh blueberries

Instructions to make Blueberry lemon cake:
