Steps to make Cartoons yummy chocolates:
  1. Take a 1 cup milk compound and 1 cup of white compound.
  2. Grade or crush both the compounds and mix it together as in below pictures.
  3. Melt in double boiler (take 1 glass of water in a container and boil it put another container over the bowl and low the flame) transfer all your grated white and milk compound in bowl and melt the same double boiler as in below pictures.
  4. Keep stirring and mix the chocolate well while melting process be careful not to add a single drop of water in same transfer the melted chocolate and add chopped almonds.
  5. Take a silicon or normal ice tray and put the same freezer for 5 minutes your is ready to after freeze.
