The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Cookie Crumble Brownies:
  1. Make ready 4 oz unsweetend baking chocolate
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup butter
  3. Take 2 cups sugar
  4. Make ready 5 eggs
  5. Take 1 cup flour
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup natural peanut butter (try to find one w/o added ingr)
  7. Get 5 +3 Nutter Butter cookies, separated

Instructions to make Peanut Butter Cookie Crumble Brownies:
  1. Melt baking chocolate and butter in microwave-safe bowl for 2 minutes or until fully melted. Stir.
  2. Pour 2 cups sugar in the bowl, stir well.
  3. Stir in eggs, flour. Mix well using whisk.
  4. In a food processor, separately crush Nutter Butter to crumbles. Set aside the other 3 crushed Nutter Butter.
  5. Pour peanut butter and crushed Nutter Batter into the batter.
  6. Preheat oven to 350F. Prepare the pan by lining it with foil and/or cover it in a thin layer of butter or cooking spray
  7. Pour the batter in, sprinkle it with crushed Nutter Butter.
  8. Bake in the oven for 35-40 minutes.
  9. Using a toothpick, place it in the center of the baked brownies. If nothing sticks to the toothpick, then you're done.
  10. Cool down for 15-20 minutes. Serve and enjoy!
