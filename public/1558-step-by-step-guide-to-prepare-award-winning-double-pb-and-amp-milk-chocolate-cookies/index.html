The ingredients needed to make Double PB & Milk Chocolate Cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 cups flour
  2. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  3. Prepare 1 cup softened butter
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  5. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup brown sugar
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1 egg
  9. Take 1-2/3 cups milk chocolate peanut butter morsels

Instructions to make Double PB & Milk Chocolate Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven 375° F
  2. In a small bowl mix flour and baking soda
  3. In a large bowl combine butter, peanut butter, sugars and vanilla till light and creamy
  4. Mix in egg.
  5. Mix in flour a little at a time
  6. Stir in morsels
  7. Drop on to a increased Baking sheet tablespoon at a time
  8. Bake for 8-10 min or until edges are set but centers are still soft..cool completely
  9. Makes about 3 doz.
