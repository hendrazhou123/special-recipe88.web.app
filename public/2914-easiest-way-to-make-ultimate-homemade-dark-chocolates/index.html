The ingredients needed to make Homemade Dark Chocolates:
  1. Get 1 Cup Sugar
  2. Make ready 1/2 Cup Cocoa Powder
  3. Take 1 Cup Coconut Oil
  4. Get 1 Spoon Butter
  5. Prepare 1 Drop Vanilla Essence

Steps to make Homemade Dark Chocolates:
  1. At First, Grind the Sugar and keep it aside
  2. Take out the Residues from Sugar & Cocoa Powder and Mix them well
  3. Then keep a Pan and add Water and Boil it
  4. Then keep another Bowl over it and through Double Boiling Method, Start Cooking
  5. Pour some Coconut Oil in the Bowl and add the Sugar with Cocoa Powder in it Gradually
  6. Mix it well and Add some Butter and 1 Drop of Vanilla Essence to it
  7. Then take out the Batter and Pour it in the ice tray
  8. Then Refrigerate it for 15 Minutes
  9. After 15 Minutes, you can take it out and our Homemade Dark Chocolates are Ready Now!!
