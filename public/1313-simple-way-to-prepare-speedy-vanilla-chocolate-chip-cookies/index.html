The ingredients needed to make Vanilla chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Take 250 grams butter
  2. Prepare 300 grams plain flour
  3. Get 85 grams white chocolate chips
  4. Make ready 85 grams milk chocolate chips
  5. Get 2 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  7. Get 140 grams caster sugar

Instructions to make Vanilla chocolate chip cookies:
  1. preheat oven to 180°
  2. mix the butter and sugar together then add the vanilla essence,egg yolk, sugar and flour until combined, then add the chocolate chips, use hands to mix into a dough.
  3. Then once the dough is made make little balls with the dough, place the balls onto some grease proof paper and squash them to make a cookie shape, space them evenly as they will spread slightly.
  4. place in oven for 15-20 minutes and then transfer to a cooling rack.
