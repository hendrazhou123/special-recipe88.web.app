The ingredients needed to make gingerbread cookie:
  1. Make ready 4 cup ap flour
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup jaggery, palm brown sugar
  3. Take 3/4 cup molasses
  4. Get 2 tsp ground ginger
  5. Get 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Prepare 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  7. Take 1/2 tsp ground cloves
  8. Take 1/2 tsp allspice
  9. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  11. Get 1 tsp pure vanilla extract
  12. Get 1 extra-large egg beaten
  13. Make ready 4 tbsp demerara sugar
  14. Make ready 1 cup butter melted

Steps to make gingerbread cookie:
  1. melt butter add molasses and jaggery wait till jaggery is desolved I crushed the jaggery to make this easier
  2. let cool whisk in beaten egg
  3. mix flour baking soda and baking powder very well add molasses mixture mix very well make into two ball put into fridge for 1 hour
  4. preheat oven 350°F Fahrenheit
  5. roll out dough thickness you desire the thicker it is the softer it is the thinner the more crispy. cut the cookies how you like the other half of dough roll put into a 8x12 inch baking pan cover bottom of pan bake same time in top of oven
  6. bake for 8-12 minutes
  7. sprinkle demerara sugar on top of cookies
