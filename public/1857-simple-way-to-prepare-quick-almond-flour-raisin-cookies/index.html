The ingredients needed to make Almond Flour Raisin Cookies:

  2. Take 1/4 cup Brown Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 Whole Egg
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup Raisin

Steps to make Almond Flour Raisin Cookies:
  1. Whisk almond flour and baking powder in a medium bowl.
  2. Beat sugar and butter in the large bowl until smooth. Add egg and blend well.
  3. Add raisins and flour mixture, and blend until just combined.
  4. Place the dough on a large piece of plastic wrap and make log shape. Freeze until very firm, at least 2 hours.
  5. Heat oven to 180°C/350°F, coat the baking pan with non-stick cooking spray.
  6. Slice the dough and place on the baking pan.
  7. Bake about 12 minutes or until the edges and bottoms of the cookies are golden
  8. Transfer them to the racks to cool completely.
