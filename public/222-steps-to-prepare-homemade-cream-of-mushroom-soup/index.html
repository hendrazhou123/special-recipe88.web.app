The ingredients needed to make Cream of Mushroom Soup:
  1. Prepare 1 hunk of butter
  2. Get 1 large onion
  3. Make ready 2 portobello mushrooms
  4. Make ready 5-10 crimini mushrooms
  5. Take 5-10 shiitake
  6. Get Oyster mushrooms if you want
  7. Make ready 24 oz beef broth
  8. Get half pint Heavy whipping cream or half and half
  9. Prepare Seasoning
  10. Get 1/4 cup flour
  11. Take 4 cloves garlic

Instructions to make Cream of Mushroom Soup:
  1. Put some butter in a cooking pot. And heat it up.
  2. Cut up your onions and toss those in. Season with some salt and pepper.
  3. Start cutting all your mushrooms and add those to the mixture in the pot. I like to dice some mushrooms and leave some bigger. Sauté this till desired texture.
  4. Add seasoning. I add different stuff sometimes like: cayenne, creole seasoning, oregano, bay leaf, Italian seasoning, sriracha, soy sauce.
  5. Once mixture is soft add in flour and then slowly Add in beef broth and cook for 20 min.
  6. Add in heavy whip. Cook for 3-5 minutes.
  7. Serve!
