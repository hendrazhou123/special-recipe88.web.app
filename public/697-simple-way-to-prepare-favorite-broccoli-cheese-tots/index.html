The ingredients needed to make Broccoli Cheese Tots:
  1. Make ready Medium sized Broccoli (florets only)
  2. Prepare medium carrot
  3. Take medium potato
  4. Prepare cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare breadcrumbs
  6. Take red chili flakes
  7. Prepare Salt as required (not too much as the cheese is salty as well)
  8. Take Oil for shallow frying

Instructions to make Broccoli Cheese Tots:
  1. Cut and boil broccoli and carrot together
  2. Strain keep aside. Repeat the same with potato
  3. Put them together in a good processor and pulse 4-5 times
  4. Add the grated cheese, breadcrumbs, salt and chili flakes
  5. Mix well
  6. Shape then in tots
  7. Freeze at least for 2 hours or more (some could be stored and used later)
  8. Shallow fry/Air Fry/Bake it
