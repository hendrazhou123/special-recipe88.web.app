The ingredients needed to make Dark Chocolate Cakey Brownies:
  1. Take 1/2 cup Dark Chocolate:
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Sugar
  4. Make ready 3 Eggs
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup Cocoa powder
  6. Prepare 1 cup Flour
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp Vanilla essence
  8. Get as required Butter to grease the baking dish

Instructions to make Dark Chocolate Cakey Brownies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Lightly grease a baking dish with a little butter and put aside.
  3. In a microwave-safe bowl, add butter and chocolate, microwave for 2 minutes, stirring every 20 seconds, until the ingredients have melted and are smooth.
  4. Add flour and sugar to this mixture and whisk till incorporated.
  5. Add the eggs, one by one and mix well.
  6. Pour this batter in the prepared baking dish. I added some swirls of Nutella to give it a nutty taste.
  7. Bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove from the oven and let it cool down completely. Cut into squares and enjoy these cakey brownies by themselves or with ice cream and chocolate sauce.
