The ingredients needed to make Vegetable Egg Omelet *Vegetarian:
  1. Prepare 3 eggs
  2. Make ready 1 carrot
  3. Get 1/2 red capsicum
  4. Get 1 spring onion
  5. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper
  7. Take 3 tbsp margarine

Steps to make Vegetable Egg Omelet *Vegetarian:
  1. Grate carrot, chop spring onion and red capsicum
  2. Mix all ingredient in a bowl, add salt and pepper.
  3. Pre-heat margarine in a saute pan, cook the egg mixture until both sides well done.
  4. Enjoy with sauce or rice. Its also perfect for sandwich.
