The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip muffin:
  1. Make ready 150 g butter
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 200 g sugar
  4. Take 350 g flour
  5. Take 250 milk
  6. Take 1 pack Yeast
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Salt
  9. Get Baking soda

Instructions to make Chocolate chip muffin:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 f
  2. Add all wet ingredients, melted butter, eggs, sugar, vanilla extract.
  3. Add all dry ingredients Flour, yeast, baking soda.
  4. Mix dry & wet ingredients.
  5. Separately add to a bowl a little flour toss chocolate chip cookies in it.
  6. Slowly add milk to mixture- should have a creamy texture.
  7. Pour in chocolate chips in batter and pour into muffin cups
  8. Place tray in the oven for 30minutes or less depending on until fork slides out of batter clean.
