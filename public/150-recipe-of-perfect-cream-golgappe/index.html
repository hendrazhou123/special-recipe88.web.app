The ingredients needed to make Cream Golgappe:
  1. Make ready 4-5 Golgappe
  2. Get 1/2 cup potatoes boiled
  3. Prepare 1 onion chopped
  4. Prepare 3 tsp lemon Juice
  5. Make ready to taste salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  8. Make ready 4-5 Golgappe pakodi
  9. Get as needed mayonnaise cream
  10. Get As needed sev for Garnishing

Instructions to make Cream Golgappe:
  1. In a bowl add potatoes.then add all spices and mix well.now add onion and lemon juice.keep aside.
  2. Now take a Golgappa and break little from upper side.fill it with potato filling.then add cream.
  3. Again add potato filling.then add cream.
  4. Keep pakodi and garnish with sev.
  5. Cream Golgappe's are ready to serve.
  6. Serve immediately.
