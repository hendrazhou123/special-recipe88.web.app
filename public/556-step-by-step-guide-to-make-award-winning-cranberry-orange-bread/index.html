The ingredients needed to make Cranberry Orange Bread:
  1. Get 2 cup flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1 tbs baking powder
  4. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Make ready rind of a orange
  6. Get 2/3 cup fresh orange juice
  7. Prepare 2 eggs,beaten
  8. Take 6 tbs butter(or margarine)melted
  9. Make ready 1 1/4 cup fresh cranberries
  10. Get 1/2 cup chopped walnuts

  3. Stir from the center until the ingredients are blended add the cranberries and walnuts,stir,
