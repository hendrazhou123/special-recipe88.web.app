  2. Make ready 1 1/4 Teaspoon baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  4. Take 1/2 Salt
  5. Prepare 2 Large eggs
  6. Take 3/4 Cups Sugar
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  8. Make ready 1/2 Cup Canola Oil
  9. Take 1/2 Cup buttermilk

Instructions to make Fluffy vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F and line a cupcake/muffin pan with cupcake liners.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together 1¼ flour , 1¼ tsp baking powder, ½ tsp baking soda and ½ tsp salt. Set flour mix aside.
  3. In the bowl of an electric mixer, beat 2 eggs with the whisk attachment on medium speed (15-20 seconds).
  4. Add vanilla and oil and beat on medium speed (1 minute)
