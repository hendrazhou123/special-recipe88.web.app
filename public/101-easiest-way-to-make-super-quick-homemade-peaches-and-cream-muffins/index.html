The ingredients needed to make Peaches and Cream Muffins:
  1. Get 1 cup chopped fresh or frozen peaches
  2. Prepare 1 egg
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup milk or sour cream
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup canola oil
  5. Get 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  6. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  7. Take 2 teaspoons baking powder
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt

Steps to make Peaches and Cream Muffins:
  1. In a bowl, beat egg; add milk (or sour cream) and oil.
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  3. Stir into the egg mixture just until moistened. Stir in peaches.
  4. Filled greased or paper-lined muffin cups three-fourths full.
  5. Bake at 400 degrees for 20-25 or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean.
  6. Cool for 5 minutes before removing from pan to a wire rack.
