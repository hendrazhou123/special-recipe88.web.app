Steps to make Peanut Clusters:
  1. Melt chocolate over a double boiler
  2. Add peanut butter and stir until melted
  3. Add rice krispies
  4. Remove from heat and rest for 5-10 minutes
  5. Add marshmallows and stir until evenly distributed
  6. Spoon out in heaps onto parchment paper and let cool completely
