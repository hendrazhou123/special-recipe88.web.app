The ingredients needed to make Sugar Free Jello Cheesecake (no crust):
  1. Take 2 pkgs Sugar Free Jello
  2. Get 1 cup water
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 cups whipped cream
  4. Take 3/4 cup cream cheese

Finally a cheesecake that is easy to make, low in fat and even has no added sugar. I used strawberry-kiwi jello (I'm not a big lemon fan) and added sliced fresh strawberries on top. I didn't notice any aftertaste from the sugar-free jello. I first made this using orange-flavored sugar-free Jell-O.

Steps to make Sugar Free Jello Cheesecake (no crust):
  1. Blend whipped cream and 1/4 cup of cream cheese.
  2. Boil water and dissolve jello. Add cream cheese, blend til smooth
  3. Slowly add jello mix to whipped cream. Blend til smooth.
  4. Pour into pie pan, ramekins, small bowls. Chill in fridge for 1 hour.
