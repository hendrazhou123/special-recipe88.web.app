The ingredients needed to make French Fries with numbing chilli salt:
  1. Prepare numbing chilli salt
  2. Take 4 green lemon peels dried in oven until hard not brown
  3. Prepare 1 tsp dried chilli flakes
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp pepper
  5. Make ready 2 tsp salt
  6. Take 1/2 tsp indian gooseberry pods
  7. Take Fries
  8. Get 1 kg potatoes with skin
  9. Prepare 1 oil for frying
  10. Make ready 1 salt to taste

Instructions to make French Fries with numbing chilli salt:
  1. Combine all the ingredients for numbing chilli salt and grind fine in a blender. Store in air tight container.
  2. Cut potatoes length wise as required for french fries. Place in cold water until needed.
  3. Heat oil for frying. Drain potatoes and pat well with a towel. Fry until golden.
  4. Toss with chilli salt. Serve immediately. Enjoy!
