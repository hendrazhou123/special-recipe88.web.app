Steps to make Sliced Cheese Cheesecake:
  1. Line a cake pan with parchment paper. (It's easier to line a cake pan if you secure it with pegs or paper clips.) Preheat the oven to 170℃.
  2. Tart Crust: Crush the biscuits in a food processor, and add melted butter. Please refer tofor detailed instructions. - - https://cookpad.com/us/recipes/147541-quick-simple-delicious-no-bake-tart-crust
  3. If you make this cake with the tart crust, pour the batter over it before baking.
  4. Batter: Mix all the ingredients in a blender, and it's ready Please shred the sliced cheese by hand before adding it to the blender with the rest of the ingredients. If you don't have a blender, follow the instructions in Step 7.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake pan, and bake in a preheated oven for 45-50 minutes.
  6. Chill the cake really well, and it's done.
  7. For those who don't have a blender or food processor: Step 1: Melt the milk, cheese and margarine in a double boiler. Step 2: Lightly mix the other ingredients with a hand mixer in a double boiler. Add Step 2 into Step 1, mix well, pour the batter into the cake pan, and bake in the oven.
