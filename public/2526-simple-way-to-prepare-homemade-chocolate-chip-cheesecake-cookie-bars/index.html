The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip cheesecake cookie bars:
  1. Prepare 1 package chocolate chip cookie dough
  2. Take 1 package regular cool whip
  3. Make ready 1 package brownie or Oreo cool whip
  4. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1 block cream cheese
  6. Take 1 package chocolate pudding instant
  7. Take Chocolate chips and Hershey’s syrup

Instructions to make Chocolate chip cheesecake cookie bars:
  1. Press cookie dough in a lightly greased pan and bake as directed. Let cool completely.
  2. Mix cream cheese, sugar and tub of regular cool whip and spread on top of cookie previously cooked and set aside
  3. Prepare instant pudding as directed on box and spread on top of the cream cheese cool whip mixture
  4. Spread the brownie or Oreo cool whip on top and drizzle with chocolate syrup and sprinkle with chocolate chips
