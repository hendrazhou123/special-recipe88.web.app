The ingredients needed to make Red velvet Cookie 🍪:
  1. Prepare 2 cups flour
  2. Take 200 g butter
  3. Prepare 1 large egg
  4. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp on baking soda
  6. Prepare 1 tbs red food color
  7. Get 1 tbs cocoa powder
  8. Get 1 tsp banana flavor
  9. Get 1/3 cup raisins/chocolate
