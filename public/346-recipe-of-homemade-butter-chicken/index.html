Steps to make Butter chicken:
  1. Take 1/2 cup yogurt in bowl. add salt red chilli powder and garam masala
  2. Add boneless chicken cubes
  3. Marinate for 30 minute.
  4. Add butter in a wok add chicken.
  5. Cover and cook for 6 to 8 minute or until tender and water evaporate.
  6. Now add oil in a pan add onion saute for 2 minute add ginger garlic paste cook for 1 minute.
  7. Add green chillies and spices. add some water cook for 1 minute with spices
  8. Add tomato puree cover and cook until mix and oil separate.
  9. Mix half cup cream with half cup milk
  10. Add in prepared masala.
  11. Cook until bubbling.
  12. Add cooked chicken mix and cook for 1 minute
  13. Turn off the heat
  14. Garnish with dry fenugreek leaves
