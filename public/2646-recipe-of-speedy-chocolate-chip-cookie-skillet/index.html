Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookie Skillet:
  1. Place cookie dough in a 5" iron skillet. (1 Large cookie scoop full and 1 small cookie scoop per skillet)
  2. Bake in iron skillet at 375 degrees for 15 minutes.
  3. Top hot cookie with 1 large scoop of vanilla ice cream, warm chocolate and caramel toppings.
  4. Serve while still hot from the oven.
