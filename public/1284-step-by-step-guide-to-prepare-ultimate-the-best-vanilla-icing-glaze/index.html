The ingredients needed to make The Best Vanilla Icing/Glaze:
  1. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp heavy cream
  3. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 2 cup confectioners sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
