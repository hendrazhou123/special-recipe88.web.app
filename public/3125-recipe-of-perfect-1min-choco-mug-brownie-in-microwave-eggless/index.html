  4. Prepare 3 tbsp milk

Steps to make 1Min Choco Mug Brownie in microwave (eggless):
  1. Take a mug and add all the dry ingredients(brown sugar,flour,cocoa powder).
  2. Then mix melted butter,vanilla essence,milk,stir into a paste consistency.
  3. Microwave it for 1.5 to 2mins. Then the brownie is ready to serve.
