The ingredients needed to make Chicken with milk and curry:
  1. Make ready 500 grams chicken
  2. Get 2 apples
  3. Take 4 nuts
  4. Prepare 1 shallot
  5. Take 2 tsp sweet curry
  6. Get 1 salt
  7. Take 1/2 liter milk

Steps to make Chicken with milk and curry:

  2. heat the oil in a pan; when it‘s hot, put the chopped shallot in the pan
  3. put the chicken in the pan and pour some milk
