The ingredients needed to make Green Tea Soy Milk Latte:
  1. Take 1 tbs matcha powder
  2. Take 1/4 glass water (125cc)
  3. Take 3/4 glass soy milk /almond milk (375cc)
  4. Take 1 tbs creamer (optional) (I used 2 tbs)
  5. Get 1 tbs sugar / honey (adjust to your sweetness level)
  6. Get (I used 2 tbs of sugar)
  7. Take glass with capacity around 500cc

Instructions to make Green Tea Soy Milk Latte:
  1. Boil 1/4 glass of water, add matcha powder, creamer, and sugar using low heat. Let them dissolve completely.
  2. Add soy milk, stir them well and boil it for a bit.
  3. Serve with ice or let it cool inside the fridge half day.
