The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake:
  1. Get 1 cup biscuit crumbs
  2. Get 1 cup milk
  3. Get 2 tbsp Cocoa powder
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp Sugar
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp butter
  6. Get Pinch Salt
  7. Take 1/2 tbsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tbsp baking powder
  9. Take As Required Chocolate for garnish and some choco chips

Steps to make Chocolate cake:
  1. Grind biscuit in a mixer
  2. Add all dry ingredients and mix with out lumps
  3. In other pan add all wet ingredients then mix baking powder and baking soda.
  4. Now add dry ingredients with the help of spatula not enough.
  5. In the cake mould brush oil add put butter paper pour the mixture into the container and tap it so that no lumps.
  6. Put the cake tin in pre heated microwave at 180 degrees for 30 minute let it check with knife.
  7. Remove it and garnish with ganache and choco chips and chocolate
  8. Put it 2 hour in the refrigerator and enjoy
