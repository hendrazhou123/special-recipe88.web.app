The ingredients needed to make Super Easy Pumpkin 🎃Pie:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup Bisquick mix
  2. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 cup evaporated milk
  4. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  5. Get 1 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  6. Take 1 tablespoon margarine, softened
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  8. Prepare 2 eggs
  9. Prepare Butter cooking spray
  10. Prepare Whipped topping or ice cream

Steps to make Super Easy Pumpkin 🎃Pie:
  1. In a large bowl whisk together Bisquick, sugar, evaporated milk,margarine, pumpkin pie spice,cinnamon powder, vanilla extract and your 2 eggs…..Mixture will be smooth…..
  2. Spray a 9 inch pie pan with the butter cooking spray……
  3. Pour wet mixture into pie pan…..
  4. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 40 minutes……
  5. Let cool for about 30 minutes, or refrigerate overnight, serve with my homemade whipped topping and enjoy 😉!
