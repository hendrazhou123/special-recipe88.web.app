The ingredients needed to make French Vanilla Smore Mountains:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Mini Marshmallows
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Milk Chocolate Chips
  3. Make ready 1 tsp Milk Chocolate Cocoa
  4. Get 1/2 tsp Milk Chocolate Cocoa
  5. Take 1/4 cup Crushed Gramm Crackers
  6. Get 6 tbsp Crushed Gramm Crackers

  9. Get 1/4 cup Granulated Sugar
  10. Get 1/2 cup Brown Sugar
  11. Get 4 tbsp Salted Butter
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup Skim Milk
  13. Make ready 3 cup Quick Oats
  14. Get 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  15. Get 6 tbsp Whipped Cream

Instructions to make French Vanilla Smore Mountains:
  1. Cream together the butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and 1/4 cup of the gram Crackers.
  2. Mix in the coca, drink mix, and milk.
  3. Bring this mixture to a boil. Hold the boil for 1minute.
  4. Let cool. Then mix in the vanilla, Marshmallows, chocolate chips, and oats.
  5. Form this mix into 6 large '' Mountains '' on a cookie sheet. Place the tray into the freezer for about 30 minutes.
  6. Top each mountain with 1 tablespoon of cream, and crushed Gram Crackers, and 1 treaspoon of syrup.
