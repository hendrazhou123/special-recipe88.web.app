The ingredients needed to make Pumpkin butter:
  1. Get 1 can pureed pumpkin
  2. Take 1 cup applesauce
  3. Prepare 2 tb honey
  4. Get 1 tsp grated lemon zest
  5. Get 2 tsp pumpkin spice
  6. Prepare to taste molasses (optional, I used just a touch)
