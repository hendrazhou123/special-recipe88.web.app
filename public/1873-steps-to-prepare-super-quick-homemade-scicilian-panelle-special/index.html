  3. Take 1 pinch salt

  5. Take 1 lb fresh ricotta cheese
  6. Get 1 cup shredded pecorino romano cheese
  7. Prepare 4 fresh bread rolls

Instructions to make Scicilian Panelle Special:
  1. In pot add chickpea flour & water and pinch of salt & wisk well
  2. put on medium flame & continue wisking…do not stop until batter thickens
  3. once batter thickens remove from flame & spread on wet marble or formica top…spread out evenly to a 1/4 inch thickness ..try to make a square
  4. when batter cools cut into squares the size of your rolls
  5. fry the patties in a frying pan with the oil of your choice until golden brown on both sides..u will have to flip them in frying pan
  6. remove & set aside to cool some
  7. cut roll in half & put 3 panelle patties on roll…add generous scoop of fresh ricotta on top of patties & sprinkle shredded cheese on top of ricotta
  8. bake sandwich in oven until cheese melts & bread is lil toasty
  9. eat & enjoy
