The ingredients needed to make Halloween Sugar Monster Cookies:

  2. Get 1 cup sugar (Group 1 Ingredient)

  6. Take 3 cups AP flour (Group 3 Ingredient)
  7. Make ready 3/4 tsp Baking Powder (Group 3 Ingredient)
  8. Get Several Jars of Sugar Sprinkles (Group 4 Ingredient)
  9. Get A Few Pkgs of Different Size Wilton Eyes (Group 5 Ingredient)

Steps to make Halloween Sugar Monster Cookies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375 F. Add Group 1 ingredients into the mixer on low speed
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk the Group 2 ingredients, and add to the mixer (still on low).
  3. Sift the Group 3 ingredients, and then add to the mixer (still on low), and mix until homogenous.
  4. Roll the cookies into 1 1/2 inch diameter balls, roll each ball in a bowl of the colored sprinkles (aka Group 5), and place in a parchment-lined baking sheet.
  5. Bake 8 to 10 minutes, to your liking, and then add various combinations of candy eyes (aka Group 5) to the cookies while the cookies are still warm. Let rest 10 minutes on a cookie rack and serve.
