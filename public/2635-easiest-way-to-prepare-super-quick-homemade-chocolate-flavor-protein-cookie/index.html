The ingredients needed to make Chocolate flavor protein cookie:
  1. Take 1/2 cup protein powder
  2. Take 1 cup oats powder
  3. Make ready 3 spoons sugar
  4. Prepare 2 spoons peanut butter
  5. Take Few spoons Milk to knead the dough
  6. Prepare 1 egg or 1 spoon veg egg replacement powder as preferred
