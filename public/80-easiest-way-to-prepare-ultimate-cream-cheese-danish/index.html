Instructions to make Cream Cheese Danish:
  1. Preheat oven to 400. Beat cream cheese and sugar til smooth. Add egg. Beat til combined
  2. Leaving an edge, spread spoonful of cream cheese mixture then a spoonful of pie filling on top of that to each rectangle. Brush edges with cream and dust with sugar.
  3. Bake about 15-20 minutes or til edges are puffed and brown. Allow to cool before storing.
