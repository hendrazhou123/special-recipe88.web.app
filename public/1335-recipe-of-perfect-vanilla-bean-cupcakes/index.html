The ingredients needed to make vanilla bean cupcakes:
  1. Get 1 cup whole milk, room temp
  2. Take 1 1/2 tsp vanilla bean paste
  3. Make ready 2 1/2 cup flour
  4. Take 1 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 4 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  7. Take 12 tbsp butter
  8. Make ready 50 cupcake tins
  9. Make ready fronting
  10. Take 24 oz wipped cream cheese
  11. Take 1 powder sugar to tast
  12. Prepare 2 1/3 tsp vanilla bean past
  13. Prepare 1 2/3 cup heavy wipping cream

Steps to make vanilla bean cupcakes:
  1. pre heat oven to 325
  2. cream butter,sugar and salt together
  3. add vanilla paste
  4. add this mixture to all other ingredients
  5. fill each each cupcake tin 3/4 full
  6. place in the oven till golden brown
  7. Fronting
  8. place 2 stainless steel bowel in freezer for 1 hour
  9. to one bowel add cream cheese add half of vanilla bean paste and powder sugar to taste
  10. to the second bowl add the heavy whipping cream, vanilla bean past , and sugar to taste
  11. whip both until thick and then combine
  12. after cupcakes have cool for 1 hours pipe on fronting
