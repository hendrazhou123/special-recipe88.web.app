The ingredients needed to make Swedish nut cake:
  1. Make ready Cake
  2. Get 2 c flour
  3. Take 2 c sugar
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 2 eggs, beaten
  6. Make ready 2 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 1 can(s) (20 oz.) crushed pineapple, undrained
  8. Take 1/2 c chopped walnuts or pecans
  9. Make ready Icing
  10. Get 8 oz pkg. cream cheese
  11. Get 1 stick butter
  12. Take 1 1/3 c powdered sugar
  13. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  14. Take 1/2 c chopped walnuts or pecans

Instructions to make Swedish nut cake:
  1. Mix all cake ingredients together by hand. Pour into ungreased, unfloured 9 x 13" pan. Bake at 350 degrees for 30-35 minutes. Usually 30 minutes do not over cook it will be dry.
  2. Mix together icing ingredients with electric mixer. Spread over hot cake; sprinkle with more nuts if wanted. Refrigerate immediately.
  3. Serve and enjoy!
