Instructions to make Pumpkin Vanilla Cookies:

  3. in another bowl soften butter and combine wet ingredients
  4. slowly stir wet ingredients into dry ingredients. texture should be similar to biscuit dough
  5. spoon onto cookie sheet and bake for 12 minutes. then allow to cool.
