The ingredients needed to make Mini Baked Chocolate Glazed Donuts:
  1. Take 1 cup flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Take Pinch salt
  6. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 1 large egg
  8. Get 6 tbsp full fat sour cream
  9. Prepare 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  11. Prepare 1 cup powder sugar
  12. Make ready 3 tbsp milk
  13. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make Mini Baked Chocolate Glazed Donuts:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F and spray mini donut pan with nonstick spray.
  2. In a small bowl combine flour, sugar, cocoa powder baking soda and salt. Set aside
  3. In a medium Bowl whisk together egg, sour cream, oil, 1/4 cup milk and vanilla until smooth.
  4. Stir in the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients until JUST combined. Don't overmix
  5. Using a Piping Bag or a Ziploc bag, fill each mini donut well about halfway.
  6. Baked in the oven for 8 minutes, or until a toothpick runs clean when inserted.
  7. Combined three tablespoons of milk, with one cup of powdered sugar and vanilla extract. Dip each donut, set on wire rack to cool completely
