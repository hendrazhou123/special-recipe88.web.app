The ingredients needed to make Milk Chocolate Mint Chip Cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup unsalted cocoa powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 3.5 ounces good quality milk chocolate, such as Lindt
  6. Make ready Exellence Extra Creamy Milk Chicolate, chopoed
  7. Prepare 4 ounces (1 stick) unsalted butter at room temperature
  8. Take 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  9. Prepare 2 large eggs
  10. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  11. Make ready 1 1/4 cups Andies Peppermint chips

Instructions to make Milk Chocolate Mint Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat the ove to 325. Line baking sheets with parchment paper
  2. In a bowl whisk together flour, cocoa powder baking soda and salt
  3. Melt the butter and milk chocolate in the microwave until smooth. Cool to room temperatur
  5. In a large bowl add sugar, eggs, vanilla and cooled chocolate mixture, beat until combined
  6. Slowly mix in flour mixture until incorporaed
  7. Mix in peppermint chips
  9. Roll 8nto 1 inch balls and place 2 inches apart on prepared baking sheets. Bake 12 to 15 minutrs until flat and cookies surface appears just set.
