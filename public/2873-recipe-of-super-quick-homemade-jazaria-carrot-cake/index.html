The ingredients needed to make “Jazaria” Carrot Cake:
  1. Make ready Cake
  2. Take 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  3. Take 1 cup light brown sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup regular white sugar
  5. Get 3 Large eggs (4 Medium)
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  7. Make ready 2 Cups all-purpose flour sifted
  8. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  10. Get 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  11. Take 1/2 tsp ground allspice (optional)
  12. Prepare 2 cups loosely packed, finely grated carrots
  13. Make ready 3/4 cup lightly toasted walnut pieces (you can replace with raisins)
  14. Prepare Frosting Ingredients
  15. Make ready 225 g cream cheese at room temperature
  16. Take 1/4 cup unsalted butter at room temperature
  17. Get 3 cup icing sugar “sifted”
  18. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  19. Make ready 1 tsp lemon juice
  20. Make ready Lightly roasted walnuts pieces for garnish

Instructions to make “Jazaria” Carrot Cake:
  1. Whisk your wet ingredients with the sugars.
  2. Sift all your dry ingredients and add the carrots with the dry mix.
  3. Mix your dry to the wet mix. Then add roasted walnuts and mix again.
  4. Divide the mix in two equal size pans and bake for 28-30 mins at 175c (350f). Make sure when you insert a skewer it comes out clean.
  5. Add three frost mix between your cake layers and in top and on sides. Garnish with roasted walnuts and refrigerate for 90 mins. Enjoy after
