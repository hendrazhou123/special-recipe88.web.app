The ingredients needed to make Dump cake:
  1. Get 1 can cherry filling
  2. Get 1 can crushed pineapple
  3. Make ready 2 sticks butter not salted
  4. Make ready 1 yellow cake mix

Steps to make Dump cake:
  1. 9x13' pan stove to 350
  2. Drain pineapple and spread in pan next cherry filling
  3. Add cake mix to top spread out evenly as possible
  4. Cut butter into 1 tsp sized and place all over the top of the cake
  5. Bake till golden brown the darker the crispier it will be I prefer a well mix of brown edges and a golden center
  6. Put over vanilla ice cream or just enjoy as is
