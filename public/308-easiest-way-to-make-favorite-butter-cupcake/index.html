The ingredients needed to make Butter cupcake:
  1. Take 500 g flour
  2. Get 300 g unsalted butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon baking powder
  4. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
  5. Take 7 eggs
  6. Make ready Vanilla
  7. Take 3 tablespoons whole milk
