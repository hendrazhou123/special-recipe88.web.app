The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 c flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1/2 cup butter
  5. Make ready 1 cup granulated sugar
  6. Take 3/4 cup milk
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla

Steps to make Vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Sift dry ingredients and set aside
  2. Mix on high until light and fluffy butter and sugar
  3. Add eggs one at a time and blend
  4. Add dry ingredients 3 parts alternating with the milk
  5. Bake at 180° for 15 mins
