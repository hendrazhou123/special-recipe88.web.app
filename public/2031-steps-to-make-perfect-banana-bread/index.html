The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 2 bananas
  2. Take 1/4 cup honey
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp almond milk
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 2 cup whole wheat flour
  7. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup Greek yoghurt

  1. Put all the ingredients into the bowl

  3. Preheat the oven to 350F

  5. Put the mixture into the loaf pan
  6. Bake for about 45 minutes
