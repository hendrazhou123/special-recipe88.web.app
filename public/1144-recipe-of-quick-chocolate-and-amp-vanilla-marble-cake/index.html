The ingredients needed to make Chocolate & Vanilla Marble Cake:
  1. Take 250 grams Butter (at room temp)
  2. Take 2 1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
  3. Get 1 cup Sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Buttermilk
  5. Take 2 1/2 teaspoons Baking Powder
  6. Get 1/4 teaspoon Baking Soda
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon Salt
  8. Get 2 tablespoon Vanilla Extract
  9. Make ready 1/4 cup Milk
  10. Get 4 Eggs
  11. Make ready 200 grams Pure Chocolate (Bittersweet) or Semi Sweet Chocolate
