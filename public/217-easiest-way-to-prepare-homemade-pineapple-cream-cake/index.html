The ingredients needed to make Pineapple cream cake:
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Prepare 1 cup all-purpose flour
  3. Get 1 cup caster sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  6. Take tin pack Pineapple chunks with juice
  7. Get For frosting
  8. Get Whipped cream
  9. Prepare few drops Pink food color

Instructions to make Pineapple cream cake:
  1. Beat eggs with sugar by electric beater on high speed for 5 to 6 mints or until foamy texture
  2. Add vanilla essence and mix well
  3. Now add flour and baking powder (sifted together) and fold gently.
  4. Pour into 8/8 inch greased mold and bake on 180 c for about 20 to 25 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  5. When cake sponge cool completely
  6. Cut the sponge in 2 layers and soak with pineapple juice, spread some whipped cream evenly put pineapple chunks.
  7. Cover with 2nd layer again soak with pineapple juice and cover the whole cake with whipped cream evenly
  8. In remaining cream add pink food color and mix well
  9. Fill the piping bag with pink cream and decorate the whole cake.
  10. Serve chilled.
