The ingredients needed to make Chilled, Green Tea, Soba Noodles with Mandarin Orange:
  1. Get 10 oz Green Tea Soba Noodles
  2. Get 1 clove, rough chopped garlic
  3. Take 1 tsp soy sauce
  4. Make ready 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  5. Prepare 1 tsp sciracha sauce
  6. Take 1/2 cup mandarin oranges
  7. Make ready 1 tsp chili flake
  8. Make ready 2 tsp simple syrup
  9. Get 1 tbsp chopped, pickled ginger
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp fresh mandarin orange or tangerine juice

Instructions to make Chilled, Green Tea, Soba Noodles with Mandarin Orange:
  1. Boil noodles to Al Dente …
  2. Strain noodles immediately.
  3. Put noodles in a bowl and cover in ice to chill and put in fridge.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix garlic, soy sauce, sesame oil, sciracha sauce, mandarin oranges, orange juice, chili flake, simple syrup and, pickled ginger and MIX WELL. Let sit for 15 minutes room temp.
  5. Get a presentation bowl or plate ready.
  6. Twirl noodles with a large fork and transfer to final bowl for serving.
  7. Spoon over the fruit and sauce mixture on top of the noodles.
  8. Optional seaweed, dried egg yolk and bonito flakes for extra garnish.
