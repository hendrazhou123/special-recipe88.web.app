The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. Take 1/4 cup unsalted butter (room temperature)
  2. Take 1/4 cup+2tbsp castor sugar
  3. Take 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Prepare 1 pinch salt
  7. Take 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. Into a big bowl, cream together butter and sugar untill it's light in colour, soft and fluffy. You can either use hand whisk or electric beater.
  2. Now seive all purpose flour, baking powder and salt into this bowl. Mix everything using spatula.
  3. Lastly add vanilla extract and choco chips and mix well using your hands. Make the cookie dough gently.
  4. Preheat oven to 180 °C and line baking tray with parchment paper.
  5. Divide the cookie dough into equal sized balls using hands.
  6. Place them on cookie sheet and slightly press to give them a cookie/peda shape. Top them with some more chocolate chips.
  7. Pop it in oven and bake for 15 mins.
  8. Let it rest for 10-12 mins once they are baked and then transfer to cooling rack. Serve and enjoy.
