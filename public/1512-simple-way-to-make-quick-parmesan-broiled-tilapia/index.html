The ingredients needed to make Parmesan Broiled Tilapia:

  2. Get 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese
  3. Make ready 2 tbls butter
  4. Take 1 1/2 tbls mayo
  5. Take 1 tbls lemon juice
  6. Get 1 tsp dill
