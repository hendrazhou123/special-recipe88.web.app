The ingredients needed to make Butter scones:
  1. Make ready 210 g cake flour
  2. Prepare 80 g butter
  3. Prepare pinch salt
  4. Get 2 tbsp sugar
  5. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 50 ml whole milk

Instructions to make Butter scones:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients. Then add cold butter and use pastry cutter to blend butter in flour until observe sand-like mixture.
  2. Add milk and mix the dough gently.
  3. Knead the dough until well combined but do not over mixed.
  4. Roll the dough to 3/4 inch thick, use cookie cutter 2 inch diameter to cut the dough (make sure do not turn your cookie cutter)
  5. Take scrap dough and mix it with cocoa powder for the pug face decoration.
  6. Bake at 190C for 15 minute
