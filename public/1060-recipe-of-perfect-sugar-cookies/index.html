The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 cup white sugar
  2. Take 1/2 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 2 1/2 cup flour
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup butter, ONE STICK
  5. Prepare 2 each eggs
  6. Take 1 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  9. Get 1 tsp nutmeg (optional)

Steps to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Mix butter and sugars together until creamy
  2. Add eggs and vanilla extract
  3. Mix flour, baking soda, (nutmeg if desired), and salt together. Add to wet mixture.
  4. Mix well, cover and refrigerate for at least one hour.
  5. Scoop out small amount and roll into a ball. Roll in sugar (and cinnamon for snickerdoodle) and place onto parchment lined cookie sheets. (if no parchment, I simply buttered the sheet after each use.)
  6. Bake at 350° until lightly brown (approximately 11 minutes), let cool for two minutes on cookie sheet.
  7. Enjoy.
