Instructions to make Simple Milk Chocolate Souffle:
  1. preheat oven 400
  2. butter eight 7oz ramekins and coat the inside with sugar
  3. combine chocolate and cream in a large glass bowl over barley simmering water and stir until smooth
  4. add egg yolks and salt to mixture and set aside to cool
  5. poor egg whites into seperate bowl and beat with an electric mixer until soft peaks form on the end of the beaters when you lift them
  6. them gradually add sugar to egg whites until peaks become sturdy
  7. using a plastic spatula fold 1/4 of egg whites into chocolate mix until light brown
  8. then repeat with the rest in 2 lots do it gently so not to break the air bubbles
  9. poor into ramekins a half inch bellow the top
  10. cook for 14-18 mins check frequently until raised and the tops firm
  11. top with powdered sugar (optional )
