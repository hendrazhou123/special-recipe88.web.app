The ingredients needed to make Famous Peanut Butter Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 4 teaspoons baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 1 1/3 cup milk
  6. Take 1/2 cup peanut butter
