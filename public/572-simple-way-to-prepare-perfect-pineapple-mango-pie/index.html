The ingredients needed to make Pineapple Mango Pie:
  1. Take 1 (20 oz) can mango, undrained
  2. Get 1 (20 oz) can crushed pineapple, undrained
  3. Get 3 tbsp cornstarch
  4. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 2/3 cup brown sugar
  6. Take 1 tbsp vanilla
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice
  8. Get 2 tbsp butter or margarine
  9. Take 2 refrigerated pie crusts
  10. Prepare 1 egg, beaten with a splash of milk
  11. Make ready Sugar for sprinkling

Steps to make Pineapple Mango Pie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Mix first 8 ingredients in a saucepan and bring to a boil on med/high heat. Turn down heat and allow it to bubble with the lid on for a couple of minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Take filling off the heat and allow to cool for about 20 minutes. You want the filling this thick:
  3. While filling cools, line your pie dish with bottom crust. Pour cooled pie filling into crust and top with top crust. Pinch edges and brush with egg/milk mixture then sprinkle with sugar. Cut slits in top and bake for 45-60 minutes, until crust is golden.
  4. Wait until the pie is COMPLETELY cooled before cutting!
