The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. Take 115 g butter
  2. Prepare 100 g sugar
  3. Prepare 1 egg
  4. Get 1 tsp vinegar
  5. Prepare 11/4 cups flour
  6. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Get Chocolate Chips
  8. Take Blueberries
