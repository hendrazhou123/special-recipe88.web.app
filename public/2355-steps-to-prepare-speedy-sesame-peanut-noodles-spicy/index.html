Steps to make Sesame Peanut noodles, spicy:
  1. Boil your pasta according to directions. I'm trying a microwave pasta cooker here. Note - first time using the microwave pasta cooker, it worked really good.
  2. I'm using Broccolini here. I have seen it called baby broccoli also.
  3. Chop your veggie. If boiling your pasta, add your veggie 3 minutes before done. I microwaved mine separately, you could also blanch or steam.
  4. Mix all the sauce ingredients in a blender and blend until smooth.
  5. Stir your veggies into the pasta
  6. Add sauce to your pasta, stir it in.
  7. Serve. Garnish as desired. I'm using chopped green onions and sesame seeds here.
