The ingredients needed to make Strawberry cupcakes with strawberry buttercream:
  1. Make ready Cupcakes
  2. Take 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 1/2 becel olive oil margarine @ room temperature
  6. Make ready 1 cup white sugar
  7. Prepare 1 egg

  9. Prepare 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  10. Take 1/4 cup original almond milk
  11. Get 1 cup finely chopped strawberries
  12. Prepare Frosting
  13. Take 12 tablespoon becel olive oil margarine
  14. Make ready 2 cups powdered sugar
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup frozen strawberries
  16. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps to make Strawberry cupcakes with strawberry buttercream:
  1. Pre-heat oven at 350F
  2. Mix sifted dry ingredients except sugar
  3. With a electric mixer on medium beat butter until light, slowly add sugar, on slow- add eggs, vanilla extract and milk
  4. Fold chopped strawberries
  5. Bake 18-20 mins, let cool
  6. For the frosting - using food processor or ninja blender puree frozen strawberries
  7. Beat butter until light and fluffy then gradually add sugar, add vanilla extract lastly add puréed strawberries - adjust according to taste or color.
  8. Let it cool in the fridge for half an hour or until cupcakes are completely cold. Pipe then add half fresh strawberry for decoration
