The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Butter
  2. Prepare 1 cup Peanut Butter
  3. Make ready 2 cups Sugar
  4. Make ready 2 Eggs
  5. Get 2 cups Flour
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Baking Soda
  7. Make ready 2 cups Chocolate Chips
