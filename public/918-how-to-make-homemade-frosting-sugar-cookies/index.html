The ingredients needed to make Frosting sugar cookies:
  1. Make ready 4 cups All purpose flour
  2. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  3. Get 1/3 cup corn starch
  4. Prepare 2 egg
  5. Get 1 cup sugar
  6. Get 1 cup butter
  7. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare For frosting
  9. Take 1 cup Butter
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup Cream
  11. Prepare 3 cups Icing Sugar
  12. Prepare Few drop vanilla essence
  13. Take Few drop pink food colour

Steps to make Frosting sugar cookies:
  1. Beat together butter and sugar.
  2. Add eggs one be one and keep beating.
  3. Swift flour, salt and corn strach.
  4. Now ads flour mixture in egg mixture.
  5. Beat until soft dough forms. Wrap in cling and refrigerate it for 20 mins
  6. Cut desired shapes from cookie cutter.
  7. Set on oven tray.
  8. Keep in preheated oven for 12 mins on 180 degrees.
  9. For butter cream frosting: in a bowl beat butter until Creamy. Now add sugar and beat.
  10. Now add cream and beat and remember to scrap down your bowl from time to time. Im the end add pink food colour and vanilla extract. Mix and keep refrigerated until use.
