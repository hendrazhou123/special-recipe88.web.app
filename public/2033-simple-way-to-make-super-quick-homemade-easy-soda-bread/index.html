Instructions to make Easy Soda Bread!!!:
  1. Pre heat oven 180c (fan)
  2. Prepare baking tray with baking paper
  3. Mix all dry ingredients in a large bowl
  4. Add milk slowly and yogart also, combine together…
  5. Pop onto a floured board, and squish together, don't over work it, and add more flour if too wet…
  6. Make into one large round flat ball,,,, score with a "X" in middle… sprinkle a little flour on top!!!!
  7. Pop in oven on middle shelf, for 25-30minutes !! Leave to cool for 5 minutes and cut up and eat warm with home made jam (see my other recipe)
