The ingredients needed to make Corn Casserole:
  1. Take 16 ounce can of cream of corn
  2. Get 32 ounces can whole kernel corn
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup evaporated milk
  4. Make ready 1/2 stick margarine or butter
  5. Make ready 1/4 cup cornstarch
  6. Prepare 5 large eggs
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon salt
