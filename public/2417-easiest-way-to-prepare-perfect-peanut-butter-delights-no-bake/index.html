The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Delights (No Bake):
  1. Take 4 cup granulated sugar
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup cocoa
  3. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  5. Take 1/2 lb butter
  6. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla
  7. Take 1 cup milk
  8. Prepare 4 cup quick oats
  9. Take Variations (Not Required)
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup coconut
  11. Take 1/2 cup raisins

Steps to make Peanut Butter Delights (No Bake):
  1. Mix the sugar, cocoa, milk and butter in a medium to large pot.
  2. Bring the contents of the pot to a full boil, and let it boil heavily for 1 minute.
  3. Remove from heat and add the peanut butter, oats and vanilla (add the coconut or raisins if you chose a variation).
  4. Mix all ingredients thoroughly.
  5. Create clumps or balls of the mixture using about 2 tbsp per cookie. Place cookies on wax paper or another nonstick surface and let stand until firm and cool.
