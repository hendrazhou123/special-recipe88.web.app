The ingredients needed to make Cheese Roll:
  1. Take 8-10 bread slices
  2. Get 2 cheese cubes grated
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp capsicum chopped
  4. Get 2 tbsp onion chopped
  5. Take 2-3 green chillies
  6. Prepare 1 cup gram flour
  7. Prepare 1 tbsp coriander leaves
  8. Make ready to taste Salt
  9. Get as needed oil for frying
