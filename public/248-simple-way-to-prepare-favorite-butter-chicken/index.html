The ingredients needed to make Butter Chicken:
  1. Take 1 kg Chicken breasts, cut in cubes
  2. Prepare 1 cup tomato purée
  3. Get 1 cup heavy cream
  4. Prepare 1.5 cup white onion, chopped
  5. Take 3 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  6. Take Softened butter
  7. Get Olive oil
  8. Get Salt and pepper
  9. Make ready Cayenne pepper
  10. Prepare Cumin
  11. Get Turmeric
  12. Make ready Chilli powder
  13. Get Paprika
  14. Get 3 tsp Garam Masala
  15. Take 1 small cinnamon stick

Instructions to make Butter Chicken:
  1. In a bowl, season the chicken. Add salt and pepper, 1tsp chilli powder, 1/2tsp turmeric, 1tsp paprika and 1tbsp olive oil. Mix well
  2. In a pan, melt 2 tbsp butter and cook the chicken until golden brown colour. Put aside in a bowl.
  3. In the same pan, melt 2 tbsp butter with 1tbsp olive oil and add onion then garlic. Season with salt and pepper, 3tsp garam masala, 1tsp chilli powder, 1tsp cumin, 1tsp cayenne, 1tsp turmeric and 1 cinnamon stick.
  4. When they reach a golden colour, add the tomato puree with 1 cup of water and the heavy cream. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Add the chicken and let simmer for 10-15 minutes. Add 2tbsp butter in the process. Don't forget to stir from time to time and put a lid on the pan.
  6. Serve with white rice on the side and sprinkle with parsley.
  7. Tip: for extra taste, add some chicken broth to the rice.
