The ingredients needed to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Get 2 cups sugar
  2. Take 1 cup butter (2 sticks), softened
  3. Take 2 cups flour
  4. Get 5 eggs
  5. Make ready 6 Tbsp. milk
  6. Get 2 tsp. baking soda
  7. Get 1 1/2 cups chopped ripe banana (about 3 bananas)
  8. Get 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps to make Banana Nut Bread:
  1. Place all ingredients into mixer.
  2. Blend well; allow to sit at room temperature for 1 hour.
  3. Split equally among 2 greased bread pans.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 50 minutes. Allow to completely cool.
  5. Once cooked wrap in plastic wrap and place in ziplock bag. I store mine in fridge and freeze some for later.
  6. Enjoy!
