Instructions to make Apple Dates Milkshake(No sugar):
  1. Wash & clean the apples and remove skin if required. But it is good to prepare milkshake with skin.
  2. Cut the apples into small pieces and put them into a blender or mixer jar. Then add 2-3 dates.
  3. Add 1 cup cold milk and 1/4 water(optional). You can add only milk also.
  4. Blend it to smooth juice texture for 2-3 mins, transfer it to a glass and serve chilled. No need to filter also.
