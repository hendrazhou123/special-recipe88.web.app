Steps to make Cinnamon Sugar Shortbread Cookies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350 line a cookie sheet with parchment parr
  2. In a bowl beat butter and sugar until well bleded
  3. Add flour salt and cinnamon and beat until if comes together
  4. Formonto ball
  5. Take portions of dough and roll into 1 inch balls
  6. Make cinnamon sugar
  7. Combine sugar and cinnamon in a shallow bowl
  8. Dip each dough ball in egg wash
  9. Roll I'm cinnamon sugar
  10. Place on prepared pan. Flatten tops with the palm of your hand and center a chewy red hot in each cookie. Bake 11 to 14 minutes until golden and set. Cool 2 minutes then transfer to racks to cool compleatly
