The ingredients needed to make Vanilla frappe:
  1. Make ready 1 cup water
  2. Take 1.5 tbsp coffee
  3. Take 2 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Get 1/4 tsp cocoa powder
  6. Prepare to taste whipped cream
  7. Take to taste sprinkles
  8. Get to taste chocolate
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup milk
  10. Take to taste ice

Instructions to make Vanilla frappe:
  1. First add one cup of water to the pan and let it boil
  2. When water boiled nicely close the stove and add the coffee,coco powder and vanilla essence
  3. Mix nicely and boil again, when done add in a cup and leave in the fridge for 5-10 minutes (depends on you fridge temperature)
  4. Add ice in you cup
  5. Add the coffee mixture
  6. Add your half a cup of milk
  7. And serve with whipped cream and chocolate and sprincke ENJOY 🙂
