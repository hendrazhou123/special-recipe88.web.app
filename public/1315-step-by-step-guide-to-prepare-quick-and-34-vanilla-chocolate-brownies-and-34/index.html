The ingredients needed to make "Vanilla Chocolate Brownies":
  1. Get Ingredients
  2. Make ready 1 pack or 2 cups - own choice powdered chocolate or cocoa powder
  3. Make ready 1 cup - water
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp - oil
  5. Get 1 pc - egg (whole)
  6. Get 4 packs - Vanilla cream o cookies or Oreo :
  7. Take 1 cup - white sugar
Instructions to make "Vanilla Chocolate Brownies":
  1. Pre-heat the oven at 180°c to 200°c. Grease the baking pan with butter side by side then set aside. Crash the Vanilla cookies into thin pieces and place it in a different bowl.
  2. In a bowl mix the sugar and the water, stir it with wire whisk occasionally, then add the egg whisk it until the sugar dissolve into the water and egg.
  3. Now add the powdered chocolate and whisk it until the chocolate mixed and you will have a smoothy brown thick texture.
  4. Now add the crushed Vanilla cookies into the brown mixture and mix it together. And place it into the baking pan cover it with foil on top so you can prevent it from burning the top of your brownies cookies.
  5. Enjoy and Eat!! :) HAPPY EATING!!
