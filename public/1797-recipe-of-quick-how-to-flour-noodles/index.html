Steps to make How to: Flour Noodles:
  1. Place flour into a bowl.
  2. Place the eggs (scrambled) on top of the flour.
  3. Mix. Add olive oil if it is too dry.
  4. Once the egg and flour are mixed: place the noodle flour into saran wrap. Let it sit for 20 minutes.
  5. Flatten the noodles on floured cutting board.
  6. Use a pizza cutter to slice the noodles.
  7. Place into boiling water. Cook for about five minutes… Or as desired.
