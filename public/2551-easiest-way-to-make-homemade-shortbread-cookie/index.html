The ingredients needed to make Shortbread Cookie:
  1. Make ready 225 grms Cold Butter
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Powdered Sugar
  3. Make ready 2 cups Whole wheat flour

Steps to make Shortbread Cookie:
  1. Whisk the butter till creamy
  2. Add sugar and whisk well for 7 to 10 mins
  3. Gradually add flour and whisk well. Use all the flour till a soft dough is formed. Refrigerate for 30 mins
  4. Take the dough and place between two butter paper and roll the dough
  5. Cut desired shapes with the help of cookie cutter and then remove the scrapes and reroll
  6. Make holes so the cookies don't puff up.Bake them in preheated oven at 180 degree Celsius for 10 to 15 mins
  7. Let the cookies cool on a rack till cool
