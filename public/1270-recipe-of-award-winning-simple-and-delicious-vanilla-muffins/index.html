The ingredients needed to make Simple and Delicious Vanilla Muffins:
  1. Take baked goods
  2. Get 1 1/4 cup self raising flour
  3. Prepare 1 cup caster sugar
  4. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 cup butter or vegetable oil
  8. Prepare 4 eggs
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup milk

Steps to make Simple and Delicious Vanilla Muffins:
  1. First, mix all the dry ingredients.
  2. Add the wet ingredients one by one by one.
  3. Mix all ingredients on medium speed and gradually bring to high. Mix until light and airy.
  4. Bake for 30 minutes on 180°F C /350 degrees F.
  5. Cool completely be for eating.
