The ingredients needed to make Oven Toaster Bread:
  1. Make ready 2 2/3 cups all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp sugar
  3. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp instant dry yeast
  4. Get 1 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp butter
  6. Get 1 cup lukewarm water
  7. Get butter or oil for greasing

Steps to make Oven Toaster Bread:
  1. Combine all dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. Add butter to the mixture
  3. Form a well in the middle of the mixture. Add water and combine.
  4. Knead on a floured board until well combined
  5. Transfer on a greased bowl, cover and let it rise for 2 hours, until double in size.
  6. Divide into prepared pan with cover and let it rise for another 30 minutes. Alternately, form into buns.
  7. Bake in the oven toaster for 30 minutes
  8. Take out of the oven and let it rest on the pan for 5 mins. Flip over to remove from the pan and let it cool for another 10 minutes before slicing.
