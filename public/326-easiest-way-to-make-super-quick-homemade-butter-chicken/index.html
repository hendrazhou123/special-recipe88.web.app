The ingredients needed to make Butter chicken:
  1. Prepare Half kg boneless Chicken
  2. Get 2 onion
  3. Prepare 3 tomatoes
  4. Take 2 tbsp oil
  5. Get 2 tbsp butter
  6. Get 1/2 cup milk
  7. Prepare 1/2 cup cream
  8. Take 1/2 lemon juice
  9. Take 2 tbsp butter chicken masala
  10. Make ready 1-2 green chilli
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste

Instructions to make Butter chicken:
  1. Heat oil fry tomatoes.(fry kar ke nikal lain)
  2. In the same pan add blended onion with chilli and water.when Onion change its colour add ginger garlic paste cook for 2 min
  3. Now add chicken bhonify add butter chicken masala
  4. Now blend fried tomatoes.and add in chicken mix it well cover the lid cook until chicken tender
  5. Now add 1/2 cup of milk cook it add cream butter and lemon juice mix it well
  6. Serve with nan
