Instructions to make Cheese Omelette:
  1. Beat 2 eggs and add salt and schezwan chutney and mix well.
  2. Put little butter or oil on tawa and heat it,put onions and saute it,Now pour beated egg and spread nicely.
  3. When it's little cooked sprinkle grated cheese on it. Now crack third egg on it and spread nicely to cover cheese.Cook on slow flame.Cut from center and cook on both sides.
