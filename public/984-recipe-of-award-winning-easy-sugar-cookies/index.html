The ingredients needed to make Easy Sugar Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 egg
  2. Get 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  3. Get 344 g all purpose flour
  4. Take 1 tsp. baking soda
  5. Take 227 g butter (softened)
  6. Get 302 g white sugar
  7. Get 1 tsp. vanilla extract
