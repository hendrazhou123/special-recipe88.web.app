The ingredients needed to make Basil & Chocolate Mint Cobbler:
  1. Get 2 large gala apples (peeled and in slices)
  2. Prepare 2 cups fresh blueberries
  3. Prepare 4 cups strawberries
  4. Prepare 2 cups blackberries
  5. Get 1 stick butter (1/2 cup)
  6. Make ready 1 cup packed brown sugar
  7. Take ¹/² tsp almond extract
  8. Make ready 10 chocolate mint leaves
  9. Prepare 4 Basil leaves
  10. Take 1 TBS cinnamon
  11. Take 1 tsp nutmeg
  12. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp cloves
  14. Take 2 cups granola
  15. Prepare 1 TBS lime juice

Instructions to make Basil & Chocolate Mint Cobbler:
  1. Rinse and prep fruit and cut basil and chocolate mint leaves.
  2. Melt butter in Dutch oven, then add brown sugar, spices, basil, chocolate mint, lime juice and almond extract.
  3. Add in fruit, mix gently. Let it cook for 15 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and add the granola. Let sit for another 10 minutes.
  4. Serve warm with ice cream or mascapone/whipping cream.
