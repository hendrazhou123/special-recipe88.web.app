The ingredients needed to make Fire Garlic Butter Colossal Crab!:
  1. Prepare 4 colossal crab legs
  2. Prepare 2 cup melted butter
  3. Get 2 tbsp crushed garlic
  4. Get 1 tbsp Cajun blacking seasoning
  5. Prepare 1 tsp ground black pepper
  6. Get 1 fresh lemon for juice

Instructions to make Fire Garlic Butter Colossal Crab!:
  1. Wash crab legs
  2. Mix butter, cajun seasoning, garlic, black pepper
  3. Poke holes in to crab joints then put ingredients and crab in to a bag for 10 to 15 mins
  4. Put on grill cook to preferred liking
