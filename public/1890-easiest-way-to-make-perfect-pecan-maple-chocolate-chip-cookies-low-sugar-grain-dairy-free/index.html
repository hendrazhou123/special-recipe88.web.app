The ingredients needed to make Pecan Maple Chocolate Chip Cookies (low Sugar, Grain-Dairy Free):
  1. Get Wet Ingredients
  2. Get 3 eggs
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup Eloo
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup truvia brown sugar blend
  5. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Prepare 1 tsp maple flavor
  7. Make ready Dry Ingredients
  8. Prepare 2.5 cups Cashew flour
  9. Take 1/2 cup Chopped Pecans
  10. Take 1/2 cup sf chocolate chips
  11. Make ready 1/2 cup sf vanilla chips
  12. Get 1/2 cup chopped dates
  13. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  14. Make ready 1/2 tsp pink salt

Steps to make Pecan Maple Chocolate Chip Cookies (low Sugar, Grain-Dairy Free):
  1. Mix wet ingredients in a large bowl and combine til smooth and well blended
  2. To that bowl add ingredients in that order and mix well. Let sit 5 minutes for the cashew flower to absorb moisture for a more evenly baked cookie.
  3. Bake in a preheated 375°oven for 10 minutes, checking halfway to turn rack for even baking.
