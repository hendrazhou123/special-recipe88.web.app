The ingredients needed to make Low Carb Coleslaw (sugar free!):
  1. Take 250 g shredded cabbage
  2. Make ready 1 small carrot, julienned then cut in half
  3. Get 5 stalks green onion, chopped
  4. Take 6 Tbsp thick cream
  5. Make ready 4 Tbsp mayonnaise
  6. Prepare 3/4 tsp, or to taste, dijon mustard
  7. Make ready 2 packets (2 g) stevia
  8. Make ready 2-3 tsps,or to taste, apple cider or white vinegar if not available
  9. Take 1/4 tsp, or to taste, pink salt or regular
  10. Take Few dashes of pepper

Steps to make Low Carb Coleslaw (sugar free!):
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Adjust to preferred taste- tangy, sweet or creamy, thin or thick - by adding more vinegar or dijon mustard (tangy), stevia packets (sweet) or cream &/or mayo to make it thicker.
  3. Refrigerate before serving. It's nicer when it's chilled. =)
