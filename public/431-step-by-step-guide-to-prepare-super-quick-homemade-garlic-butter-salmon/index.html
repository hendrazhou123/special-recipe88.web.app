The ingredients needed to make Garlic butter salmon:
  1. Prepare Parchment paper 12x18 inches
  2. Take 1/2 white potato, thinly sliced
  3. Get 1/4 Tbsp Salt
  4. Take 1/4 Tbsp pepper
  5. Take 6 ounces skinless salmon
  6. Prepare 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  7. Take 2 clove garlic
  8. Get 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
