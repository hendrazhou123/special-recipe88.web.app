The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Balls:
  1. Take 1 egg
  2. Make ready 2 cups powdered sugar
  3. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 1/3 cup peanut butter
  6. Make ready 1 Tbsp margarine
  7. Make ready 1 cup crushed salted peanuts

Steps to make Peanut Butter Balls:
