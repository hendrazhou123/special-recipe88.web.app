The ingredients needed to make French vanilla chess bars:

  2. Prepare 3 eggs

Steps to make French vanilla chess bars:
  1. Grease 9 x 13 glass dish
  2. With hand mixer mix cake mix, melted butter and 1egg
  3. After forming a soft dough press in bottom of pan. It helps to dip fingers in flour before you spread dough. Set pan aside
  4. Cream together cheese and 2 eggs. Add sugar slowly until very creamy.
  5. Pour over dough and spread evenly
  6. Preheat oven to 350. Cook for 45-60 minutes or golden brown. 'Very important' let cool completely before cutting. At least 1 hour!
