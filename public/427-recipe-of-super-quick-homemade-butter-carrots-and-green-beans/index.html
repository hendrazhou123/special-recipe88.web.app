The ingredients needed to make Butter Carrots and Green Beans:
  1. Prepare 30 ounces green beans
  2. Prepare 1/2 stick butter
  3. Take 1 pound carrots chopped
  4. Get 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon white pepper
