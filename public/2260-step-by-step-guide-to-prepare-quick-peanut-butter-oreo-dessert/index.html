The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Oreo Dessert:
  1. Make ready 30 Oreos
  2. Get 1/2 cup melted butter
  3. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup peanut butter
  5. Get 1 cup powdered sugar
  6. Take 8 oz whipped topping
  7. Take 9 peanut butter cups chopped
  8. Get 3.9 oz pkg instant chocolate fudge pudding mix
  9. Make ready 8 oz whipped topping
  10. Get 2 cups milk
  11. Get 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Steps to make Peanut Butter Oreo Dessert:
  1. Crush oreos into fine pieces. Save 1/4 cup for topping.
  2. Mix remaining oreos with melted butter. Press in greased 8" square baking dish.
  3. Beat cream cheese, peanut butter and powdered sugar until smooth.
  4. Fold in whipped topping and spread over crust.
  5. Top with peanut butter cups.
  6. Beat milk, pudding mix and powdered sugar about 2 minutes
  7. Fold in whipped topping and spread over peanut butter cups.
  8. Top with remaining cookie crumbs.
  9. Refrigerate 2 hours.
