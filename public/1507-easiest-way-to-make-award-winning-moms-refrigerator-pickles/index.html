The ingredients needed to make Moms refrigerator pickles:
  1. Take 12 pickling cucumbers
  2. Prepare 2 green peppers sliced thin
  3. Get Onions cut thin
  4. Prepare 2 cups sugar
  5. Get 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  6. Get 2 tablespoons salt
