The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip Cookies:

  5. Get S2: Wet Ingredients

  10. Get 2 tsp vanilla

  12. Make ready 2 cups chocolate chips

Instructions to make Chocolate chip Cookies:
  1. Mix wet ingredients all together really well with a hand mixer for 2-3 minutes until all well blended
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a separate bowl
  3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients gradually to form a hard dough texture
  4. Add the chocolate chip right at the end
  5. Refrigerate for atleast 1 hour. The longer you leave it the better the taste
  6. Preheat oven to 190° for 10-15 mins
  7. Meanwhile form 1/4 size ball shapes from the dough and place spaced out on a tray
  8. Bake for atleast 12-15 mins. The longer you cook the less chewy, the more crunchy it gets
  9. When removed the cookie will still be really soft but will harden as cooled down
