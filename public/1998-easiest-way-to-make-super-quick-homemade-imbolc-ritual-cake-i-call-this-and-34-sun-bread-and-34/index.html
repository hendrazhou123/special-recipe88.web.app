  2. Get 3/4 cup water
  3. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  4. Take 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  5. Take 2 tbs. poppyseeds
  6. Prepare 1 tbs. grated lemon peel
  7. Take 1 tsp. baking soda
  8. Make ready 2 tbs. lemon juice
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp. salt
  10. Take powdered sugar
