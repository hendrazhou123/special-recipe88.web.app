The ingredients needed to make Loaded cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Get 16-20 peeled and devined jumbo shrimp
  2. Make ready 1 pack bacon
  3. Get 2 green onions sliced
  4. Take 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  5. Take 8 tablespoons melted butter
  6. Prepare 1 cup best foods mayo
  7. Make ready 8 ounces shredded mozzarella cheese
  8. Make ready 5 ounces shredded Parmesan cheese
  9. Get 2.5 ounces shredded cheddar cheese
  10. Get to taste Salt and pepper
  11. Prepare 2 loaves sweet French bread

Steps to make Loaded cheesy garlic bread:
  1. Cook bacon and let cool after cools chop it up. Set aside
  2. Slice green onion set aside
  3. Split shrimp and chop. Add garlic and let sit in fridge til ready to mix in.
  4. Mix mozzarella, parmesan, cheddar, melted butter, green onions, mayo, 1/2 of bacon,(save other half for later),salt and pepper to taste. Then fold in shrimp and garlic.
  5. Preheat oven to 350*
  6. Cut French bread loaves in half and spread mixture over all 4 pieces of bread.
  7. Place in oven for 20 mins. Add rest of bacon on top and Broil for the last 2 minutes keeping an eye on it while broiling.
  8. Cut like garlic bread then cut each piece in half and serve
