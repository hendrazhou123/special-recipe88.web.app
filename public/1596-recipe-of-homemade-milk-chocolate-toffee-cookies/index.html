The ingredients needed to make Milk Chocolate Toffee Cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 4 ounces good quality milk chocolate, chopped
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup unsalted butter (1 stick)
  7. Get 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  8. Prepare 2 large eggs
  9. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  10. Get 2 cups toffee chips
