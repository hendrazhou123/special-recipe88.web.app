The ingredients needed to make Almond milk Matcha pudding:
  1. Prepare 300 grams almond milk
  2. Get 3 tablespoons brown sugar
  3. Prepare 4 teaspoons custard
  4. Get 1 teaspoon Matcha green tea
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon orange flower water

Steps to make Almond milk Matcha pudding:
