  2. Make ready 1/2 cup Sugar

  4. Take 1 Egg
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Vanilla flavor

Instructions to make Giant Cookie:
  1. Cream butter, sugar, egg and vanilla flavor in a bowl, add flour.
  2. Knead to form a dough.
  3. Place the dough on a leather(this will ease transfering to the baking tray). Deflate the dough.
  4. Transfer the dough (up-side down) on a lined baking tray.
  5. Stick your Robo sweets and bake until golden brown.
