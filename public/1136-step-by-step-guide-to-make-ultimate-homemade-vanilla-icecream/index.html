The ingredients needed to make Homemade Vanilla Icecream:
  1. Get 1 cup milk
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 2 cup heavy whipping cream
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup whipped cream

Steps to make Homemade Vanilla Icecream:
  1. Freeze your metal or glass mixing bowl for 1 hr
  2. Mix sugar and milk in frozen bowl til sugar is fully dissolved on medium with mixer
  3. Add heavy cream and mix til soft peaks form
  4. Mix in vanilla til soft firm peaks form
  5. Fold in whipped cream
  6. Freeze 1 hr
  7. Mix and refreeze
  8. Repeat steps 6 and 7 for 4 hours
  9. Transfer to a bread pan, glass and wrap in saran wrap
  10. Freeze til its hardened like icecream
