The ingredients needed to make Thai Peanut Sriracha Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 lb sliced boneless, skinless chicken breast
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  3. Get sauce
  4. Prepare 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  5. Get 1 1/2 tbsp finely minced fresh ginger
  6. Make ready 3 clove fresh garlic, minced
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup soy sauce
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup teriyaki sauce
  9. Get 2 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  10. Get 2 1/2 tbsp Sriracha sauce
  11. Take 1/4 cup hot water
  12. Make ready adding to chicken
  13. Prepare 15 oz baby corn, drained
  14. Take 8 oz can water chesnuts, drained
  15. Get garnish
  16. Make ready 1/4 cup peanuts to top each serving

Instructions to make Thai Peanut Sriracha Chicken:
  1. In a large, deep skillet over medium heat add oil.
  2. Once oil is hot add your sliced chicken. Cook until no longer pink. This takes several minutes.
  3. Once chicken is cooked, add corn and chestnuts. Stir and cook 3 minutes.
  4. In a large bowl whisk all sauce ingredients together. This can be done while you wait the three minutes.
  5. Add to chicken and cook on medium low heat for 5 minutes. Stirring occasionally . Sauce thickens up during this cook.
  6. Serve over pretty cooked white or brown rice. Sprinkle peanuts over and serve.
