The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Banana Cupcakes:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup soft butter
  2. Prepare 1 cup icing sugar
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup milk
  5. Make ready 1 mashed banana
  6. Take 1 tbsp vanilla
  7. Prepare 1 cup self-raising flour
  8. Take 1 cup flour

Instructions to make Vanilla Banana Cupcakes:
  1. Set oven to 180°c. Cream sugar and butter. Add eggs. Add mashed banana. In a separate bowl, mix milk and vanilla. In a third bowl, mix flours. Fold in flour and milk mix.
  2. Make sure the batter is cake texture (more fluid-like than paste). Add flour or milk to get it right.
  3. Bake until knife comes out clean, about 20min.
