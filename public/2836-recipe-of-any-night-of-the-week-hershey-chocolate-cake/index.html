The ingredients needed to make Hershey chocolate cake:
  1. Take 2 cups sugar
  2. Take 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup Hershey’s cocoa
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tea spoons of baking powder
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tea spoon baking soda
  6. Take 1 tea spoon salt
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Make ready 1 cup milk
  9. Take 1/2 cup veggie oil
  10. Prepare 2 tea spoons of vanilla ecstasy or the powder
  11. Get 1 cup boiling hot water

Steps to make Hershey chocolate cake:
  1. Stir sugar,flour,cocoa,baking powder,baking soda,salt in a lath bowl
  2. Add eggs,milk,oil, and vanilla
  3. Beat on medium speed for 2 minutes
  4. Stir in boiling water
  5. Poor into cake pan and bake for 30 to35 minitse @ 180 degrees
