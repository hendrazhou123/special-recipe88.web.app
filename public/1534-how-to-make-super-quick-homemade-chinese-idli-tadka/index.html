The ingredients needed to make Chinese idli tadka:
  1. Take 1 cup semolina
  2. Prepare Half cup curd
  3. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  4. Take 1 tsp black pepper
  5. Make ready 1 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Take 1 tsp chaat masala
  7. Prepare 1 tsp eno fruit salt or baking powder
  8. Prepare Half tsp baking soda if eno not available
  9. Get Small onion sliced for tadka
  10. Prepare 1 tsp cumin and mustard seeds
  11. Get 1 packet Chinese chilli masala if available
  12. Get Ketchup and soy sauce optional
