The ingredients needed to make EnsaladaDe Bacalao (Bacalao Salad):
  1. Get 1 lb bacalao (boned)
  2. Take 1/2 cup white vinegar
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 salt and pepper to taste
  5. Make ready 1/2 head lettuce (chopped)
  6. Prepare 3 medium tomatoes (sliced)
  7. Prepare 1 small onion (sliced)

Instructions to make EnsaladaDe Bacalao (Bacalao Salad):
  1. rinse bacalao with cold water and place in large bowl
  2. fill with cold water just enough to cover the fish
  3. cover and let soak overnight in refrigerator
  4. remove from frige and rinse with cold water
  5. place bacalao in large pot cover with cold water just enough to cover the fish
  6. bring it to a hard boil and cook for 5 minutes
  7. boil the fish a total of 3 times rinsing it with cold water inbetween (steps 4-6)
  8. strain and lightly rinse with cold water…when cooled break fish down with fingers and place in large bowl
  9. add onion and tomatoes to fish…set aside
  10. in small bowl wisk together oil, vinegar, salt, and pepper. pour dressing over the fish and stir
  11. add lettuce. seal the container with a lid and shake so that the dressing coats the lettuce
  12. refrigerate for at least an hour….be sure to strain some of the dressing when servinvg
  13. Tip: the longer it sits the better
