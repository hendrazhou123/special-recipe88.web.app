The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Marscapone Cream Filling / Frosting:
  1. Get 16 oz marscapone cheese, at room temperature
  2. Prepare 3 cup heavy whipping cream
  3. Get 1 cup confectioner's sugar
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
