The ingredients needed to make Hershey chocolate cake:
  1. Get 2 cups sugar
  2. Take 1 3/4 cups all purpose flour
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup Hershey’s cocoa
  4. Take 1 1/2 tea spoons of baking powder
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 tea spoon baking soda
  6. Take 1 tea spoon salt
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Get 1 cup milk
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup veggie oil
  10. Get 2 tea spoons of vanilla ecstasy or the powder
  11. Take 1 cup boiling hot water

Instructions to make Hershey chocolate cake:
