The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Choc Chip Cookie:
  1. Take 400 grams Plain Flour
  2. Take 250 grams Caster Sugar
  3. Prepare 250 grams Unsalted Butter
  4. Make ready 100 grams Choc Chip
  5. Prepare 70 grams Desiccated Coconut
  6. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Baking Powder
  7. Get 2 Eggs
  8. Make ready 200 grams Unsalted Peanut Butter

Steps to make Peanut Butter Choc Chip Cookie:
