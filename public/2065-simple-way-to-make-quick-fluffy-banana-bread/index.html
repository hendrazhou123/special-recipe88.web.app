The ingredients needed to make Fluffy Banana Bread:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Get 1/3 cup sugar
  4. Get 2 mashed bananas
  5. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1/2 cup melted butter
  7. Take 1 pinch salt
  8. Get Raisins (optional)

Instructions to make Fluffy Banana Bread:
  1. Add eggs and sugar, mix on high speed until foamy.
  2. Add mashed bananas into the egg foam and mix on high speed for 1 minute.
  3. Add flour, baking powder and salt. Mix on low speed until incorporated.
  4. Add melted butter in the mixture and mix with a spatula until even.
  5. Coat a baking pan with oil and pour the batter into it.
  6. Sprinkle raisins on top of the batter and bake at 350 degrees F for about 35 minutes.
