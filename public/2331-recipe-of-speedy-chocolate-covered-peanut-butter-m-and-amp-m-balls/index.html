The ingredients needed to make Chocolate covered peanut butter M&M balls:
  1. Make ready 1 bag chocolate chips
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cups peanut butter (I use Reese)
  3. Take 2 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  4. Get 1/2 melted butter
  5. Take 2 share size bag M&M's

Instructions to make Chocolate covered peanut butter M&M balls:
  1. Mix pb, melted butter and m&M's and powdered sugar together. Make 1" balls and let freeze 20 min
  2. Melt chocolate chips
  3. Roll balls in chocolate and place a m&m on top. Freeze to let chocolate harden and enjoy
