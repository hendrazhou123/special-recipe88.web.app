The ingredients needed to make Chocolate fiber cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 Cup All purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup rolled oats
  3. Get 1/4 cup even portion of chia, flaxseed & sesame
  4. Get 1/2 cup sweetened Powder Cacao
  5. Prepare 4 tbspn Honey
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Take 2 tbspn Cooking oil (no olive oil)

Steps to make Chocolate fiber cookies:
  1. Pre-Heat oven to 392 Fahrenheit.
  2. Bring all ingredients in a bowl. Flour, oats, mixture of seeds, Cacao powder, honey, 2 eggs & cooking oil
  3. Mold the mixture to individual cookies. Should have enough to make 15 individual cookies.
  4. Place in oven for 20 minutes.
