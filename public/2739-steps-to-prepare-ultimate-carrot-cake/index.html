The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Get 3 cups shredded carrots
  3. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 4 large eggs
  6. Get 2 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1 1/4 cups vegetable oil
  8. Get 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  9. Get 1/2 cup brown sugar
  10. Prepare 1 cup chopped pecan + 1/2 cup for garnishing
  11. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla
  12. Prepare 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  13. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  14. Prepare For Frosting
  15. Make ready 8 oz cream cheese
  16. Make ready 4 cups powdered sugar
  17. Get 1/2 cup butter
  18. Get 1 tsp vanilla

Steps to make Carrot Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. In a large bowl break n whisk eggs, sugar oil and vanilla.
  2. Then stir in flour, baking soda, cinnamon powder, salt, baking powder, nutmeg. Then fold in shredded carrots and pecans
  3. Grease and flour 9 inch baking pan.(I used 2 round ones)
  4. Add the batter to the baking pan and place in the oven to bake for 40 to 50 mins. When finish baking put to cool for abt 1hr
  5. For frosting: In a bowl add powdered(confectionery) sugar, vanilla, soft cream cheese and butter and mix until creamed.
  6. Once cake is cool remove from baking pan and place on a cake rack. Spread frosting to the top and then topped with pecans. (Nb because I made 2 cakes I shave the top of one of the cake to be flat enough then I spread frosting then I place the second cake on top then spread frosting again n all over the cakes).
