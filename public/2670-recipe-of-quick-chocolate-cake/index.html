The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake:
  1. Prepare 50 g butter
  2. Take 2 cups sugar
  3. Get 1 cups flour
  4. Get 1 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  5. Make ready 1 cup milk
  6. Take 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  7. Get 2 tablespoon oil
  8. Take 3-4 drops vanilla essence
  9. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  10. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  11. Prepare 150 g butter
  12. Make ready Marshmallow fluff
  13. Make ready 2-3 tablespoons confectionery sugar

Instructions to make Chocolate cake:
  1. In a mixing bowl, add 50g of butter, sugar, vanilla essence and oil and whisk is together
  2. In another mixing bowl, add flour, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder and put it through a sieve
  3. Now in the first mixing bowl, add the dry ingredients and sweetened condensed milk and milk as needed and make a batter
  4. Grease the cake pan with butter and flour and add the batter and bake it for 35-40 mins. To check if its baked, put a toothpick into it, if its dry, its done if the batter comes with it, it needs more time to be done baking
  5. While the cake's baking, take a mixing bowl over a boiler and add butter till its melted, then add marshmallow fluff and confectionery sugar to it
  6. Take the bowl out of the boiler and whisk it in high speed till its light and fluffy
  7. When the cake's done, decorate it with the icing made above as you please and you're good to go.
