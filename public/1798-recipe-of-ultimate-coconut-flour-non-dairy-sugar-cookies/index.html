The ingredients needed to make Coconut Flour Non Dairy sugar cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  2. Get 1 stick soy butter ( earth balance brand)
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup vanilla soy milk
  4. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Take 3/4 cup white flour
  6. Make ready 1/3 cup Coconut Flour
  7. Take 2 tbsp corn starch
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda

Instructions to make Coconut Flour Non Dairy sugar cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Blend together the sugar and butter until very well mixed. Then add the Milk and vanilla. if using a mixer mix on med speed for about 2 min. it should look very runny and watery.
  3. Mix the dry ingredients very well in a seperate bowl. I always like to use a wire whisk.
  4. Add the dry ingredients to your first mixing bowl and mix on med to med high for about 2 min. the Batter should be very moist and look fluffy and light but not runny.
  5. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls onto a greased baking sheet. Bake for about 8-10 min. cookies should just start to turn a very light golden brown.
