The ingredients needed to make Overload Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. Prepare 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  2. Make ready 1 1/4 cups refined flour
  3. Take 1/2 cup butter
  4. Take 3/4 cup castor sugar
  5. Make ready 1 egg
  6. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla essence
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Take 1 pinch salt
  10. Prepare 1/4 cup walnuts chopped

Instructions to make Overload Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. Preheat oven at 180℃. Line baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Cream butter and castor sugar in a bowl till soft and fluffy. Add egg and vanilla essence and whisk well.
  3. Seive together flour and cocoa powder in butter-sugar mixture. Add baking soda and salt, fold well.
  4. Now add walnuts, chocolate chips and mix well to make a dough.
  5. Divide the dough into equal portions, shape them into cookies and place on the lined baking tray.
  6. Place baking tray in preheated oven and bake for 12-15 mins.
  7. Remove from heat and cool for 10 mins and serve.
