The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Chicken with Bacon:
  1. Make ready 4 skinless boneless breasts
  2. Take 8 oz cream cheese
  3. Take 2 heaping tbsp sour cream
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp ranch seasoning
  5. Get Onion and garlic powders, salt and pepper
  6. Take 1-2 cups shredded cheese
  7. Prepare Bacon bits

Steps to make Cream Cheese Chicken with Bacon:
  1. Preheat oven to 400. In a sprayed casserole, lay breasts smooth side down. Make a pocket in the center of each piece. I seasoned both sides with salt pepper onion and garlic powder.
  2. In a bowl combine the cream cheese, the sour cream, salt, pepper, onion and garlic powders, and ranch seasoning. Spread equal amounts in each breast then cover with shredded cheese. Make sure to cover the cream cheese. It acts like a cap.
  3. Bake, uncovered, for 30 minutes. After 30 minutes I covered it just to make sure it baked through completely. Bake about 15 minutes longer and done. Top with sour cream and bacon bits.
