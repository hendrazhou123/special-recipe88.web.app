The ingredients needed to make Hot Vanilla Milk:
  1. Take 1 tbsp honey
  2. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Get 1 cup milk
  4. Prepare Optional
  5. Get 1 dash cinnamon/nutmeg
  6. Get 1 tsp sugar

Steps to make Hot Vanilla Milk:
  1. Pour your milk into a pot, over the stove. Turn it on medium heat.
  2. Add your tablespoon of honey, and stir until it dissolves.
  3. Take a teaspoon of the vanilla extract, just when it's about to boil (bubbles along the edge), and add it in. Stir until mixed together.
  4. Pour your drink into a mug. Sprinkle with cinnamon or nutmeg. Enjoy!
