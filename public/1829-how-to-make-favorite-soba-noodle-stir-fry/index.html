The ingredients needed to make Soba Noodle Stir Fry:
  1. Get 4 oz soba noodles
  2. Prepare 1 clove garlic, minced
  3. Get 1 fresh ginger, minced
  4. Take 2 spring onions, chopped
  5. Prepare 1 carrot, sliced
  6. Make ready 3 mushrooms, chopped
  7. Take bunch long green beans, halved
  8. Take 1/4 cup soy sauce
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp hot sauce
  10. Make ready 1 tsp sesame seeds
  11. Take 1 tsp chili flakes

Instructions to make Soba Noodle Stir Fry:
  1. In a pot boil water and cook soba noodles until tender, not fully cooked.
  2. In a wok or skillet heat a tablespoon of olive oil. Add the spring onions. Cook until tender, add the garlic. Stir for 2 minutes.
  3. Add the sliced carrots and chopped green beans. Season with salt and pepper and stir.
  4. Add the chopped mushrooms. Add a little bit of the soy sauce and 1 tbsp of hot sauce. Stir, cover and lower the heat. Leave for 15 mins, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the soba noodles and stir. Add the remaining soy sauce and more if necessary. Cook for a further 10 mins.
  6. Serve and top with sesame seeds and chili flakes.
