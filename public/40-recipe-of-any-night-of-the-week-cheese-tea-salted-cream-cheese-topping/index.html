  2. With a hand whisk or mixer, beat cream cheese and condensed milk until creamy. Set aside.
  3. With a cold bowl and whisk, beat whipping cream until thickened. The cream will whip faster when everything is cold.
  4. Add the cheese mixture and a pinch of salt. Then combine well.
  5. Add ice cubes into a glass (optional) and cold tea. Then top with the cheese foam. Enjoy!
