The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Mug Cake:
  1. Get Dry Ingredients:
  2. Prepare 4 tbsp. all purpose flour
  3. Get 3 tbsp. granulated sugar
  4. Make ready 1 heaping tbsp. unsweetened cocoa powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  6. Make ready Pinch salt
  7. Make ready Wet Ingredients:
  8. Prepare 5 tbsp. milk
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp. unsalted butter, melted
  10. Get 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  11. Take 2 tbsp. semi-sweet chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate Mug Cake:
  1. In a bowl, whisk together the dry ingredients. Then whisk in the wet ingredients until well combined. Stir in the chocolate chips. Pour this mixture into a greased mug (at least 12 oz. size or a little bigger).
  2. Pop the mug into the microwave for 90 seconds. That's it. Sprinkle some powdered sugar over the top or plop a scoop of ice cream on top and enjoy it!
