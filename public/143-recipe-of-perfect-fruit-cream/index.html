The ingredients needed to make Fruit cream:
  1. Prepare 250 ml amul fresh cream
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup powder sugar or according to ur taste
  3. Prepare 1 bowl Chopped Mix fruits (apple,banana,grapes, pomegranate)
  4. Prepare as needed Chopped dry fruits(almonds,cashew)

Steps to make Fruit cream:
  1. In a mixing bowl take cream and beat with a hand whiskers for untill it comes in a foamy texture. Then add sugar and mix well.
  2. Add any mix chopped fruits of ur desire mix lightly.keep the bowl in a fridge for 1-2 hours.keep out from the fridge add chopped dry fruits and serve.
