The ingredients needed to make Cream Golgappe:
  1. Get 8-10 Golgappe
  2. Prepare 1 cup potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  3. Get to taste salt
  4. Prepare 1 tsp cumin powder
  5. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Prepare 3 tsp coriander leaves chopped
  7. Get as needed amul cream
  8. Take 1/2 bowl tamarind chutney
  9. Take as needed sev
