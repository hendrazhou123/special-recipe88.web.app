The ingredients needed to make Almond Butter Blondies:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup almond butter
  2. Take 1 large egg
  3. Take 1/4 cup brown sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp milk or plant milk
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup almond flour
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1/4 tsp cinnamon
  9. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  11. Make ready 1/4 cup coconut flakes
  12. Prepare Sea salt for sprinkling

Instructions to make Almond Butter Blondies:
  1. Preheat oven to 325F. In a medium sized bowl, whisk together almond butter, egg, sugar, vanilla, and milk. Mix until thoroughly combined and batter forms thick ribbons when you lift the whisk.
  2. Switch to a spatula or wooden spoon and mix in almond flour, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. Once combined, stir in coconut (optional) and chocolate chips. Spread into a loaf pan that is lined with parchment paper and sprayed with cooking spray. Top with additional chocolate chips and flaky sea salt.
  4. Bake for 25 minutes, rotating pan halfway through. Allow to cool on a wire rack for 15 minutes before lifting parchment out of pan and slicing. Makes 8 blondies.
