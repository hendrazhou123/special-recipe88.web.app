The ingredients needed to make milk moist sweets (charniqueiras ):
  1. Prepare 1/2 liter milk
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Get 200 grams sugar
  4. Get 100 grams melted butter
  5. Make ready 100 grams flour

Instructions to make milk moist sweets (charniqueiras ):
  1. beat eggs with sugar until smooth
  2. add the flour stir well
  3. add the butter and the milk blend well
  4. pour in grease little tins and take to oven at 350 Ffor about 20minutes
  5. let cool down before you take the charniqueiras out of the tins
  6. sprinkle with sugar and cinnamon
