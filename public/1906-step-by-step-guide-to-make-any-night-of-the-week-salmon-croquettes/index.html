The ingredients needed to make Salmon croquettes:
  1. Prepare 1 cup boned salmon
  2. Get 1 tablespoon butter
  3. Make ready 1 tablespoon flour
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  5. Get 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  6. Get 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
