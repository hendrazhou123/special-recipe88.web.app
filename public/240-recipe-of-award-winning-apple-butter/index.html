Steps to make Apple Butter:
  1. Wash, core and dice apples. Place in a large pot.
  2. Add in everything but the root beer. Stir throughly to coat apples. Add in the root beer, but don’t stir it. Allow the seasonings to stay adhered to the apples. Cover. Turn stove on medium heat and bring to a simmer. Then reduce to low.
  3. Keep an eye on the apples. It’ll take a while, but you can periodically take a look at them until they resemble this.
  4. At that point, you’ll want to put them in the blender and purée them. Return to the pot on low heat and continue cooking uncovered. You’ll want the steam to escape to allow it to reduce and thicken. STIR OCCASIONALLY.
  5. When it has reduced to the point where when you stir it, it takes a few seconds to cover the bottom of the pot, it’s done. It will almost seem like it wants to hold it’s shape. Remove, store in a jar, refrigerate when cooled. Can it if you’the like to.
