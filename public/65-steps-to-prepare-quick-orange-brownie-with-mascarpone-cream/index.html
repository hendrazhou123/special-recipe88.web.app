The ingredients needed to make Orange Brownie with Mascarpone Cream:
  1. Prepare 250 g unsalted butter
  2. Prepare 200 g dark compound chocolate
  3. Get 80 g cocoa powder
  4. Take 64 g all-purpose flour
  5. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  7. Get 180 g sugar
  8. Prepare 4 large eggs
  9. Make ready 1 tsp orange extract
  10. Prepare Cream
  11. Prepare 250 g mascarpone
  12. Get 35 g confectioners sugar

Instructions to make Orange Brownie with Mascarpone Cream:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree Celsius.
  2. Melt the butter and the chocolate in a double boiler (boil some water in a large pan, put another heat proof bowl on top to melt the ingredients). You can save your time by melting them in microwave but the final texture won’t come out as smooth.
  3. Sift and mix the dry ingredients
  4. Whisk the dry ingredients to the chocolate mixture. Add the dry ingredients a spoon at a time to make sure there’s no lump
  5. Add the eggs and orange extract. Mix well until you get the silky texture
  6. Line and grease a 20x20 cm baking pan with parchment paper, butter, and some flour. Pour the batter into the pan. Bake in the oven for 30 minutes
  7. Cool the brownie on a wire rack while preparing the cream
  8. Mix the mascarpone and the sugar with a plastic spatula until well incorporated
  9. Once the brownie is cooled down, cut the outer layer of the brownie to create a nice even layer
  10. Cut the brownie in half. Smear the cream on top of one part
  11. Stack the other part of the brownie and dust with a spoonful of confectioners sugar
  12. The perfect way to enjoy the brownie is pairing it with a cup of black coffee
