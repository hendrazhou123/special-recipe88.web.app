The ingredients needed to make Indonesian Chicken Satay With Peanut Sauce:
  1. Take 500 grams Chicken breast Fillet thinly Sliced,cubed
  2. Prepare 1/4 tsp Salt
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Lime Juice
  4. Prepare 4 cloves Garlic
  5. Take 1 Canadian Shallot
  6. Get 3 candle nuts
  7. Take 5 tbsp peanut butter(I use craft)
  8. Prepare to taste kecap manis(sweet soy sauce)
  9. Prepare 1 cup water
  10. Get 2 tbsp oil
  11. Take as needed skewers

Instructions to make Indonesian Chicken Satay With Peanut Sauce:
  1. Pour Lime juice and salt into the chicken,put aside.
  2. Mince Garlic,shallot and candle nut through food processor.
  3. Make the sauce : Heat 2tbsp oil then add the minced garlic,minced shallot and minced candle nut. Add peanut butter,water and kecap manis. Let the sauce come to boil. Try to taste. let the sauce cool down.
  4. Pour a half of sauce into the chicken and leave it for minimum 30minutes or just leave it overnight in fridge.
