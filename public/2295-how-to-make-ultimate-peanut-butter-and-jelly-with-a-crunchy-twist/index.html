The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter and Jelly with a crunchy twist:
  1. Take 2 eggs
  2. Get 1 Cup Milk
  3. Get 2 tbsp Cinnamon
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp Sugar
  5. Get 1/2 tsp nutmeg
  6. Take 6 slices bread
  7. Get Creamy peanut butter
  8. Get Strawberry Jelly
  9. Prepare Butter
  10. Make ready 2 cups Frosted Flakes cereal

Instructions to make Peanut Butter and Jelly with a crunchy twist:
  1. Mix eggs, milk, sugar, nutmeg and cinnamon in a deep bowl.
  2. Make 3 peanut butter and jelly sandwiches.
  3. Crush cereal in a zip lock bag pour onto a plate.
  4. Heat butter in a skillet. (Nonstick preferably)
  5. Dip sandwiches in egg mixture on both sides and into cereal on both sides as well until fully coated.
  6. Put sandwich in skillet and cook on both sides for 2 minutes or until cereal is golden brown. Enjoy!
