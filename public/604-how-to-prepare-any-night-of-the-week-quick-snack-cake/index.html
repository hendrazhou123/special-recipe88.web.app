The ingredients needed to make Quick snack cake:
  1. Prepare 1 box + 1/2 of another box white cake mix
  2. Take 2/3 cup oil
  3. Prepare 1 cup sour cream
  4. Get 1 cup milk
  5. Get Frosting:
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  7. Take 1/3 cup cocoa powder
  8. Make ready 3 cups powdered sugar
  9. Get 1/3 cup milk

Instructions to make Quick snack cake:
  1. Set oven to 350. Mix all cake Ingredients well then pour into well greased cake pan. I used a bundt pan. Bake about 25-30 mins.
  2. To make the frosting, soften butter and mix with cocoa powder. Add powdered sugar and milk and mix well. Use to frost completely cooled cake.
