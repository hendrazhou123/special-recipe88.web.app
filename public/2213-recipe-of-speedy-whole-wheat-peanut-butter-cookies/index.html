The ingredients needed to make Whole Wheat Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Take 1 cup peanut butter
  2. Get 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup maple syrup
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup packed brown sugar
  5. Take 1 egg
  6. Take 1 1/4 cups whole wheat flour
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions to make Whole Wheat Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
  2. Soften the butter by microwaving it for 30 seconds.
  3. In a large bowl, mix together peanut butter, butter, maple syrup, brown sugar, and egg until completely smooth. Stir in the baking powder. Next add the whole wheat flour and mix until totally blended. If the dough ends up being too soft to work with, refrigerate for an hour.
  4. Roll dough into palm size patties and place on a baking sheet covered with parchment paper. Flatten slightly with a fork.
  5. Bake for 20 minutes.
