The ingredients needed to make Fruit salsa with cinnamon sugar chips:
  1. Make ready Cinnamon chips:
  2. Make ready 6 10" flour tortillas
  3. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  4. Get 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. Take Olive oil spray
  6. Prepare Fruit salsa:
  7. Take 1 granny Smith apple
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup diced honey dew melon
  9. Take 1/2 lb strawberries
  10. Take 1/2 lb raspberries
  11. Make ready 1-2 tbs preserves (I used raspberry)
  12. Make ready 1 lemon

Instructions to make Fruit salsa with cinnamon sugar chips:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°. Combine cinnamon and sugar, set aside. Working with 3 tortillas at a time, spray both sides of tortillas and sprinkle each side lightly with cinnamon sugar. Stack 3 tortillas and cut into wedges. Spread them out on a baking sheet and bake 10-12 minutes.
  2. Zest lemon and set aside. Peel and finely chop apple, squeeze 1 tsp lemon juice over apples and stir.
  3. Finely chop strawberries and melon, add to bowl with apples. Then add raspberries, preserves, and how ever much lemon zest you want. Stir well to combine.
