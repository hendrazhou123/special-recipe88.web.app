The ingredients needed to make Butter Naan:
  1. Prepare 2 cups maida/all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup curd
  3. Get 1/2 cup Baking powder
  4. Get 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  5. Make ready 1 tbsp Sugar
  6. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Nigella seeds(kalonji)
  8. Get leaves Mint/coriander
  9. Get Butter

Steps to make Butter Naan:
  1. Take a vessel add maida, baking powder, baking soda, salt, sugar mix well.
  2. Now add curd, mix it well. Add water and knead the flour into dough.
  3. Cover it & leave for 30 mins
  4. After 30 minutes knead the dough once again.
  5. Make dough balls.
  6. Take one dough ball and dust it with flour & roll it.
  7. Sprinkle kalonji & coriander leaves and roll it again.
  8. Use iron tawa for making naan
  9. Keep water in a bowl.
  10. Heat the tawa, and take one naan and apply water on the back.
  11. Place it on tawa & cook it for few seconds on medium flame.
  12. Flip the tawa on direct flame.
  13. Cook it till it turns brown.
  14. Scratch it to remove the naan from tawa.
  15. Apply butter on naan.
  16. Now butter naan is ready to serve.
