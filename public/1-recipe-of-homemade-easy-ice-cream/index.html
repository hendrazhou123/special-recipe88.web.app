Instructions to make Easy ice cream:
  1. In a large bowl add heavy cream and whip to soft peeks
  2. Slowly add condensed milk and whip to stiff peeks
  3. Optional fold in flavorings i used 1tsp vanilla extract and crushed Oreos
  4. Freeze over night for at least 6 hours
