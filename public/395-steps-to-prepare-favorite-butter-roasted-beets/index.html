Steps to make Butter Roasted Beets:
  1. Preheat oven 400 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Peel the beet
  3. Dice and put into an oven safe pan with butter
  4. Sauté for 15 minutes
  5. Add orange juice
  6. Let reduce for 12 minutes then put into oven
  7. Roast for 20-25 minutes
  8. Let rest 10 minutes serve. I hope you enjoy!
