The ingredients needed to make Delicious Corn Bread:
  1. Get 1 cup yellow corn meal
  2. Make ready 1 cup flour
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  4. Take 3 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 2/3 cup milk
  7. Take 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps to make Delicious Corn Bread:
  1. Stir together the dry ingredients(corn meal, flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt).
  2. Mix together the wet ingredients(Milk, eggs, and oil).
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients until blended. Pour into a greased 8x8 pan.
  4. In a pre-heated 400 degree oven, bake the corn bread for 25-30 minutes. Or until golden brown.
  5. Enjoy!
