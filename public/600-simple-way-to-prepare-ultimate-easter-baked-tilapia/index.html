The ingredients needed to make Easter Baked Tilapia 🐟:
  1. Make ready 1 large pack Tilapia
  2. Take Sprinkle lemon pepper seasoning
  3. Take Sprinkle Black Pepper
  4. Take Sprinkle Parsley Flakes
  5. Take 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  6. Get Butter or margarine
  7. Make ready Butter cooking spray

Instructions to make Easter Baked Tilapia 🐟:
  1. Rinse tilapia under cold water…..pat dry and place in a large baking pan sprayed with butter cooking spray……
  2. Season tilapia on both sides with lemon pepper, garlic salt,black pepper and parsley flakes…..Place a dollop of butter into each piece of tilapia….
  3. Bake in a preheated 375 degree oven for 25 minutes, uncovered…….
  4. Serve with some lemon 🍋 slices and enjoy 😉!!!!
