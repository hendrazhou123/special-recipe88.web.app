The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms:
  1. Make ready 6 medium to large button mushrooms
  2. Get 4 oz cream cheese (約120g）
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon onion salt
  4. Take 1/4 cup Mexican blend or cheddar cheese
  5. Prepare 1/4 grated Parmesan cheese
  6. Get 2 Tablespoon pickled jalapeno chopped
  7. Take Dried parsley flakes to garnish

Instructions to make Cream Cheese Stuffed Mushrooms:
  1. Mix the cheese, onion salt, shredded cheese, Parmesan cheese, and jalapeno together until well combined. Set aside.
  2. Wipe the mushrooms clean and remove the stems. Scoop the cheese mixture into the mushroom. Air fry at 350F (175C) for about 6-7 minutes.
  3. Sprinkle some parsley flakes to serve.
