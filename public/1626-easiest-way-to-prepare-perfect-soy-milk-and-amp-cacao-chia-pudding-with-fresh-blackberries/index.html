Steps to make Soy milk & Cacao Chia Pudding with Fresh Blackberries:
  1. Mix dark chocolate and honey until emulsified
  2. Slowly add soy milk until well mixed.
  3. Add chia seeds and stir for steady minute.
  4. Store 4 hours to overnight in refrigerator and serve with fresh blackberries (or any other berries). Add honey as needed.
