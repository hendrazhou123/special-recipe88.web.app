The ingredients needed to make Coconut Cream Pie:
  1. Take 1 Baked pie crust
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup cornstarch
  4. Get 2 cups whole milk
  5. Prepare 4 egg yolks
  6. Get 3 tbsp butter
  7. Get 1 cup sweetened flake coconut
  8. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Make ready 2 cups Heavy whipping cream
  10. Get 1/2 cup sugar

Steps to make Coconut Cream Pie:
  1. Bake your favorite pie crust according to directions. Set aside and cool.
  2. Combine sugar and cornstarch in sauce pan. Whisk egg yolks and whole milk in bowl. Whisk into sugar and cornstarch mixture in saucepan over medium heat. Continue whisking until creamy.
  3. Remove custard from heat. Add butter, coconut, and vanilla. Stir until all is incorporated. Place plastic wrap directly over custard in pan to prevent air. Set aside to cool for 30 minutes.
  4. Spoon custard into baked pie crust. Place in refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  5. Whip heavy cream to stiff peaks. Add sugar towards end of whipping. Pipe or spoon cream over the top of the custard. Garnish with toasted coconut. Enjoy.
