The ingredients needed to make Cream Corn:
  1. Take 1 (10 ounce) package frozen kernel corn, thawed
  2. Get 1 //2 cup heavy cream
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 tbs sugar
  5. Make ready 1/8 tsp pepper
  6. Prepare 1 tbs butter
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  8. Make ready 1 tbs all-purpose flour
  9. Make ready 1/4-1/2 cup parmesan cheese

Instructions to make Cream Corn:
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix the corn, cream, salt, sugar, pepper and butter.
  3. In a separate bowl whisk together the milk and flour, stir into the corn mixture.
  4. Stir in the parmesan cheese.
  5. Pour into a greased baking dish.
  6. Bake for 30-45 minutes until golden brown.
