The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Frosting:
  1. Prepare 3 cup powdered sugar
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup peanut butter
  3. Get 1/4 cup butter
  4. Make ready 1 and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 2-4 tablespoons milk
