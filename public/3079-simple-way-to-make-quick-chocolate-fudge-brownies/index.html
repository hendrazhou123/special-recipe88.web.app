The ingredients needed to make Chocolate fudge brownies 😊:
  1. Get 2 eggs,1cup butter,3/4 cup brown sugar,1/2 cup sugar(can add)
  2. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla essence, 3/4 cup all purpose flour,1/4 cup cocoa
  3. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  4. Get 1/2 cup grated bitter chocolate
  5. Get 1 cup semi sweet chocolate chips
  6. Make ready Some chopped walnuts n 1/4 cup melted chocolate

Wrap in plastic wrap and store in an air tight container at room temperature or in the refrigerator. Steps to make Chocolate fudge brownies 😊:
  1. First mix sugar,eggs then add vanilla essence n butter whisk it well.next add the sifted flour cocoa, flour n baking pwd. Mix well.
  2. Now add the chocolate chips n grated chocolate. Now remove on the bake tray.
  3. Now put extra chocolate chips n chopped walnuts n bake 180°for 10 to 15 mins.when done drizzle some melted chocolate
  4. Cut with desired shape.can serve wit icecream,strawberry etc.
