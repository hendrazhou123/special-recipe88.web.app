The ingredients needed to make Steam Banana Cake:
  1. Prepare 2 ripe banana
  2. Make ready 1 cup flour
  3. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  4. Get Half tsp salt
  5. Take 3 tbsp melted butter
  6. Prepare 1 egg
