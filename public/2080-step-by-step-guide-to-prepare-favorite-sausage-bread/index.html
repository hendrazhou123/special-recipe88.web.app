  2. To make a dough add flour,vanilla, salt, sugar and mix properly. Then the wet ings, egg, yeast diluted in water, melted butter andb160ml water. Mix well and knead fir 5 to 10 mins. Then put in a bowl, brush with butter on the surface and cover with towel and let it rise for an hour.
