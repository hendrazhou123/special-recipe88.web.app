The ingredients needed to make Hot chocolate:
  1. Prepare 10 Oz Almond Milk
  2. Prepare 2 Oz Almond Creamer
  3. Get 1 teaspoon cocoa
  4. Get 1 teaspoon honey
  5. Get 1 teaspoon Agave syrup

Steps to make Hot chocolate:
  1. Pour milk and creamer into a pot at medium heat to keep at a simmer. Stir occasionally
  2. Add cocoa powder, honey, and agave syrup to your mug of choice. Add a little of you simmering milk with a spoon to make a dark chocolate mixture
  3. Once the cocoa powder has been mixed thoroughly, you pour the rest of the milk in and enjoy.
