Steps to make French omelette cheese bites:
  1. Take one egg, break and mix it really well in a bowl. Add pinch of pepper and salt as per taste.
  2. Now take a pan and add 1/2 tablespoon of butter and warm it up on a light flame.
  3. Let the butter melt and pour the egg. Take a fork or spatula and stir the egg for 15-20 seconds in a pan.
  4. You will see the egg lumps while you are stirring. After that spread it evenly and let it cook now to set it evenly. Make a roll out it.
  5. Take a slice of bread. Put the egg roll inside it, add a cheese slice and cover it with another slice of bread.
  6. Cook both sides of the sandwich with butter. Cut it in pieces you like and serve it 🙂
