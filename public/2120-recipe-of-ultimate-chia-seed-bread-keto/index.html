The ingredients needed to make Chia seed bread (keto):
  1. Get 1/4 C almond milk
  2. Prepare 4 eggs
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1 C almond flour
  5. Prepare 1/4 C butter, melted
  6. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  7. Make ready 1/2 C chia seeds

Instructions to make Chia seed bread (keto):
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
