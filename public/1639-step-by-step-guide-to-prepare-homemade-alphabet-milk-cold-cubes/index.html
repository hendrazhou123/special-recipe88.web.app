Steps to make Alphabet Milk Cold Cubes:
  1. Mix milk , syrup and yogurt together.
  2. Pour the mixture into the alphabet ice cubes tray.
  3. Freeze overnight or at least 4 hours.
  4. Pull milk cubes out of the ice cubes tray
  5. Arrange milk cubes to be a row.
