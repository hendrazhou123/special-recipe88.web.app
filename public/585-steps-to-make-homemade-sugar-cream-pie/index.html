  1. Get 1-9 inch deep dish frozen pie crust
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 1/4 cup cornstarch
  4. Take 2 cups milk
  5. Make ready 1/2 stick margarine or butter
  6. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Get 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
