The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Banana Brownies:
  1. Prepare 1 ripe banana
  2. Prepare 60 g caster sugar
  3. Take 40 g dark brown butter
  4. Make ready 50 g unsalted butter (melted)
  5. Take 30 g compound chocolate (melted)
  6. Get 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Take 60 g flour
  8. Take 40 g cocoa powder
  9. Prepare Peanut butter
  10. Prepare Salt

Instructions to make Peanut Butter Banana Brownies:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees
  2. In a bowl mash the banana, then ads the sugar, melted chocolate, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Then whisk it until combined.
  3. Add the flour and cocoa powder, whisk it until just combined
  4. Pour the batter into prepared pan. I use 5x5cm aluminium foil cup. Sprinkle a little bit of salt on each of it.
  5. Add the peanut butter on it. Then swirl the peanut butter into the brownie batter.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes until set in the middle. Then, let it cool, before serving
