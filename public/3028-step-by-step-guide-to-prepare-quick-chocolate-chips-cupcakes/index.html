The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chips Cupcakes:
  1. Prepare 300 g Flour (I use gluten free)
  2. Take 130 g Melted butter
  3. Get 220 g Greek yogurt
  4. Take 4 Large Eggs
  5. Get 1 Tsp Baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 Tsp dry yeast
  7. Take 1/2 Lemon skin (grated)
  8. Take 150 g Chocolate chips
  9. Get 1 Tbsp Vainilla extract

Instructions to make Chocolate chips Cupcakes:
  1. In one bowl, mix the flour, the baking powder, the dry yeast and the lemon skin. In another bowl, add the butter melted and the 4 eggs one bye one, already beat it ups, after add the greek yogurt and the vainilla extract. Mix everything until you get a very creamy mixture.
  2. Then, add by tablespoons the flour mixture that we have previously made and with wrapping movements we will integrate it until we get a creamy mass and a little thick at the same time. Add the chocolate chips and let the mass rest for 30 min in the fridge covered with a paper. After the 30 min put the mixture in the molds. Bake it for 20-15 min at 200°C in the oven (previously heat it at 220)
  3. Let the Cupcakes cool a bit and enjoy them.
