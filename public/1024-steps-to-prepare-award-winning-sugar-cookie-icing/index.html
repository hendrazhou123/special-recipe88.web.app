The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookie Icing:
  1. Take 4 cups powdered sugar
  2. Take 9 tsp milk
  3. Prepare 4 tsp corn syrup
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Almond emulsion
  5. Take 2 tsp lemon oil or extract

Instructions to make Sugar Cookie Icing:
  1. Measure out powdered sugar and set aside
  2. Measure milk and pour into medium to large bowl
  3. Slowly add powdered sugar, making sure to mix well after each addition, or it will become clumpy.
  4. After all sugar has been added, mixture WILL BE VERY THICK. Add your corn syrup, and mix well.
  5. Add your extracts. I like almond emulsion because it is thicker and tastes better, as with the lemon oil, however, extracts will work.
  6. If mixture is still too thick, you can add more corn syrup, but only a little at a time.
  7. Separate and color!
