The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup Soft butter
  2. Take 3/4 cups sugar
  3. Get 1 Eggs
  4. Prepare 1 tesp vanilla essence
  5. Get 1 &¾ cups flour
  6. Get 1/2 tesp baking powder
  7. Take 1/4 tesp salt

Instructions to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. In a mixer beat the butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  2. Add the egg and mix well. Sifted flour salt and baking powder add in this and mix to crest a dough.
  3. Wrap dough and keep in fridge for one hour.
  4. Roll ½ inch thick dough and cut in desired shape.
  5. Bake them on 180°C for 15 minutes. They are ready allow to cool and then use.
