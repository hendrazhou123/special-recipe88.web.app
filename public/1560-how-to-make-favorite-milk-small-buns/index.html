The ingredients needed to make Milk small buns😊:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Take 1 cup hot milk
  3. Make ready 1 tbsn yeast
  4. Prepare 1 tsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 1/2cup butter
  7. Take 1 egg yolk
  8. Take ghee to grease tray

Steps to make Milk small buns😊:
  1. Take a bowl add hot milk, sugar yeast.
  2. Salt and mix 2 mins now add the flour and form the dough… now add the warm butter and knead the dough..
  3. Now cover the dough and keep it in warm place to rise…(1 hour) now again punch the dough make 2 equal dough balls.
  4. Now each ball cut 8 pieces and form small dough balls and keep on a greased baking tray..now again cover and keep for an hour to rise the dough.pic3 preheat ur oven for 5 mins at 180°.
  5. Now brush with egg yolk and bake for 180° for 15 to 20 mins or acc to ur oven settings.when done it's looks so beautiful.. check pic3
  6. Now soon cover the buns with wet cloth this will keep ur buns soft and spongy…enjoy😊Please watch out and subscribe my #youtube blog Tanvi.s 25 for all the clips.. #happycooking #lockdown
