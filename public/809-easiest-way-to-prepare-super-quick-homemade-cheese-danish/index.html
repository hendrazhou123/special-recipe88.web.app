The ingredients needed to make Cheese Danish:
  1. Get cream cheese(room temperature or a bit warmer)
  2. Get pastry dough(or how ever many you would like)
  3. Get Lemon juice
  4. Prepare Butter (I melted mine)
  5. Take sugar (I added to my liking)
  6. Get vanilla
  7. Get sour cream
  8. Take Powered sugar (optional)

Danish cheese is the name of cheese that is made in Denmark. Go through the article and get information on the various types of Danish cheese. This Cheese Danish Recipe will blow your socks off! It's not only so easy but also so SO delicious and drizzled with icing on top!

Steps to make Cheese Danish:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F
  2. Dice the cream cheese into small cubes. Put those into a mixing bowl and put that on a low speed.
  3. Add all the other ingredients once the cream cheese is loose enough. (It should look smooth with a bit of chunks).
  4. Gradually increase the speed of the mixer
  5. It should be creamy.
  6. Take pastry dough a assemble to your liking (I looked up 5 minute crafts pastry and got mine)
  7. Add as much as needed to each pastry.
  8. Then add in what you like.
  9. Place them on a sprayed cookie pan and place in oven.
  10. Take them out. Top with powder sugar
