Steps to make Buttery cheesee garlic bread:
  1. Keep ready all ingredients for this preparation.
  2. Now take Lukewarm milk add yeast and sugar keep aside for 10 mins.
  3. Meantime sieve dry ingredients of dough 2 times. add yeast mix slowly mix and knead add 1tbsp oil. Around this and keep aside for 1 hour
  4. After one hr we check the dough it rise and doubled. Now punch it and rest for 5-10 mins again. Then divide the dough into 2 equal portions.
  5. Take one portion, roll it with help of dusting flour on the counter top.
  6. Meanwhile at that time add butter and herbs in garlic.
  7. Spread the mix on 1/2portion of rolled sheet cover with another half. Put it in the microwave at 180 in convection mode for 14-16 min.
  8. Serve hot.
