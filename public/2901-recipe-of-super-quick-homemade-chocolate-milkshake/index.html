The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Milkshake:
  1. Make ready 2 scoops vanilla/chocolate ice cream
  2. Get 3 tbsp cocoa powder/1 and 1/2 tbsp if using chocolate ice cream
  3. Take 1 small frozen banana
  4. Get 4-5 tbsp milk of choice
  5. Make ready Afew chocolate biscuits (This is for texture)
  6. Get Sugar, to taste (I used 1 tbsp)
  7. Make ready Salt, to taste (I used 1/8 tsp)
  8. Make ready Toppings
  9. Take Biscuits of choice
  10. Prepare Chocolate syrup

Whipping cream makes this chocolate milkshake creamy and as light as air. Drizzle with chocolate syrup after you pour in the cream for an even more sensational chocolaty treat. Instructions to make Chocolate Milkshake:
  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Blend everything together until smooth. Pour in a glass. Drizzle on top some chocolate syrup and serve with biscuits if desired. Serve cold. Enjoy!
