Instructions to make Peanut chutney (navratri special):
  1. Firstly dry roast peanuts… Remove brown part…
  2. Take peanuts.. add ginger, chillies, salt, jeera n lil water… Grind by adding lil water… N make fine paste
  3. Then put that mix in bowl… Make tadka.. take pan add 1 spoon ghee.. add jeera n kadi pta… Saute n pour on mixture…
  4. Ur chutney is ready… Enjoy with pakodas, tikkis n theplas☺️
