The ingredients needed to make Quick vanilla cake:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cup flour
  5. Take 1 3/4 baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup vanilla almond milk or regular milk
  7. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract

Steps to make Quick vanilla cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F
  2. Grease and flour cake pan
  3. Cream butter and sugar
  4. Add eggs and beat well
  5. Beat in vanilla
  6. Add flour and baking powder and beat well
  7. Stir in milk
  8. Pour in cake pan and bake for 20-25 minutes
