The ingredients needed to make Totally Stolen Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Get 1 1\4 Cups Firmly Packed Brown Sugar
  2. Make ready 3 \4 Cup Smooth Peanut Butter
  3. Prepare 1 \2 Cup Vegetable Shortening
  4. Prepare 1 Large Egg
  5. Prepare 3 Tablespoons Milk
  6. Prepare 1 Teaspoon Vanilla Extract
  7. Get 1 3\4 Cups AP Flour
  8. Make ready 3 \4 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  9. Make ready 1 \2 Teaspoon Salt

Steps to make Totally Stolen Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Mix first 6 ingredients together until smooth.
  2. Mix the 3 dry ingredients together until well combined.
  3. Add dry ingredients into the wet until mixture forms a smooth dough. A stand mixer works wonders here.
  4. Put dough balls on baking sheet. Roughly the size of a golf ball. Using a fork slightly press down making a criss cross pattern.
  5. Bake at 375" for 8-9 minutes. Don't over bake. They may not look done, but they are. Cookies will be soft and chewy. Enjoy
