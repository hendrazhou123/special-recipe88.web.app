Instructions to make Breakfast Egg bread:
  1. Have your egg and bread ready
  2. Beat your eggs well,at this stage you can also add any ingredient of your choice.
  3. In a non stick pan start melting your butter at a medium heat
  4. After your butter has melted, place your slice of bread,let it cook for few seconds and turn it then place the beaten eggs on top it
  5. Turn it and let it cook on the other side
  6. After that enjoy your breakfast egg bread
