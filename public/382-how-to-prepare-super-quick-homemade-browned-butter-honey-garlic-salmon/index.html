The ingredients needed to make Browned Butter Honey Garlic Salmon:
  1. Get 2-4 salmon filets (about 1/2 pound each)
  2. Make ready 1 lemon
  3. Get 3 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Get 1/4 cup butter
  5. Take 1/4 cup honey
  6. Prepare to taste salt and pepper

This is one of the easiest salmon dishes to make at home. My mouth waters when I think of Brown Butter Honey Garlic Salmon. I've used the same recipe on other fish, but I like it best with salmon. It is so delicious, as just about anything is wonderful with brown butter!

Instructions to make Browned Butter Honey Garlic Salmon:
  1. Arrange oven shelf about 8 inches away from heat element. Set oven to broil
  2. Season salmon steaks with salt & pepper, set aside.
  3. Cook butter over medium heat, stirring for about 3 minutes or until the foam settles and the butter browns. (Use an oven proof frying pan.. if not you will have to transfer later to a baking dish)
  4. Pour in the honey and let it dissolve into butter.
  5. Add garlic, saute 1 minute
  6. Add juice from 1/2 lemon. Save the other half and cut into wedges.
  7. Add the salmon steaks skin side down and cook for 4 minutes while basting the tops with pan juices.
  8. (Transfer to oven proof pan if necessary) Arrange lemon wedges around the salmon. Place in oven and broil for another 6 minutes until tops of filets are charred.
  9. Serve and be sure to pour all of the sauce over the top of the filets! Sooooo yummy!
