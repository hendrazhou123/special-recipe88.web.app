The ingredients needed to make No bake peanut butter balls:
  1. Get 1 cup sifted powdered sugar
  2. Take 1 and a half cup of peanut butter
  3. Make ready 3 tbs butter, softened
  4. Make ready 1 lb dipping chocolate

Instructions to make No bake peanut butter balls:
  1. Stir together peanut butter, powder sugar, and butter until mixed well.
  2. Shape into 1 inch balls place them on wax paper. Let balls stand for 20 minutes until dry.
  3. Melt chocolate, dip balls in chocolate using a fork letting excess chocolate drip off. Place back on wax paper, let stand until drop.
  4. Store tightly covered in a cool dry place.
