The ingredients needed to make Homemade Instant Taco 🌮 (leftover chapati/wheat bread):
  1. Get 6 Leftover chapatis
  2. Make ready 1 big potato - boiled, peeled & mashed well
  3. Get 2 medium potatoes - boiled, peeled & mashed well
  4. Prepare 3 small potatoes - boiled, peeled & mashed well
  5. Get As needed Cheese of choice; grated
  6. Prepare 1 medium onion diced
  7. Get 1 tsp red pepper flakes (as needed; optional)
  8. Prepare To taste Salt
  9. Take As needed Tabasco Smoked Paprika sauce (optional)
  10. Make ready 5 gms Crushed red pepper or as per preference

Steps to make Homemade Instant Taco 🌮 (leftover chapati/wheat bread):
  1. To the mashed potato; mix in salt, onion and crushed pepper and Tabasco sauce. Let sit for 2 minutes.
  2. Meanwhile, gather the rest of the ingredients. Now, open a leftover chapati and add cheese on to it. Then spoon in the masala prepared in Step 1. Fold the chapati in half thereby closing it to form the shape of a taco.
  3. Repeat with remaining chapatis. Serve hot with tomato ketchup.
