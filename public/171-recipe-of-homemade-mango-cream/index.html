Steps to make Mango cream:
  1. Beat Cream and powdered sugar. Beat till the cream thickens. Add vanilla extract and mix
  2. Peel and chop one mango in small cubes
  3. Peel and puree another one in mixer/blender. Make a smooth puree
  4. Take a cup, at the bottom pour mango puree, then some mango cubes and then cream. You can layer it same way again. Sprinkle orange zest.
  5. On the top garnish it with mango cubes.
  6. Chill this in freezer for half and hour and serve chilled.
  7. Tip-Beat cream after keeping it in freezer for half an hour. Beat till you have soft peaks in cream.
