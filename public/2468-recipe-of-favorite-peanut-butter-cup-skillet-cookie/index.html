The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Cup Skillet Cookie:
  1. Get 1 package Betty Crocker peanut butter cookie mix
  2. Make ready 1 large egg
  3. Make ready 6 tbsp unsalted butter, softened
  4. Take cups Mini unwrapped peanut butter
  5. Prepare Chocolate syrup
  6. Get Vanilla ice cream
  7. Make ready 8 ” shallow cast iron skillet

Steps to make Peanut Butter Cup Skillet Cookie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Spray skillet with cooking spray.
  2. Make cookie dough according to package instructions, mixing in egg and butter.
  3. Press dough into the skillet using your hands. Can also spray the back of a large spoon with cooking spray and use that to press the dough down. You want it flat and even all over.
  4. Push peanut butter cups into the dough, about 1 inches apart.
  5. Bake approximately 35 mins until the outer edges are browned and middle is done.
  6. Serve with chocolate syrup and ice cream.
