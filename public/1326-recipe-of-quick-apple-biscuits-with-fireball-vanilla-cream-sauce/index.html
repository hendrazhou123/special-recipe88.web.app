  2. Prepare 1 lb Biscuit mix- I use Krusteaz biscuit mix
  3. Prepare 1 cup Buttermilk
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp Cinnamon

  11. Take fireball sauce
  12. Take 1 packages Vanilla pudding - small pack

  14. Make ready 2 oz Fireball whisky or cinnamon liquor
  15. Take Topping
  16. Take 1 cup Brown sugar

Instructions to make Apple Biscuits With Fireball Vanilla Cream Sauce:
  1. Core and slice apples. In small pot place sliced apples, water, sugar and cinnamon and cook on high for 7 min. Your not looking for thickened liquid. After you cook the apples set aside.
  2. Put dry biscuit mix in mixing bowl add buttermilk, cinnamon, cheese and cooled apples. Mix well by hand.
  3. Divide apple and biscuit mix into 12 muffin slots. I use a non stick muffin pan. Sprinkle brown sugar on top of all muffin.
  4. Bake in 375°F oven for 16 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  5. For fireball sauce: make vanilla pudding according to directions on box. After add fireball and heavy cream. Whisk until smooth.
  6. Garnish with caramel. Enjoy!!!
