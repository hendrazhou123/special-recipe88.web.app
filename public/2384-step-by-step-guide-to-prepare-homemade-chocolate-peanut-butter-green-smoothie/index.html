The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Peanut Butter Green Smoothie:
  1. Get 1 frozen chopped banana (peeled)
  2. Make ready 1 cup tightly-packed fresh spinach
  3. Prepare 1 cup Ice
  4. Get 1 cup Milk (dairy milk, almond milk, etc.)
  5. Get 3 tbsp. Peanut butter
  6. Prepare 3 tbsp. Unsweetened cocoa powder
  7. Prepare 2 tbsp. Chia seeds (optional)
  8. Make ready 1-2 tbsp. Honey
  9. Make ready 1/2 tsp. Vanilla extract
