The ingredients needed to make Strawberry Vanilla Overnight Oats:
  1. Get 1/4-1/3 cup milk of choice (less if you like it thicker)
  2. Prepare 4 oz. yogurt of choice
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup sliced strawberries
  4. Get 1/2 tsp. vanilla extract
  5. Get 1/2 cup old fashioned rolled oats
  6. Take 1 tbsp. each chia seeds, ground flax seed, hemp seeds (optional)
  7. Make ready 1-2 tbsp. additional sweetener (optional)
