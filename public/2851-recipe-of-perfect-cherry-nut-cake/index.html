The ingredients needed to make Cherry Nut Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 box white cake mix (yellow will work also)
  2. Take 1 small box instant vanilla pudding (I prefer French vanilla)
  3. Make ready 1 small jar Maraschino cherries drained, but save the juice (reserve 3 tablespoons juice for frosting)
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  5. Take 3 eggs
  6. Get 1/3 cup oil vegetable or canola
  7. Make ready 3/4 cup water (up to 3/4 cup can be used, use your cherry juice minus 3 tablespoons, plus water to equal 1 cup)
  8. Get Cherry Buttercream icing (see last step of instructions if you want to use canned icing)
  9. Take 1 stick room temperature butter or margarine
  10. Take 3 tablespoon Cherry juice
  11. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla
  12. Take Milk if needed
  13. Prepare 4 cups or more of Powdered sugar

Steps to make Cherry Nut Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees farenheit
  2. Chop 12 cherries into halves or thirds and put into mix last.
  3. Mix cake mix and pudding mix together.
  4. Set aside 3 tablespoons cherry juice for icing.
  5. Pour remaining cherry juice into a 1 cup measure and add water to equal one cup.
  6. To the dry mixture add the 1 cup of juice/water mixture, oil, eggs, beating about 2 minutes till well mixed then add 1/4 cup chopped nuts, chopped cherries and mix those in.
  7. Bake. Cupcakes take about 20 min, bundt 45-50 min, 9x13 pan 25-33 min. Use the cake mix instruction as a guide. Cake is done when toothpick inserted comes out clean.
