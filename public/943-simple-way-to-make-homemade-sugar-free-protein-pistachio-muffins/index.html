The ingredients needed to make Sugar Free Protein Pistachio Muffins:
  1. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  2. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Take 1/2 cup Splenda
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 1 box sugar free pistachio pudding
  6. Take 1 1/3 cup fat free milk
  7. Take 1 3/4 c flour
  8. Make ready 2 scoops unflavored protein powder or vanilla
  9. Prepare 2/3 cup pistachios

Steps to make Sugar Free Protein Pistachio Muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees. Cream softened butter and Splenda. Add eggs, vanilla extract, and baking powder
  2. Add milk, flour, protein powder, and pudding mix. Add pistachios last.
  3. Bake for 20 min. No longer or they’ll be dry

These Pistachio Muffins taste like they came from a bakery with their perfectly domed tops and delicious pistachio flavor.
