The ingredients needed to make Vegan Keto Peanut Butter Protein Bars:
  1. Get 1 tbs ground flax seeds
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup nondairy milk
  3. Make ready 2 tbs powdered sweetener
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup unsweetened creamy peanut butter
  5. Get 1/2 cup pea protein powder or any vegan protein powder
  6. Prepare 2 tbs cacao nibs

An easy no-bake recipe, these gluten-free bars are made without graham crackers. Chocolate and peanut butter: the perfect match ❤️. I adore these keto peanut butter bars! They are also vegan, paleo These simple energy bars are ketogenic because I use a keto-friendly brown sugar substitute.

Steps to make Vegan Keto Peanut Butter Protein Bars:
  1. Line a9 by 5 “ pan with parchment paper. In a bowl whisk together the flax seeds and non dairy milk, sweetener.
  2. Add peanut butter and protein powder until thickened.
  3. Press the dough into lines pan and sprinkle with cacao nibs in the top. Gently press the bibs into the dough so they stick.refrigerate for an hour or so. Until firmed. I kept in the freezer 45 mints - 1 hour, iAnd slice to serve.
  4. You will have 5-6 bars(my daughter already eat when I arrives home)
