Instructions to make Sprouts moong & paneer kulcha:
  1. First take a bowl & add whole wheat flour
  2. Add curd sugar salt oil and cooking soda & mix very well & make dough like pratha
  3. Keep a side dough for rest 20 min
  4. Boiled potatoes & moong mash it
  5. Take sprouts moong & panner & crush it
  6. Take a bowl & add crush sprouts moong & panner mash potatoes & boiled moong
  7. Add green chilli & garlic paste salt chopped coriander turmeric powder aamchor powder chat masala & garam masala & mix very well like dough
  8. Make small ball
  9. Make ready dough & roll kulcha add sprouts moong & panner stuffing & again roll & sprinkle coriander
  10. Spread one side water on kulcha
  11. Gas on medium flame make tawa & water side kulcha on it
  12. See bubbles on kulcha & one side rost on direct medium slow flame
  13. Ready kulcha & keep side & spread butter ya cheese
  14. Ready & enjoy very healthy sprouts moong panner kulcha & serve to curd
