The ingredients needed to make Qamardeen milk pudding:
  1. Take 2 1/2 cups milk
  2. Get 1/2 cup cream
  3. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Take 4 tbsp cornstarch, dissolved in 1/2 cup milk
  5. Prepare 2 tsp orange blossom water
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp ground mastic
  7. Prepare For qamardeen topping:
  8. Take 3 cups hot water
  9. Get 200 g qamardeen
  10. Get 3 tbsp sugar
  11. Get 4 tbsp corn starch
  12. Prepare 1 tsp orange blossom water

Steps to make Qamardeen milk pudding:
  1. Soak qamardeen in hot water for a couple of hours.
  2. Meanwhile, in a medium saucepan, bring milk, cream & sugar to a boil, add the mix of cornstarch dissolved in milk, and keep stirring over medium heat. Keep stirring for 20 minutes or till milk gets thickened.
  3. Add orange blossom water and mastic. Pour in serving cups and leave to cool.
  4. Now place qamardeen, water, cornstarch and sugar in a blender, strain the mix into a saucepan and bring to a boil over medium heat wait till thickened.
  5. Pour it carefully over milk pudding cups. Refrigerate and garnish with pistachios & qamardeen swirls.
