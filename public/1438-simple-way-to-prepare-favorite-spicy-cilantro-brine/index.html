The ingredients needed to make Spicy Cilantro Brine:
  1. Take 1 C hot water
  2. Take 1 C cold water
  3. Make ready 3 T salt
  4. Prepare 2 T sugar
  5. Prepare 1 T brown sugar
  6. Make ready 1/4 C vinegar
  7. Get 2/3 C cilantro; chopped
  8. Take 1 jalapeño; minced
  9. Make ready 1 1/2 t ground coriander
  10. Take 1 t minced garlic
  11. Take 3/4 t dried oregano
  12. Take 3/4 t onion powder
  13. Prepare 3/4 t crushed pepper flakes
  14. Prepare 1/2 t black pepper
