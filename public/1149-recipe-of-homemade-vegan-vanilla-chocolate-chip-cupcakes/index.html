The ingredients needed to make Vegan Vanilla Chocolate Chip Cupcakes:
  1. Take 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 3/4 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup vegan butter
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  6. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Take 2 flaxseed eggs
  8. Take 1 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup soy milk
  10. Prepare 1/2 cup vegan chocolate chips (EX: enjoy life)

Steps to make Vegan Vanilla Chocolate Chip Cupcakes:
  1. Blend butter and sugar
  2. Whisk in a separate bowl, baking powder, baking soda, flour, and salt.
  3. Add whisked ingredients to butter and sugar.
  4. Add flax eggs, vanilla, and soy milk.
  5. Fold in chocolate chips.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 25 minutes on the middle rack.
  7. Cool for 10 minutes and top with your favorite icing or buttercream.
  8. Enjoy!
