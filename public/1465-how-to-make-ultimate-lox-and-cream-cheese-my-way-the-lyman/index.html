The ingredients needed to make Lox and cream cheese MY WAY- THE LYMAN:
  1. Make ready 1 bagel (i like "everything" bagels for this)
  2. Get 1 oz lox (smoked salmon)
  3. Prepare 1 cup plain cream cheese
  4. Prepare 1 tomato
  5. Take 1 small white onion
  6. Get 1 brocolli sprouts
  7. Prepare 1 can capers

Instructions to make Lox and cream cheese MY WAY- THE LYMAN:
  1. Slice and toast the bagel
  2. While the bagel is toasting, slice both the tomato and onion THINLY
  3. Smear cream cheese on both sides of bagel and spread capers (about 20 - 30) around both slices
  4. Add the lox, tomato, onion, and brocolli sprouts in that order on the "bottom side" of the bagel so the sprouts sit on top
  5. Make into a sandwich and enjoy!!!! Slicing helps not to gorge the sandwich
  6. NOTE: Goes well with a dill pickle - nice fresh crunch in between bites
