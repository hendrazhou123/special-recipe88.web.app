The ingredients needed to make Yummy cookie cake:
  1. Get 2 tsp coco powder
  2. Take 3 tsp Powder sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup maida
  4. Get 3 tsp unsalted butter
  5. Get 1 pack Gems
  6. Take as required Vanilla essence
  7. Take as required Milk
  8. Take 1 Small pieces of dairy milk (optional)

Instructions to make Yummy cookie cake:
  1. Melt butter and add powder and whisk.
  2. Add maida, cocoa powder and vanilla essence and mix (add small pieces of dairy milk).
  3. Add some milk to make it slightly sticky.
  4. Keep it in the fridge for half an hour.
  5. Remove it from the fridge make round dough.
  6. Preheat the oven on 180 degrees and put the round balls in the oven set the time 25 mins.
  7. After 25 mins remove the cookie cakes and let it cool.
  8. Decorate it with powder sugar and gems.
  9. Your delicious cookie cake is ready to enjoy.
