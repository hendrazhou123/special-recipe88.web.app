The ingredients needed to make Brown Sugar Crinkles:
  1. Get 3/4 cup butter, softened
  2. Get 1 cup packed brown sugar
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup molasses
  5. Take 2 1/4 cup flour
  6. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Get 1/2 tsp cloves
  9. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  10. Get 1 tsp ginger

Instructions to make Brown Sugar Crinkles:
  1. Mix butter, sugar, egg, and molasses. (I used a duck egg here.)
  2. Add dry ingredients and chill. (I used whole wheat flour.)
  3. Heat oven 375 degrees roll dough in sugar, (one-inch balls) - 7-10 minutes
