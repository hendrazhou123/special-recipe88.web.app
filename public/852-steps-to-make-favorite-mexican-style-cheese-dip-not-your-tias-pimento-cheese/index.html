The ingredients needed to make Mexican-Style Cheese Dip (Not your Tia’s pimento cheese):
  1. Prepare 4 oz cream cheese spread (or softened…but NOT whipped)
  2. Prepare 2 oz mayo
  3. Take 1/3 cup cotija cheese
  4. Make ready 4 tbs red bell pepper, finely diced
  5. Make ready 3 tbs Jalapeno, finely diced (only seed it if you want less spice)
  6. Make ready 2 tsp green or spring onion, finely diced
  7. Get 1 tsp minced garlic
  8. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/2 tsp crushed dried habanero (optional depending on your heat tolerance)
