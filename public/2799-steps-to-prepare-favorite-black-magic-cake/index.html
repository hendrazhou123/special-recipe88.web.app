The ingredients needed to make Black Magic Cake:
  1. Make ready 2 cups sugar
  2. Prepare 1 and 3/4 cup flour
  3. Take 3/4 cup cocoa powder
  4. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder (if using local brand)
  6. Get 1 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 2 eggs
  8. Prepare 1 cup strong black coffee
  9. Get 1 cup sour milk (mix 1 tbsp vinegar to milk and let stand 5 mins)
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Instructions to make Black Magic Cake:
  1. Heat oven to 350F.
  2. Grease and flour 2 8in round pans or 1 13x9x2in rectangular pan.
  3. In a large bowl, mix large ingredients.
  4. Add in remaining ingredients and beat on medium speed for 2 minutes. The batter will be very thin.
  5. Pour into pans/ rectangular pan.
  6. Bake for approx. 40-45 mins for round pans or 50-55 mins for rectangular pan.
  7. Test with a toothpick to make sure it comes out clean, especially in the center-you may need to add extra baking time.
  8. Cool 10 minutes and transfer the cakes to wire racks. Or leave in rectangular pan and cool completely.
