  2. Take 1 1/2 cup boiling water

  5. Get Cake

Steps to make Oatmeal Cake:
  1. Pour 1 1/4 cups boiling water over 1 cup oatmeal and a stick of butter. Let stand for 20 minutes.
  2. Beat together eggs and sugar, then add oatmeal mixture. Add all additional ingredients for the cake and mix well.
  3. Pour cake mix into a greased 9x13 inch pan.
  4. Toss ingredients for topping together in a separate bowl (not including powdered sugar). Before baking, sprinkle topping gently over cake. Bake at 350 degrees for 45 minutes.
  5. Once the cake is cooled 30 minutes dust with powdered sugar. Enjoy
