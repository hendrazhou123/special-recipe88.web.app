The ingredients needed to make cod fish with scramble eggs and fries (portuguese bacalhau a bras ):
  1. Make ready 600 grams drie salt cod (can use fillets if you prefer )
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp olive oil
  3. Get 2 medium onions thinly sliced
  4. Prepare 2 clove garlic shopped
  5. Prepare 500 grams potatoes ,julienned
  6. Get 8 eggs beaten
  7. Prepare 1 black olives and parsley to garnish
  8. Take 1 salt and pepper to taste

Steps to make cod fish with scramble eggs and fries (portuguese bacalhau a bras ):
  1. soak the cod in water for 48hours ,then shred it making sure you remove all bones and skin (if you use fillets no need to soak )
  2. in a large pan cook onions and garlic in olive oil until light golden,add the cod and stir
  3. in a separate pan deep fry the potatoes until golden ,add to the cod and mix through very gently with a fork
  4. before serve add the eggs and mix gently until the eggs are cooked
  5. season to taste
  6. garnish with black olives and parsley
