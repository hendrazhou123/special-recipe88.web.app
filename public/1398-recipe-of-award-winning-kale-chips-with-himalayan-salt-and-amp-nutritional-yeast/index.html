Olive oil and seasoned salt are all you need to turn fresh kale leaves into delicious good-for-you baked snacks. Be sure to use the "curly" kale variety as it tend to be less bitter. DO NOT EXPECT them to taste like potato chips. that was just an expression I used in the description.

To get started with this particular recipe, we must prepare a few components. You can have kale chips with himalayan salt & nutritional yeast using 4 ingredients and 7 steps. Here is how you cook it.

The ingredients needed to make Kale Chips With Himalayan Salt & Nutritional Yeast:
  1. Take 1 head Kale
  2. Make ready 3 tbsp Olive Oil
  3. Take 1 tsp Himalayan salt
  4. Get 6 tbsp Nutritional yeast

Though best served fresh, they can also be saved for later! I just made two batches seasoned with salt. The first batch I ate all of them before I could transfer them to. I've suffered through one too many batches of burned or soggy kale chips over the years.

Steps to make Kale Chips With Himalayan Salt & Nutritional Yeast:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F then wash, dry and tear Kale into large 4" pieces.
  2. Put Kale in a large bowl and rub olive oil into the Kale by hand to coat fully and evenly.
  3. Sprinkle Kale with salt and yeast and toss to coat.
  4. Spread Kale in large baking pan. I use a lasagna pan for the high sides.
  5. Put pan in oven and watch very carefully to avoid burning Kale. It will shrivel down to 1/4 the amount you started with. Stir frequently while cooking.
  6. You will be able to tell its done by feeling it. Kale should be crispy and dark
  7. Eat right away. Serves 4 as a small side dish or as a nutritious snack.
