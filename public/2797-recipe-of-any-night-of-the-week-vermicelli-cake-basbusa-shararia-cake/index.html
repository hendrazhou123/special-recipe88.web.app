The ingredients needed to make Vermicelli Cake/ Basbusa Shararia Cake:
  1. Take Cake mix:
  2. Get 2 eggs
  3. Take 1/2 cup oil
  4. Get 1 cup sugar
  5. Make ready 1 cup yogurt/ 1 can nestle cream
  6. Get 1 cup flour all purpose
  7. Get 3/4 cup coconut powder
  8. Take 1 tbsp baking powder
  9. Get Top of cake mix:
  10. Prepare 1 packet (150 g) vermicelli roasted pasta
  11. Prepare For garnishing:
  12. Take 3/4 can condensed milk

Instructions to make Vermicelli Cake/ Basbusa Shararia Cake:
  1. Mix egg,oil and sugar first then and yogurt. Using hand mixer mix it well then add all dry ingredients.
  2. Then put in a glossy tray. Then the vermicelli,make it small before spread up the cake mixed.
  3. Spread it good then put inside the oven for 25-25 minutes with the heat of 150°c.
  4. If the cake finish take it out from oven then add condensed milk. And wait to cold before you slice it so that it will not damage.
  5. Then serve.
