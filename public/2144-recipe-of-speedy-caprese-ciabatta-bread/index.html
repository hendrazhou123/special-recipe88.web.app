The ingredients needed to make Caprese ciabatta bread:
  1. Get 1/4 ciabatta baguette
  2. Get 1 Roma tomato
  3. Make ready Basil pesto
  4. Get Mozzarella cheese
  5. Take 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette

No kneading is required for this classic ciabatta bread. This bread is very moist and loose, but only use as much additional flour as you need to be able to handle it without it sticking. Steps to make Caprese ciabatta bread:
  1. Cut 1/4 of loaf of bread 1/2 inch slices. Place on baking pan and toast 8 minutes on 350.
  2. Spread pesto on each piece And top with mozzarella. Dice tomato and let soak in balsamic about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Place tomatoes on top of bread and serve.
