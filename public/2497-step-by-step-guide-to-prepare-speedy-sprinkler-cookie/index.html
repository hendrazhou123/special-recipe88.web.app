The ingredients needed to make Sprinkler Cookie:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Take 250 g butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 tspn vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp powdered milk
  6. Prepare Sprinkles

Steps to make Sprinkler Cookie:
  1. In a bowl, mix butter, vanilla extract, sugar and powdered milk to make a cream.
  2. Add flour and use your hand to knead the dough.
  3. Mold the dough into a flat balls and slightly press it into the sprinkles.
  4. Bake in a preheated oven at 180C for 15 minutes.
