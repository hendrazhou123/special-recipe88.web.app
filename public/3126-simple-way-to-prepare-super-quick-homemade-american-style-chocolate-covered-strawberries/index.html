The ingredients needed to make American style chocolate covered strawberries:

  2. Get 1 teaspoon extra virgin olive oil
  3. Get 1 pint strawberries

Steps to make American style chocolate covered strawberries:
  1. Rinse the strawberries nicely and let them dry
  2. In a sauce pan, melt chocolate with oil
  3. Dip the strawberries in the chocolate
  4. Lay the dipped strawberries on parchment paper and let dry
  5. Enjoy!
