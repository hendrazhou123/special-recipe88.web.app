The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 3/4 bananas
  2. Get 4 eggs
  3. Take 2 scoops peanut butter
  4. Prepare 2 scoops honey
  5. Get 2 cups gluten free flour
  6. Make ready 1 spoon baking powder
  7. Make ready 1 spoon vanilla extract
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup walnuts
  9. Take 2 spoons sweet chocolate powder
  10. Prepare 1/2 spoon salt
