The ingredients needed to make Gado Gado (Indonesian Salad with Peanut Dressings):
  1. Make ready Lontong (Indonesian rice cake)
  2. Prepare 2 medium potato, boiled, peeled, cut into small chunks
  3. Take 1 medium cucumber, cut into small chunks
  4. Get 150 gr bean sprouts, put into boiling water for 1 minute, drained
  5. Take 200 gr long beans, trimmed, cut into 1.5 inches pieces, boiled or baby spinach, put into boiling water for 1 minute, drained
  6. Prepare 2 pieces fried tofu, cut into small chunks
  7. Make ready 2 pieces fried soy bean cake, cut into small chunks
  8. Take 8 sheets lettuce, cut
  9. Take For the sauce:
  10. Take 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  11. Make ready 3 tsp coconut sugar
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp tamarind juice, from 1 tsp wet tamarind mixed with 2 tbsp water
  13. Get 200 ml instant coconut milk
  14. Prepare 3 kaffir lime leaves, cut away the bones
  15. Prepare 200 gr peanuts, fried, ground
  16. Make ready For the ground spices:
  17. Take 8 cloves garlic
  18. Prepare 5 red peppers
  19. Prepare 1 inch cutchery
  20. Make ready For the complement:
  21. Take 2 tomatoes, divided by 8
  22. Get 2 hard cooked large eggs, peeled, halved
  23. Make ready Emping (Indonesian chips)
  24. Prepare Indonesian crispy crackers
  25. Make ready Chili sauce (made of 8 bird eyes chili peppers and 4 cloves garlic, boiled, drained, ground)
