Instructions to make Pizza dough:
  1. Take 1/2 C warm water and add the yeast, 1 tablespoon flour and the sugar. Still until dissolved, cover and let sit to become frothy about 10 min.
  2. Take the remaining dry ingredients and blend together. To the dry ingredients add 1 C. Warm water PLUS the 1/2 C. Yeast water. Stir by hand until ball forms. Knead it for about 10 min.
  3. Cover dough and let sit to rise for 1 hour.
  4. Spread out onto baking sheet and bake just the crust for 10 min at 350 degrees Fahrenheit. Take out of oven and add whatever topping you fancy.
