The ingredients needed to make Garlic Butter Pork Belly:
  1. Prepare 300 g pork belly
  2. Make ready 1/4-1/2 c water
  3. Make ready 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Get 1/2 tbsp Cooking oil
  5. Make ready 1/4 bar butter
  6. Get to taste Salt and pepper

Steps to make Garlic Butter Pork Belly:
  1. Simmer whole pork belly (salted) in water in a pan, flip when water has reduced halfway.
  2. Add cooking oil and let fat render and brown. Cover, careful with oil spits. Flip on other side and brown. Do all of this in 5 mins. As pork easily gets tough when cooked too long.
  3. Cut into bite size, return to pan with browned garlic in butter. Season with salt and pepper. Sauté quickly and serve.
  4. Optional Fried Rice: In same pan, place some day old rice, spread out and let it absorb juices. Sauté til rice has separated individually and changed color because of drippings. Enjoy with your pork.
