Instructions to make Brown Sugar Frosting:
  1. Combine brown sugar and butter in a sauce pan. Cook until bubbly. Whisk constantly so it doesn't burn.
  2. Take off the heat and stir in the milk.
  3. Gradually add in powdered sugar. Whisk until smooth. The frosting is ready.
  4. The cake doesn't have to be cooled completely to top with this frosting. Work quickly. The frosting hardens fast as it cools.
