The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Banana Breakfast Cookies:
  1. Get 2 overripe bananas
  2. Get 5 tbsp rolled oats
  3. Prepare 7 tbsp peanut butter
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  5. Prepare 3/4 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare Pinch salt
  7. Make ready bars Chocolate chips or chocolate

You need only four ingredients to make them and they're completely sugar-free. And these vegan chocolate banana cookies fulfill both of these criteria. The recipe seriously couldn't be easier! An easy recipe for chewy banana oatmeal cookies studded with plenty of chocolate chips!

Steps to make Chocolate Banana Breakfast Cookies:
  1. Mash the bananas until smooth, set aside.
  2. Combine the rest of ingredients together, until all mixed well. Mix the mashed banana together. Leave to rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Take the cookie dough using spoon and put it on parchment paper. Put some chocolate chips or any toppings you like on top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven, 170 degrees Celcius, for 10-15 minutes or until the cookies are nicely set.
