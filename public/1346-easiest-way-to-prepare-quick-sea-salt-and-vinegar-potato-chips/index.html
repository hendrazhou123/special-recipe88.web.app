The ingredients needed to make Sea salt and vinegar potato chips:
  1. Take 1 large potato (Yukon gold, or any all purpose potato)
  2. Get 3 teaspoons black pepper
  3. Get 3 teaspoons ground sea salt
  4. Get 2 cups distilled white vinegar

Steps to make Sea salt and vinegar potato chips:
  1. Thinly slice one large potato into 1/2 cm slices.
  2. Soak the sliced potatoes in 2 cups of vinegar for 10 minutes.
  3. Drain the potatoes, and rub in the black pepper and sea salt.
  4. Spread onto a baking tray and bake in a preheated oven at 190 degrees celsius for 35 minutes.
