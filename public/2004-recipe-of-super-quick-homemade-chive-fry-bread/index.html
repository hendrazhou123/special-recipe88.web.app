The ingredients needed to make Chive Fry Bread:
  1. Take 2 cups all-purpose flour plus more for dusting
  2. Take 1 tablespoon leveled baking powder
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup peanut oil
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 1 cup warm water
  6. Take 2 tablespoons chopped chives
  7. Make ready As needed peanut oil to fry in
  8. Make ready To taste butter

Mix the ingredients except the butter. Add flour ontop of the mixture so it won't stick to your hands and fingers. Heat the oil to shallow fry the bread. Turn often to fry all the way through.

Steps to make Chive Fry Bread:
  1. Mix the ingredients except the butter. Add flour ontop of the mixture so it won't stick to your hands and fingers.
  2. Heat the oil to shallow fry the bread. Turn often to fry all the way through. When done move to paper towels to absorb excessive amounts of oil. Top with butter. Serve I hope you enjoy!!!!
