The ingredients needed to make Easy Garlic Butter Monkey Bread:
  1. Make ready 2 cans refrigerated biscuits (8 count) - such as Pillsbury
  2. Get 1/2 cup grated parmesan
  3. Prepare 2 tsp dried parsley
  4. Take 2 tsp garlic powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup finely shredded mozzarella or Italian Cheese blend
  6. Take 1 stick butter - melted

Instructions to make Easy Garlic Butter Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°F. Spray a bundt pan generously with cooking spray. Set aside.
  2. Put parmesan, parsley, and garlic powder in a gallon size ziploc bag (crush parsley slightly to almost make it powder). Seal and shake to mix.
  3. Open and separate biscuits. Cut each biscuit into quarters. Roll each quarter into a ball.
  4. Working in small batches add dough balls to parmesan mixture, seal bag, and shake to coat.
  5. Distribute coated biscuits evenly in bundt pan, layering with shredded cheese.
  6. Sprinkle remaining parmesan cheese mixture evenly over the top. Then drizzle melted butter evenly all around the top, making sure to get some around the outer edge.
  7. Bake uncovered until a skewer inserted in the center comes out clean 25-30 minutes. Note: Place foil on top if it looks like it's getting too dark at any point.
  8. Remove from oven. Let cool in pan 10 minutes. Carefully turn out onto a serving plate. Pull apart. Dip in marinara, pizza sauce, additional garlic butter, ranch or my Pizza dip. Enjoy!
