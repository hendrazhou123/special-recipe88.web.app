The ingredients needed to make Guacamole raisin peanuts POHA:
  1. Prepare 1 cup rinsed poha
  2. Get 1 big chopped onion
  3. Make ready 1 green chilli optional
  4. Make ready Few soaked raisins
  5. Take 2 tbsp guacamole
  6. Get Few peanuts optional
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp curry powder
  8. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  9. Make ready 1 tsp red chili powder
  10. Get Half tsp black pepper
  11. Take 1/3 tsp turmeric
  12. Prepare Half tsp chaat masala
  13. Take Half tsp cumin seeds
  14. Take Half tsp mustard seeds
  15. Make ready Half tsp coriander powder
  16. Prepare Half tsp garam masala

Instructions to make Guacamole raisin peanuts POHA:
