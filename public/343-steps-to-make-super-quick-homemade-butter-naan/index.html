The ingredients needed to make Butter Naan:
  1. Prepare 2 cups whole wheat flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup curd
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp. Baking powder
  4. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Get to taste Salt
  6. Make ready 1 tbsp chopped coriander
  7. Take 1 tsp. Black sesame seeds
  8. Get 1 tbsp Butter

Instructions to make Butter Naan:
  1. Take whole wheat flour in a paraat add salt baking soda baking powder and curd. Mix well and knead a tight dough. Leave it for 2 hours.
  2. Now take a dough and make a balls. And roll in oval shape. Sprinkle chopped coriander and black sessme seeds. And roll lightly to stick.
  3. Now heat tawa on a flame. Apply water on back side naan and roast on flame. 3-4 mins. Take in a plate and apply butter. And ready to serve with subji.
