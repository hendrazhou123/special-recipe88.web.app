The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip cookie cheesecake bars:
  1. Take Crust:
  2. Get 2 cup Flour
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp Baking Soda
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
  5. Make ready 2/3 cup softened Butter
  6. Get 1/2 cup Sugar
  7. Make ready 2/3 cup Brown Sugar

  10. Take 2 cup Chocolate Chips
  11. Get Filling
  12. Prepare 8 oz softened Cream Cheese
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup Sugar

  15. Take 1 tsp Vanilla

Steps to make Chocolate chip cookie cheesecake bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. - Line a 9x9-inch square baking pan with parchment paper or aluminum foil and spray with nonstick spray. - Whisk together flour, baking soda and salt for crust.
  2. Cream together butter, brown sugar and sugar until fluffy.
  3. Add egg and vanilla and whip to incorporate. - Add flour mixture and mix until incorporated. - Fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Press 2/3 of the mixture into the bottom of the prepared pan to form an even layer. - Combine filling ingredients and beat until blended.
  5. Spread filling over the cookie dough layer and level.
  6. Crumble remaining crust dough over the filling layer. - Bake for 40 minutes, or until cheesecake is set and cookie dough has browned. - Allow to cool completely and chill before slicing.
