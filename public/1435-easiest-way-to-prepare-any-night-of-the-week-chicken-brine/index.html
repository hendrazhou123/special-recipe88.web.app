The ingredients needed to make Chicken Brine:
  1. Prepare 2 ounces Sage

  3. Get 2 ounces Tlyme

  5. Make ready 1/2 Cup Granulated Sugar
  6. Take 1/4 Cup Brown Sugar
  7. Get 1/4 Cup Apple Cider Vinegar
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup Kosher Salt
  9. Take 4 Gallons Water
