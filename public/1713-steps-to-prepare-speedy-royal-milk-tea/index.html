The ingredients needed to make Royal Milk Tea:
  1. Prepare 2 bag red/ black tea (I used red)
  2. Prepare 1/4 mug water (I used my coffee mug)
  3. Make ready 3/4 mug milk (I used low fat)
  4. Prepare 1 tbs brown sugar (adjust your sweetness level)
