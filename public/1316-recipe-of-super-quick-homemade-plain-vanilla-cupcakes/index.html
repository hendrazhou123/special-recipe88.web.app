The ingredients needed to make Plain Vanilla Cupcakes:
  1. Make ready 2 large Eggs
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup Whole Milk
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup Vegetable Oil
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Imitation Vanilla Extract
  5. Take 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  6. Take 1/2 cup Granulated Sugar
  7. Take 2 1/4 tbsp Baking Powder
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp Salt

Instructions to make Plain Vanilla Cupcakes:
  1. Preheat the oven for 350°F.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the egg, oil, milk, and vanilla.
  3. In a separate bowl, sift the flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  4. Add the flour mixture to the wet ingredients.
  5. Spoon the batter into the muffin pan lined with some parchment wrappers.
  6. Bake it for 28 minutes.
  7. After its baked, cool on the wire rack, and let it cool completely before you can frost.
