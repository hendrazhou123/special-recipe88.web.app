The ingredients needed to make A simple home made mawe tatu cake:
  1. Take 2 cups flour(self raising)
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  4. Make ready 1 pckt of milk
  5. Get 4 eggs
  6. Make ready 250 g Chocolate
  7. Take 1 lemon
  8. Make ready Water
  9. Take Margarine

Instructions to make A simple home made mawe tatu cake:
  1. Put the margarine in a bowl and mix with sugar. Mix till its whitish
  2. Add the beaten eggs to the mixture and use a whisk to stir
  3. Add the flour and stir until its well mixed
  4. Add milk to the mixture and stir until the whole mixture is light
  5. Divide the mixture into two portions..in two separate bowls. In one bowl, add chocolate and star until its distributed evenly to the whole mixture. Don't add chocolate in the other bowl.
  6. Put three stones and two jugs of water in one big sufuria and let it heat for 5 minutes.
  7. Emmerse in the sufuria with your dough mixture in the big sufuria and cover with a lid
  8. Let it cook for 1½hours. Serve while hot with the drink of your choice.
