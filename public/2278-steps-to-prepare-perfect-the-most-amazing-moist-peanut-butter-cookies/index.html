The ingredients needed to make The most amazing moist peanut butter cookies!:
  1. Make ready 1 stick butter (softened)
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  3. Take 1 1/4 cup white flour
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  5. Get 1/2 light brown sugar (or 1/4 cup honey)
  6. Take 1 egg (room temperature)
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  9. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla
  10. Prepare White sugar (separate)

Steps to make The most amazing moist peanut butter cookies!:
  1. Mix together (with an electric mixer) butter and peanut butter until smooth
  2. Add 1/2 cup flour, both sugars, baking soda, egg, baking powder, and vanilla. Mix well
  3. Make your dough into balls and place on an un greased cookie sheet. Then roll in white sugar
  4. Make a cross cross mark with a fork.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 7-9 minutes
