The ingredients needed to make Oreo cookie shake:
  1. Prepare 6-8 oreo biscuits
  2. Get 11/2 cup milk
  3. Make ready 1 tbsp chocolate syrup
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp Coffee powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tbsp sugar
  6. Take 5-6 cubes ice

Instructions to make Oreo cookie shake:
  1. In a mixer jar add 6 oreo biscuits.
  2. Pour milk, chocolate syrup, sugar, coffee powder & 2-3 ice cubes.
  3. Churn it for a while and mix well.
  4. Transfer it to a serving glass. Top up with oreo biscuit and serve.
