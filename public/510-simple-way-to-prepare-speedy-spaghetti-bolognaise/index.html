The ingredients needed to make Spaghetti Bolognaise:
  1. Get 250 g spaghetti
  2. Make ready 500 g minced beef
  3. Take 1/2 onion (chopped)
  4. Take 3 garlic (chopped)
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp ketchup
  6. Get 1 tbsp margarine / butter (I used Blue Band)
  7. Get Salt
  8. Take Sugar
  9. Make ready Oil
  10. Get Grated parmesan cheese
  11. Get Oregano

Instructions to make Spaghetti Bolognaise:
  1. Boil the water, cook the pasta for 6 minutes to get it al dente (10 minutes for my choice), add a pinch of salt and two drops of olive oil
  2. Heat a pan, put margarine or butter in, fry onion then followed with garlic until it's golden brown.
  3. Add the ground beef and when it's cooked, add ketchup, salt and sugar. Taste as preferred.
  4. Add a bit of water to have more sauce, and sprinkle with oregano.
  5. On a plate, take the boiled pasta, add some bolognaise sauce, then sprinkle with parmesan cheese.
