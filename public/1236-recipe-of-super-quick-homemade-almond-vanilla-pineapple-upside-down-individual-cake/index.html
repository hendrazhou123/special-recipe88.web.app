Steps to make almond,vanilla,pineapple upside-down individual cake:
  1. Set oven to 350°F. cook cake according to instructions.
  2. Add 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract, 1 tablespoon almond extract into batter
  3. Spread butter inside pan then pack in brown sugar make sure to spread brown sugar all around pan almost to top. You can use individual pans or any one you have
  4. Put in pineapples then cherry in center,pour in batter
  5. 30-35 minutes depending on oven temp. Check with toothpick..enjoy:)
