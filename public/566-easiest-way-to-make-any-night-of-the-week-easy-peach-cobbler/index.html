Steps to make Easy Peach üçë Cobbler:
  1. In a bowl whisk together: Bisquick, milk, cinnamon powder and vanilla extract…….Batter will be slightly lumpy…..
  2. Add melted butter to Bisquick mix…..Stir well…..
  3. Pour Bisquick mix into an 8x8 baking dish that has been slightly sprayed with butter cooking spray…….
  4. Take your can of peaches and pour into a bowl, then add the sugar and mix all well…..
  5. Spoon peach mixture over bisquick mix….
