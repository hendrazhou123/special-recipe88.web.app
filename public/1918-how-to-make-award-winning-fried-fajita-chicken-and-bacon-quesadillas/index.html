The ingredients needed to make Fried Fajita Chicken and Bacon Quesadillas:
  1. Get 8 slices Bacon
  2. Take Fully cooked, chicken fajita strips(I used Tyson brand)
  3. Take Hidden Valley Ranch Dressing
  4. Take Fiesta blend shredded cheese
  5. Make ready Flour tortillas

Steps to make Fried Fajita Chicken and Bacon Quesadillas:
  1. Cut the 8 strips of bacon in half. Cook bacon in skillet(your bacon of choice, I used turkey bacon). I cook my bacon with a lil crunch to it. If you use turkey bacon, I recommend putting a little vegetable oil in the bottom of the skillet, to prevent sticking, because turkey bacon doesn't produce a lot of grease.
  2. Take out the bacon after it's done cooking and set aside.
  3. I turn off the stove as I'm preparing each quesadilla in the skillet, to prevent any burning. Because sometimes I'm a little slow at preparing things, and I like to take my time. I turn the stove back on, when I'm done preparing the quesadilla and it is ready to be cooked.
  4. Add the flour tortilla to the skillet, with just enough oil to fry it.
  5. Add the bacon(4 slices), fajita chicken, hidden valley ranch, and fiesta cheese blend to the tortilla.
