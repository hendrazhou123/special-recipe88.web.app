Instructions to make Strawberry cream cheese sandwich:
  1. Scoop the cream cheese into a bowl
  2. Juice 5 strawberries over the bowl
  3. Add the sugar
  4. Mix until it's a light pinkish color
  5. Scoop and spread cream onto both slices of bread
  6. Take any remaining cream and put it on the side
  7. Thinly slice the three remaining strawberries
  8. Then take the sliced strawberries and put six of them on 1 slice of bread then take the ones left and put them next to the cream on the side
  9. Finally press the two pieces of bread together
