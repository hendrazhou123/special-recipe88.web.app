The ingredients needed to make Garlic butter bread cubes for salad or mushrooms soup:

Steps to make Garlic butter bread cubes for salad or mushrooms soup:
  1. Cut 6 bread slices into even-sized cubes. It's up to u whether u want to slice the edges or not. Keep aside.
  2. Mix 3 tablespoons melted butter, 1 teaspoon garlic powder and a pinch of salt and 1 teaspoon parsley flakes until butter mixture is dissolved. Omg! Forget to buy parsley flakes!
  3. Toss butter mixture with bread cubes in a medium bowl until bread cubes are evenly coated.
  4. Spread coated bread cubes on a baking sheet in an oven pan. Bake for 2 or 3 minutes and check to see if they are dry, crispy, and light golden brown till done. After baking, cool them before storing bread cubes in air-tight container or ziplock bag. Enjoy!
