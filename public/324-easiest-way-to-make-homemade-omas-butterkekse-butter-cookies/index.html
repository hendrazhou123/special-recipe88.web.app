The ingredients needed to make Omas Butterkekse (Butter Cookies):
  1. Make ready 3 stick butter
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 3 cup flour
  5. Get 1/3 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Take 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  8. Take 1 cookie press or pastry bag

Instructions to make Omas Butterkekse (Butter Cookies):
  1. preheat oven to 375 fahrenheit
  2. In a large bowl cream butter
  3. slowly beat in sugar
  4. lightly beat eggs then add to sugar mix
  5. in a separate bowl mix flour and salt
  6. gradually add flour mix to sugar mix
  7. mix until well blended then add vanilla
  8. place quarter size cookies on sheet 2 inches apart
  9. bake for 8 to 10 min
  10. place on cooling rack and after cooled dust with powdered sugar
  11. store cookies in an air tight container
