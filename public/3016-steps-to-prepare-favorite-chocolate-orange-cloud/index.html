The ingredients needed to make Chocolate orange cloud:
  1. Make ready 200 g 70% dark chocolate
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  3. Take 1/2 cup plus 2 tsp sugar
  4. Get 1/4 cup hot whole milk
  5. Take 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  6. Get 2 cups heavy cream
  7. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare Finely grated zest from one large orange

Instructions to make Chocolate orange cloud:
  1. Whisking continuously, melt the chocolate and butter in the top of a double boiler. Whisk in the 1/2 cup sugar and a pinch of salt, then add the milk a little at a time until smooth and the sugar's melted. Add the mascarpone 1 tbsp at a time, whisking until it melts into the chocolate. You should end up with what looks like smooth, slightly loose pudding. Cover and put in the fridge to chill.
  2. Add the 2 tsp sugar and vanilla to the heavy cream and whip to soft peaks. Sprinkle in the orange zest and stir carefully to combine.
  3. Transfer the chocolate mixture to a large mixing bowl. Stir in a couple of tbsp whipped cream to thin it out a little. Pour in the remaining whipped cream and fold it into the chocolate. Keep going until you have a uniform mixture. Transfer the cloud to the container of your choice and let it chill in the fridge. Serve with fresh fruit and/or extra whipped cream.
