The ingredients needed to make Who needs Hagan Daas? Peanut Butter, Dark Chocolate & Raspberry No Churn Ice Cream:
  1. Prepare 1 pint (2 c) Whipping Cream
  2. Take 1 (14 oz) can of Fat Free Condensed Milk
  3. Get 1 Tbsp Vanilla
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 c Peanut Butter (I used Peanut Butter & Co White Chocolate Wonderland)
  5. Get 1 c Mini Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips
  6. Take 1 Handful Trader Joe's Dark Chocolate Chunks
  7. Prepare 1/2 c Bonne Maan Raspberry Preserves

Instructions to make Who needs Hagan Daas? Peanut Butter, Dark Chocolate & Raspberry No Churn Ice Cream:
  1. Beat whipping cream and vanilla for a couple of minutes with a hand mixer or kitchen aid mixer until it is somewhat stiff.
  2. Add the condensed milk. Fold in by hand and mix until combined.
  3. Pour mixture into a loaf or casserole pan.
  4. Drop clumps of raspberry preserves over ice cream mixture and dark chocolate chunks and fold under.
  5. Measure out peanut butter and then melt it in microwave for 60 seconds at half power.
  6. Pour peanut butter over ice cream mixture and marble it with a fork.
  7. Sprinkle mini chocolate chips over ice cream.
  8. Cover with saran wrap or lid and place in freezer until firm (roughly 2 hours)
  9. Grab a spoon and ascend to cloud 9!
