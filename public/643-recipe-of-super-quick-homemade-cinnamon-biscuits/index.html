Instructions to make Cinnamon biscuits:
  1. Mix cinnamon and sugar together in a bowl and set aside
  2. Melt butter in bowl that can be used to dip your biscuits in
  3. Dip each biscuit one at a time into the butter then into the cinnamon sugar mixture and place on your baking sheet.
  4. Cook 20-25 minuets at 375 in a preheated oven until biscuits are cooked. I used frozen biscuits today and they are very light and fluffy.
