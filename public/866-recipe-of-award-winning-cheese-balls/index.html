The ingredients needed to make Cheese balls:
  1. Prepare 2 med sized Capsicum Finely Chopped
  2. Take 2 med Sized Onions Finely Chopped
  3. Make ready 4 cubes Amul Processed Cheese
  4. Prepare 12 side cut bread slices
  5. Take as required Water to soak
  6. Prepare As needed Oil to deep fry
  7. Get to taste Salt
  8. Get 2 tsp black pepper

Steps to make Cheese balls:
  1. Step 01 : PREPARATION OF STUFFING - In a bowl add finely chopped onion + finely chopped capsicum + salt(use moderately as cheese is already salty)+ black pepper +4 cubes amul grated cheese
  2. Step 02 : ASSEMBLY- Take a bread slice soak in a lil water not too much and squeeze out extra water, stuff the stuffing and form a ball out of bread like we do in bread rolls.
  3. Step03 : FRYING- Deep Fry these balls in hot oil on med to high flame untill golden brown in colour.
  4. Step 04 : Serve with green chutney or tomato ketchup.
