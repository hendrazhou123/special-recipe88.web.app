The ingredients needed to make Cream scones:
  1. Get 1/2 cups cream
  2. Make ready 2 teaspoons sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 teaspoons salt
  4. Get 4 tablespoons butter
  5. Get 2 eggs
  6. Get 2 cups flour
  7. Take 4 teaspoons baking powder

Instructions to make Cream scones:
  1. Sift dry ingredients
  2. Blend in butter with a pastry knife
  3. Add well beaten eggs and cream
  4. Turn out onto floured surface
  5. Roll till 3/4 inch thick
  6. Cut into triangles
  7. Place on greased baking sheet
  8. Bake for 15 minutes in a 450 oven
