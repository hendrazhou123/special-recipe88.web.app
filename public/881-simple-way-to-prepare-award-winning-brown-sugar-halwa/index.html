The ingredients needed to make Brown sugar halwa❤:
  1. Get 1 cup semolina
  2. Prepare Half cup brown sugar
  3. Get Nuts optional
  4. Take Half cup desi ghee
  5. Take 2 cup water double of semolina
  6. Prepare 1 cup warm milk
