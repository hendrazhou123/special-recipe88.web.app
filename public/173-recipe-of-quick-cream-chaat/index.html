The ingredients needed to make Cream Chaat:
  1. Prepare 200 ml Cream
  2. Take 2 tbsp Crushed sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup Condensed milk
  4. Take 1/2 cup Yogurt
  5. Prepare 1 pinch Salt
  6. Make ready 1/4 tsp Black pepper crushed
  7. Take Apple 2 big
  8. Take 6 Banana
  9. Take 5-6 Dates
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp Raisin
