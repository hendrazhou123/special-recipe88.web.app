The ingredients needed to make Strawberry ice cream:
  1. Prepare Strawberry sauce
  2. Prepare 1-11/2 cups strawberries cut in quarters
  3. Get 1/3 cup brown sugar
  4. Get 3/4 cup water
  5. Get 1 tsp strawberry essence
  6. Get Cream mixture
  7. Take 500 ml cream
  8. Prepare 1/2 can condensed milk
  9. Take 1 pinch red food coloring (optional)

Creamy, scoopable, no ice crystals at all, no ice cream maker required! Use your ice cream maker to create this rich and creamy strawberry ice cream. There are no eggs in this recipe. Find images of Strawberry Ice Cream.

Steps to make Strawberry ice cream:
  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. NOTE* you can skip making the strawberry sauce if you wish and instead just use the strawberry essence and coloring.
  3. In a saucepan, mix strawberries, water and sugar. Cook until it starts to boil. Reduce the heat and let it cook till it thickens.
  4. Stir in the strawberry essence then set aside to cool.
  5. Whip the cream until thick then add the condensed milk and mix.
  6. Add in the strawberry sauce and coloring. Combine well and freeze overnight.
  7. Enjoy
