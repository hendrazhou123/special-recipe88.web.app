Instructions to make Chicken Cheese Burrito:
  1. Season the chicken with salt and, pepper then add canola oil to a non stick pan and, heat cook until no longer pink, take out and, let rest.
  2. Cut chicken into cubes and, set aside in a medium skillet add tortilla and, heat take out then, assemble chicken, cheese, roll pour sauce on burrito.
  3. Add sour cream then, garnish with parsley and enjoy.
