The ingredients needed to make Rainbow cake with vanilla buttercream icing.:
  1. Take 3 cup self-raising flour
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup white sugar
  3. Get 6 medium eggs
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp of water
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Get 1 cup margarine
  7. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp each of different colours (i use powdered colours)
  9. Get Icing
  10. Make ready 1/4 cup margarine
  11. Take 1/4 cup shortening
  12. Make ready 3/4 tsp vanilla
  13. Get 2 cup icing sugar
  14. Prepare 1 tbsp milk

Steps to make Rainbow cake with vanilla buttercream icing.:
  1. Pre-heat oven at 160°C. It's a slow-bake cake.
  2. Grease & flour a 7 inch diameter round baking dish.
  3. Sieve flour & salt. Keep aside.
  4. In a bowl, add margarine + sugar & mix well with a mixer at high speed till fluffy and creamy.
  5. Add the eggs + vanilla & mix well till well combined & mixture looks curdy.
  6. Add the flour mixture & mix well till all combined.
  7. If mixture is too heavy, add the water & mix well on high speed.
  8. Take 7 bowls. Scoop using a serving spoon, the cake mixture & distribute evenly in the bowls. Repeat till all the mixture is used up.
  9. Now add the food colouring. Red, orange, yellow, green, blue, pink, purple. Different colour for each bowl. ( unfortunately, i ran out of blue colour, so i used 6)
  10. Mix well. DONT use the same spoon for all the bowls…
  11. Pour 1 bowl at a time, into the baking dish, spreading CAREFULLY to the edge of the dish. Make sure not to mix-up the colours.
  12. Place in the oven. Bake for about 1& a half hours or until a wooden pick inserted in the middle, comes out clean.
  13. Cool completely on wire rack. Make sure the cake is COMPLETELY COOL before spreading the icing.
  14. For the Icing:
  15. In a large bowl, cream margarine & shortening till light & fluffy. Add vanilla, icing sugar & milk. Mix well. (U can add a tablespoon more of milk to make it the consistency u require.)
