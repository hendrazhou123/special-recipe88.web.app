The ingredients needed to make Golden Grahams s’mores bars:
  1. Take 6 cups golden graham cereal
  2. Get 1 bag mini marshmallows for in the bars
  3. Prepare 1 stick butter or margarine
  4. Get 1 cup mini marshmallows for topping
  5. Prepare 8-10 fun size Hershey’s bars broken into pieces
  6. Make ready Hershey’s syrup

Steps to make Golden Grahams s’mores bars:
  1. On low heat melt the butter on the stove and slowly mix in marshmallows stirring until smooth. Remove from heat and stir in cereal.
  2. Smooth into a 13x9 pan that’s been coated heavily with butter
  3. Sprinkle marshmallows, Hershey’s bar pieces and then drizzle with Hershey’s syrup on top. (I crushed about a 1/4 cup of additional cereal to put on top)
