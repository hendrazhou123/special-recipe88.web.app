The ingredients needed to make Pound cake (Vanilla):
  1. Get 100 grams unsalted butter (softened)
  2. Take 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  3. Get 1 cup powdered sugar
  4. Get 4 eggs medium (3 large)
  5. Take 1 cup plain yogurt
  6. Get 1 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  7. Get 1 tbsp powdered milk
  8. Get 1 tbsp baking powder
  9. Get Brushing
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp honey

Instructions to make Pound cake (Vanilla):
  1. Mix the butter to become creamy. Add powdered sugar (gradually). Add the eggs one at a time. Add the vanilla extract and continue mixing for 2-3 mins.
  2. Sift the other dry ingredients and mix gradually. Continue mixing until you reach a creamy texture.
  3. Spray oil on your pan and pour the mix to medium level. Bake at 180 C (360) for 45 mins or until your insert a toothpick and comes out clean.
  4. Brush with honey immediately after taking it out and let it rest for 30 mins.
  5. Cut and enjoy your #poundcake
