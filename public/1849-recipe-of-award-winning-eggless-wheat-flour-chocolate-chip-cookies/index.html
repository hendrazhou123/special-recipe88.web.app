The ingredients needed to make Eggless Wheat flour chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 kg Wheat flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 kg powdered sugar
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1/2 kg pure butter/ Ghee
  5. Prepare Pinch Baking Soda
  6. Prepare Milk for kneeding

Steps to make Eggless Wheat flour chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Take Wheat flour in a bowl
  2. Add Salt, Soda and mix it well
  3. Add Melted ghee / Toop
  4. .Mix it well
  5. Kneed it properly by adding milk slowly into it.
  6. Make nice soft dough
  7. Preheat oven at 350 f
  8. Take baking pan And spread baking sheet on it.
  9. Make small ball of Gough and spread it on the sheet evenly to make small Tikki.
  10. You can add some Chocolate chip on top of the cookie.
  11. Bake it for 7-8 min.
  12. Let it cool and serve with Tea/Coffee.
