The ingredients needed to make Puerto Rican rice milk soup:
  1. Prepare 3 cups water
  2. Make ready 1 tbs butter
  3. Prepare 2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 cup rice
  5. Make ready 2 cups milk

Steps to make Puerto Rican rice milk soup:
  1. Boil water, salt and butter on high heat
  2. Turn down to medium heat. Add rice. Cook first about 15 minutes or until water almost gone but rice is moist. Turn on low heat
  3. Add milk. Simmer on low for 5 minutes to warm milk.
  4. Serve and enjoy
