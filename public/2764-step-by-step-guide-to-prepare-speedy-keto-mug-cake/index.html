The ingredients needed to make Keto mug cake:
  1. Make ready Cake
  2. Take 1 egg
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp almond meal
  4. Get 1 tsp almond butter
  5. Prepare 2 tsp stevia
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  7. Take 1 pinch baking powder
  8. Get Topping
  9. Take 2 tsp thickened cream
  10. Take 2 tsp sugar free maple syrup

Instructions to make Keto mug cake:
  1. Whisk the egg in a mug
  2. Add Stevia and whisk again
  3. Add cocoa powder, baking powder and almond meal and mix again
  4. Microwave for 30 secs
  5. Add topping and enjoy
