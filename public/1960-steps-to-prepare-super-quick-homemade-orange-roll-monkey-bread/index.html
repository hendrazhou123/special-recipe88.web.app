The ingredients needed to make Orange Roll Monkey Bread:
  1. Take 2 cans orange cinnamon rolls, cut each roll into 4’s, save the frosting
  2. Get White sugar and cinnamon for dredging
  3. Take 1 stick butter melted
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup brown sugar

Steps to make Orange Roll Monkey Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Roll the cut rolls in the cinnamon sugar and place in a well sprayed Bundt pan.
  2. I usually don’t have much cinnamon sugar left so I just sprinkle it in too. Add your brown sugar to your melted butter and mix well. Pour evenly over the rolls. Bake until the top is deep golden brown and when you touch it, it feels firm, about 40 minutes.
  3. Once done, wait a few minutes then flip it out onto a plate. Add the frosting evenly over the top and watch it melt.
