The ingredients needed to make Brown sugar pancake:
  1. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour

  4. Take 3 tablespoon dark brown sugar

Instructions to make Brown sugar pancake:
  1. Sieve flour in a bowl,add brown sugar,baking powder,salt and mix mix all the wet ingredients in another bowl
  2. Whisk,add the flour little at a time
  3. Set pan on fire add butter,add 1cup of the mixture
  4. Flip over to the other side until golden brown, continue with the rest of the batter until done.Serve and enjoy with tea or syrup of your choice
