The ingredients needed to make Butter Chicken:
  1. Make ready Meats
  2. Get 500 g Chicken Breast (Diced)
  3. Get Vegetables
  4. Prepare 1 tb Lemon Juice
  5. Make ready 3 tb Tomato Paste
  6. Make ready 60 g Roasted Cashews
  7. Make ready 1/2 Large Onion (Finely Chopped)
  8. Prepare Flavour
  9. Get 30 g Butter Chicken Blend (Aussie Spices)
  10. Make ready 125 ml (1/2 c) Natural Yoghurt
  11. Prepare 125 ml (1/2 c) Thickened Cream
  12. Prepare 30 g Butter
  13. Take 1 tb Vegetable Oil
  14. Prepare 1 c Water

Instructions to make Butter Chicken:

  3. Fry onion in the butter and oil until softened, add the chicken and marinade, tomato paste, chopped cashews, water. Cook for a few minutes, lower the heat and simmer for 12 minutes.
