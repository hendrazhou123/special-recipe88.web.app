The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Balls:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups peanut butter
  2. Take 1 cup butter
  3. Get 4 cups confectioners' sugar
  4. Prepare 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  5. Get 1 1/3 cups graham crackers finely ground
  6. Prepare 1/2 Tbs Canola oil
  7. Make ready 72 mini cupcake papers

Instructions to make Peanut Butter Balls:
  1. Mix together peanut butter, melted butter, confectioners' sugar, and graham cracker crumbs. Shape into 1 inch balls. Place balls into mini cupcake holders.
  2. Melt chocolate chips and canola oil in the microwave for 30 seconds intervals about 2 minutes total. Mix after each interval until chocolate chips are fully melted and smooth. drizzle chocolate over balls. Refridgerate when done.
