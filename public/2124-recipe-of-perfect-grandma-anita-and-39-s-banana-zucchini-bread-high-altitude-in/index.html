The ingredients needed to make Grandma Anita's banana/zucchini bread

High altitude in ():

  1. Make ready 3 eggs
  2. Take 2 cups (-2 tbsp) sugar
  3. Prepare 2 cups mashed banana or grated zucchini
  4. Get 2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready (3 tbsp milk)
  6. Make ready 3 cups AP flour, can sub in up to half whole wheat flour for a healthier loaf
  7. Take 1 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp (1/8) baking powder
  9. Take 1 tsp (1/2) baking soda
  10. Make ready 3 tsp cinnamon
