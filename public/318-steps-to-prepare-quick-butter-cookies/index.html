The ingredients needed to make Butter cookies:
  1. Get 700 g butter
  2. Take 350 g sugar
  3. Make ready 3 eggs
  4. Get 1000 g flour
  5. Take Vanilla extract or any desired flavor
  6. Get Red jam (optional)

Steps to make Butter cookies:
  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and creamy
  2. Add the eggs one at a time and mix well between each addition
  3. Fold in flour and vanilla extract
  4. Using a piping bag pipe (with a desired nozzle) little cookies on a lined baking tray
  5. Pipe some jam in the middle of each cookies.
  6. Bake in a pre-heated oven at 180°C for 20 mins
