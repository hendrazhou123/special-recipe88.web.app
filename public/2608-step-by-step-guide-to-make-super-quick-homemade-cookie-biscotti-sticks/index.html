The ingredients needed to make Cookie Biscotti Sticks:
  1. Make ready the crust
  2. Get 1 cup packed brown sugar
  3. Get 2/3 cup butter
  4. Take 1/4 cup light corn syrup
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  6. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  7. Get 3 1/2 cup uncooked regular oats
  8. Prepare chocolate filling
  9. Make ready 1 - 12 oz. bag semi sweet chocolate morsels
  10. Make ready 1 cup butterscotch morsels
  11. Prepare 2 tbsp shortening
  12. Get 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup coarsely chopped peanuts

Instructions to make Cookie Biscotti Sticks:
  1. Stir together Brown sugar , butter , corn syrup in a large pot. Cook over medium high heat until sugar dissolves & butter melts Remive from heat stir in 1/4 cup peanut butter & vanilla.Gently stir in oats. Press in bottom of a 9x13" pan lined with foil.Leave some hang over the sides so you can pick it out later. I lightly sprayed mine. Bake at 375º for 20-25 minutes or until brown.
  2. Mix together the chocolate & butterscotch morsels & shortening in a saucepan over medium high heat. Stirring until smooth. Stir in peanut butter. Spread over baked crust. Sprinkle with peanuts. Let cool in pan on wire rack 20 minutes.cover and chill 2-3 hours.
  3. Lift uncut cookies out of pan using foil handles. Cut crosswise into 1" thick sticks. Using a large Chefs knife.
