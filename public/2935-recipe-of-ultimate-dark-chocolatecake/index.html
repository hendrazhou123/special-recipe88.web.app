The ingredients needed to make Dark ChocolateCake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup flour/Maida
  2. Take 3/4 cups sugar
  3. Prepare 4 tablespoon butter
  4. Prepare 2 Eggs
  5. Take 3 tablespoons. Cocoa powder
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Prepare 2 teaspoons vanilla essence

Instructions to make Dark ChocolateCake:
  1. Beat sugar powder and butter till stiff
  2. Add Vanilla essence
  3. Beat the eggs till stiff
  4. Mix the butter sugar mixture in the eggs and blend well.
  5. Sieve together Maida, Baking powder, baking soda and Cocoa powder. Mix with egg mixture. Blend till you get a pouring consistancy batter.
  6. Preheat oven at 180 degrees. Pour the batter in a baking tin.
  7. Bake at 180 degrees for 30 mins.
