The ingredients needed to make Cookie Dough Truffles:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup (1 stick) butter, room temperature
  2. Get 3/4 cup packed brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  4. Make ready 2 cup all-purpose flour
  5. Get 1 can (14 ounces) sweetened condensed milk
  6. Take 1/2 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  7. Take 1/2 cup finely chopped walnuts or pecans
  8. Prepare 1 lb white, dark, or milk chocolate candy coating (I normally use white and milk chocolate)

The cookie dough is completely gluten-free and egg-free. Smothering the cookie dough in chocolate is an optional step. Melt chocolate bark candy coating in a double boiler. Cookie Dough Truffles are a delectable dessert made with edible cookie dough and chocolate.

Steps to make Cookie Dough Truffles:
  1. In a bowl combine butter and sugar on med - high speed until light and fluffy (about 2 mins)
  2. Beat in the flour, sweetened milk, and vanilla until incorporated. Beating well after each addition
  3. Stir in chocolate chips and walnuts
  4. Cover and refrigerate for 2 hours so it's firm enough to handle
  5. Shape mixture into 1 inch balls by rolling a spoonful in the palm of your hand. The dough is sticky so you can roll your fingers in flour to make it easier
  6. Place on wax paper. Loosely cover and refrigerate for about 20-30 mins
  7. In a bowl melt chocolate candy coating
  8. Dip the cookie dough balls into the chocolate and place on parchment paper
  9. Let sit until hard (about 15 mins)
  10. Once set, remelt candy coating and drizzle over truffles
  11. Store in refrigerator and serve cold. They can also be frozen for longer storage
