The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Prepare 150 g soften butter
  2. Make ready 150 g caster sugar
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 190 g plain flour
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 200 g chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Mix the butter and sugar until light with an electric whisk
  2. Beat in the egg and the vanilla
  3. Fold in the flour,baking powder and chocolate
  4. Cover with cling Film Chill for half an hour in the fridge
  5. Line two trays with baking parchment
  6. Once cooled shape them in to balls and spread them out
  7. Put in the oven for 10 minutes on gas mark 4
  8. Done ✅
