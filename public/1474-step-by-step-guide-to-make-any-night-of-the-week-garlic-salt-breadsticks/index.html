The ingredients needed to make Garlic Salt Breadsticks:
  1. Make ready 2 1/2 cup bread flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup cake flour
  3. Get 1 pack instant yeast (1/4 oz, 2 1/4 tsp)
  4. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 2 Tbsp maple syrup
  6. Make ready 2 Tbsp veg. oil
  7. Get 1/2 cup milk
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  9. Prepare ~~ Seasoning~~
  10. Take garlic salt
  11. Take unsalted butter

Steps to make Garlic Salt Breadsticks:
  1. Combine milk and water then microwave for 30 seconds or till lukewarm. Lightly stir yeast into it with maple syrup then sit for 5 minutes. The yeast will become foamy.
  2. Pour yeast mixture into a large bowl. Add in oil. Stir in salt and flour (1 cup a time) till forming a sticky ball.
  3. Cover with a cloth or plastic wrap and let rise for 1 hour or till the dough is 2 times bigger.
  4. Gently punch the air out of the dough then knead it on a lightly floured surface. Roll the dough into a large rectangle (about a size of your baking sheet). Cut it into strips. Place the striped dough on a baking pan that lined with a cookie sheet. Covered with a cloth or plastic wrap and let rise 30 minutes. Meanwhile, preheat oven to 375°F.
  5. Before put them in the oven, melt butter in a microwave about 20-30 seconds. Brush it on the striped dough then sprinkle garlic salt on top of them.
  6. Bake the breadsticks for 15 minutes or until golden brown. Enjoy~
