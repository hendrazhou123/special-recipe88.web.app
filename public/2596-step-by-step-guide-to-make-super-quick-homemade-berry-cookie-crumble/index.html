Steps to make Berry Cookie Crumble:
  1. Preheat oven to 350"
  2. Now add your defrosted berry mix to a large bowl, add in flour,sugar,seasonings and finely chopped granola, i used(nature valley crunchy oats and honey bars package of 2) mix well and add to baking dish
  3. Now add some cookie crumbles to the top of your berry mix, just use your hands and tear apart small pieces top sparatickly over the top
  4. Now bake for 30-35 minutes top with cool whip or ice cream enjoy!!
