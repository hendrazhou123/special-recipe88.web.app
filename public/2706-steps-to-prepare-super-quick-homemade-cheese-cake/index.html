The ingredients needed to make Cheese Cake:
  1. Get 1 pack cheese
  2. Prepare 1 small sachet of apricot sauce
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Get 45 ml fresh milk
  5. Make ready 45 gram butter(melted)
  6. Prepare Cake moulder
  7. Take Vanilla

Instructions to make Cheese Cake:
  1. Take out the baking tray from oven. Preheat oven to 160c
  2. Mix the melted butter with egg, vanilla and milk in a mixing bowl and whisk.
  3. Then add the cheese and continue to whisk
  4. Place moulderon baking tray. Pour in the mixture and smooth the surface. Bake for 30 mins. After 30 mins insert a toothpick, when it comes out cleans means it's ready.
  5. Drop the cake from moulder to plate after baking to release air.
  6. Spread the apricot sauce on top of cake with spoon. Done
