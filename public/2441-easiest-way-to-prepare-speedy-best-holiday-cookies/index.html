The ingredients needed to make Best Holiday Cookies:
  1. Take 1/2 C + 2 tblsp soft or melted butter
  2. Prepare 1/2 C brown sugar
  3. Take 1/2 C white sugar
  4. Take 1 large or Xlarge egg
  5. Take 1 1/2 tblsp brandy
  6. Prepare 1 1/2 C flour
  7. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1 C dried cranberries
  9. Make ready 3/4 C white chocolate chips
  10. Prepare 1/2 C chopped walnuts (optional)
  11. Make ready Preparation
  12. Prepare Preheat oven to 375*
  13. Prepare Line a baking sheet with parchment paper

Instructions to make Best Holiday Cookies:
  1. In a large bowl, stir together the sugars
  2. Stir in butter until smooth
  3. Stir in the egg
  4. Stir in the brandy and baking soda
  5. Stir in the flour 1/2 C at a time
  6. Stir in the cranberries, chips and walnuts (if using walnuts) -the dough will be a little stiff
  7. Using a 2-inch scoop, place cookie dough 3 inches apart on the sheet
  8. Bake for 10-11 minutes until lightly golden brown
  9. Remove from the oven and let the cookies cool on the sheet for 3 minutes before transferring to a rack or plate to cool completely
  10. Enjoy! And happy holidays!
