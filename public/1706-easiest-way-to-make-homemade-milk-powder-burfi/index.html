The ingredients needed to make Milk powder burfi:
  1. Get 100 g dry milk powder
  2. Make ready Half cup milk
  3. Make ready 5 tbs condance milk
  4. Take 2 tbs powder sugar
  5. Make ready 1 tbs cardamom powder
  6. Take 2 tbs butter or ghee
  7. Make ready Pinch yellow food color

Steps to make Milk powder burfi:
  1. Sabse pehle frying pan me butter dale melt hone par milk dale
  2. Milk boil hone par dry milk powder or food color dale or low heat pe regularly stir karte Jae jab tak ki yeh thoda thick ho Jae
  3. Then condance milk add kare sath hi sugar or cardamom powder b dal de
  4. Thick hone par palate me nikal de or 1-2 hrs fridge me rakh de or set hone par pieces cut kar le or serve kare.
