Instructions to make Brownie Bottom Vanilla Butter Cake Cupcakes:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350. Spray 12 standard muffin tins with bakers spray
  2. Make Brownie layer
  3. Melt butter and chocolate in the microwave until smooth, cool to room temperature
  4. Add brown sugar, vanilla and salt and stir well
  5. Add egg and yolk and blend in
  6. Add flour and stir until oncorporated
  7. Divide into cupcake tins evenly, Bake 5 minutes, remove to cool 15 minutes and prepare vanilla cake layer
  9. Make Vanilla Cake Layer
  10. In a bowl whisk together, flour, baking powder and salt
  11. In another bowl beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  12. Beat in eggs and vanilla
  13. Fold in flour mixture alternating with milk.
  14. Divide batter evenly over brownie layer and bake about 15 minutes until set. Cool completely before removing from tindpns
  15. Make chocolte glaze
  16. Melt chocolate in the microwave until smooth d
  17. Dip tops in chocolaye
  18. Add sprinkles while chocolate is wet and allow to set
