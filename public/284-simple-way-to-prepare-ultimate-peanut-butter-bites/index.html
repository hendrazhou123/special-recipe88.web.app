Instructions to make Peanut butter bites:
  1. Grind the peanuts with sugar and cinnamon (if using honey add after grinding)
  2. Add 1 tsp of melted butter and mix thoroughly till homogenous (we need a hard doughy consistency)
  3. Roll into small balls and freeze
  4. Prepare the chocolate sauce by adding the ingredients in a bowl and mixing
  5. After the balls feezed dip in sauce or in coconut flakes
  6. Freeze the balls again and voila!
