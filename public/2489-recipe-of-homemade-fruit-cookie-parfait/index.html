The ingredients needed to make Fruit cookie parfait:
  1. Take 1/2 cup hung curd
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp powered sugar
  3. Get 1/4 cup fresh cream
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Get 1 cup mixed fruits (small pieces)
  6. Make ready 6-7 chocolate cookies or Oreo biscuits

Steps to make Fruit cookie parfait:
  1. In a bowl, add curd, sugar, cream and vanilla essence and mix it well.
  2. In a mixer jar add cookies and grind it to a coarse powder.
  3. For serving, take a serving glass and add 1 to 2 tbsp cookies powder to make first layer.
  4. Then add fruits on it to make second layer.
  5. Now spread curd mixture carefully so that the layers will not get mix.
  6. Like this make the glass full by adding this layers and keep it refrigerator for 30 minutes.
  7. Garnish it with some fruits and mint leaves and serve it cool cool.
