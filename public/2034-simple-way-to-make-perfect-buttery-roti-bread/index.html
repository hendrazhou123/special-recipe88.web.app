The ingredients needed to make Buttery Roti bread:
  1. Prepare 125 g plain flour
  2. Make ready 80 ml water
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 2 cloves or 3 of garlic pressed
  5. Take 1 tbsp melted unsalted butter

Instructions to make Buttery Roti bread:
  1. Mix flour, salt, garlic, melted butter together until crumbly
  2. Add water and knead together on a floured board
  3. Break into 2 pieces, and roll out flat and thin…
  4. Fry a little butter in a frying pan, and pop the roti bread in and fry for a few minutes on each side, about 40 seconds each side… enjoy hot!!!
