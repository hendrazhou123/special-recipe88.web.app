Instructions to make Creamy chicken noodle bake:
  1. Pre heat oven to 375 degrees and spray a 9 Ã— 13 inch baking dish with I can't believe its not butter.
  2. Mix sour cream, both cans of cream of chicken, better than bouillon, salt, pepper, garlic and parsley. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Mix noodles and vegetables together and spray with butter spray and then mix with sour cream soup mixture till it's well combined then evenly pour in 1/2 cup chicken broth.
  4. Top with prepared stuffing evenly and spray with butter spray.
  5. Bake covered for 30 minutes and then 20 minutes with foil off
  6. Let it rest for a few minutes then enjoy.
