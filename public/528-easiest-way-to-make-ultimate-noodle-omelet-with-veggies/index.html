The ingredients needed to make Noodle Omelet with Veggies:
  1. Get 1 instant noodle (any variant)
  2. Prepare 2 eggs
  3. Make ready 1/2 red capsicum
  4. Prepare 1 spring onion
  5. Get 3 tbsp margarine

Instructions to make Noodle Omelet with Veggies:
  1. Chop spring onion and red capsicum.
  2. Boil instant noodle, drain the water, mix all ingredient including the instant noodle seasonings.
  3. Pre-heat saute pan with margarine, add noodle mixture, cook until both sides well done.
  4. Enjoy.
