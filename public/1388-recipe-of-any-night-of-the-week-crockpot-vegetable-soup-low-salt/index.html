The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Vegetable Soup (low salt):
  1. Prepare 1 quart Vegetable broth (low sodium preferred)
  2. Take 3 Potatoes sliced or cubed
  3. Prepare 12 oz Diced onions and celery (which I purchased at the supermarket)
  4. Get 1 can Diced tomatoes
  5. Get 1 can Tomato paste
  6. Prepare 1 can Chopped spinach
  7. Get 1 Bay leaf
  8. Prepare 1 Season with salt free Mrs. Dash
