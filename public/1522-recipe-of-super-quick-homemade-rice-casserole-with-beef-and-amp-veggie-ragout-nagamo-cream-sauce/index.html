The ingredients needed to make Rice Casserole with Beef&Veggie ragout, Nagamo cream sauce:
  1. Get 1 cup Rice
  2. Make ready 100 g Nagaimo (Japanese yam)
  3. Prepare 400 ml Milk
  4. Prepare 300 g Minced beef
  5. Prepare 1/4 Onion finely chopped
  6. Make ready 1/4 Carrot finely chopped
  7. Prepare 1/2 Zuccini roughly chopped
  8. Make ready 5-10 Mushrooms roughly chopped
  9. Take 1 cup Tomato sauce
  10. Prepare Salted Butter, Salt, Pepper
  11. Make ready Shredded cheese

Steps to make Rice Casserole with Beef&Veggie ragout, Nagamo cream sauce:
  1. Wash the rice and cook with a pinch of salt and 1 teaspoon butter.
  2. Cut Nagamito into small pieces and blend with Milk until smooth. Pour the liquid to a pan and heat gently for 5 min. Add 1 teaspoon butter and salt.
  3. Sautée minced beef and veggies, add tomato sauce. If you use canned or fresh tomato i/o tomato sauce, add salt and pepper or beef bouillon powder to taste.
  4. Coat the baking dish with oil. Put 1/2 quantity of rice in the dish, then spread the 1/2 of ragout. Repeat once more. Spread Nagaimo cream on the top and sprinkle shredded cheese. Bake in oven of 390°F for 10 min.
