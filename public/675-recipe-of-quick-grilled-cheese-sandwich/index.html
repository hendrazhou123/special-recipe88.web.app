Instructions to make Grilled Cheese Sandwich:
  1. Baste bread slices on one side with butter.
  2. Arrange 4 cheese.
  3. Drizzle oil on a medium-hot pan.
  4. Put buttered bread slices on pan (butter side down).
  5. Add cheeses on bread slices. Top with buttered slices (butter side up).
  6. Move bread around on pan as cheese melts. Check color of bread slices, when golden brown flip bread. Butter top of sandwich lightly again.
  7. Flip, butter new top of sandwich. keep moving sandwich around on pan.
  8. Remove from pan. Slice sandwich into half.
  9. Serve hot, gooey and cheesy sandwich. Enjoy!
