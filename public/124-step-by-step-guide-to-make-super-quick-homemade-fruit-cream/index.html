  1. Make ready 1/2 kg fresh cream
  2. Prepare 1 cup powdered sugar

Steps to make Fruit Cream:
  1. Put cream in a bowl.
  2. Add powder sugar in it.
  3. Mix well with light hands.
  4. Add canned fruits and chopped fruits.
  5. Mix well.
  6. Garnish with dry fruits.
  7. Serve chilled.
