The ingredients needed to make Tami's Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Get 1 Cup Organic Peanut Butter
  2. Get 1/4 cup Organic Honey
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon Organic Vanilla
  5. Take 1/4 cup chocolate chips

Instructions to make Tami's Flourless Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. In a bowl combine all ingredients then stir in chips.
  3. Bake for 8 to 10 minutes.Enjoy!!
