The ingredients needed to make Reese’s Cookie 🍪Dough Cheesecake:
  1. Make ready 2 packages cream cheese (8 ounce), softened
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup white sugar
  3. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  4. Get 2 eggs
  5. Get 1/4 teaspoon cinnamon powder
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup Reeses’s cookie dough
  7. Prepare 1 graham cracker crust

Steps to make Reese’s Cookie 🍪Dough Cheesecake:
  1. Beat together in large bowl with an electric mixer : cream cheese, sugar and vanilla extract…..
  2. Add eggs and cinnamon powder, mix well……
  3. Break up part of the cookie dough, about 1/2 cup, and stir into cream cheese mixture…….
  4. Pour mixture into graham cracker crust…….
  5. Top pie with remaining cookie dough…….
  6. Bake in a preheated 350 degree oven for 40 minutes……
  7. Let cool before serving, or refrigerate overnight for best flavor ! Serve and enjoy 😉!!!
