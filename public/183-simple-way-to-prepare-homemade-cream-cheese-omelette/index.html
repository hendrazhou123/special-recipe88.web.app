The ingredients needed to make Cream Cheese Omelette:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Get 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  3. Make ready to taste Salt
  4. Take 1 small onion chopped
  5. Get 2 tbsp green chilli + green onions and green coriander leaves (see recipe)
  6. Take 1/2 tomato slices
  7. Make ready 1 piece cream cheese
  8. Take 2 tbsp oil

Instructions to make Cream Cheese Omelette:
  1. Take a bowl add eggs,red chilli powder and salt.
  2. Add onion and green coriander, green chilli and green onions and mix well.
  3. Take a pan add oil and add eggs mixture one side cook flip the other side.
  4. Add tomato slices and cream cheese.
  5. Fold one side and fold the other side like this.
  6. Cream Cheese Omelette is ready to serve.
