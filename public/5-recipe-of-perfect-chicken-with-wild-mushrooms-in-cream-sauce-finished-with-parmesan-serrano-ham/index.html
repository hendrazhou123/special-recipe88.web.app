The ingredients needed to make Chicken With Wild Mushrooms in Cream Sauce Finished With Parmesan / Serrano Ham:
  1. Take 1 onion
  2. Prepare 2 garlic
  3. Take Wild mushrooms
  4. Make ready Chicken (filet)
  5. Get 300 ml Chicken broth
  6. Make ready 25 ml heavy Cream
  7. Prepare 25 ml milk
  8. Make ready Half lemon
  9. Make ready 75 gr flour
  10. Make ready 50 gr butter (to use in 3 times)
  11. Get Oil (olive)
  12. Make ready Dried oregano - garlic powder - gember powder - salt - black pepper - dark soja - nutmeg
  13. Prepare Parmesan
  14. Take Serrano ham

Steps to make Chicken With Wild Mushrooms in Cream Sauce Finished With Parmesan / Serrano Ham:
  1. Dice onion - garlic - mushroom - chicken (season chicken with salt-pepper-garlic powder-gember powder-dried oregano-olive oil)
  2. Heat up the oil and butter - add the onion - add mushrooms (fry till all the water is gone) season with a little bit dark soja -salt -black pepper- few lemon drops
  3. Add garlic - take out and set a side - don’t clean the pan - add butter
  4. Fry the chicken - take out and set a side - dont’t clean the pan - add butter - flour - chickenbroth - milk (bechamelsauce) - heavy Cream - season with salt - black pepper - dried oregano - nutmeg - few lemon drops - (meanwhile u can put the ham under the grill or fry it) let the sauce cook 2 min. Then add the mushrooms-chicken. Let it cook On a low fire for 10 min. (meanwhile u can make your pasta)
  5. Chop the ham, u can dress ure plate with the parmesan/serrano ham and a little bit of dried oregano. Bonne apetit.
