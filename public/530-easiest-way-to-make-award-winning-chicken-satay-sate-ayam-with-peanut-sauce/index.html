The ingredients needed to make Chicken Satay (Sate Ayam) with Peanut Sauce:
  1. Take 1/2 kg chicken breast
  2. Get 1 cup sweet soy sauce
  3. Get 2 tbsp margarine
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp lemon juice
  5. Prepare 2 pcs Skewer sticks
  6. Take Peanut sauce:
  7. Get 250 gr peanut
  8. Prepare 1 red onion
  9. Prepare 3 garlic cloves
  10. Take 3 long red chilies
  11. Make ready 2 kaffir lime leaves
  12. Take 3 candle nuts
  13. Take 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  14. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  15. Prepare 1 tbsp palm sugar
  16. Get 200 ml water
  17. Prepare 2 tbsp cooking oil
  18. Take Cooking oil to fry peanuts

Steps to make Chicken Satay (Sate Ayam) with Peanut Sauce:
  1. Dice chicken breat (i like it small pieces), skewer them with skewer sticks.
  2. Mix sweet soy sauce, lemon juice, and margarine well.
  3. Coat chicken skewers with the mixture of sweet soy sauce and margarine.
  4. Grill them properly (electric, charcoal, or just on the pan are fine).
  5. Peanut sauce: fry peanuts, grind them, put aside.
  6. Grind red onion, garlics, kaffir lime leaves, candlenuts, palm sugar, salt, long red chilies.
  7. Pre-heat 2 tbsp cooking oil in saute pan, add ground spices, stir it until it cooks well, add water, stir it and wait until the water boils.
  8. Add gound peanuts, mix well,
  9. Add sweet soy sauce, stir it for 5 mins.
  10. Serve chicken satay with peanut sauce.
