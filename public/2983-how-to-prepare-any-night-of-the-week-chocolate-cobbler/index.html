The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Cobbler:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 tsp fine salt
  4. Make ready 3/4 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  6. Take 1/2 cup milk
  7. Make ready 6 tbsp melted butter or margarine
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Make ready Topping:
  10. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  11. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  12. Get 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  13. Make ready 1 1/2 cup HOT water

Instructions to make Chocolate Cobbler:
  1. Mix the first 8 ingredients in a bowl and spread in a 9x13 glass dish, sprayed with nonstick spray.
  2. In a separate bowl mix the topping ingredients and spread evenly over batter.
  3. Pour the hot water GENTLY over the top. DO NOT MIX. Slide it into a 350 degree oven and bake for 40-45 minutes. Let sit to cool and set up.
