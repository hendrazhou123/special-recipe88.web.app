The ingredients needed to make The Best Vanilla Butter Cream Icing:
  1. Take 50 grams melted butter
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup icing sugar
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp milk
  4. Take 3 tsp vanilla essence

This recipe can be easily altered by removing the vanilla extract and adding your favorite flavor. Don't say I didn't warn you when you become in charge of all the family cakes! Instructions to make The Best Vanilla Butter Cream Icing:
  1. Melt the butter in a heatproof bowl but don't let it over heat and boil.
  2. Beat the butter with an electric beater until aerated and light.
  3. Sift the icing sugar into the butter and add the milk and vanilla.
  4. Beat with the electric beater until the mixture is stiff and fluffy.
  5. I like to put my icing in the fridge to rest for 10 - 15 minutes before piping, sometimes the butter can make the mixture runny if it's too hot.
  6. Spoon into piping bag and ice your delicious Cupcakes! Decorate with sprinkles or chocolate melted into shapes for wow factor. Yum!
