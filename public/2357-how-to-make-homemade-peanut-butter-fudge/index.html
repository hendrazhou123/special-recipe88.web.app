Steps to make Peanut Butter Fudge:
  1. In medium pot, add 3/4 margarine, milk, and sugar.
  2. Stir over medium high heat until mixture forms a stable ball when dropped into cold water (drop little drops of the mixture into a small glass of cold water)
  3. Quickly add peanut butter and vanilla. Mix well.
  4. Use 1/4 margarine to butter a large plate.
  5. Pour mixture onto the buttered plate.
  6. Let set until hardened.
