The ingredients needed to make Butter chicken with sweet soy sauce:
  1. Take 1 chicken, wash clean and cut into pieces
  2. Prepare 3 tbsp butter
  3. Take 4 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  4. Get 1 tbsp soy sauce
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Take 2 tbsp sugar
  7. Take 1 clove shallot, sliced

Instructions to make Butter chicken with sweet soy sauce:
  1. Marinade chicken with salt, let it sit for 1 hour in the fridge.
  2. Heat up butter in a wok, put shallots in, stir until fragrant. Add the chicken and stir for a while. Pour the rest of the ingredients and simmer for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, stirring occasionally. Serve with fried shallot as garnish.
