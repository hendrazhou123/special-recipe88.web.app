The ingredients needed to make Parmesan Garlic Butter Chicken Pasta Bake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup diced chicken breast
  2. Get 1 cup diced green bell peppers
  3. Prepare 1 cup shredded parmesan cheese
  4. Prepare 1 box cooked pasta of choice
  5. Get 1 tablespoon garlic salt
  6. Take 1/2 table spoon of crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
  7. Make ready 1/2 stick butter of choice

Steps to make Parmesan Garlic Butter Chicken Pasta Bake:
  1. Boil pasta until cooked, drain in cold water and set aside.
  2. Pre-Heat oven to 350
  3. Melt a little butter on pan, add a little bit of salt and black pepper while cooking the diced chicken until finished. Add a little olive oil and add diced green bell peppers with chicken and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  4. In a large pot, melt the butter on low heat and once butter is melted, add in the cooked pasta, cooked chicken, bell peppers, garlic salt and parmesan cheese. Mix ingredients and place into baking dish.
  5. When oven is ready, cover the dish and bake on 350 for 10-15 minutes and serve!
