Steps to make Chocolate Cheese Cake:
  1. For the cake,the ingredients of this is written at the back. Just follow what is written and how to do.
  2. Then put the mixture in round and removable base, put inside the oven 180°c for 20-25 minutes.
  3. Crash the biscuit.
  4. In blender,put all ingredients of cream cheese then blend it until it will mix together before put in the center of cake.
  5. Remove the center of the cake,then put biscuit crashed then add the fillings and decorate the caramellized nuts up.
  6. To make a caramel nut, put first sugar in pan wait until melt then add nuts.. Stir until brown.
  7. Then put inside the refregirator,2 or more hours. Then serve.
