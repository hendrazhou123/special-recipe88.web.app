Steps to make Cookie Pops:
  1. Bake cookies.
  2. On medium speed, beat together sugar, butter, thick frosting is made.
  3. Spread about 2 teaspoons of filling onto flat side of one cookie.
  4. Press a lollipop stick into frosting, then press on another cookie.
  5. Refrigerate until firm.
  6. Makes 2 1/2 dozen.
