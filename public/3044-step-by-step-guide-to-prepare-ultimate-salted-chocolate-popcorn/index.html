Steps to make Salted Chocolate Popcorn:
  1. Pop a bag of microwave popcorn. Snack size, for best results. Set aside.
  2. Melt the chocolate in the microwave. Stirring 15 to 20 seconds at a time until mostly melted. Stir until fully melted.
  3. Combine the popcorn and chocolate. Stir until popcorn is nicely coated. Add the pinch of salt. Stir. Enjoy!
