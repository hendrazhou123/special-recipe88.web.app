The ingredients needed to make Banana Bread:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup (113 grams) softened BUTTER
  2. Take 1 cup granulated SUGAR
  3. Get 2 beaten EGGS
  4. Prepare 4 pcs BANANAS
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 cup all-purpose FLOUR
  6. Take 1 tsp BAKING SODA
  7. Make ready 1/2 tsp SALT
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp VANILLA

Steps to make Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees Celsius (350 degrees Fahrenheit)
  2. Cream together butter and sugar
  3. Add eggs and crushed bananas. Combine well.
  4. Sift together, flour, soda and salt. Add vanilla. Do not over mix.
  5. Pour into greased loaf pan.
  6. Bake for 55 minutes.
