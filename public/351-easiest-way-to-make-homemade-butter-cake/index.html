The ingredients needed to make Butter cake:
  1. Take 1 cup Maida
  2. Get 3 tbsp Butter
  3. Get 3 Eggs
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup Sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp Baking powder
  6. Get 1 tbsp Vanilla essence
  7. Prepare Milk 3 to 4 tbsp for softness

Instructions to make Butter cake:
  1. Aik bowl ma maida nikaal le us ma baking powder add kar ke spoon se achi tarah mix karle.
  2. Eggs KO aik bowl ma nikaal le.
  3. Aik bowl ma butter Dale us KO melt karle beater se then add sugar and mix it with butter then add eggs one by one and beat it.
  4. Then add maida slowly slowly and beat it with beater then add vanilla essence and beat all mixture with beater fluffy sa paste banaale.
  5. Aik non-stick pan lele us KO Grease karle phir us ma cake mixture daal ke 20 to 30 min ke lia low flame Pe bake karle.
  6. Or agar app ke paas oven ha to us ma bake karle.
  7. Mazedaar cake tayyar……
  8. Serve with tea..
