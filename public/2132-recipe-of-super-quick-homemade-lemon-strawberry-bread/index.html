The ingredients needed to make Lemon Strawberry Bread:
  1. Get 1 1/2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1 tsp salt

  12. Prepare 1 tbsp flour
  13. Get Glaze
  14. Get 2 tbsp melted butter
  15. Make ready 1/2 cup powdered sugar

Instructions to make Lemon Strawberry Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 and grease 9x5 pan
  2. In medium bowl whisk together flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.
  3. With electric mixer mix butter, sugar, eggs, vanilla extract, lemon zest and lemon juice until well combined.
  4. Slowly combine flour mixture and milk into mixture, mix until just combined.
  5. Coat blueberries with flour
  6. Gently and quickly add blueberries. Immediately put in baking pan
  7. Bake for 55-60 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Cool for 30 minutes in pan, then cool on wire rack.
  9. Prepare glaze by whisking together butter, powdered sugar, lemon juice and vanilla extract. Pour over loaf and enjoy.
