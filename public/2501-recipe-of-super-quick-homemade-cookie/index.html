The ingredients needed to make Cookie:
  1. Get 200 gram Flour
  2. Take 50 gram icing sugar
  3. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Prepare 180 gram butter
Instructions to make Cookie:
  1. Beat together butter and icing sugar, vanilla extract until well combined for about 5 minutes.
  2. Then add flour in two stages and beat well until creamy soft batter consistency. Transfer to a piping bag with big star nozzle pipe rosette shaped cookies on to a baking tray lined with butter paper. Bake in pre-heated oven of 180°C for 8 minutes. Bake it upper rack or double tray method. Cool down for 2 minutes and it is ready to eat.
