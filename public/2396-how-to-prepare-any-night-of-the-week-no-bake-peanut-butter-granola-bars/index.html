The ingredients needed to make No Bake Peanut Butter Granola Bars:
  1. Take 2 cups peanut butter.
  2. Take 1 cup honey.
  3. Take 3 cups old fashioned oats (I used quick oats).
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla.
  5. Get Chocolate chips to sprinkle on top (about 1/4 cup).

Steps to make No Bake Peanut Butter Granola Bars:
  1. In saucepan mix peanut butter, honey and vanilla. Cook on medium heat (stirring constantly) until melted.
  2. Remove from heat and immediately add the oats and stir to combine.
  3. Pour into greased 8x8 pan (I lined the pan with foil and sprayed with non-stick cooking spray).
  4. Place in refrigerator until firmed up.
  5. Slice and enjoy!
