The ingredients needed to make Basic white bread for bread machine:
  1. Make ready 1 1⁄3 cups luke warm water
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons powdered milk
  3. Make ready 1 1⁄2 teaspoons salt
  4. Take 1 tablespoon sugar
  5. Get 2 tablespoons butter (or 2 tablespoons margarine)
  6. Get 3 3⁄4 cups white flour (or 3 3/4 cups bread flour)
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon yeast
