Steps to make Cookie dough shapes:
  1. Add oil,brown sugar, sugar to bowl and whisk
  2. Add buttermilk,flavor,pinch of salt and whisk add flour and mix using spatula
  3. Until a dough is form like this,add chocolate chips and mix
  4. Put parchment paper,baking paper,or normal paper on a tray or baking tray pour the dough..using ur hand spread it and let it chill in frigde for 30min
  5. After 30 min using different cutter shape..cut the dough into desired shape place it in tray and chill it again for 3_5 hrs or over night
  6. For the glazing I'm using magic.. You can use any chocolate you want
  7. Remove the chocolate from the pack,water bath the chocolate when it start to melt remove it from the gas and add 3 tbsp hot water and keep stirring until its completely melted
  8. Put the dough in the melted chocolate,apply the chocolate on it,remove it using fork and place on wire rack
  9. And apply some Sprinkle's and chill it over night,or for 6hrs
  10. Ready to eat
