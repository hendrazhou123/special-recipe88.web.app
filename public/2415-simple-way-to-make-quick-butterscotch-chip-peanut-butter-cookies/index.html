The ingredients needed to make Butterscotch Chip Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Take 1/2 cup sugar
  2. Get 1/2 cup firmly packed brown sugar
  3. Take 3/4 cup Butter
  4. Get 1/2 cup Peanut Butter
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Get 1 eggs
  7. Prepare 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1 tsp Baking Soda
  9. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  10. Get 1 cup Butterscotch Chips

Instructions to make Butterscotch Chip Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. In large bowl beat sugar, brown sugar, butter, and peanut butter until light and fluffy.
  3. Add vanilla and egg and blend well.
  4. Stir in flour, baking soda, and salt. Mix well.
  5. Stir in butterscotch chips.
  6. Scoop or form into 2 inch balls. Place two inches apart.
  7. Flatten slightly in a criss cross pattern with a fork dipped in sugar.
  8. Bake for 6 to 10 minutes or until golden brown.
  9. Immediately remove from cookie sheet. Allow to cool completely.
  10. Enjoy.
