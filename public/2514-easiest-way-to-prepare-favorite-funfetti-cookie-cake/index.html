The ingredients needed to make Funfetti Cookie Cake:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  2. Take 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  3. Take 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1 egg yolk
  5. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 1 & 1/2 cups flour
  7. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Make ready 1 & 1/2 tsp cornstarch
  11. Make ready Chocolate or white chocolate chips
  12. Prepare Sprinkles, if you choose too make a funfetti cake!

Instructions to make Funfetti Cookie Cake:
  1. Beat butter until smooth
  2. Add in sugar and beat until fully blended
  3. Beat in your egg, egg yolk and vanilla extract
  4. In a seperate bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking soda, baking powder, and cornstarch
  5. Slowly begin to mix dry ingredients in with your wet ingredients. Add a little at a time. Mix until dough is formed.
  6. Add in your chocolate chips and/or sprinkles. Mix together
  7. Spread evenly in a 9 inch pan and bake in 350° oven for 25 minutes
  8. Let cool for 30 mins on cooling rack before decorating. Enjoy!
