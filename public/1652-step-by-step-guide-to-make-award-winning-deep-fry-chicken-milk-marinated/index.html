Steps to make Deep fry chicken milk marinated:
  1. Arrange chicken in the marinate bowl
  2. In small bowl add milk, sugar, light soy sauce, pepper and chicken stock powder, mixed well
  3. Pour over the marinate sauce in the chickens bowl and start mixing by hand, try to massage everything into the chickens meat.
  4. Marinate for 1 hour
  5. In deep fryer pour in oil at temperature 180c ,fry chicken for about 10 minutes
