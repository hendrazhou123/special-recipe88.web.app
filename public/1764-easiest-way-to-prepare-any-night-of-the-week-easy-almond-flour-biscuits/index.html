The ingredients needed to make Easy Almond Flour Biscuits:
  1. Take 2 cups Almond Flour
  2. Prepare 2 Tsp Baking Powder
  3. Prepare 2 LG Eggs (beaten)
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup Butter (melt before mixing)

Instructions to make Easy Almond Flour Biscuits:
  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl
  3. Beat eggs and melt butter then add to mixture and mix until blended
  4. Roll the dough out into small balls and flatten with fingers. Place on a parchment paper lined cookie sheet
  5. Bake for 15 min, until firm and golden brown
  6. Cool then eat!
