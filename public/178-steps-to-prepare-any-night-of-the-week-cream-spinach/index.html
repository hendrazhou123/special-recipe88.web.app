The ingredients needed to make Cream spinach:
  1. Get 500 g Baby spinach leaves
  2. Make ready 1/4 Cup butter
  3. Get 3 Cloves minced garlic
  4. Make ready 4 Tablespoons flour
  5. Prepare 1 cup heavy cream
  6. Prepare 1/2 Teaspoon ground nutmeg
  7. Prepare 1/4 Grated Parmesan cheese
  8. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper

Instructions to make Cream spinach:
  1. Melt the butter in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the spinach and cook for around 4 minutes with the lid on.
  3. Once the spinach has cooked down add the garlic and flour and cook for a further 2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the cream and bring to a simmer.
  5. Turn off and remove the pan from the heat, Season with Nutmeg, Salt, Pepper and stir in the Parmesan until its melted.
