The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake:
  1. Prepare 300 gr Gluten free flour
  2. Take 130 gr butter
  3. Make ready 220 g whipping cream
  4. Prepare 100 g dark chocolate
  5. Take 16 g yeast
  6. Make ready 2 Tbsp Vainilla essence
  7. Make ready 5 g salt
  8. Make ready 4 Eggs
  9. Make ready 1/2 lemon skin

Instructions to make Chocolate cake:
