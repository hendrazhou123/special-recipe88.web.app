The ingredients needed to make Butter Shrimp:
  1. Make ready 1/2 kg shrimp
  2. Prepare 3 tablespoon salt
  3. Prepare 3 tablespoon pepper
  4. Take 1 lime
  5. Take as needed Butter
  6. Prepare 3 tablespoon chopped garlic
  7. Make ready as needed Parsley
  8. Take as needed Oregano

Steps to make Butter Shrimp:
  1. Put shrimp in a bowl and mix it with water from the lime, salt and pepper
  2. Add a bit of butter and store it for 1 hour so the taste seeps in.
  3. Heat butter on frying pan and put the garlic in until it is brownish
  4. Put in the shrimp and flip it as we go. We want the shrimp to be cooked all sided and the taste seeps in. If needed add more lime and salt to the frying pan.
  5. Add oregano and parsley and stir
  6. It is ready to serve
