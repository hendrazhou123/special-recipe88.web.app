Steps to make Big poppa's slow cooked African chicken with a curry peanut butt:
  1. Heat oil over medium heat add onions saute until soft then add garlic, oregano, salt. Add chicken stock, tomato sauce and bay leaf. Saute for 10 min
  2. Place chicken in slow cooker. Combine sauce and chicken mix and cook on low for 6 hrs or on high for 3 hrs.
  3. In a bowl, combine peanut butter, lemon juice, yellow curry and all peppers. Add a little of the sauce from the crock pot about 1/2 cup mix very well. Add to slow cooker 20 min before you take the chicken out to serve.
  4. Serve on a hot bed of white rice. So ooooooo yummy!
