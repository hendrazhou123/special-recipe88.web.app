The ingredients needed to make Sweet Yam Casserole:
  1. Get 2 cans yams (40 ounce), drained
  2. Prepare 1 cup light brown sugar
  3. Take 1/4 teaspoon salt
  4. Get 1 stick margarine (softened)
  5. Make ready 1 bag mini marshmallows
  6. Prepare Sprinkle cinnamon powder

Instructions to make Sweet Yam Casserole:
  1. In a large bowl mix together brown sugar and salt……
  2. Combine together in bowl with the brown sugar mixture your drained cans of yams and softened margarine…….break up any large pieces of yams……
  3. Pour yam mixture into a large baking pan……sprinkle mixture with cinnamon powder…….
  4. Bake uncovered in a preheated 350 degree oven for 40 minutes…..
  5. After baking remove pan from oven and place bag of marshmallows over top of yams…..
  6. Place back into oven and continue to bake until marshmallows are golden brown…….
  7. Serve and enjoy 😉!
