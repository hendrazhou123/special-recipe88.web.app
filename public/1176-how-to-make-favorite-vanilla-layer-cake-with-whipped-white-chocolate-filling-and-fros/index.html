Instructions to make Vanilla Layer Cake with Whipped White Chocolate Filling and Fros:
  1. To dye coconut green for garnish
  2. Place 1 teaspoon water and a few drops of green food color in bowl and mix
  3. Add coconut and stir with a fork to color evenly
  4. Spread out on parchment paper to dry
  5. MAKE WHITE CHOCOLATE GANACHE FILLING AND FROSTING, Do this first as it needs time to chill
  6. Have chopped white chocolate in a large bowl.Heat cream in microwave or on stove top until hot but not boiling, pour over white chocolate, let sit 1 minute then stir until smooth. Let cool to room temperature then cover and chill until very cold, at least 4 hours or overnight. Do not beat for filling until cake is cooled and ready to fill and frost.
  8. Make vanilla cake
  9. Preheat oven to 350. Spray 4 - 8 - inch cake pans with bakers spray
  10. Whisk in a bowl flour, baking powder and salt
  11. In another bowl beat butter and sugar until light and fluffy
  12. Add flour mixture alternating with milk just until blended, then beat in eggs an vanilla just until combined
  13. Divide batter in 4 bowls
  14. Color one bowl pink
  15. Color second bowl blue
  16. Color third bowl yellow
  17. Color fourth bowl green
  19. Fill prepared cake pans with each color and bake about 15 to 18 minutes, until a toothpick comes out just clean. Cool 15 minutes in pans then remove to cool completely on wire racks
  20. Finish filling and frosting
  21. Beat cold white chocolate/ cream mixture until light and fluffy
  22. Assemble cake
  23. Place blue cake layer bottom up on serving plate, the bottoms are mot pretty but will be pretty in the end!
  24. Frost with som of the white chocolate filling
  25. Add green cake layer, bottom up
  26. Frost with white chocolate filling
  27. Add yellow cake layer
  28. Frost again with some white chocolate filling
  29. Add pink layer and frost entire cake with remaining white chocolate ganache
  31. Garnish with the green cocont, blue and pink sparkle sugar, blueberries, strawberry and chocolate eggs. Chill cake at least 2 hours to firm up for easy slicing. Serve at room tempersure for best flavor
