The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Wafer Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup butter
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Take 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  5. Get 1 1/3 cup all purpose flour
  6. Make ready 3/4 tsp baking powder

Instructions to make Vanilla Wafer Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. In a mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar. Beat in egg and vanilla. Combine dry ingredients - add to creamed mixture and mix well.
  3. Drop by teaspoonfuls 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until edges are golden brown.
  5. Remove to a wire rack to cool
  6. Yield : approx. 3 dozen
