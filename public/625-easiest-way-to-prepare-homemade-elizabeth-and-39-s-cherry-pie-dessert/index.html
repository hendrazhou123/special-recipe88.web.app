The ingredients needed to make Elizabeth's Cherry Pie Dessert:
  1. Get 1 stick (or less) margarine
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 cup flour
  4. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp. baking powder
  5. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  7. Make ready 1 can cherries, drained
  8. Get 3/4 cup sugar

Steps to make Elizabeth's Cherry Pie Dessert:
  1. Melt margarine in baking pan.
  2. Combine 1 cup sugar, flour, baking powder, salt and milk in bowl, mixing well.
  3. Spoon into baking pan.
  4. Mix cherries with 3/4 cup sugar and 1 cup water, stirring to dissolve sugar.
  5. Pour over batter. Do not stir.
  6. Bake at 350 degrees until brown.
