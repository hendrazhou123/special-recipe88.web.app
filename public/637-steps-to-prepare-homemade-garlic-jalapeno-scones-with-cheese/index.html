The ingredients needed to make Garlic jalapeño scones with cheese:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup melted butter (not margarine as butter is better)
  3. Take 1/4 cup chopped jalapeños
  4. Take 1/8 cup chopped garlic cloves
  5. Take 1 cup shredded marble cheese
  6. Make ready 1 egg
  7. Get 1 1/2 tablespoons sugar
  8. Make ready 2 teaspoons baking powder
  9. Make ready Dash salt

  11. Make ready Add extra flour to make a non sticky consistency
