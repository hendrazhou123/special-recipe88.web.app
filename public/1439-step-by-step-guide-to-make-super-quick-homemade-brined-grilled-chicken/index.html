The ingredients needed to make Brined Grilled Chicken:
  1. Prepare chicken
  2. Get 2 1/2 lb chicken thighs boneless and skinless
  3. Get 1 tbsp granulated garlic powder
  4. Take 1 tbsp paprika
  5. Take 1 tbsp fresh ground black pepper
  6. Get 1 tbsp salt
  7. Get 2 cup water
  8. Take 1/4 cup mild vinegar I used coconut vinegar

Steps to make Brined Grilled Chicken:
