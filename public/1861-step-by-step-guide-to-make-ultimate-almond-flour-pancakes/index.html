The ingredients needed to make Almond Flour Pancakes:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 Cups Almond Flour
  2. Take 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  3. Take 1/4 Teaspoon Salt
  4. Take 1/4 Cup Dark Chocolate Chips or Blueberries
  5. Prepare 3 Eggs
  6. Take 5 Tablespoons Unsweetened Almond Milk
  7. Take 1-2 Tablespoons Honey or sweetener of choice
  8. Get 1 Teaspoon Vanilla

Steps to make Almond Flour Pancakes:
  1. Blend dry ingredients in a bowl
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together eggs, honey, milk and vanilla.
  3. Whisk together wet and dry ingredients then add fruit or chocolate chips.
  4. Heat skillet to medium heat. Measure 1/4 Cup of batter into greased skillet. Cook on each side for 2-3 minutes or until browned.
