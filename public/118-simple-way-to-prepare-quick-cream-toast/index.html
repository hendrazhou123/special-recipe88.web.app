  5. Take 1/2 tsp red chilli powder (optional)
  6. Take to taste Salt
  7. Get as required Fresh malai

Instructions to make Cream Toast:
  1. Take a tawa and put on flame and preheat for 1 or 2 seconds.
  2. Take a bowl put tomatoes, onion, black pepper powder, salt, red chilli powder (optional), fresh cream and mix well.
  3. Take bread slices and spread the mixture over it on one slice and then cover with second slices of bread.
  4. Then put the bread set on the pan and and put some ghee to roast in lite golden brown colour.
  5. After that the crispy and delicious sandwich is ready.
  6. Cut in any shape and serve it hot with tea or coffee.
