The ingredients needed to make Snickerdoodle & Sprinkles Cookie:
  1. Prepare 1/2 Cup Butter
  2. Make ready 3/4 Cup Sugar
  3. Take 1 Egg Large
  4. Get 1 Teaspoon Almond Extract (or Vanilla Extract)
  5. Prepare 1 1/3 Cups Flour
  6. Get 1 Teaspoon Cream of Tartar
  7. Get 1/2 Teaspoon Baking Soda
  8. Take 3 Teaspoons Apple Pie Spice (or Cinammon)
  9. Get 2 Tablespoons Sprinkles
  10. Take Dash Salt

Steps to make Snickerdoodle & Sprinkles Cookie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Cream butter and sugar together. Beat egg in wet mixture until throughly combined, add vanilla or almond extract.
  3. In a separate bowl, combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, Apple pie spice or cinnamon and salt.
  4. Blend dry ingredients into butter mixture, 1/3 at a time.
  5. Gently stir in rainbow sprinkles.
  6. Chill dough in saran wrap for about 20 min.
  7. Roll out dough by hand on chilled cookie sheet with parchment paper.
  8. Optional: Sprinkle some sugar on top.
  9. Bake for 10-12 minutes.
