The ingredients needed to make Rhubarb-vanilla-cake:
  1. Get 50 grams butter
  2. Prepare 100 grams sugar
  3. Make ready 100 grams yoghurt
  4. Make ready 125 grams flour
  5. Prepare 1 packages vanilla pudding
  6. Take 500 ml milk
  7. Make ready 2 stick rhubarb
  8. Get 1 vanilla extract
  9. Make ready 1 eggs
  10. Make ready grams baking soda
  11. Get 50 grams white chocolate
  12. Make ready 200 ml whipping cream

Instructions to make Rhubarb-vanilla-cake:
  1. Prepare dough, mixing butter, sugar and egg with a handmixer
  2. Stir in flour, baking soda and yoghurt. Blend
  3. Place in a baking tray. Meanwhile, cook milk, stir in pudding powder, stir and let cool for a while.
  4. Cut rhubarb into small pieces. Season with vanilla extract
  5. Preheat oven to 175ºC
  6. Pour pudding over dough, place rhubarb inbetween.
  7. Bake for 22 minutes. Meanwhile melt chocolate
  8. Take the cake out of the oven and let cool. Meanwhile use a latte-spoon to form a grid with the melt chocolate ontop of the cake. Let cool until the chocolate hardens. Serve wizh whipped cream
