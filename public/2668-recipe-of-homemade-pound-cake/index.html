The ingredients needed to make Pound Cake:
  1. Get 1 lb butter (softened)
  2. Make ready 2 1/4 cups sugar (split)
  3. Take 8 eggs (split whites and yolks)
  4. Get 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 2 tsp almond extract
  6. Take 1/3 cup bourbon/whiskey
  7. Take 3 cups flour
  8. Take Your choice of frosting

Steps to make Pound Cake:
  1. In a large mixing bowl, beat butter and 2 cups sugar until it’s a light yellow color. Gradually add in egg yolks. Mix in Bourbon, almond extract and vanilla to the mixture. Slowly add in flour.
  2. In a separate bowl add egg whites and remaining sugar. Beat until stiff peaks. Fold in into batter.
  3. Pour into a greased angel food cake pan. Cook at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 15 minutes. Let cool and frost.
