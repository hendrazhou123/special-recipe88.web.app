The ingredients needed to make Mini Chocolate Chip Cookie Cereal:
  1. Take 160 g (1 1/3 sticks) + 1 tbsp) Butter
  2. Get 100 g (1/2 cup) White sugar
  3. Prepare 100 g (1/2 cup) Brown sugar
  4. Get 1 Large egg 1 tsp Vanilla 1 tbsp Milk
  5. Take 300 g (2 cups) All purpose flour
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Baking powder ½ tsp Salt Chocolate Chips
  7. Take Makes approx. 150 mini cookies

Steps to make Mini Chocolate Chip Cookie Cereal:
  1. #Method: - In a large bowl melt the butter. Add the vanilla extract, white sugar and brown sugar.
  2. Add the egg and sifted dry ingredients, and mix to combine. - - Stir in the chocolate chips. - - Transfer the batter/dough to a piping bag, and pipe cookies onto a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Bake the cookies in a preheated oven at 160 °C (320 °F) for 5 - 8 minutes or until golden. - - Allow to cool completely.
  4. #Tip: - - Cooking time will vary depending on the size of your cookies.
