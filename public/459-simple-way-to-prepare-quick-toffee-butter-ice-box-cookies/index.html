The ingredients needed to make Toffee Butter Ice Box Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 Cup butter - softened
  2. Make ready 1 Cup sugar
  3. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  4. Take 1 Egg - Room temp.
  5. Make ready 2 1/2 cups Flour
  6. Get 1 Cup Heath Toffee Bits

Instructions to make Toffee Butter Ice Box Cookies:
  1. Cream buttrr & sugar together. Add in egg and vanilla mix until well combined. Stir in flour. Mixing until fully incorporated. Fold in Heath Toffee Bits.
  2. Divide dough in half and place dough onto plastic wrap. Form dough into a log about 6" long and 1&1/2" round. Refrigerate dough 1-2 hours. Or until nice and firm.
  3. Preheat oven to 375ºF. Line baking sheet with parchment paper. Remove plastic wrap. Cut into 1/4" slices and place 2" apart. Bake 8 minutes or until lightly brown.
