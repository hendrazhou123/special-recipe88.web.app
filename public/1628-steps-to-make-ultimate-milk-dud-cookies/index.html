The ingredients needed to make Milk Dud cookies:
  1. Take 1/2 cup butter softened
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup chunky peanut butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup brown sugar
  4. Get 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  5. Prepare 1 egg
  6. Take 1 1/4 cup sifted flour
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 1 Milk Duds 5 ounce box

Instructions to make Milk Dud cookies:
  1. Beat together softened butter,peanut butter and sugars.
  2. Beat in the egg.
  3. Add flour,baking powder,baking soda and salt. Mix thoroughly.
  4. Chill dough. Roll into small balls. Press Milk Dud in each ball hiding it inside the dough. Roll in granulated sugar. Bake at 350°F 10-12 minutes until lightly browned. Cool on sheet for a few minutes to finish baking process. Cool. Enjoy and indulge!
