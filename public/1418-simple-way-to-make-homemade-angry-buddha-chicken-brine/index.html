The ingredients needed to make Angry Buddha Chicken Brine:
  1. Make ready 10 cups cold water
  2. Get 1 tbsp sesame oil
  3. Make ready 5 tbsp kosher salt
  4. Get 5 tbsp brown sugar
  5. Get 3 tbsp sriracha sauce
  6. Prepare 4 star anise
  7. Get 1 bay leaf
  8. Make ready 2 limes sliced
  9. Make ready 1 tsp curry powder
  10. Make ready 2 tbsp garlic minced
  11. Prepare 1 tsp ground black pepper
  12. Take 4 tbsp soy sauce
  13. Prepare 1 tbsp ground ginger
  14. Get 1/2 cup apple cider vinegar
  15. Make ready 3 tbsp hoisin sauce
  16. Get 3 tbsp oyster sauce
  17. Make ready 1 tbsp red pepper flake

Instructions to make Angry Buddha Chicken Brine:
  1. Add all ingredients to a large pot. Bring to a boil. Whisk thoroughly.
  2. Remove from heat and let cool to room temp. Roughly 2 hrs.
  3. Use it for a brine for pork, chicken, beef, lamb… or let cool and use to add to soup bases or washing your car with😉
  4. Be sure to discard remaining brine after using with raw meats
