The ingredients needed to make Sugar cookies:
  1. Make ready 500 ml flour + extra flour for rolling
  2. Take 100 ml maizena (cornflour)
  3. Get 125 ml sugar, castor
  4. Get 2 ml salt
  5. Make ready 125 ml margerine
  6. Take 2 eggs
  7. Get 5 ml vanilla essences
  8. Make ready As needed Spray & Cook

Instructions to make Sugar cookies:
  1. Sift flour, maizena, castor sugar and salt together in bowl
  2. Rub margarine in flour mixture with thumbs until it looks like bread crumbs
  3. Whisk eggs and vanilla in separate bowl until fluffy
  4. Knead eggs and flour mixture together until it forms a ball.
  5. Wrap dough and rest in frigde for 30 minutes.
  6. Preheat oven to 180°C. Spray & Cook baking pans
  7. Lighty dust surface with flour. Take quarter of dough and roll out between 3-5mm thick
  8. Press desired shapes with cookie cutter and place it on baking pan
  9. With left over dough after cutting, add some new dough and roll out again. Continue this process until dough is finished
  10. Bake cookies for 15 minutes
  11. Cool cookies on drying rack
  12. Eat them as they are or decorate with royal icing
