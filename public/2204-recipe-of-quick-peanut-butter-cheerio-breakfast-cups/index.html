The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Cheerio Breakfast Cups:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  2. Get 1/2 cup all natural organic honey
  3. Prepare 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  4. Get 4 1/2 cups honey nut cheerios

Steps to make Peanut Butter Cheerio Breakfast Cups:
  1. Place honey and peanut butter in large glass bowl and mix together.
  2. Place in microwave for 1 minute and 30 seconds; Stir well.
  3. Mix in vanilla extract.
  4. Fold in the cheerios carefully until fully coated.
  5. Line two muffin tins with cupcake liners. I usually get 18 portions.
  6. Spoon equally into muffin cups and allow to sit out for several hours to set. I freeze these and pull them out the evening before to set in fridge.
  7. Enjoy!
