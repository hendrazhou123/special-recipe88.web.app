The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Peach Kickin' Sweet Tea:

  2. Take 2 quart Sweet Tea
  3. Prepare 2 can Peach halves
  4. Prepare 12 pints sized mason jars

Steps to make Vanilla Peach Kickin' Sweet Tea:
  1. Open peach halves and place one peach half per jar
  2. In small pot bring 3 or 4 cups of sweet tea to a boil
  3. Pour super hot tea (evenly distributed) in the mason jars over the peaches. This brings out the peach flavor in the tea
  4. Let sit for about 20 to 30 minutes
  5. Mix vodka and remaining sweet tea together and pour that mixture into the mason jars (once again, evenly distributed)
