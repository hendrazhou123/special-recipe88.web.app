Steps to make Vanilla ButterCream:
  1. Add butter and mix til fluffy.
  2. Add in around 2 cups of Powdered Sugar and mix with butter.
  3. Add Vanilla Flavor.
  4. Add 2 Tablespoons of milk.
  5. Stop the mixer and scrape sides to make sure everything is mixing.
  6. Add in remaining sugar.
  7. Add in remaining Milk. (Gradually)
