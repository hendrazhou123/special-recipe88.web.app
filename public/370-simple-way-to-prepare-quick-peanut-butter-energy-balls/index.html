The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Energy Balls:
  1. Prepare 2 cups rolled oats
  2. Make ready 1 cup natural peanut butter
  3. Take 1/4 cup mini chocolate chips
  4. Take 1/4 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  5. Get 1/2 cup honey

Steps to make Peanut Butter Energy Balls:
  1. Add all ingredients to a large mixing bowl. Use hands to combine well.
  2. Roll into teaspoon sized balls. Store in airtight container in refrigerator.
