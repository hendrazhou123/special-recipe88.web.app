The ingredients needed to make Mahogany Cake:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Prepare 1 tsp. baking soda
  3. Make ready 1 tsp. salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  5. Get 1 1/2 cups sugar
  6. Prepare 3 eggs
  7. Get 2 oz. unsweetened chocolate
  8. Prepare 1 cup strong black coffee, divided
  9. Get 1 tablespoon vinegar
  10. Get 1 tsp. vanilla extract
  11. Get 9 Frosting: See in STEPS

Steps to make Mahogany Cake:
  1. Cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add eggs one at a time, incorporating each egg before adding another.
  2. Heat chocolate and 1/2 cup of coffee together until chocolate is completely dissolved.
  3. Beat chocolate-coffee mixture into butter-sugar mixture.
  4. Sift flour, soda, salt together.
  5. Stir in a little of the flour mixture, alternating with the divided 1/2 cup of coffee until all used up.
  6. Add vinegar and vanilla and beat well.
  7. Divide into three 9” pans.
  8. Then, bake at 325F for 25-30 minutes.
  9. Frosting: * With 1/3 cup heavy whipping cream, add 1 tablespoon of strong coffee, 1 tablespoon of cocoa, and 2 tsp. sugar. - * When thoroughly chilled, add 2/3 cups of heavy whipping cream and whip. - * Spread between layers and top of cake.
