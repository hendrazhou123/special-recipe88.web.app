The ingredients needed to make Creame of chicken soup:
  1. Take 1 tbsp butter
  2. Make ready 1 onion chopped
  3. Prepare 1 small piece of celery stick
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp all purpose flour
  5. Make ready as needed chicken stock
  6. Get 100 ml milk
  7. Prepare as needed Boiled and shredded chicken
  8. Get to taste Salt and black pepper powder

Steps to make Creame of chicken soup:
  1. Boil chicken in a pressure cooker for 2-3 whistles
  2. Take Shredded pieces of chicken and keep a side.
  3. Take butter in a pan and put in the flour, and cook over low heat for a few minutes, stirring all the time.
  4. Slowly pour in the milk, stirring continuously and let it come to the boil.
  5. Add the cooked chicken and let it boil for 5-7 minutes. Can add chicken stock to adjust the consistency.
  6. Pour the soup into a food processor or blender and puree until smooth. Pour the soup back to the pan and add salt and pepper.
  7. Note: soup tastes equally good without blending too.
