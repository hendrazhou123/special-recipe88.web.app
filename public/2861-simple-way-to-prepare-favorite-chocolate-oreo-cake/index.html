The ingredients needed to make Chocolate oreo cake:
  1. Prepare 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  3. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tblsp baking powder
  5. Get 1 tblsp baking soda
  6. Take 1 cup butter milk
  7. Prepare 1 tblsp vanilla flavor
  8. Make ready 2 tblsp chocolate syrup
  9. Prepare Oreo biscuits
  10. Make ready 1 tblsp milk powder
  11. Make ready 2 eggs

Steps to make Chocolate oreo cake:
  1. In a bowl add all dry ingredients flour,baking powder,milk powder,cocoa powder,sugar,baking soda mix and keep aside
  2. Crack eggs and use mixer whisk until pale and fluffy add vanilla flavor and chocolate syrup
  3. And whisk,then add butter milk
  4. Pour ur dry mixture using spoon or spatula and fold until u make a thick batter,crush Oreo biscuits
  5. Mix and grease ur baking pan and pour ur batter then bake until done
  6. Insert toothpick it comes out clean it’s done,Allow to cool down completely,spread whipped cream and garnish with chocolate syrup
  7. Enjoy😋
