Steps to make Homemade chocolates:
  1. In a double boiler add 2 teaspoon of choco powder and melted butter mix it well add 3 teaspoon of powdered sugar mix it well for few seconds.. pour it another bowl add jelly ball one by one and keep in a tray and freeze it for 2 hours
  2. In a double boiler add butter and milk powder mix it well add powdered sugar and milk mix it well.. pour it another bowl at butterscotch and pour in mould and freeze it for 2 hours
  3. In a mould add one teaspoon of dark chocolate and 1 teaspoon of white chocolate put stick and freeze it for 2 hours.. marble chocolate ready
