The ingredients needed to make Semovita steam bread(Alkubus):
  1. Get 3 cups semovita
  2. Prepare 1 cup flour
  3. Take 1 tbs yeast
  4. Make ready 3 tbs oil
  5. Take Warm water
  6. Get 1 tsp Sugar

Instructions to make Semovita steam bread(Alkubus):
  1. Dissolve 1tsp of sugar in 2½ cup of warm water.
  2. Add your yeast to proof it and set aside for five minute.
  3. Sift your semovita and flour in a bowl mixed it well.
  4. Add ur yeast water and continue to mixed, until a soft dough is form. This will take about 15min.
  5. Put 3tbs of oil and rub on your dough. Cover it n allow to rest for 30min. The dough will double size.
  6. Beat your dough to release air.
  7. Grease ur cups, or tin, or baking pan with oil, scoop your dough n put in d cups. Arrange ur cups in a pot or bowl n allow to rise again for 15min.
  8. When the dough rise then arrange it in your steamer and steam. Or put little water in a pot n bring it to boil, then arrange ur cups in d pot n cover.
  9. Serve with sauce or vegetable soup.
