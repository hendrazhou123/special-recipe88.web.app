The ingredients needed to make Easy Addictive Cheese Ball:
  1. Get 1 block softened cream cheese
  2. Make ready 1 container Philadelphia whipped cream cheese
  3. Make ready 1 dash worecestershire sauce
  4. Take 1 packet dry Knorr veggie soup mix
  5. Take Shredded cheddar cheese
  6. Make ready Bacon bits

Steps to make Easy Addictive Cheese Ball:
  1. Mix the block of cream cheese, whipped cream cheese, worecestershire sauce, and knorr veggie soup mix together well in a bowl. Use an electric hand mixer if needed.
  2. Cover the bowl with saran wrap and chill in the fridge for an hour or so (so the mixture firms up).
  3. Take the mixture out of the fridge and then shape it into ball form using a spatula.
  4. After you get a roughly shaped circle with the spatula (make sure your hands are washed) use your hands to make the circle shape firm.
  5. In a different bowl put the shredded cheese and bacon bits together.
  6. Now roll the cheese ball in this mixture making sure every spot gets covered. Place in fridge.
  7. Before you serve make sure its at room temperature. Serve with crackers and a cheese knife.
  8. If the whole cheese ball doesn't go – store the leftovers in the fridge.
