The ingredients needed to make Shenga chutney (Peanut powder chutney):
  1. Take 1 cup peanuts
  2. Take 2 tsp chilly powder
  3. Get 4-5 big cloves of garlic
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to make Shenga chutney (Peanut powder chutney):
  1. Roast and peel the peanuts
  2. Add all ingredients in the blender and grind to a coarse consistency
  3. Serve with oil or dahi. Goes well with Dosa, Bhakri, Upma.
