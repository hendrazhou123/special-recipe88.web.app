The ingredients needed to make Cream muffins:
  1. Make ready 1 egg
  2. Get 11/4 cups heavy cream
  3. Take 2 cups sifted flour
  4. Prepare 1/4 teaspoons salt
  5. Make ready 3 teaspoons baking powder
  6. Take 2 tablespoons sugar

Steps to make Cream muffins:
  1. Sift dry ingredients
  2. Beat egg well
  3. Stir in cream
  4. Add to flour mixture
