The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Filled Doughnuts:
  1. Make ready 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1 cup glutinous flour
  3. Get 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 2 tsp active dry yeast
  5. Get 1 cup water
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup butter melted
  7. Take 2 eggs medium
  8. Make ready Vegetable oil
  9. Get 2 bars tablea pure chocolate
  10. Get 1 cup milk
  11. Take 1 tsp salt
  12. Prepare 1 bar dark chocolate for filling

Instructions to make Chocolate Filled Doughnuts:
  1. Mix the yeast into warm milk with sugar for 5 minutes to activate
  2. In a separate bowl mix the all-purpose flour and glutinous rice, salt, melted butter and egg and sugar
  3. Pour in the milk with yeast by portions and mix with the dry ingredients
  4. Knead for 3 minutes then leave it for an hour for the dough to rise. Add a little bit of oil to grease
  5. Punch out the air from the dough, knead for 15 minutes then leave it for another 30 minutes to rest
  6. Repeat step 4 and then cut into equal portions to mould the doughnuts.
  7. Cut the chocolate bars into square portions and put into the doughnuts as fillings.
  8. Add oil in a pan and quickly deep fry each doughnuts. Drain the excess oil and then start melting the tablea pure chocolate for coating
  9. Dip each doughnuts into the melted tablea pure chocolate and serve!
