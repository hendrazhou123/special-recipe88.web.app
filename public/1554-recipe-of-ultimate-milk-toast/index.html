Steps to make Milk Toast:
  1. In a small saucepan, heat your milk to hot but not boiling.
  2. Toast your bread and butter each piece, followed by a tsp of sugar and a sprinkling of cinnamon on each piece.
  3. Pour your hot milk over your stack of toast and enjoy. It absorbs the milk. DELISH!
