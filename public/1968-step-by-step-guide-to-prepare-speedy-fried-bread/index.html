The ingredients needed to make Fried Bread:
  1. Prepare 600 ml all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  3. Get 1/4 tsp onion powder
  4. Get 1 tsp sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
  6. Take 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  7. Get 200 ml warm water
  8. Get Oil for frying

Instructions to make Fried Bread:
  1. Combine all dry ingredients and mix well.
  2. Pour warm water bit by bit and mix with a spatula or spoon.
  3. Knead. If the dough is too dry, add water. If it's too wet, add flour.
  4. Cover and wait for 10 minutes. Divide into 12 parts.
  5. Spread about 1 tablespoon of flour on your working space. Flatten the dough and roll into roundish shape.
  6. Heat oil in a pan. I use medium heat. Fry flattened dough. It takes less than one minute for each side to brown, so flip after one side has become brown.
