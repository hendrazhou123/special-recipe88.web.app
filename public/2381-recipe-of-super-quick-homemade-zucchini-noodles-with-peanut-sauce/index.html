The ingredients needed to make Zucchini noodles with Peanut Sauce:
  1. Get For Sauce:
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup smooth peanut butter
  3. Prepare 3 Tbsp Low Sodium Soy Sauce
  4. Prepare 1 cloves garlic
  5. Take 1 Tbsp grated ginger
  6. Get 1 tsp red chili paste
  7. Prepare 2 Tbsp rice wine Vinegar
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup hot water
  9. Make ready 1 Tbsp honey
  10. Get 2 tsp sesame oil
  11. Get For the salad:
  12. Make ready 3 Zucchini's spiraled into noodles
  13. Get 4 Scallions chopped
  14. Get 1/4 cup Roughly chopped Peanuts
  15. Get Fresh cilantro roughly torn

Steps to make Zucchini noodles with Peanut Sauce:
  1. In a food processor or blender, add all of the ingredients to make the sauce and blend until smooth, set aside or store it in a tightly sealed container in the fridge until you’re ready to use it.
  2. In a large bowl, toss the spiralized zucchini with some of the dressing, then sprinkle with the nuts, scallions and cilantro and dig right in
  3. Side note: This dressing gets better and better as it sits. My suggestion is to store the zucchini and dressing separate from each other so that the zucchini remain nice and crispy.
