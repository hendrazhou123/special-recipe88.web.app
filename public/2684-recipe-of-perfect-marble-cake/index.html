The ingredients needed to make Marble Cake:
  1. Prepare 5 eggs
  2. Prepare 140 gr sugar (add more if you prefer)
  3. Prepare 150 gr all-purpose flour
  4. Get 50 ml milk
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 tsp emulsifier
  7. Make ready 100 gr butter (melted)
  8. Get 50 ml vegetable oil
  9. Prepare (Combine both butter & oil)
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder (chocolate paste: dissolve with 2 tbsp hot water)

This marble cake can be kept at room temperature (summer/tropical weather) for a day. Make your marble cake using a purchased cake mix. You can bake a round layered cake or a large single layered cake. Then frost the moist marble cake with your favorite frosting or ganache.

Steps to make Marble Cake:
  1. Place eggs, sugar, flour, milk, vanilla, emulsifier in a mixing bowl. Whisk with high speed until reaching a thick and fluffy consistency.
  2. Add butter & oil to the batter. Do not over mix.
  3. Take a small portion of batter and combine with the prepared chocolate paste.
  4. Pour batter into a baking pan then add randomly chocolate butter on top. Make a random pattern with a toothpick or any kind of thin stick.
  5. Bake (pre-heat oven at 180 degC) for about 45-50 minutes. ENJOY!
