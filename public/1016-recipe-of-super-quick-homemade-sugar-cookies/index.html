Instructions to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Mix butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla.

  3. Mix flour mix with the rest of the ingredients then chill overnight.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°.
  5. Roll out dough and cut cookies out then bake until light brown.
