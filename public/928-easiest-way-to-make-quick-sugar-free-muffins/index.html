The ingredients needed to make Sugar-free Muffins:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup oats
  2. Make ready 1 banana
  3. Get 2 tbsp honey
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  5. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp cinnamon powder
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  9. Make ready 1 tbsp butter
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp Choco chips

Steps to make Sugar-free Muffins:
  1. Mash banana. Add honey and butter. Mix well. In one bowl mix all dry ingredients. Add it to wet ingredients. Mix thoroughly. Fill half cups. Add chocolate chips
  2. Preheat oven. Add batter to muffin molds. Bake for 25 minutes. Enjoy hot with chocolate drizzled.
