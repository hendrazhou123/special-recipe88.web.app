The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Get 190 g cake flour
  2. Take 100 g sugar
  3. Get 60 g brown sugar
  4. Make ready 120 g unsalted butter, melted
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Take 1 egg
  8. Prepare 2 drops vanilla extract
  9. Prepare 50 g chocolate chips

Steps to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C. In the meantime, sift the cake flour and baking powder.
  2. Combine the sugar, brown sugar, and melted butter in a bowl and mix until slightly white. Then, add the salt, egg, and vanilla extract.
  3. Next, add the cake flour and baking powder to the mixture in step 2 and fold with a rubber spatula.
  4. Combine the chocolate chips to the batter and mix.
  5. Using a spoon, roughly divide the batter into 8 and place on a baking tray lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake in the oven for 9 to 11 minutes and let cool before serving.
