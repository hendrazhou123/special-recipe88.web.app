The ingredients needed to make Salt crusted leg of lamb:

  3. Take 3/4 cup softened margarine

Instructions to make Salt crusted leg of lamb:
  1. Get a grill hot, and preheat the oven 300° Fahrenheit.
  2. Coat the leg of lamb with salt put on the grill roast it. Char the fat quite a bit. Take off grill after about 30-40 minutes. Put into a roaster.
  3. Coat with margarine and mustard. bake in oven covered for an hour or till it your personal doneness.
  4. Serve hope you enjoy!
  5. Serve with rice, carrots, or potatoes.
