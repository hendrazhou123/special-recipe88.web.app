Steps to make Chocolate Cookie:
  1. Cream butter and sugar together.
  2. Add Venila essence, beaten egg, baking soda and mix properly.
  3. Add refine flour and coco power in the mixer together and make a dough.
  4. Now add the flakes in the dough and mix together.
  5. Now, make small small balls and put in tray.
  6. Bake at pre heated oven at 180°C for 20 min.
