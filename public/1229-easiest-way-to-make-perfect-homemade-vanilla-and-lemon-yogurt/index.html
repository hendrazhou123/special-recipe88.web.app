The ingredients needed to make Homemade Vanilla and Lemon Yogurt:
  1. Make ready 1/2 gallon Liquide milk
  2. Get 1/2 lb Powdered milk
  3. Get 2 cup Sugar
  4. Take 2 Yogurt yoplait (vanilla)
  5. Get 1 Lemon
  6. Take 1 tsp Pure vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 ml Ground nutmeg

Instructions to make Homemade Vanilla and Lemon Yogurt:
  1. Put a pot on the stove and add the milk. For about 10-15 minutes for the milk to boil
  2. Remove the milk from the stove and pour it in the container with the powdered milk and mix well then add the 2 yoplait yogurts.
  3. Close your container and wrap it with a clean sheet and keep it in a warm place. I usually put mine in one of my kitchen drawer. Wait for about 18- 24 hours
  4. After 22 hours you should have get a fermented milk. Add the sugar, the pure vanilla extract, one lemon and the ground nutmeg. Mix very well and filter the mixture with a sieve. Use a sieve with some very small hole
  5. Here is how much yogurt I was able to get. You can keep it in the fridge or the freezer. You can keep it for 2 weeks.
