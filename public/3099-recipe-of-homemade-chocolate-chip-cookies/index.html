The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup softened butter
  2. Get 1/4 cup white sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Prepare 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/2 cup oatmeal
  10. Prepare 1 (6 oz) package chocolate chips

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Mix first five ingredients with mixer.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 F and grease a cookie sheet with butter.
  3. Add flour, baking soda, salt and oatmeal to your mixture, then chocolate chips. Mix with spoon.
  4. Use a cookie scoop and your hands to put large teaspoonfuls onto baking sheet.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 10-12 minutes until lightly browned. Cool on cookie rack. When cooled, store in tightly covered container or cookie jar.
  6. Pour a glass of milk and eat at least three.
  7. Bake in a happy kitchen.
