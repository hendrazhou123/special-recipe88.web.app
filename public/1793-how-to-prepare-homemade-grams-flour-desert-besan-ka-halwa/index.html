The ingredients needed to make Grams Flour Desert ( Besan ka halwa):
  1. Get 1 cup Gram Flour (Besan)
  2. Take 1 cup milk
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Make ready 70 grams Melted butter
  5. Prepare 4 piece Small cardamom
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp Pistachios
