The ingredients needed to make Spicy Peanut Butter Slaw:
  1. Take dressing:
  2. Get 1/2 reduced-fat (2%) Greek yogurt
  3. Get 1/4 c creamy Peanut butter
  4. Take 1/4 c rice-wine vinegar
  5. Get 2 tsp hot chili sauce
  6. Make ready 2 tsp dark sesame oil
  7. Get 1 tsp honey
  8. Make ready to taste Kosher salt
  9. Prepare slaw:
  10. Prepare 6 c shredded green cabbage
  11. Take 1 c matchstick carrots
  12. Prepare 1/2 c thinly sliced radishes
  13. Get 1/2 c finely chopped scallions (white & green parts)
  14. Make ready 1/2 c fresh cilantro leaves
  15. Take 1/2 c golden raisins, chopped
