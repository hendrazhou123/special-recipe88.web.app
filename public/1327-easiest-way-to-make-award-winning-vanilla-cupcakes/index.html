The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Cupcakes:
  1. Get 1 cup Sugar
  2. Take 2 Eggs
  3. Make ready 1 cup Flour
  4. Make ready 100 grams Butter
  5. Take 1/2 tsp Vanilla
  6. Get 1 tsp Baking Powder

Instructions to make Vanilla Cupcakes:
  1. Preheat oven at 180°C.
  2. Line 24 cupcake tins with cupcake holders.
  3. Cream butter and sugar together.
  4. Add in eggs.
  5. Mix well and add flour.
  6. Mix thoroughly and add vanilla and baking powder.
  7. Pour batter in the tins and bake for 10-15 minutes.
