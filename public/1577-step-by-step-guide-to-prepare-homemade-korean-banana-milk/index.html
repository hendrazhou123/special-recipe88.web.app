The ingredients needed to make Korean Banana Milk:
  1. Prepare 1 ripe banana, sliced or broken into pieces
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup regular milk
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup water
  4. Make ready 1 Tbsp maple syrup
  5. Get 1 small drop of pure vanilla extract
