The ingredients needed to make Cakey cinnamon rolls:
  1. Get 420 g all purpose flour
  2. Take 6 tablespoons white/ brown sugar
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 1.5 tbsp yeast
  5. Take 150 ml buttermilk
  6. Prepare 1 tablespoon cinnamon
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  8. Make ready 7 tbsp butter/margarine
  9. Prepare Cinnamon filling
  10. Get 3 tbsp butter
  11. Prepare 3 tbsp brown sugar
  12. Take 1 tbsp cinnamon
  13. Take 1/4 tsp cocoa
  14. Take Glazing
  15. Make ready 100 g icing sugar
  16. Get 4 tbsp milk

Steps to make Cakey cinnamon rolls:
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl and knead. You may use a spatula as the dough will be runny
  2. Cover the dough with cling wrap and let it rest for at least an hour… After rising you may place it in the fridge overnight to make the dough firm
  3. As the dough is rising, make the cinnamon filling. Melt the butter and mix it with the sugar and cinnamon and set aside
  4. Take your risen dough and knead for a few seconds, roll it out to about a quarter inch thickness
  5. Spread the filling evenly through the rolled out dough and roll it tightly from one end to the other. It's much easier to use the filling in this liquid form. It spreads better and tastes better
  6. Use a string or knife to cut the dough into sections
  7. Butter and dust your baking pan and make sure your oven is preheated to 180 degrees
  8. Place the cinnamon rolls evenly on your baking tray/pan. If you want to use an egg wash for an attractive color, mix one egg with water and brush the mixture over the cinnamon rolls
  9. Place your cinnamon rolls in the oven and bake for 30-35 minutes
  10. Mix the icing sugar and milk and pour it over your cooked cinnamon rolls. Enjoy
