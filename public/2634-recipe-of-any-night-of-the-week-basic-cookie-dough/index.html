The ingredients needed to make Basic Cookie Dough:
  1. Make ready 1 cup butter flavor crisco
  2. Prepare 3/4 cup brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup white sugar
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 2 eggs, beaten
  6. Prepare 2 cup all-purpose flour
  7. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1 cup Milk Chocolate chocolate chips
