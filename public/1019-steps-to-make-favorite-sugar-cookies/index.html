The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookies !:
  1. Take 3 eggs
  2. Prepare 3 1/2 cup unbleached flour
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 2 tbsp baking powder
  5. Get 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  6. Get 2 stick softened butter
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
Instructions to make Sugar Cookies !:
  1. preheat oven to 350
  2. stir flour, salt, & baking soda.
  3. in another bowl stir butter and sugar then start adding eggs in one by one stirring after each.
  4. mix in dry mixture until thick and in cookie dough form
  5. spray spoon with non stick cooking spray and plop cookies on a greased pan.
  6. bake at 350 for about 10-13 minutes. mine are usually done in 11 minutes.
  7. I like to add colored sugar sprinkles to them before baking. you can also use m&m's.
  8. make's about 30-45 cookies
  9. more creative idea's… add a few drop's of food coloring to cookie dough for colored cookies!
