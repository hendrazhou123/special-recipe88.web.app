Instructions to make Taco stuffed biscuits with Taco Butter:
  1. Preheat oven to 350*,Brown hamburger meat, drain fat.,add taco seasoning and cook
  2. Use hand and smash biscuits add about 1/2tsp of meat and add cheese. Fold to close the biscuit.
  3. Melt butter and add about 1/2 tsp of taco seasoning, brush over stuffed biscuits.
  4. Bake at 350* for 8-12 mins or biscuits are golden brown
