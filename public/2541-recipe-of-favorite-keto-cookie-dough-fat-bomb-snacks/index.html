The ingredients needed to make Keto Cookie Dough Fat Bomb Snacks:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup butter (melted)
  2. Prepare 1/3 cup powdered erythritol (or more to taste)
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp full flavor molasses
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup almond flour
  6. Take 1/2 cup vanilla flavored collagen or keto shake powder
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp sea salt
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup Lilly's semisweet chocolate chips

Steps to make Keto Cookie Dough Fat Bomb Snacks:
  1. Using blender and mix butter, erythritol, vanilla, and molasses in a large bowl until smooth and somewhat fluffy
  2. In another bowl, mix dry ingredients well except for chocolate chips
  3. Add dry ingredients slowly to butter mixture beating on medium speed
  4. Dough should form into clumps once well mixed. Test for sweetness then add chocolate chips
  5. Refrigerate for twenty minutes then divide into 1TBS balls and refrigerate for an hour or freeze into tubs for later
