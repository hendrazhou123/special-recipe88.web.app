The ingredients needed to make Mixed berries, Almond Milk and Oats Breakfast Smoothie:
  1. Make ready 250 grams frozen fruits
  2. Make ready 250 ml unsweetened almond milk
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp honey
  4. Take 2 tbsp oats

Steps to make Mixed berries, Almond Milk and Oats Breakfast Smoothie:

  2. Change the amount of almond milk according to your desired consistency. Start with adding about 200ml and add more as you blend.
