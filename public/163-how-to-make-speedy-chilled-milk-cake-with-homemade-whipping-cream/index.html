  1. Get 1 ) 100g Melted Butter

  4. Take 4 )Baking Powder 1 teaspoon (leveled)

  6. Make ready 6 )Cream 300mls
  7. Prepare 7 )Sugar (for topping) 1/4 cup
  8. Make ready 8 )Chilled Water As per required
  9. Get 9 )Sprinkles For decoration

Steps to make Chilled Milk Cake with Homemade Whipping Cream:
  1. Add melted butter in a mixing bowl… blend well…
  2. When done… add 3 eggs and blend well…
  3. After properly blending… whisk white flour and baking powder… and now mix these dry ingredients with spatula…
  4. Pour the batter in a greased baking tray and bake in a preheated oven for 40-45 mins or until done…
  5. When baked… pour condensed milk over the cake and let it cool for 30 mins…
  6. For preparing topping… add chilled water in a big bowl… then add 300ml cream in small bow… then put this small bowl in chilled water bowl and continue to blend until doubles…
  7. At that time add sugar… and continue blending until fluffy… homemade whipping cream is ready to use…
  8. In the last, add decorations of ur own style… if u don't have pipping gun just use simple plastic bag… add whipping cream in the bag and insert simple nozzle…
