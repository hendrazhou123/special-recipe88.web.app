The ingredients needed to make Matcha red bean braided bread:
  1. Make ready 100 g red bean and 80g sweetener (for red bean paste)
  2. Get 250 g bread flour
  3. Make ready 300 ml warm water (can try from 280g then slowly add to prevent watery dough)
  4. Take 5 g dried yeast
  5. Get 20 g oil
  6. Make ready 20 g sweetener
  7. Get 3/4 tsp salt
  8. Get 1 tbsp matcha powder

Instructions to make Matcha red bean braided bread:
  1. Boil red bean, throw boiling water away as soon as water boil
  2. Add cold water and keep simmering until soft
  3. Add sweetener and keep simmering until water is almost gone
  4. Blend all of the dough ingredients except matcha
  5. Knead well (put some flour on table surface to prevent stick)
  6. Divide dough into two and add matcha powder into one of it
  7. Wait for it to ferment until double its size (around an hour)
  8. Divide both of the dough into two pieces (get 2 white 2 green dough) and roll out
  9. Place one white dough at the bottom, then red bean, then one green dough, then red bean, then one white dough, then one green dough
  10. Cut dough into 6-8 pieces and slash two lines then braid the dough
  11. Ferment again until almost double its size
  12. Bake at 180c or 350F for 20mins
