Instructions to make Chicken Teriyaki Noodle Stir-Fry:
  1. Season chicken cubes with salt and pepper. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet or Wok on medium high heat. Add 1 Tablespoon oil add chicken and cook til chicken is no longer pink. Remove and set aside.
  3. Cook Yokisoba Noodles according to package directions and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet or wok u cooked the chicken in add all the vegetables and rest of olive oil. Cook 3-4 minutes stirring frequently.
