The ingredients needed to make Brown sugar bourbon ham:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 deadwood bourbon
  2. Take 1 cup brown sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup southwest chipotle spice
  4. Take 2 TBSP cumin
  5. Prepare Salt (2 big pinches worth)
  6. Prepare black pepper (1 big pinch worth)
  7. Take 1/2 a large white onion (microplan)
  8. Prepare 1 club of garlic (microplan)
  9. Get 1/2 apple cider vinegar
  10. Take 1 cup maple syrup

Steps to make Brown sugar bourbon ham:
  1. Cut off extra fat from the ham and place it in slow cooker, flat side down
  2. Add all the spices, brown sugar bourbon and syrup
  3. Microplan vegetables over the ham
  4. Cover and set for 5 hours on low
  5. Check every once in a while to pour liquid over the top to keep the top from drying.
