The ingredients needed to make Penne pasta filled with milk,:
  1. Take 2 tbsp olive oil
  2. Prepare onion, sliced
  3. Prepare 200 ml skim milk
  4. Make ready 2 pinch curry powder
  5. Make ready leeks, frozen
  6. Prepare carrots, frozen
  7. Prepare 200 ml water
  8. Take 3 pinch ground cumin
  9. Get 1 tsp salt
  10. Take 1 cube,
  11. Make ready 150 grams penne, cooked

Steps to make Penne pasta filled with milk,:
  1. (435 calories a serving) Firstly, you need to prepare the pan with 2 tablespoons of olive oil, after the oil is ready you put onions and you wait until they become toasted.
  2. Then you add milk, a pinch of powder, leeks, carrots and water.
  3. Stir, close the pan and wait 3 minutes.
  4. After, you add cumin, salt, and one dice of cube.
  5. Stir and wait 10 minutes.
  6. And finally add cooked penne, and just wait a little and it's ready. Good luck! It's yummy really. All this is for one serving for 435 calories.
