The ingredients needed to make Super Yummy Vanilla French Toast:
  1. Make ready 8 slice bread, preferably a nice thick kind, like potato or challah bread
  2. Get 2 slightly beaten eggs
  3. Make ready 4 tbsp sugar
  4. Get 1 1/2 tbsp pure vanilla extract (not imitation)
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  6. Make ready 1/8 tsp salt
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup milk
  8. Get 1/4 cup cream
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp butter, for skillet

Steps to make Super Yummy Vanilla French Toast:
  1. Beat egg in shallow bowl, then add milk, cream,sugar, salt, vanilla, and cinnamon. mix well with a fork
  2. Melt just enough butter over medium heat, to coat skillet
  3. Dip each slice of bread in egg mixture. coat well but drain off extra egg so its not soggy
  4. Add bread to skillet and cook each side until golden brown.
  5. Serve with syrup or powdered sugar…and if you're like us, lotsa butter! mmmm
