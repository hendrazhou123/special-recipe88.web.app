The ingredients needed to make Seafood a la King:
  1. Prepare 3 tbls butter
  2. Take 1 cup chopped onion
  3. Get 3 tbls flour
  4. Prepare 2 cups milk
  5. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Make ready 1/8 tsp nutmeg
  7. Take 12 oz tilapia cut into 2 in pcs
  8. Prepare 6 oz cooked shrimp
  9. Make ready 4 oz imitation crab meat
  10. Make ready 1 cup frozen peas

Steps to make Seafood a la King:
  1. Melt butter in large skillet
  2. Add onions and cook until soft
  3. Add flour and cook 2 - 3 minutes
  4. Add milk slowly whisking until blended
  5. Add seasonings and cook for 2 minutes
  6. Add tilapia and cover with sauce and cook for 5 minutes
  7. Add shrimp, crab and peas and cook until heated through.
