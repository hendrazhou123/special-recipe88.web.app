Instructions to make Hacked 'Sugar' Cookies (Sugar/Gluten Free/Low Cal):
  1. Pre heat oven to 375. Use a tablespoon to pluck down each cookie, spread them out about an inch and half so apart. Press them down a little as well. However be gentle, they will be very light and will indeed swell a bit when baked.
  2. Bake for about 15 minutes (cookies should generally start to get some browning on top not just edges.)
  3. Open the oven and turn it off, let the oven cool off with the cookies inside. Leave them like this for about 10 minutes.
  4. Pull them out and let them cool. Coconut flour is finicky about drying out, this extra drying step should allow them to solidify a bit more (and weirdly increase density).
