Instructions to make Cheese Stuffed Meatballs:
  1. Line the fryer basket with grill mat or a lightly greased aluminum foil.
  2. Mix all the ingredients together, except mozzarella cheese. Scoop about 2 Tablespoon of the meat mixture and wrap one pieces of the cheese in the middle to form a ball. Place the meatballs inside the air fryer.
  3. Spray the meatballs with some oil. Air fry at 380F (190C) for about 6 minutes. Flip, spray some oil again, and air fry at 380F (190C) for another 4-5 minutes.
  4. Take a pot to heat the spaghetti sauce. When the meatballs are done, simmer the meatballs in the sauce for a few minutes.
  5. Serve the meatballs and sauce with your favorite pasta.
