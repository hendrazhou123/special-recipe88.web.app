The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake:
  1. Get Batter
  2. Prepare 3 cups grated carrots
  3. Make ready 2 cups sugar
  4. Take 1 1/2 cups Mazola oil
  5. Get 4 eggs (unbeaten)
  6. Prepare 3 tsp cinnamon
  7. Prepare 3 cups sifted flour
  8. Get 3 tsp baking powder
  9. Take 2 tsp baking soda
  10. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Take 1 cup walnuts chopped
  12. Prepare Frosting
  13. Take 1 Tbs butter
  14. Make ready 1 large package Philadelphia cream cheese
  15. Get 1 cup powdered sugar
  16. Take 1 cup chopped walnuts for garnish
  17. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla

Instructions to make Carrot Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Grate carrots. Squeeze out all liquid using a cheesecloth or clean dish towel.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, mix carrots, sugar, and oil. Next, beat in eggs. Lastly, mix in dry ingredients.
  3. Bake for 50 minutes - 1 hour at 350 degrees.
  4. Frosting: Mix softened butter, vanilla, and cream cheese. Gradually add powdered sugar. Frost cake. Add nuts on top.
