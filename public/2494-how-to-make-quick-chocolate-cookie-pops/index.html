Steps to make Chocolate cookie pops:
  1. Line a baking tray with parchment paper.
  2. Chop the chocolates.
  3. Melt in a double boiler.
  4. Open the chocolate flavored cookies apart. Dip the stick in melted chocolate and place it in the center of a cookie.then close it with other half again to seal it.
  5. Repeat the process for all the batch you hv planned to make. And let it set for 10- 15 mins. So the chocolate within hardens.
  6. Meanwhile separate the white cream from few of the cookies.
  7. Take a piping nozzle and using it's bottom and top cut the cream part to make out circles.
  8. As shown in the picture I took a rough idea for mouth and eyes on the cookie. Keep all the cutouts aside.
  9. Next, add some cocoa powder and black food gel colour to the left over white cream.knead it until well combined.
  10. We will roll it real thin to make very tiny balls as eyes. Keep aside.
  11. By the time your cookies with sticks are ready to be dipped in chocolate. So heat up the chocolate little bit. Dip the cookies in it and tap off the excess chocolate.place them on lined baking tray.
  12. While the chocolate is still wet, place the cream cutouts and attach the heart shaped sprinkles as shown in the final picture using a drop of melted chocolate. Do the same for eye balls too. Repeat the process for whole batch. Set aside to firm up.
  13. Your chocolate dipped cookie pops are ready to serve. Believe me it's a tough job to keep kids away from them any more.
