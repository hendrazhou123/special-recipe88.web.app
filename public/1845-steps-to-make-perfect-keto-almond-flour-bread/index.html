The ingredients needed to make Keto Almond Flour Bread:
  1. Make ready 3/4 cup almond flour
  2. Get 1/2 cup flax meal
  3. Take 3 tbsp psyllium husk powder
  4. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Take Pinch salt
  6. Get 2 Eggs
  7. Take 2 tsp apple cider vinegar
  8. Take 1 cup boiling water

Instructions to make Keto Almond Flour Bread:
  1. Combine dry ingredients. Mix well.
  2. Add eggs, vinegar and water. Mix.
  3. Place in baking dish. Shape as desired- bread will retain shape.
  4. Bake at 400 degrees for 50 minutes.
