The ingredients needed to make #cakebakingwithfruits blueberry cake:
  1. Take 3/4 cup all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup margarine/butter
  4. Prepare 3/4 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 1 egg beaten
  6. Prepare 1/4 cup milk
  7. Take 1/4 cup blueberries
Instructions to make #cakebakingwithfruits blueberry cake:
  1. In a bowl beat the sugar & butter till light & fluffy
  2. Add the egg. Beat again
  3. Now add the flour in batches followed by milk
  4. Mix well.
  5. Add a few berries.
  6. Transfer in a baking dish or muffin tray
  7. Bake in a preheated oven @160°c for 30-40 mins
