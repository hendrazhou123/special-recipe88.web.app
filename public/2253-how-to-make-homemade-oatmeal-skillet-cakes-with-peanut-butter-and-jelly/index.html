The ingredients needed to make Oatmeal Skillet Cakes with Peanut Butter and Jelly:
  1. Prepare 1/4 cup unbleached all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1/4 cup quick cooking oats
  3. Make ready 1 tsp brown sugar
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 1/8 tsp baking soda
  6. Take 1/8 tsp salt
  7. Take 1 egg white
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup "milk" of choice
  9. Prepare Peanut Butter and Jelly

Instructions to make Oatmeal Skillet Cakes with Peanut Butter and Jelly:
  1. Stir together all dry ingredients.
  2. Add egg white and stir with fork until mixture pulls together.
  3. Add 1/4 cup "milk" of choice and mix well without over mixing. This is a hearty/dense cake and too much mixing will make it tough.
  4. Heat lightly oiled skillet on low-medium heat for a couple minutes. This allows time for the oats to soften up. Pour mixture into pan all at once and do not adjust or attempt to spread evenly. It'll do its thing.
  5. Wait to flip until bubbly throughout.
  6. Cook an additional minute or two on other side.
  7. Top with peanut butter and jelly and enjoy.
