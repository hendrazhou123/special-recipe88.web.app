Instructions to make Hot chocolate popcorn:
  1. Make popcorn, Spread out on cookie sheet
  2. Add marshmallows
  3. Melt chocolate chips in the microwave use instructions on the bag.
  4. Drizzle melted chocolate over popcorn and marshmallows add sprinkles.
  5. Notes- can put in refrigerator for 5 mins or so it sets faster.
