The ingredients needed to make Peanut butter cookies with dried plums:
  1. Prepare 2 sticks unsalted butter, room temperature
  2. Make ready 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup light brown sugar
  4. Prepare 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Get 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  7. Take 2 cups Unbleached all purpose flour
  8. Prepare 1 teaspoon baking soda
  9. Take 1 teaspoon kosher aalt
  10. Take 1 cup dried plums it cranberries, chopped
  11. Take 1/2 cup peanuts, copped

Steps to make Peanut butter cookies with dried plums:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 f. With mixer beat butter until smooth, add peanut butter and both sugars beat until light 2 minutes.
  2. Add eggs and vanilla beat until combined
  3. Whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt. Add this mixture to peanut butter mixture.
  4. Chop dried cherries or plumbs and peanuts, add to the mixture. Stir.
  5. Scoop heaping table spoon balls onto baking sheets. Leave 3 inches apart.
  6. Cook about 18-20 mins. Until edges get colors. Be careful, Cookies still hot and can break easily, let them cool. You will get 5 dozen of cookies. I used half of the ingredient made up 16 cookies
