The ingredients needed to make Lu's vanilla cake:
  1. Prepare 1/4 lb butter, softened
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 2 eggs
  4. Get 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Prepare 1 1/3 cup flour
  6. Get 1/4 tsp scant - salt
  7. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/3 cup milk

Instructions to make Lu's vanilla cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°.
  2. In medium bowl, blend butter and sugar until light and fluffy.
  3. Add eggs, one at a time. Then add vanilla.
  4. In small bowl, mix flour, salt, and baking powder with a fork.
  5. Slowly add flour mixture to butter mixture.
  6. Slowly add milk, blending until just combined.
  7. Grease and flour 9" cake pan.
  8. Pour batter into pan. Bake 23- 27 minutes, or until cake pulls away slightly from edge of pan and toothpick inserted near center comes out clean.
