The ingredients needed to make Brazilian Strawberry Pie:
  1. Take Dough
  2. Take 1 cup flour
  3. Get 1 egg yolk (beaten)
  4. Prepare 100 g margarine
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp baking powder
  7. Take Filling
  8. Make ready 700 ml milk (I used almond milk)
  9. Take 1 can condensed milk
  10. Take 4 tbsp cornstarch
  11. Prepare 2 egg yolks (beaten)
  12. Make ready Toppings
  13. Make ready Strawberries (cut in half)
  14. Take Strawberry Jello (use it when still soft)

Instructions to make Brazilian Strawberry Pie:
  1. Mix all ingredients to make the dough and place it in the pie dish
  2. Make holes in the bottom of the dough with a fork and bake it at 400F for 15-20 min
  3. Make the pie filling by combining the milk, condensed milk, and eggs in a saucepan. Mix it constantly at medium high heat
  4. Once it starts boiling, add the cornstarch (dissolved in milk) and keep mixing it until thick
  5. Add the cream filling into the pie dough using a strainer
  6. Add the strawberries on top of the cream, then the jello
  7. Refrigerate for a few hours, and enjoy!
