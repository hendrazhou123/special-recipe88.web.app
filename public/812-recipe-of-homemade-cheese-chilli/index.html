Instructions to make Cheese Chilli:
  1. Take a big bowl add cornflour, salt, black pepper powder and mix all spices and heat oil in a pan and then dip cheese cubes in batter and deep fry.
  2. And then take another side take a wok add oil and heat it add ginger and garlic paste and then add chopped capsicum, onion and cooked it for 2 mins.
  3. Now add cheese cubes, soya sauce, tomato sauce, salt, red chilli powder and blk pepper powder and mix it well.
  4. Now take a bowl add water and corn flour mix it and pour in wok and cook it 2-3 min and cheese chilli is ready and serve it. Garnish with coriander leaves.
