The ingredients needed to make Nana's Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Get 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 2 Tbl milk
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  4. Make ready 2/3 cup peanut butter (creamy not crunchy)
  5. Get 2/3 cup unsalted butter (melted)
  6. Take 1 tsp salt (or alternatively use salted butter)
  7. Prepare 2 eggs
  8. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  9. Make ready 1 cup sugar

Instructions to make Nana's Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Cream together the butter and both sugars.
  3. Add the eggs and peanut butter
  4. Add the flour, salt, and baking soda
  5. Add the milk
  6. Once mixed well, pick up dough with hands and shape into a ball. Then, flatten with the palms of your hands. Place cookie on greased cookie sheet.
  7. Bake for 15 minutes (or longer, depending on your oven)
