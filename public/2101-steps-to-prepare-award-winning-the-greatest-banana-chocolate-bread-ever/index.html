The ingredients needed to make The greatest Banana chocolate bread ever:
  1. Get 1.5 cups self raising flour or 280gr
  2. Prepare Pinch sea salt
  3. Prepare 1 cup dark brown sugar or 150grms
  4. Get 80 grms yoghurt
  5. Prepare 150 grms butter soft
  6. Take 2 eggs
  7. Make ready 4 mashed bananas
  8. Get 1 cup Mixed nuts and choc chips
