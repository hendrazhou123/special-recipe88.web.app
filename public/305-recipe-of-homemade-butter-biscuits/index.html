The ingredients needed to make BUTTER BISCUITS::
  1. Prepare 100 g Butter (at room temperature)
  2. Get 150 g icing sugar (grind normal sugar)
  3. Make ready 1 egg
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 6 Tbsp dry milk powder
  6. Get 200 g plain flour
  7. Make ready as needed Melted chocolate
  8. Take as needed Some sprinkles
