The ingredients needed to make Mango bread:
  1. Get 0.25 cup milk
  2. Prepare 1 cup wheat flour
  3. Get 1 cup maida or all purpose flour
  4. Get Optional almond flour + Ragi flour
  5. Prepare 1 mango puree
  6. Prepare 1 banana mashed
  7. Prepare 0.5 cup jaggery
  8. Get Saffron rinsed in milk
  9. Take Raisins and cashews
  10. Get Cinnamon powder
  11. Prepare Cardamom crushed
  12. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  13. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  14. Take 0.5 tsp baking soda
  15. Get Pinch salt

Steps to make Mango bread:
  1. Wet ingredients: milk + mango purée + banana mashed + saffron milk mix well
  2. Now sieve dry ingredients to it. Add flour (all specified flours)+ jaggery + raisins + cashews + crushed cinnamon and cardamom. Add baking soda + baking powder + pinch salt.
  3. Now mix the batter and pour it in a cake mould
  4. Preheat the oven to 350 deg F in the beginning and place the mould and bake for 1 hour 10 minutes. Serve it once cooled.
