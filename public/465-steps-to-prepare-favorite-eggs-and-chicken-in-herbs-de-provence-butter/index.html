The ingredients needed to make Eggs and Chicken in Herbs De Provence Butter:
  1. Prepare Herbs De Provence butter
  2. Make ready 1 tsp Herbs De Provence
  3. Take 1/3 cup butter
  4. Take chicken
  5. Prepare 7 cup cubed chicken breast
  6. Take 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Make ready eggs
  8. Take 6 large eggs
  9. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking powder, double acting
  10. Prepare cheese
  11. Take 2 cup cheddar cheese
  12. Prepare 1/4 cup grated parmesan cheese

Steps to make Eggs and Chicken in Herbs De Provence Butter:
  1. Preheat oven 390° Fahrenheit.
  2. Cube the chicken.
  3. In an oven safe and stove top safe dish, Heat the butter and add Herbs De Provence. Add salt to chicken. Add chicken to butter. Sauté till pinkness in chicken is gone. Remove from heat.
  4. Mix the eggs and baking powder very good.
  5. Break the cheese into small chunks add half to the top of chicken.
  6. The dish should still be hot. With chicken and cheese. Pour the egg mixture on top. Add rest of cheddar cheese and parmesan cheese.
  7. Put the dish into oven. The egg mixture will cook from both sides.
  8. Bake in oven 10-15 minutes make sure eggs are done.
  9. serve I hope you enjoy!
