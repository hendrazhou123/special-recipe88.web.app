The ingredients needed to make Cream cheese pasta with pine nuts and cranberries:
  1. Prepare 1 pound (454 g) penne pasta
  2. Get 1 ounce (226 g) cream cheese
  3. Take 2 TBSP butter
  4. Take 3/4 cup water
  5. Take 1 and 1/2 cups (120g)Parmesan cheese
  6. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  7. Take Pepper
  8. Make ready 30 basil leaves, chopped
  9. Prepare 1 TBSP dry parsley
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup pine nuts
  11. Prepare 6 TBSP dry cranberries
  12. Get 4 clove garlic, minced
  13. Get Truffle salt
