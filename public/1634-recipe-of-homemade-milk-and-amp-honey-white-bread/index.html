The ingredients needed to make Milk & Honey White Bread…:
  1. Take 2 pks. active dry yeast
  2. Get 2 1/2 cup warm milk -110° -115°
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup honey
  4. Prepare 1/4 cup melted butter
  5. Get 2 tsp salt
  6. Get 8 cup to 8 and 1/2 flour

Instructions to make Milk & Honey White Bread…:
  1. In large bowl dissolve yeast in warm mlk. ( I used my kitchen - aid mixer )
  2. Add honey, butter, salt. 5 cup of flour. Beat until smooth. Add enough flour to form soft dough.
  3. Turn onto floured board. Knead 6-8 min. Placed in a greased bowl. Turning to coat top. Let rise 1 hour. Or until doubled. I check it . If you push on it a little and a dipple stays in . It is ready.
  4. Pull dough out of your bowl. Shape into loaves. This makes 2 loaves. Grease bread pans. I sprayed mine.
  5. Let your loaves rise again. Till they are doubled. I brushed mine with an egg. And sprinkled sesame seeds on top.
  6. Bake at 375° . Till they are golden brown . If they start to get to brown. Lay a piece of foil over them. Bake 25-30min.
