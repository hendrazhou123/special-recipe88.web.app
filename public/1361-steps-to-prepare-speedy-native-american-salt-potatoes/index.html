The ingredients needed to make Native American Salt Potatoes:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup salt natural large grain
  2. Make ready 1 pound red potatoes skin on
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup melted butter
  4. Prepare 2 quarts water
  5. Take To taste fresh ground black pepper

Instructions to make Native American Salt Potatoes:
  1. Add salt to the pot I used Pink Himalayan Salt.
  2. Boil the water with the salt
  3. Wash the potatoes. Rough chop the potatoes and add to the pot.
  4. Boil till fork tender. Drain and add back to the pot and pour the butter over the potatoes.
  5. Serve with fresh cracked black pepper. I hope you enjoy!!!
