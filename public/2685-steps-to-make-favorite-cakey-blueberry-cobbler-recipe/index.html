The ingredients needed to make Cakey Blueberry Cobbler Recipe:
  1. Get 1 stick (1/2 cup) butter- melted
  2. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Take 1 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Get 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  7. Prepare 3/4 cup milk
  8. Make ready 2 cups blueberries, works well with fresh or frozen

Instructions to make Cakey Blueberry Cobbler Recipe:
  1. Pre-heat the oven to 350 ° F
  2. Pour melted butter in an 8 x8 baking pan
  3. Sift the dry ingredients into a bowl.
  4. By hand mix together flour, baking powder, sugar, salt, vanilla, and milk to make a batter.
  5. Pour the batter over the hot melted butter. Do not stir. Arrange blueberries evenly over the top and lightly press into the batter.
  6. Bake at 350 ° for about 25- 30 minutes (Should be golden brown and delicious GBD)
  7. Sprinkle with powder sugar and add ice cream or whipped cream if you like:)
