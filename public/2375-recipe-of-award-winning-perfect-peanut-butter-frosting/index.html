The ingredients needed to make Perfect Peanut Butter Frosting:
  1. Take 1 Stick butter (1/3cup) at room temp
  2. Take 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  3. Take 2 cups powdered sugar
  4. Make ready 1 teaspoon vanilla
  5. Get 3 tablespoons whole milk

The base of the recipe is just a little bit of butter and a massive amount of peanut butter. Steps to make Perfect Peanut Butter Frosting:
  1. Add milk & vanilla to a medium size bowl. Add powdered sugar, then top with butter & peanut butter
  2. Using an electric mixer, blend on high speed about 2 minutes or until all lumps are gone & creamy consistency is accomplished
  3. Use for cakes or any dessert! It makes enough for a 9×13 size cake with a little left over!! Enjoy!!
