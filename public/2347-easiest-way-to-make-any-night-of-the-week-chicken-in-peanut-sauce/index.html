The ingredients needed to make Chicken in peanut sauce:
  1. Take Handful peanuts
  2. Get I table spoon of crushed ginger
  3. Get 1/2 cup milk
  4. Get 1 onion
  5. Prepare 1 lemon
  6. Make ready 1 tea spoon of honey
  7. Prepare 1 bunch dhania
  8. Make ready 1/4 kg diced chicken
  9. Take Salt
  10. Take Oil
  11. Take Black pepper

Steps to make Chicken in peanut sauce:
  1. Saute the peanuts with the ginger,black pepper and onion in a pan
  2. Remove and place in a blender and add peanut butter,honey,lemon juice and milk.
  3. Blend until its saucy.
  4. Season the chicken with royco and gharam masala and lightly fry in a pan
  5. Add the sauce over the chicken and let it simmer for 10 minutes.Then add dhania.
  6. Serve with carbohydrate of your choice.
