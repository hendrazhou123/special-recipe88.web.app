The ingredients needed to make classic vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup softened butter
  2. Make ready 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Prepare 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 cup flour
  6. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  8. Get 2/3 cup milk
