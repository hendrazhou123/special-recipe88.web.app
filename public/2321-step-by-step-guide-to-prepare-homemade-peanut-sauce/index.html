The ingredients needed to make Peanut sauce:
  1. Make ready 150 gms unsalted fried peanuts
  2. Make ready 4 tsp Olive oil
  3. Take 1 cup Coconut milk
  4. Prepare 1 tsp Butter
  5. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Prepare 1 Green chilli
  7. Make ready Garlic cloves 2
  8. Take Soya sauce1tsp
  9. Get 1 tbsp Lemon juice
