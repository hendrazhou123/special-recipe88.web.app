Instructions to make Ultra Smooth Milk and Dark Chocolate Bark:
  1. Have chocolate in seperate Microwave safe bowls. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Melt the milk chocolate first until smooth
  3. Pour onto parchment t paper and smooth out with an off set spatula to about 1/4 inch thick. Allow to set before melting dark chocolate
  4. Melt dark chocolate until smooth
  5. Pour over set milk chocolate and smooth over set milk chocolate quickly with off set spatula. Let set then refrigerate until hard
  6. Break onto pieces. Store in rerfigerator or freeze
