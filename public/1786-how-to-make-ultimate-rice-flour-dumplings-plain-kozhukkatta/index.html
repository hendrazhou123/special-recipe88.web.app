The ingredients needed to make Rice Flour Dumplings (Plain Kozhukkatta):
  1. Take 1/2 Cup Coconut
  2. Make ready 2 Cup Rice Flour
  3. Make ready 2 Cups Hot Water
  4. Get Salt (to taste)

Steps to make Rice Flour Dumplings (Plain Kozhukkatta):
  1. Add some salt & boil the water. Add approximately two cups of water.
  2. Once the water is boiled, add the rice flour to it.
  3. If you think the water isn't enough, add a little bit of water. If it's more wait a while till the water evaporates. Mix well.
  4. Add the grated coconut & stir. Make sure the dough is soft & smooth. Turn off the stove.
  5. Once it is cooled, knead the dough.
  6. Make oval shaped balls (or any other shape you prefer) with the dough.
  7. Heat some water. Add a little salt. Remember, you've already added it in the dough. So check & add. Put the dough in the boiling water & cook for 15-20 minutes. Serve hot.
