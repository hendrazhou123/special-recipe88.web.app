Instructions to make Roti bread (Buttery Indian flat bread):
  1. Mix flour salt and melted butter with a fork until crumbly.
  2. Add in water and kneed on a lightly floured cutting board or counter.
  3. Separate into 4-6 balls depending on size you want. And roll out very thin. Heat a skillet in medium high. I prefer cast iron. Add 1 teaspoon butter in pan and And place roti bread in pan. Fry 30 seconds to a minute per side adding butter to pan when u flip it to uncooked side.
