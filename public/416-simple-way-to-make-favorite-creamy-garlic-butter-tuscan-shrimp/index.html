The ingredients needed to make Creamy Garlic Butter Tuscan Shrimp:
  1. Prepare 2 tablespoons salted butter
  2. Prepare 6 cloves garlic finely diced
  3. Prepare 1 pound raw shrimp
  4. Get 1 sweet or yellow onion diced
  5. Make ready 12 ounce jar roasted red peppers
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup white wine
  7. Get 1 3/4 cup whole milk
  8. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  9. Take 3 cups baby spinach leaves
  10. Prepare 3/4 cup shredded Parmesan
  11. Make ready 2 teaspoon dried Italian herbs
  12. Get 1 tablespoon fresh parsley chopped
  13. Get 1 teaspoon cornstarch with 1 teaspoon water (optional)

Steps to make Creamy Garlic Butter Tuscan Shrimp:
  1. Heat in large skillet over medium heat. Melt butter and add in the garlic. Fry about 1 minute. Add in the shrimp and fry about 2 minutes until pink. Transfer to bowl and set aside.
  2. Fry onion in remaining butter and wine and reduce slightly. Add roasted peppers with juice and fry about 2 minutes to release flavors.
  3. Reduce heat to low-medium. Add milk and bring to simmer while stirring. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Add spinach and allow to wilt in sauce and add Parmesan cheese. Allow sauce to melt cheese. Add in cornstarch and water mixture until sauce thickens.
  5. Add shrimp mixture back in and add herbs and parsley…. serve over pasta, rice, veggies etc.
