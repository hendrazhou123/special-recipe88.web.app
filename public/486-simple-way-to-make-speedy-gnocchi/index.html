The ingredients needed to make Gnocchi:
  1. Prepare 4 Lg potatoes
  2. Take 1 1/2 cups flour
  3. Take 1 Egg
  4. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 tsp pepper
  6. Prepare Keep extra flour for dusting. And salt for the water
  7. Prepare Couple tbsps of margarine for fry pan
