Steps to make 卤鸭面线 Braised Duck Flour Vermicelli:

  2. 热锅，加入油。放入蒜头，姜片，把鸭腿煎香。Heat a pot, add in oil. Add garlic & ginger slices, and fry slightly the duck drumstick
