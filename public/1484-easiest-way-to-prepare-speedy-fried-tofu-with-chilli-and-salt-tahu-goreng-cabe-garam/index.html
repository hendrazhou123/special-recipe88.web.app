  2. Prepare 2 clove garlic, coarsely chopped

  6. Take 1 pinch pepper

Steps to make fried tofu with chilli and salt (tahu goreng cabe garam):
  1. cut tofu into cubes
  2. mix the coating.
  3. dip tofu in cold water and roll it in the coating mix.
  4. deep fry coated tofu in hot oil. strain the tofu from excess oil, put aside.
  5. saute chilli and garlic with a couple tbsp oil until the aroma comes out.
  6. mix in fried tofu, season with salt and pepper. mix well
  7. put on a plate and enjoy
