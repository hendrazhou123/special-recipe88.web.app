The ingredients needed to make Marble cake:
  1. Make ready 4 eggs
  2. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla powder
  3. Prepare 2 3/4 cups flour
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp milk powder
  5. Get 1 cup water
  6. Take 3 tsp baking powder
  7. Take 4 tbsp cocoa powder
  8. Take 2 cups sugar
  9. Take 3/4 cup vegetable oil

This marble cake can be kept at room temperature (summer/tropical weather) for a day. Make your marble cake using a purchased cake mix. You can bake a round layered cake or a large single layered cake. Then frost the moist marble cake with your favorite frosting or ganache.

Instructions to make Marble cake:
  1. Mix eggs and vanilla
  2. Add to mixture the other ingredients except cocoa powder on low speed for 2 min, then on high speed for 5 min
  3. Put in a circled pan with a hole in the middle half the mixture
  4. Add to the other half the cocoa powder and mix for 2 min
  5. Add the cocoa mixture slowly in circle to the pan over the vanilla mixture
  6. Put the cake in the oven for 1 hour and a quarter
