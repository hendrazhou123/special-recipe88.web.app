The ingredients needed to make Banana bread:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cup flour
  2. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Get 1/2 cup + 2 tbsp of soft butter
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 2 medium bananas
  6. Get 1 tbsp lemon juice or vinegar
  7. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup nuts (1/4 cup, 1/4 cup)

Mix sugar and butter in large bowl. Stir in eggs until well blended. Stir in flour, baking soda and salt just until moistened. In a large bowl, stir together flour, sugar, baking soda and salt.

Steps to make Banana bread:
  1. Combine ingredients in order given, except for 1/4 cup nuts. Mix well.
  2. Lightly butter a microwave pan and sprinkle with remaining nuts
  3. Pour in batter.
  4. Cook for 7 to 9 minutes on full power or until done
  5. Let stand 5 minutes before turning out of pan
