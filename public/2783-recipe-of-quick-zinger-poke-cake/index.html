Steps to make Zinger Poke Cake:
  1. Make cake according to package direction
  2. Let cool for an hour
  3. Make the jell-o according to package instructions
  4. Use a fork & poke multiple holes into the cake - then pour jell-o over the cake evenly
  5. Cover the cake & let it set in the fridge overnight or at least for 4-6 hours
  6. Spread raspberry jam over the cake & frost it with the cool whip
  7. Sprinkle over the coconut flakes & top it all off with fresh raspberries
  8. Enjoy 💗
