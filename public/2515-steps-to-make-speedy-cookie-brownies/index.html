Instructions to make Cookie Brownies:
  1. Gather ingredients
  2. In a medium sized bowl, mix the brownie mix
  3. Place parchment paper on pan. Add brownie mix to the pan
  4. With your both your hands, flatten out cookie dough.
  5. Place cookie dough on top of the brownie mix
  6. Bake according to brownie mix. Note: To get soft brownies, take them out 10 minutes prior to what the box says. Leave in pan until it has fully cooled down. Doing so, allows the brownies to cook a little longer out of the oven.
  7. Enjoy!
