The ingredients needed to make Cheeseburger Macaroni:
  1. Take 1 pound ground beef
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp. avocado oil
  3. Take 1 small onion,finely chopped
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp. tomato paste
  5. Make ready 1 1/2 tsp. paprika
  6. Get 1 tsp. onion powder
  7. Take 1 tsp. garlic powder
  8. Take 1 tsp. salt
  9. Prepare 1 tsp. sugar
  10. Get 1/2 tsp. chili powder
  11. Take 1/2 tsp. fresh ground pepper
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  13. Get 2 cups whole milk
  14. Make ready 1 3/4 cup beef broth
  15. Get 2 cups elbow macaroni pasta
  16. Get 2 cups shredded cheddar cheese

Steps to make Cheeseburger Macaroni:
  1. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat and brown the ground beef until no pink remains.
  2. Add onions and cook until softened, about 5 minutes
  3. Stir in the tomato paste, seasonings and flour
  4. Pour in the milk and broth and stir.
  5. Add the macaroni and stir.
  6. Bring to a boil then reduce to medium-low, cover and simmer for 10-12 minutes or until the macaroni is done. For al dente check after 9 minutes.
  7. Stir in the Cheddar cheese until melted.
  8. Serve immediately garnished with some chopped fresh parsley if desired.
