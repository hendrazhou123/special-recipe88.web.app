The ingredients needed to make Baked Honey Butter Loose Meat Sliders:
  1. Prepare 16-24 slider buns or small dinner rolls
  2. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
  3. Take 1 lb ground pork
  4. Prepare 1 medium Vidalia or yellow onion - finely chopped
  5. Take 3/4 cup beef broth
  6. Get 2 tbs low sodium soy sauce
  7. Get 2 tsp garlic powder
  8. Take 1 tsp onion powder
  9. Get 1/2 tsp ground mustard
  10. Make ready 2 tbs butter
  11. Prepare 1 tbs honey
  12. Prepare 8-12 slices American or cheddar cheese (optional)

Steps to make Baked Honey Butter Loose Meat Sliders:
  1. In a skillet over medium heat brown ground beef and ground pork with onion powder, garlic powder, ground mustard and chopped onion until no pink remains, breaking up to a fine crumble as you go.
  2. Drain meat when done if desired (sometimes I do, sometimes I don't. Just depends how much fat there is). Add beef broth and soy sauce, stir to combine. Bring to a rapid simmer. Let simmer uncovered until liquid reduces to almost nothing, stirring occasionally. About 10 minutes.
  3. Preheat oven to 350Â°F. Leaving buns/rolls connected to each other, carefully split tops from bottoms. Place bottoms in 9x13" baking dish. Spread meat mixture over bottoms evenly. Layer with cheese if desired (which I highly recommend). Cover with bun/roll tops.
  4. Place butter and honey in a small bowl. Microwave 20-30 seconds to melt. Stir. Brush over slider tops. Place sliders in oven uncovered and bake 10-15 minutes or until tops are golden.
  5. Remove from oven. Let cool 5-10 minutes. Pull/cut apart to serve and enjoy!
