The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Take 1 Cup peanut butter
  2. Take 1 Cup butter
  3. Prepare 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  4. Take 1 1/4 Cup Sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tsp. Vanilla
  6. Prepare 3 Eggs
  7. Make ready 4 Cups Flour
  8. Get 1/4 tsp. Salt
  9. Take 2 tsp. Baking Soda
  10. Make ready 10 oz.peanut butter chips (optional)
  11. Get 1/4 Cup Sugar

Steps to make Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. PREHEAT OVEN to 375°
  2. Mix peanut butter, butter, brown sugar, and sugar just until creamy.
  3. Add Vanilla
  4. Add eggs to other wet ingredients 1 at a time and mix on slow just until blended in. Don't over mix.
  5. Stir flour, salt, and baking soda together in a bowl before adding to wet mixture.
  6. Line cookie sheet with parchment paper.
  7. Using a small cookie scoop… Shape coomie dough into 1 1/2 inch balls. Roll each ball in the additional sugar.

  9. Bake @ 375° on dark non stick cookie sheet for 7 to 9 minutes and… Let cool on pan for 5 more minutes.
