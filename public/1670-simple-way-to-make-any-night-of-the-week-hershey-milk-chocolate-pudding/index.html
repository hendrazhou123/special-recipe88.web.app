Instructions to make Hershey milk chocolate pudding:
  1. in a saucepan add egg yokes, milk , flour, Hershey cocoa, and sugar
  2. wisk together with a wisk so it will be smooth and not clumpy
  3. cook on medium high heat while wisking continuously with the wisk
  4. as soon as it has become thick, take it off the heat and add the butter and vanilla and stir
  5. pour in a bowl or a pie crust and let cool.
