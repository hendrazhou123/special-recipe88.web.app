Steps to make Black Forest Swiss Roll Cake | Black Forest Cake | Cakes:
  1. 2 Egg White Beat Well. Add Sugar 1/3 cup,oil 2 tbsp & beat well.
  2. Flour 1/2 cup,Cocoa powder 2 tbsp & Baking Powder 1 tsp.Mix well
  3. Add chocolate syrup 2 tbsp & mix well. Greas with oil in a baking mould.
  4. Bake in preheated oven at 180c for 20-25 mins.
  5. Cake is ready. Let it cool completely.
  6. Spread cherry jelly & whipping cream. Covered the cake with whipped cream & spread chocolates.it's done
