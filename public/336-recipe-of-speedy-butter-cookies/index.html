Instructions to make Butter cookies:
  1. Take butter and powdered sugar.
  2. Mix it nicely to form smooth batter.
  3. Sieve gram flour and all-purpose flour.
  4. Mix it in butter batter in small batches.
  5. Now prepare a dough.
  6. Refrigerate it for 10 minutes.
  7. Take a mould.
  8. Make cookies put it in oven tray by greasing it.
  9. Now with fork make a design.
  10. Put sprinklers of your choice,bake it at 180 degree for 25 minutes.
