The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Milk Cake:

Steps to make Chocolate Milk Cake:
  1. Take 2 glasses milk and boil in a pan and now low flame let it cook until it remains 1/2of it, now add 1cup sugar and stir now let it cool completely. Once it’s cool now add 1/2 cup oil.
  2. Now take 1.5cup maida, 1.5 teaspoon baking powder,2tablespoon cocoa powder and sieve them in a bowl
  3. Now add the milk prepared to the dry ingredients gradually and stir continuously and mix it until it becomes a cake batter.To this add 1teaspoon vanilla essence and mix.Now grease a mould and pour the batter in it.
  4. Now in a preheated(10mins)kadhai for Aprox. 30-45 mins do check it in between with the help of a Thoothpick - Also bake it on low flame.
  5. Now take it out after it’s baked completely and decorate it!
