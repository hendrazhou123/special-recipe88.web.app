The ingredients needed to make No Sugar Chocochips Icecream:
  1. Make ready 250 gms Amul Fresh Cream
  2. Prepare 200 ml Milk
  3. Prepare 4 Tbsp Cocoa Powder
  4. Make ready 3/4 Cup or 200 Gms Grated Jaggery
  5. Get 4 Tbsp Milk Powder
  6. Take 1/3 Chocolate Flakes or pieces of Dark Chocolate (optional)
  7. Make ready 2 Bowls Ice Cubes

Instructions to make No Sugar Chocochips Icecream:
  1. Pre preparatory step is chill all the ingredients in the freezer for 2 hours.
  2. Take a container. Put ice cubes in it and place another container on it. Pour chilled Fresh Cream and Milk Powder in the container.
  3. With the help of a beater, beat till it becomes fluffy and stands on it's own. Add Cocoa Powder, Jaggery and beat it making sure that all ingredients combine well.
  4. Add milk and beat it for 2 minutes. Pour the ready mixture in a container and deep freeze it for 2 hours.
  5. With the help of a fork mix it well. Add Chocolate Pieces or flakes and deep freeze it for 3 to 4 hours.
  6. Time to enjoy your yummy icecream.
