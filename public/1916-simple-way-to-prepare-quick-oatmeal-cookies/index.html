The ingredients needed to make Oatmeal cookies:
  1. Make ready 2 cups Rolled Oats
  2. Get 1 tblsn cinammon
  3. Get 1 package yeast
  4. Take 300 ml flour
  5. Get 1 egg
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup Cooking oil
  7. Take 4 tbslm honey
  8. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla
  9. Prepare 1 tsp Baking soda
  10. Get 1 tbln white sugar
  11. Take 1/2 cup Milk

Steps to make Oatmeal cookies:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients
  2. Add in egg, cooking oil, baking soda & white sugar
  3. Mix everything together with spoon then by hand.
  4. Add 1/2 cup of milk to form pliable dough
  5. Form the cookies & put in oven for 30 minutes in 200 degree oven
