The ingredients needed to make Oreo, cinnamon, nutmeg and vanilla frappechino (amazing I promise):
  1. Prepare 1 cup 8oz hot freshly brewed coffee
  2. Take 1 cup half and half. or you can use milk
  3. Make ready 12 oz whole ice blended a little before anything is added. just to break it up a little.
  4. Get 5 oreos
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp baking cocoa. Unsweetened cocoa.
  6. Get 1/2 tsp cinnamon
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp coffee extract.
  9. Make ready 1 tsp 1\8th Nutmeg. or a small pinch.
