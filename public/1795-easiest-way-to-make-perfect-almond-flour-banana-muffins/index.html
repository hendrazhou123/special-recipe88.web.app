The ingredients needed to make Almond Flour Banana Muffins:
  1. Prepare 50 ml Almond flour
  2. Get 5 ml Baking powder
  3. Prepare 2 1/2 ml Cinnamon
  4. Take 5 ml Virgin coconut oil
  5. Prepare 5 ml Honey
  6. Get 1/2 Banana
  7. Take 1 Egg
  8. Prepare 1 Coconut flakes

Instructions to make Almond Flour Banana Muffins:
  1. First Mix almond flour, baking powder and cinnamon set aside
  2. Microwave honey and coconut oil for 35 seconds than mash and mix in the banana. After whip egg really good into this mix.
  3. Than add in dry ingredients and mix very well.
  4. Sprinkle coconut flakes on top than Microwave for 4m and 30s and enjoy
