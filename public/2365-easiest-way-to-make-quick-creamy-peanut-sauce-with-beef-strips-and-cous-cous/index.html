The ingredients needed to make Creamy peanut sauce with beef strips and cous cous:
  1. Get 1 medium sized onion
  2. Take 5 ml olive oil
  3. Make ready 300-400 g beef strips
  4. Get Sauce: Table spoon fresh chives
  5. Take Pinch pepper
  6. Make ready 2 Table spoons smooth peanut butter
  7. Take 100 ml water
  8. Prepare 15 ml soy sauce
  9. Make ready 5 ml white vinegar
  10. Make ready 5 ml white sugar
  11. Make ready 50 g cashew nuts

Steps to make Creamy peanut sauce with beef strips and cous cous:
  1. Slice onion thinly
  2. Fry onion and beef strips in olive oil
  3. Add half of the sauce once cooked.
  4. After 3 minutes add remainder of sauce.
  5. Put stove off and add cashew nuts. Serve warm with cous cous
