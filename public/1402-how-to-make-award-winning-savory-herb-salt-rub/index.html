The ingredients needed to make Savory Herb Salt Rub:
  1. Make ready 1 1/3 cup coarse sea salt or kosher salt
  2. Prepare 2/3 cup dried parsley
  3. Get 1/4 cup dried thyme
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup dried rosemary, crushed
  5. Take 1/4 cup dried shredded lemon peel
  6. Take 1/4 cup coarse ground pepper
  7. Get 2 tbsp garlic powder
  8. Take 2 tbsp dried minced onion, finely crushed
