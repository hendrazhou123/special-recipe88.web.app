The ingredients needed to make Toll house cookie Sunday / chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Get 1 stick butter
  2. Get 86 grams sugar
  3. Get 78 grams brown sugar
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla
  6. Get 158 grams All purpose flour
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1/2 salt
  9. Get 194 grams good chocolate chips
