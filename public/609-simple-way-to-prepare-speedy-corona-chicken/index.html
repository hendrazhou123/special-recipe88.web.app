The ingredients needed to make Corona Chicken:
  1. Get 1 large pack chicken thighs, with skin
  2. Prepare 1 bottle beer (I used Corona)
  3. Prepare Sprinkle Black Pepper
  4. Get Sprinkle Adobo
  5. Prepare Sprinkle Garlic Powder
  6. Make ready 2 onions, sliced
  7. Get 1 green pepper, sliced
  8. Make ready 4 Heaping tablespoons of margarine or butter

Steps to make Corona Chicken:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs and place into a large baking pan……
  2. Pour your beer over all the chicken thighs, then sprinkle Black Pepper, Adobo, and garlic powder over all the thighs…….Then place sliced onions and green pepper all around chicken thighs…….
  3. Place your heaping tablespoons of margarine or butter all around chicken thighs…….Instead of baking right away you can also cover it and refrigerate overnight and bake the next day…… 😉
  4. Bake, uncovered at 400 degrees for 1 hour or until beer has reduced and chicken thighs are nice and golden…….
  5. Serve and enjoy ! 😋
