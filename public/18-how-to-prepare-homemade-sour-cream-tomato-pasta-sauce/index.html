The ingredients needed to make Sour cream tomato pasta sauce:
  1. Make ready 4 cups chicken stock
  2. Make ready 1/2 large onion diced
  3. Make ready As needed Olive oil
  4. Get 2-3 medium tomato’s diced
  5. Take 1 cup sour cream
  6. Make ready To taste Fresh of dried parsley
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp flour mixed in 1 cup water for slurry
  8. Prepare To taste Salt, pepper, adobo seasoning

Instructions to make Sour cream tomato pasta sauce:
  1. Sauté the onion in olive oil with seasonings. Add a little broth along the way so it won’t burn
  2. Add the rest of the broth, the tomato’s, and brings to a simmer.
  3. Whisk in the flour/water flurry. And whisk in 1 cup sour cream. Simmer until it slightly thickens
  4. Add chicken, and pasta back in and top with Parmesan cheese
