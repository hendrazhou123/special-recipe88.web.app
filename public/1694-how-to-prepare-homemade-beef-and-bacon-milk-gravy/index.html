The ingredients needed to make Beef and Bacon Milk Gravy:
  1. Prepare 1/4 lb ground beef
  2. Make ready 4 slices bacon
  3. Take 3 tbs flour
  4. Get 2 cups milk
  5. Prepare Black pepper
  6. Take Garlic powder
  7. Get Red pepper flakes
  8. Prepare Brown sugar
