The ingredients needed to make Savory Smoked Sausage Soup with Veggies 🥣:
  1. Prepare 2 packages smoked sausage (sliced into coins)
  2. Prepare 2 tablespoons margarine or butter
  3. Take 2 onions, chopped
  4. Prepare 1 green pepper, chopped
  5. Take 1 bag (16 ounce) baby carrots
  6. Get 1 tablespoon oregano
  7. Get 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  8. Get 10 cloves garlic, minced
  9. Prepare 1 small bag baby red potatoes
  10. Take 1 can (14.5 ounce) fire roasted diced tomatoes
  11. Get 1 can (15.5 ounce) light red kidney beans
  12. Get 2 cans (14.5 ounce) cans of chicken broth

Instructions to make Savory Smoked Sausage Soup with Veggies 🥣:
  1. In a large pot, sauté in the margarine or butter: onions, green pepper, baby carrots until veggies are tender……Cover pot….
  2. Add the black pepper, oregano and minced garlic, sauté well……
  3. Now add your 2 packages of smoked sausage, sauté for a few minutes……
  4. Now add your baby red potatoes, stir well……
  5. Next add : diced tomatoes, red kidney beans and chicken broth, bring to a boil….. Stir well…..
  6. Continue to cook over medium heat, covered for 1 hour or until all veggies are tender…….
  7. Stir soup, spoon into a bowl and enjoy 😉!
