The ingredients needed to make Chewy, gooey cookie recipe:
  1. Get 1/2 cup caster sugar
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup brown sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup melted butter
  4. Prepare 1 egg
  5. Make ready 1 1/4 cups flour
  6. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  7. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare 220 g chocolate

Instructions to make Chewy, gooey cookie recipe:
  1. Mix together sugars and butter
  2. Whisk the egg is a separate bowl and add it in slowly
  3. Add salt and vanilla
  4. Add flour and chocolate
  5. Let sit in the fridge for 1 hour
  6. Divide into balls and put them onto a baking tray and squash them down
  7. Cool for 10-15 minutes depending on how gooey you like the cookies
