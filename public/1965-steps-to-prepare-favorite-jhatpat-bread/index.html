Instructions to make Jhatpat bread😍:
  1. First mix suji n curd n lil bit of water n leave it for 10 mins so… Mix become fluffy
  2. After suji become soft add veggies n spices
  3. Then spread this mix on one side of bread..take non stick pan, add butter n put that Bread on pan dn spread butter on upr side.. flip bread till it become crispy
