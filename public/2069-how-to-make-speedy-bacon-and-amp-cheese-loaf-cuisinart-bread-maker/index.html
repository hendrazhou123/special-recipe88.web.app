  2. Make ready 1/2 TSP salt
  3. Take 3 cups flour
  4. Prepare 2 TBSP sugar
  5. Take 1/2 cup cheddar
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup Colby Jack
  7. Take 1/4 Asiago Cheese
  8. Take 8 slice bacon, diced
