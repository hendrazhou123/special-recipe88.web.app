Steps to make Homemade Mac & Cheese!:
  1. Melt 1 tbs of butter in large pot. Pour in 5 cups of milk. Add salt,pepper and paprika. Let boil and reduce heat.
  2. Stir in pasta. Let boil and stir frequently. You will notice the sauce start to thicken and your noddles will be cooked.
  3. Take pot off the heat. Add cheese and remaining butter. Stir and serve!
