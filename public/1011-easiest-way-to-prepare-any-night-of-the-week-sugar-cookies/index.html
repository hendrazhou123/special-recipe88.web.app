The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 1/2 cup sugar
  2. Take 1 1/2 cup butter, softened
  3. Prepare 4 eggs
  4. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla
  5. Get 5 cup flour
  6. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 tsp salt

Instructions to make Sugar Cookies:

    1. Cover. Chill at least one hour (or over night)
