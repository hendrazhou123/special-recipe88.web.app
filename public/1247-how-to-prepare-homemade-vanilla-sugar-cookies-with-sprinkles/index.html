  3. Take 2 stick unsalted butter, room temp
  4. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  5. Prepare 1 large egg

Instructions to make Vanilla Sugar Cookies with Sprinkles:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. In medium bowl, combine flour and salt and incorporate
  2. In another bowl, using mixer, beat softened butter and sugar until light in color and fluffy
  3. Add flour-salt mixture and continue beating on low speed for couple of minutes until combined
  4. Roll dough into 1-inch balls and roll in your choice of sprinkles
  5. Arrange on parchment lined cookie sheet about 1-inch apart and freeze till firm - about 30 minutes
  6. Bake at 350°F for 14 minutes - do not overbake. Remove from oven and transfer to wire rack to cool
