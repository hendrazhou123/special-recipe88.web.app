Instructions to make Cheese Pizza:
  1. Take pizza base, spread butter, then put pizza sauce.
  2. Grate cheese and put some pieces of chopped onion and capsicum and sprinkle red chilli flakes.
  3. Put it inside in a preheated oven for 5 minutes or You can make it on frying pan also by covering it with a lid
  4. Sprinkle some oregano, red chilli flakes and pizza seasoning and serve it hot with tomato sauce.
