The ingredients needed to make Snickerdoodle Cookies with a Soft Chewy Vanilla Caramel Center:
  1. Prepare 3 cups all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking soda
  3. Get 1 teaspoon baking powdwe
  4. Get 1 teaspoon cream of tarter
  5. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. Get 1 cup butter at room temperature
  7. Get 1 cup granulated sugar
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
  9. Take 2 large eggs
  10. Take 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla
  11. Get 15 SOFT vanilla cream filled caramels. I used Werthers brand
  12. Take For Topping
  13. Prepare 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  14. Take 3 teaspoons ground cinnamon

Steps to make Snickerdoodle Cookies with a Soft Chewy Vanilla Caramel Center:
  1. Preheat the oven to to 350. Line cookie sheets with parchment paper. Combine Topping ingredients in a bowl.
  2. Combine in a bowl flour, baking soda, baking powder, cream of tarter and salt
  3. In another bowl beat butter and sugars until light and fluffy
  4. Add eggs one at a time, add vanilla and beat in
  5. Mix in flour mixture until well combined don’t overmix
  6. Place a disk if dough on work surface l
  7. Place a caramel in the center
  9. Roll into a ball to compleatly enclose caramel
  10. Roll each ball in cinnamin sugar topping
  11. Place 2 inches apart on prepared pan and bake until golden 12 to 15 minutes
