The ingredients needed to make Lazy Kielbasa Taco Soup:
  1. Get 2 packages kielbasa, sliced into coins
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon margarine or butter
  3. Make ready 6 cloves minced garlic
  4. Get 1 jar your favorite chunky salsa (16 ounce)
  5. Prepare 1 can chicken broth
  6. Take 1 can black beans (undrained)
  7. Get 2 1/2 tablespoons Taco seasoning mix
  8. Make ready 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Make ready 1 cup frozen corn
  10. Make ready Sour cream for topping
