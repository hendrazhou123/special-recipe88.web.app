The ingredients needed to make Peanuts Chicken:
  1. Take 2 Boneless chicken breasts
  2. Get Paprika
  3. Make ready Ground pepper
  4. Take Salt
  5. Take 2 TBSP Peanuts
  6. Make ready 2 TBSP Olive oil
  7. Prepare Garlic

Instructions to make Peanuts Chicken:
  1. Cut the chicken breasts in small cubes.
  2. Add the olive oil to a heating pan
  3. Place the chicken in the pan
  4. Put the peanuts in the pan
  5. Sprinkle the chicken and the peanuts with salt, pepper and paprika.
  6. Let it cook for 15 mins.
