The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Chicken with Red Potatoes:
  1. Get 1 large pack chicken thighs, bone in, skin on
  2. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  3. Take 6 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Get 7 red potatoes, cleaned and cubed
  5. Make ready 1 packet Ranch dressing mix
  6. Prepare 5 tablespoons melted margarine or butter

Instructions to make Crockpot Chicken with Red Potatoes:
  1. Rinse and pat dry your chicken thighs and place into crockpot…..
  2. Sprinkle chicken with black pepper and place minced garlic into crockpot on top of chicken thighs….
  3. Place cleaned red potatoes all around chicken thighs…..
  4. Sprinkle your packet of Ranch dressing mix all over potatoes and chicken thighs…..
  5. Place your melted butter or margarine all over chicken thighs and potatoes…..
  6. Cover and cook on Low heat for 8 hours……
