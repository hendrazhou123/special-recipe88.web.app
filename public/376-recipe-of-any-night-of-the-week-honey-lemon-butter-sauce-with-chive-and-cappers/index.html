The ingredients needed to make Honey lemon butter sauce with chive and cappers:
  1. Take 1 stick butter melted but not calrified
  2. Make ready Juice and zest of a whole lemon
  3. Get 2-3 tbsp honey
  4. Take Salt
  5. Make ready 2 tbsp minced chives
  6. Prepare Evo
  7. Get Garlic clove smashed
  8. Make ready 3 tbsp cappers
