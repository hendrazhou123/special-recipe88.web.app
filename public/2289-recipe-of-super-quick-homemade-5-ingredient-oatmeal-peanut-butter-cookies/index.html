The ingredients needed to make 5 Ingredient Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Take 1 cup Peanut Butter (plain or crunchy)
  2. Get 2/3 cup Sugar (may use 1/2 white, 1/2 brown)
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup Oatmeal, steel cut, quick 3 minute
  4. Prepare 2 eggs
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking Soda

Instructions to make 5 Ingredient Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Combine and mix all ingredients.
  2. Let set for 1 hr in refrigerator.
  3. Roll dough into 1 inch balls onto cookie sheet. Apply criss cross pattern with fork.
  4. Preheat oven to 350°. Bake approx. 12 - 15 minutes.
  5. Allow to cool, ENJOY !
