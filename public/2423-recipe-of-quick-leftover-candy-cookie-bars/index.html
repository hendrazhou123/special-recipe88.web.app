The ingredients needed to make Leftover Candy 🍬 Cookie 🍪 Bars:
  1. Take 2 packs (12 ounce) cookie dough (any flavor), softened
  2. Prepare 1 cup leftover chocolate 🍫 candy
  3. Take Marshmallows
  4. Make ready Cinnamon powder

Great way to use up any leftover candy 🍭!! 🙂😋. Give leftover Halloween candy new life in this over-the-top bar cookie recipe. Cool completely in pan on a wire rack. Use foil to lift uncut bars from pan.

Instructions to make Leftover Candy 🍬 Cookie 🍪 Bars:
  1. In a large bowl crumble up your cookie dough……
  2. Stir into dough your candies, breaking up any large pieces…..
  3. In a 7X11 inch baking pan press dough into the bottom of pan, DO NOT GREASE PAN !!!! Leave out just a few pieces of dough ( you’ll be using those pieces for the topping !
  4. Bake in preheated 350 degree oven for 15 minutes, then remove from oven…..
  5. Place your marshmallows on top of candy cookie crust, place as many marshmallows as you can !
  6. Take reserved cookie dough and crumble all over marshmallows…….Sprinkle top with cinnamon powder……
  7. Place back into oven and continue to cook for about 15-20 minutes more or until cookie dough is golden on top and marshmallows have melted…
  8. Remove from oven, let cool for 1 hour or more, cut into squares and enjoy 😉! Mine look funny because I used different colored marshmallows, but it’s ok, my kids loved it ! 🥰
