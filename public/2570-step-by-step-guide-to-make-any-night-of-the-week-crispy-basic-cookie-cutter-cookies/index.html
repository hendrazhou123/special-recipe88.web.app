The ingredients needed to make Crispy Basic Cookie Cutter Cookies:
  1. Get 100 grams Butter
  2. Take 60 grams Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 Egg yolk
  4. Prepare 170 grams Cake flour

Cut into shapes for the winter holidays. Cut into desired shapes with cookie cutters. The most common basic cookie cutter material is plastic. Whole Foods and Vegetarian Recipe Blog.

Steps to make Crispy Basic Cookie Cutter Cookies:
  1. Bring the butter to room temperature, and cream with a whisk.
  2. Add sugar and mix.
  3. Add the egg yolk, and mix until the mixture becomes smooth.
  4. Finally, add cake flour, and fold in.
  5. Roll into a ball, cover with plastic wrap, and let it sit in the fridge for about 1 hour.
  6. Roll out the dough to 5 mm thickness, and cut out shapes. Bake in the oven for about 20 minutes at 170℃/340℉.
  7. I usually let them cool on newspaper. It absorbs the oil well. Let cool completely, and they're done.
