The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Cupcakes w/ Vanilla Buttercream Frosting:
  1. Take 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  2. Take 3/4 cup flour
  3. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Get 3/4 cup butter/margarine
  6. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  7. Make ready 3 eggs
  8. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 1/2 cup sour cream
  10. Get frosting:
  11. Get 2 cups powdered sugar
  12. Get 1/2 cup margarine
  13. Take 1 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  14. Prepare 2 tbsp milk

Instructions to make Chocolate Cupcakes w/ Vanilla Buttercream Frosting:
  1. Sift and set aside the flour, cocoa powder, baking soda and salt.
  2. Mix the butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Add and beat in eggs, one at a time, then the vanilla. Gradually add and mix the flour mixture, alternating with sour cream, and ending with flour.
  3. Fill up 3/4 of each baking cup with the batter. Bake for approx. 20 minutes at 350°F.
  4. FROSTING: mix butter until smooth, beat in powdered sugar, then vanilla and milk.
  5. Pipe the buttercream frosting onto the cupcakes. Add strawberries and coconut on top for decoration and extra flavor 🙃(optional).
