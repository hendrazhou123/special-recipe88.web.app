The ingredients needed to make Moist Chocolate Cake:
  1. Get 3 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Take 3 cups granulated sugar
  3. Make ready 1.5 cups cocoa powder
  4. Prepare 1 tbsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 1.5 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 4 eggs
  7. Take 1.5 cups buttermilk
  8. Prepare 1.5 cups warm water
  9. Get 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  10. Prepare vanilla extract

Instructions to make Moist Chocolate Cake:
  1. Pre-heat oven 180 degC.
  2. Mix well all dry ingredients; flour, sugar, cocoa powder, baking soda, baking powder then add eggs, buttermilk, water, oil and vanilla. Whisk on medium speed until well combined.
  3. Bake in a 180 degC for about 20 minutes.
  4. Let it cool completely then decorate cupcakes & cake as you wish. Enjoy!
