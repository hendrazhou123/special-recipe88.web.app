The ingredients needed to make Sugar cookies:
  1. Prepare 400 g all purpose flour
  2. Get 250 g butter
  3. Make ready 1-1/2 cup sugar
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Take 1 egg yolk
  6. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Sugar cookies:
  1. In a clean bowl, cream butter and sugar till fluffy and white
  2. Add in egg and egg yolk, mix carefully
  3. Add in vanilla essence. Scrape side
  4. Add in flour and mix till dough is well formed
  5. Dust a surface with flour and knead the dough.
  6. Roll out dough to about 2cm high. Refigrate for atleast 2 hours and then cut to desired shape using cookie cutter.
  7. Bake at 120°c for about 12mins. Allow to cool then serve with hot coffee
