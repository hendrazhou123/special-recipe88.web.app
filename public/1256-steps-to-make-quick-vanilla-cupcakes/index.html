The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Get 1 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Get 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  5. Get 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  6. Get 2 eggs
  7. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Make ready 1/2 cup whole milk

Instructions to make Vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Preheat oven to (200 degrees C ) line cupcake pans with cupcake liners or butter the pan
  2. In a bowl beat buttef until creamy and smooth …add suger until fluffy for about a minute..add eggs one at a time ….add vanilla and mix until combained
  3. In separate bowl mix flour, baking powder and salt
  4. Mix dry ingredients with wet ingredients adding the milk while mixing
  5. Bake for about 15 - 17 minutes or until a toothpick is inserted and comes out clean.
