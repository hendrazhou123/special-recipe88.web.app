The ingredients needed to make Steamed bread:
  1. Get 2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Take 1/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1/2 TSP yeast
  4. Take 1/4 TSP salt
  5. Prepare 1/8 cup milk powder
  6. Prepare 3/4 cup water
  7. Make ready 1/4 cup butter
  8. Take Extra flour for kneading
  9. Prepare Breadcrumbs for coating

Instructions to make Steamed bread:
  1. Mix all the dry ingredients, then gradually add water
  2. While kneading dough, Add butter, knead for 5 minutes til dough is smooth, make it ball shape and place in a lightly greased container and covered with cloth
  3. Let rise for 1.5 hours in a warm environment
  4. After 1.5 hours, make a baton out of the dough and sprinkle with bread crumbs, cover with cloth for 15 minutes and cut afterwards
  5. Place in a container and leave for another 1.5 hours
  6. Steam for 15-20 minutes. If I want to make it crispier, it can be heated in a pan 🙂
