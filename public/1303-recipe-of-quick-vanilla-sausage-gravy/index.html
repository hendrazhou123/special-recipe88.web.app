The ingredients needed to make Vanilla sausage gravy:
  1. Take 2 1/3 cup Vanilla soy milk
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp Black pepper
  3. Get 1 can Cream of any soup
  4. Make ready 2 lb Sausage
  5. Make ready 1 can Water
  6. Get 1 tbsp Salt
  7. Take 1 pinch Garlic
  8. Prepare 4 tbsp flour

Steps to make Vanilla sausage gravy:
  1. Fry sausage on medium-high heat
  2. Get a bowl and mix soup,water,milk,and flour
  3. pour the mixture in with the sausage and cook on a medium-high heat stir consistently
  4. When the gravy is thick enough for your liking then the gravy is ready.
  5. Serve with biscuits (optional)
