The ingredients needed to make Cake:
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cup refined flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup milk
  3. Prepare 1/4 cup powdered sugar
  4. Get 1/4 cup refined oil
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup condensed milk
  6. Take 1/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get As needed Whipped cream
  9. Make ready 4-5 drops red food colour
  10. Take 1/4 cup milk chocolate
  11. Get 2 tbsp milk
  12. Get As needed sprinkler

Steps to make Cake:
  1. Firstly seive refined flour, powered sugar, baking powder and soda into a mixing bowl.
  2. Further add in other bowl condensed milk and refined mix well and add dry ingredients into wet ingredients.
  3. Add mix cut in fold method.
  4. Now add milk and mix well.
  5. Bake cake preheat oven upon 180 degree Celsius for 20 minutes.
  6. After baked cake keep cool down.
  7. And cut in three layers.
  8. Further all layer covered with whipped cream and deep freeze for 20 minutes.
  9. Now prepare glace add milk and milk chocolate mix and melt completely.
  10. Now add red colour mix well. And pour upon on cake.
  11. Place cake on cake plate. Make some design on cake, sprinkler on it.
  12. Before serving keep refrigerator at least 2 hours.
  13. Enjoy homemade birthday cake.
