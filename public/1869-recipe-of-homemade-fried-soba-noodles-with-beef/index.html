The ingredients needed to make Fried Soba Noodles with Beef:
  1. Make ready 1 scallion
  2. Take 1/4 inch ginger
  3. Take 2 cloves garlic
  4. Get 1 can kimchi (150 gm)
  5. Take 1 carrot, cut into thin pieces
  6. Get 300 gm beef
  7. Get 1/2 cup red wine
  8. Take 1 tbs soy sauce
  9. Prepare 1 tbs sesame oil
  10. Get 300 gm dried soba noodles
  11. Prepare Spring onion, sesame seeds garnish
  12. Make ready to taste Salt

Steps to make Fried Soba Noodles with Beef:
  1. Mince scallion, garlic and ginger using a blender or mortar
  2. Cook them on the pan with sesame oil for a minute, and then add beef. Let beef cook a bit and then add the chopped carrots.
  3. Add red wine and soy sauce, and salt to taste.
  4. Meanwhile, drain kimchi from the can and chop into small pieces
  5. Start cooking the dried soba noodles. If you don't have confidence that it will cook together with the beef, let beef stop cooking for a while.
  6. Add kimchi and stir well.
  7. Add soba noodles and fry for a minute.
  8. Remove from heat and serve with spring onions or sesame seeds (optional).
