The ingredients needed to make Fried Olive Oil Bread:
  1. Make ready 3 cups self-rising flour
  2. Get 1/3 cup extra virgin olive oil
  3. Take 3/4 cup warm water
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon salt
  5. Prepare 1 teaspoon granulated onion powder
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  7. Make ready 2 tablespoons parsley flakes
  8. Get As needed extra virgin olive oil to fry with
