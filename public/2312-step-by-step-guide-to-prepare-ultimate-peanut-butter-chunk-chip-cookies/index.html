The ingredients needed to make Peanut butter chunk chip cookies:
  1. Get 1/2 cup refined coconut oil
  2. Prepare 1 cup natural peanut butter
  3. Make ready 2 cups packed brown sugar
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Get 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup almond milk
  7. Prepare 2 tsp baking soda
  8. Make ready 3 cups AP flour
  9. Get 1 cup chopped unsalted peanuts
  10. Take 1/2 cup chocolate chips
  11. Prepare 1 cup peanut butter chips

Steps to make Peanut butter chunk chip cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 400°
  2. Cream oil, pb, and sugar
  3. Add well beaten eggs and milk. Mix.
  4. Add soda and flour, mix well
  5. Add nuts and chips, mix thoroughly
  6. Scoop on to parchment lined baking sheets. They will not spread.
  7. Bake about 12 minutes
