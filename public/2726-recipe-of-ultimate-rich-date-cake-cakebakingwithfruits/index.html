The ingredients needed to make Rich Date Cake #cakebakingwithfruits:
  1. Prepare 500 g Deseeded Dates
  2. Make ready 180 g Granulated Sugar
  3. Take 180 g Soft Salted Butter
  4. Prepare 1 Tbsp Bicarbonate of soda
  5. Take 1 Tbsp Baking powder
  6. Get 4 Eggs
  7. Prepare 1 handful Raisins
  8. Make ready 1 Tbsp Vanilla essence

Steps to make Rich Date Cake #cakebakingwithfruits:
  1. Mix half cup of water with Bicarbonate of soda and mix with the Deseeded Dates first, keep aside.
  2. Now, Mix Butter and sugar then crack in the 4 eggs, mix and add the crushed Dates with vanilla essence.
  3. Add the Raisins followed by Flour with Baking powder and mix gradually to have a uniformed mixture
  4. Mixture ready, pour batter on a greased tin, bake for 180 degrees for 50 minutes, poke with a toothpick, if comes out dry, then ready, let cool.
  5. Once cooled, slice it up.
