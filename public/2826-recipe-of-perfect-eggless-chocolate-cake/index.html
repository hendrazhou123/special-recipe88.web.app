The ingredients needed to make Eggless Chocolate Cake:
  1. Get 1 cup wheat flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup + 1 tbsp Hung Curd. 1/4 cup canola oil
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 1/2 cup water
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup unsweetened natural cocoa powder
  7. Take 1 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Take 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  9. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  10. Prepare 1 tsp coffee powder
  11. Take For the Chocolate Ganache
  12. Make ready 200 gm Dark Chocolate
  13. Make ready 3/4 cup cream

Steps to make Eggless Chocolate Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 180 C, - All make sure all the ingredients are at room temp.
  2. Spray a cake pan with cooking spray, and flour the pan, set aside.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, combine water, sugar, hung curd, oil, vanilla, until smooth.
  4. Now add coffee, cocoa powder and whisk some more until the batter was smooth and completely free from lumps. - - Sift the flour, salt,soda and baking powder
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet mix and whisk again to form a smooth batter
  6. Now pour the batter, into the prepared pan and bake for about 25 -30 minutes. - Check with a skewer - If it comes out clean means cake is done. - - Let it cool before taking it out on a plate
  7. For the Chocolate Ganache - - Place the chocolate in a microwave-safe bowl just for 1 minute to soften chocolate.
  8. Heat up the cream in a pan and pour it over the chocolate and let it stand about 1 minute. Whisk until chocolate has melted and mixture is smooth and velvety. - Let it cool for about 10 minutes, allowing ganache to cool and thicken a bit.
