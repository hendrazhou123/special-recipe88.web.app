The ingredients needed to make Delicious milk cake:
  1. Get 250 gm flour (maida)
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 3/4 cup oil
  4. Get 2 packet milk powder
  5. Get 1 tablespoon baking powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tablespoon baking soda
  7. Take 4-5 drops essense
  8. Prepare 2-3 ilach
  9. Get 1 1/2 cup milk
  10. Take Dry fruits for garnishing
