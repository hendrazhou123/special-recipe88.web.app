The ingredients needed to make Cake:
  1. Prepare 2 cup flour
  2. Take 1 simas butter
  3. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  4. Get 6 eggs
  5. Take 1 tspn baking powder
  6. Make ready 1 tspn flavour
  7. Prepare 3 tbsp Coco powder
  8. Take 1 tbsp corn flour

Steps to make Cake:
  1. Add butter and sugar together and start mixing it until is fluffy and light.
  2. Break eggs and add flavour in your mixture and continue mixing.
  3. Add flour,baking powder and corn flour and then mix it together properly.
  4. Divide the mixture in to 2 in a separate bowl. Add Coco powder in one mixture and stir it well.
  5. Rub the baking pan with butter and flour to prevent the cake from sticking to the pan
  6. Pour the mixture into the baking pan as you seen below.and repeat it 2 wise.
  7. Put inside the preheated oven then bake your cake for about 40 minutes.
  8. Check the cake by inserting a toothpick in the middle of the cake,if the comes out clean the cake is done.
