The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chunk banana ice cream:
  1. Make ready 2 large bananas
  2. Take 1/2 cup liquid coffee creamer i use vanilla almond creamer
  3. Make ready 1/4 cup almond milk
  4. Get 1 1/2 cups lite coconut cream
  5. Take 2 Tablespoons raw honey
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup dark chocolate chunks plus 1 or 2 Tablespoons separate

Instructions to make Chocolate chunk banana ice cream:
  1. Slice and freeze bananas
  2. Mix almond milk and coffee creamer and pour into a ice cube tray and freeze
  3. When bananas and almond milk mixture is frozen add to a blender and pour in coconut cream and honey and blend till smooth and well mixed then add in 1/2 cup chocolate chunks and lightly blend till chunks are slightly broken up and mixed
  4. Pour mixture into a container and sprinkle on a 1 or 2 Tablespoons of the chocolate chunks add a tight fitting lid and freeze till solid. Softened to eat. Enjoy !
