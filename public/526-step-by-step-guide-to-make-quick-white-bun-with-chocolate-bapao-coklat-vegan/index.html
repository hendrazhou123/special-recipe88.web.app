The ingredients needed to make White Bun with Chocolate (Bapao Coklat) *Vegan:
  1. Prepare 300 gr plain flour
  2. Get 3 tbsp sugar
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp margarine
  4. Make ready 1 tbsp yeast
  5. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 160 ml water
  7. Prepare Nutella for filling
  8. Prepare More water to steam

Steps to make White Bun with Chocolate (Bapao Coklat) *Vegan:
  1. Mix yeast, sugar, and water in a cup, put aside.
  2. Mix plain flour and baking powder, gradually pour yeast water, knead the dough until it doesnt stick.
  3. Add margarine, knead properly until smooth.
  4. Cover the dough with plastic wrap for 45 mins until it expands to double its size.
  5. Knead it again less than a minute, make 10 balls out of it, fill each balls with nutella. Rest them for 15 mins.
  6. Pre-heat water in a steamer, steam the balls while the water boiling. Steam them for 8-10 mins.
  7. The white buns with chocolate is ready to enjoy.
