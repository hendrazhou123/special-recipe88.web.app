The ingredients needed to make Rice Flour and Oats Banana Chocolate Muffin:
  1. Make ready Dry
  2. Get 25 ml Oats
  3. Prepare 25 ml Rice flour
  4. Get 5 ml Baking powder
  5. Take 2 1/2 ml Cinnamon
  6. Make ready Wet
  7. Make ready 5 ml Virgin coconut oil
  8. Take 1 ml Vanilla
  9. Make ready 2 1/2 Honey
  10. Take 1/2 Banana
  11. Take 1 Egg
  12. Prepare 1 small Dark chocolate mini bar (70%)

Instructions to make Rice Flour and Oats Banana Chocolate Muffin:
  1. Mix all dry ingredients and set aside.
  2. Mix coconut oil, vanilla and honey and microwave for 30s.
  3. Mash banana in melted coconut oil, honey and vanilla than mix in egg and whip it good.
  4. Combine dry ingredients with wet. Mix till smooth
  5. Crush dark chocolate and mix in
  6. Now microwave for 4m 30s
