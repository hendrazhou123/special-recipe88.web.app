The ingredients needed to make Simply Vanilla Cake:
  1. Make ready Cake
  2. Prepare 4 eggs
  3. Get 2 cup superfine sugar(can be found in baking isle at grocery store )
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Make ready 2 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  6. Make ready 2 1/4 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 1 cup milk
  8. Prepare 3/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions to make Simply Vanilla Cake:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°
  2. Grease the sides and bottoms of two 9-inch round cake pans. Line the bottoms with parchment paper that is also greased on top.
  3. In a large mixing bowl with an electric mixer , beat the sugar, eggs and vanilla together until slightly thickened, about 2 minutes.
  4. Add the flour, baking powder, milk and oil and beat for 1 minute until the batter is smooth and creamy.
  5. Pour batter into prepared baking pans.
  6. Bake in oven for 30 to 40 minutes or until the tops are golden and a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
  7. Loosen the sides of the cake from the pan with a knife, then turn out onto a rack and peel off the paper. Let cool completely before frosting with your frosting of choice.
