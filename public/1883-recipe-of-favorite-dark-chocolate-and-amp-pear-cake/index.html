The ingredients needed to make Dark Chocolate & Pear Cake:
  1. Take 1 cup whole wheat flour
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup almond flour
  3. Get 1/3 cup cacao powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready cinnamon, salt
  7. Make ready 1/3 cup olive oil
  8. Get 1/3 cup honey
  9. Prepare 4 pears
  10. Take 1/2 cup almond milk
  11. Get 1/2 cup almond milk

Instructions to make Dark Chocolate & Pear Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350F. Grease a cake pan. Peel pears and dice into small pieces.
  2. In a large bowl, whisk together almond flour, whole wheat flour, cacao powder, baking powder, baking soda, a pinch of salt, and cinnamon.
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together honey, olive oil, almond milk. Add to the dry ingredients and stir until combined. Fold in the diced pears.
