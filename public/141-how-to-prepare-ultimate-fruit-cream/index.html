The ingredients needed to make Fruit cream:
  1. Make ready 250 ml fresh cream
  2. Take 1 cup yoghurt
  3. Prepare 3 tbsp powdered sugar
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 1 apple finely(peeled and chopped)
  6. Prepare 1 banana finely(peeled and chopped)
  7. Get 1 small mango finely(peeled and chopped)
  8. Take 1/4 cup finely chopped watermelon
  9. Make ready 2 tbsp cashews (crushed)
  10. Get 2 tbsp almonds (crushed)
  11. Take 1 tbsp pistachios (crushed)

Steps to make Fruit cream:
  1. Whisk cream and yoghurt in a bowl until smooth and creamy. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract and mix it well. Add fruits and nuts and mix well. Chill for 1-2 hours and serve chilled.
