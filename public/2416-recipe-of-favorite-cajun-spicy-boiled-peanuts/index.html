The ingredients needed to make Cajun spicy boiled peanuts:
  1. Take 1 packages raw jumbo peanuts
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp hot cajun seasoning
  3. Take 2 tbsp garlic powder
  4. Prepare 1 each habanero pepper, sliced.
  5. Get 1/2 cup salt
  6. Prepare 1/2 cup red chili pepper flakes
  7. Get 1/3 packages hot peppers, chopped.

Instructions to make Cajun spicy boiled peanuts:
  1. Rinse and drain peanuts several times to get dirt or shell pieces off.
  3. Soak peanuts in lukewarm water for 30-45 minutes.
  4. Place peanuts in large stock pot, fill with water.
  5. Add all spices and water to cover. Peanuts will float to tip, stir.
  6. Bring to boil, stir frequently. Continue for 45-60 minutes.
  7. Transfer to crockpot. Simmer on low for 7+ hours.
  8. When softness of peanut has been achieved, turn off. Let peanuts soak for more flavor. Serve in cup, don't forget your extra cup for peanuts. Refrigerate leftovers for 2-3 days. You can freeze leftovers.
