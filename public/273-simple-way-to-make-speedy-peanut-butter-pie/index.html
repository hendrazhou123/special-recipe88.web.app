The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Pie:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup creamy peanut butter
  2. Take 1 (8 oz.) pkg. cream cheese, softened
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup milk
  4. Get 1 tub Cool Whip, thawed
  5. Get 1 Premade Graham Cracker Pie Crust
  6. Take Toppings:
  7. Get Herseys Chocolate Syrup
  8. Prepare Cool Whip

Steps to make Peanut Butter Pie:
  1. In a large bowl, combine peanut butter and cream cheese until well blended.
  2. Add in milk and blend together.
  3. Fold in thawed Cool Whip.
  4. Place mixture into crust and place in fridge for minimum of 4 hours.
  5. Slice into individual slices and top with toppings of choice. Enjoy!
