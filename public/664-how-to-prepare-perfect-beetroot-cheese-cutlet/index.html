  2. In another bowl add curd cheeses the other dry spices mix it, now take a small ball size beetroot mixture give it a heart shape then at the middle put the curd cheese mixture
  3. Again put the beetroot mixture then dip it all dry purpose flour then dip into slurry of all-purpose flour and corn flour mixture
