The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Get 1 stick butter
  2. Get 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Take 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  6. Take 2 cup flour

Instructions to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Pre heat oven to 200°
  2. Beat room temperature butter and sugar with mixer for 3-5 mins
  3. Beat in the egg
  4. Add vanilla extract
  5. On a separate bowl, sift baking powder and flour together. Then add this mixture to the butter and sugar mixture
  6. Knead the dough if it gets to hard to use the mixer
  7. Mold or shape your dough
  8. Add food coloring as desired
  9. Bake for 7 mins and let cool
  10. Enjoy
