Steps to make Light n fluffy vanilla cupcakes:
  1. Preheat oven to 135 Celsius
  2. sift dry ingredients into a large bowl and make a well in thr the center.
  3. Mix the wet ingredients with the dry ingredients until lihht and liquidy consistency.
  4. Whip the egg whites and sugar to make a stiff meringue.
  5. Gently fold the meringue into cake batter.
  6. Pour cupcake batter into cupcake molds and place in the oven for 20 minutes.
  7. Allow to cool before frosting with your favourite frosting or icing.
