The ingredients needed to make Easy Caramelized Onion Tart:
  1. Make ready Crust
  2. Make ready 250 g flour
  3. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  4. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  5. Make ready 80 ml olive oil (or 40ml olive oil + 40ml vegetable oil)
  6. Take 100 ml warm water
  7. Prepare Filling
  8. Prepare 6 big onions, thinly sliced
  9. Prepare 30 g margarine or vegan butter
  10. Prepare 2 tsp sugar
  11. Prepare 1 tsp red wine vinegar (optional)
  12. Make ready (to taste) salt, pepper, thyme

Steps to make Easy Caramelized Onion Tart:
  1. For the crust: combine all ingredients in a Tupperware container. Close the lid and shake it vigorously until dough comes together. Press the ball of dough in a tart pan lined with baking paper. Pierce with a fork
  2. For the filling: heat a small amount of oil in a wide pan over medium low heat. Add the onions and cook for around 20min, until jammy but not browned! Season to taste, add sugar, vinegar and butter
  3. Pour the filling into the crust and bake in a 180°C/350°F oven for 30min or until onions are caramelized and the crust is a bit golden. I bake this tart on the lower oven rack to prevent onions from burning
