Steps to make Sugar puff sticks:
  1. Pre heat the oven at 170 degrees. Bring the sheets to room temperature and flatten a little with the help of a rolling pin
  2. Then brush with egg and add a layer of sugar. Then turn the sheet half way and press from the sides.
  3. Then brush with egg again and sprinkle with some sugar and cut into thin strips
  4. Then give a complete turnover to make a twist shape
  5. Bake for 10 minutes on lower flame and 7-8 minutes or till golden brown, on upper flame. (For cooking range ovens)
  6. Let cool and serve with tea or coffee.
