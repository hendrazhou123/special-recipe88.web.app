The ingredients needed to make Steamed Banana Cake:
  1. Get 4 pcs mashed banana
  2. Make ready 2 eggs
  3. Take 1/2 cup melted butter
  4. Make ready 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  5. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  6. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  7. Get 1/2 tsp baking soda
  8. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  9. Take 1.5 cups All-purpose flour
  10. Make ready 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  11. Take 5 tbsp nuts
  12. Get 5 tbsp chopped chocolates
  13. Take 60 ml evaporated milk
  14. Take 3/4 cup sugar

Instructions to make Steamed Banana Cake:
  1. Mix wet ingredients (banana, eggs, oil, vanilla, butter, milk)
  2. Combine dry ingredients (flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, chocolates, nuts). Mix well.
  3. Combine wet & dry ingredients.
  4. Grease mold with vegetable oil.
  5. Pour batter to mould.
  6. Put 5 pcs of whole nuts on top of the batter.
  7. Cover with foil.
  8. Steam in already boiling water for 50minutes.
  9. Remove from heat. Let cool and enjoy!
