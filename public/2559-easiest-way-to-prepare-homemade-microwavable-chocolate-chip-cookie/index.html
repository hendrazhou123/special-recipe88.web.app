The ingredients needed to make Microwavable Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. Prepare 1 tablespoon melted butter
  2. Make ready 1 egg yolk
  3. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon vanilla essence
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 tablespoon brown sugar
  5. Get 3 tablespoons all purpose flour
  6. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Prepare 2 tablespoons choc chunks or 3 of choc chips

Steps to make Microwavable Chocolate Chip Cookie:
  1. In a microwave safe mug, put melted butter then add the egg yolk and vanilla then stir with a fork.
  2. Add brown sugar, be generous cause it makes a big difference on taste. Stir in the brown sugar well.
  3. Add in flour and baking powder and mix making sure the flour at the bottom has been incorporated as well.
  4. Throw in chocolate chunks or chips and mix them in. Lay some on top as well.
  5. This is how it looked inside.
  6. Microwave at the highest heat setting, mine was 900watts, for 1½ minutes. You can let it cool for 20 minutes before eating.
  7. Enjoy. I topped mine with ice cream cause I'm a sweet tooth and some raisins.
