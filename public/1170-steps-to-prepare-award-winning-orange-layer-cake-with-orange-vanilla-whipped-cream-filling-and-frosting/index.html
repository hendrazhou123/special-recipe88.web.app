The ingredients needed to make Orange Layer Cake with Orange / Vanilla Whipped Cream Filling and Frosting:
  1. Get For Orange Cake
  2. Take 2 1/2 cups cake flour
  3. Prepare 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon salt
  5. Take 10 tablespoon unsalted butter, at room temperature

  11. Get 1 cup fresh orange juice
  12. Take For Orange Whipped Cream Filling and Frosting
  13. Make ready 3 cups cold heavy whipping cream
  14. Make ready 1/2 cup confectioner's sugar
  15. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla
  16. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon pure orange extract

  18. Take 1/2 cup boiling water
  19. Get 1/2 cup fresh orange juice

Instructions to make Orange Layer Cake with Orange / Vanilla Whipped Cream Filling and Frosting:
  1. Make Orange Cake
  2. Preheat the oven to350. Spray 3 - 8 inch cake pans well with bakers dpray
  3. In a bowl whisk together flour, baking powder and salt
  4. In a large bowl beat butter, sugar and orange zest until fluffy, about 3 minutes
  5. Add eggs one at a time, beating in after each egg. Beat in vanilla and orange extract
  6. Add flour mixture alternating with orange juice just until combined, don't over beat
  7. Divide batter evenly in prepared pans and bake 18 to 20 minutes until a toothpick comes out just clean. Cool 10 minutes in pan then remove to wire racks to cool completely before frosting
  8. Make Orange Whipped Cream Filing and Frosting
  9. Dissolve orange gelitin in boiling water, stirring until fully dissolved. Add cold orange juice and combine, refrigerate while whipping cream
  10. Whip cream tosoft peaks
  11. Beat in sugar, extracts, orange zest and cool but still liquid gelatin. Beat until cream holds its shape
  12. Assemble cake
  13. Place one cake layer, bottom side up on serving plate
  14. Add some orange whipped cream
  15. Add second cake layer, bottom up
  16. Add some more orange filling
  17. Add third cske layer and frost entire cake with the orange whipped cream. If the fillingvis still quite soft then you can refrigerate the cake and remaining frosting for about an hour to firm it up if frosting is difficult
  19. Garnish with shaved chocoolate and orange sprinkles. Refrigerate cake at least 4 hours to allow whipped cream to firm up before slicing
