The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Frosting:
  1. Make ready 1/2 cup butter, softened
  2. Get 1 cup natural peanut butter
  3. Get 2 cup confectioners sugar
  4. Make ready 1-2 Tbs milk
  5. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla

Steps to make Peanut Butter Frosting:
  1. In large bowl, with electric mixer beat butter until fluffy
  2. Add peanut butter and blend thoroughly
  3. Add sugar 1/2 cup at a time, blending between additions
  4. Add vanilla
  5. Add milk a little at a time until desired consistency is achieved
  6. Will make enough to frost one 9x13, or a 9" two layer cake
  7. Chop peanut butter cups for garnish, drizzle with ganache
