The ingredients needed to make Cashew Toffee Milk Chocolate Bark:
  1. Make ready 16 ounces good quality milk or dark chocolate, I used Lindt
  2. Prepare Extra Creamy milk chocolate, chopped
  3. Prepare 2 cups lightly salted whole cashews
  4. Get 1 cup toffee bits

Steps to make Cashew Toffee Milk Chocolate Bark:
  1. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Melt chocolate either in the microwave or over a double boiler on the stove top until smooth
  4. Spread on parchment paper to about 1/4 inch thickness
  5. Place cashews evenly over wet chicilate
  6. Sprinkle evenly with toffee bits. Refrigerate to harden. Break into pieces. Store in refrigerator
