Steps to make Cookies ice cream:
  1. Crush Oreo biscuits roughly and keep aside.
  2. Whip up the cream along with condensed milk until soft peaks
  3. Fold in majority of biscuits, leaving a little aside.
  4. Pour into a container and sprinkle the leftover biscuits on top.
  5. Let it freeze for 4-5 hours in freezer.
  6. Serve cold with more Oreo biscuits.
