The ingredients needed to make Honey Butter Buscuits:
  1. Make ready 300 g Flour
  2. Get 200 ml Buttermilk
  3. Take 3 tbsp Baking Powder
  4. Prepare 3 tbsp Sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp Salt
  6. Get 3 tsp Vanilla Essence
  7. Prepare 100 g Butter (chilled)
  8. Make ready Coating
  9. Prepare Melted Butter
  10. Prepare Honey
  11. Make ready Sugar

Steps to make Honey Butter Buscuits:
  1. Mix all the ingredients in order
  2. Put on baking tray or baking sheet. Coat the top with melted butter & sprinkle some sugar.
  3. Bake for 20 minutes on 200 degree celcius
  4. After 20 minutes, coat the top again with melted butter & honey.
  5. Bake for another 5 minutes
