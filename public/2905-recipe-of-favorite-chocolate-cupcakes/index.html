The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Cupcakes:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 Cup Stiffed All-purpose flour
  2. Get 1/2 tsp Salt
  3. Take 1/2 cup Cocoa powder
  4. Take 1 tsp Baking Soda
  5. Get 1 Cup Caster Sugar
  6. Prepare 1 tsp Coffee
  7. Make ready 1 Cup Water
  8. Make ready 1/3 cup Oil
  9. Prepare 1 Egg
