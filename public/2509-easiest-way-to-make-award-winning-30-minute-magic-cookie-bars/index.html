The ingredients needed to make 30 minute Magic cookie bars:
  1. Prepare 1.5 cups graham crackers crumbs
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup soft or melted butter
  3. Make ready 1-14 oz can sweetened condensed milk
  4. Take 3/4 cup butterscotch chips
  5. Make ready 3/4 cup chocolate chips
  6. Get 3/4 cup white chocolate chips
  7. Get 1.5 cups shredded sweetened coconut
  8. Take 1 cup chopped walnuts

Instructions to make 30 minute Magic cookie bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Mix butter and graham cracker crumbs in mixing bowl and press onto a greased or parchment paper lined 13x9” baking pan. Pour condensed milk evenly over it.
  3. Spread all 3 kids of chips over graham crackers and press down a little bit into the crust.
  4. Sprinkle walnuts and coconut evenly on top and press down as well. Bake 20 minutes or until edges are a golden brown. Allow to cool for an hour and cut into squares. You can eat as is or place in fridge to firm up even more.
