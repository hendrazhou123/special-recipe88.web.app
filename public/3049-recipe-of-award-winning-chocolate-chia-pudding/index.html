The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chia Pudding:
  1. Get 4 Tablespoon Chia Seed
  2. Prepare 1 Cup Coconut Milk
  3. Take 1 Tablespoon Yogurt
  4. Get 1 Tablespoon Cacao
  5. Prepare 1 Tablespoon Honey
  6. Make ready Pinch Salt
  7. Take 1/2 Teaspoon Vanilla
  8. Prepare 1 Banana
  9. Prepare Dark chocolate for Shredding (topping)
  10. Take Berries (to your preference)
  11. Prepare Cinnamon (sprinkle on bananas)
  12. Make ready 1 Teaspoon Peanut Butter

  1. Add chia seeds, yogurt, honey, vanila, salt, to coconut milk

  3. Put in the refrigerator over night
  4. Add your toppings and drizzle honey on top of them, shred dark chocolate and sprinkle on top. Add peanut butter
