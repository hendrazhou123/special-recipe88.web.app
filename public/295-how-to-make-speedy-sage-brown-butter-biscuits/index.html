The ingredients needed to make Sage Brown Butter Biscuits:
  1. Prepare 2-1/2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp baking soda
  5. Get 1 cup cold butter (1 stick)
  6. Get 4 tbsp chopped fresh sage (dry ok)
  7. Take 3/4 cup half and half or whole milk
  8. Get 1 tsp lemon juice or white vinegar
  9. Make ready 1 egg and 1 tbsp water or milk whisked together to create an egg wash for the biscuits
