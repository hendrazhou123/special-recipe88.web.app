The ingredients needed to make Sharon Press Cookies (cookie press):
  1. Make ready 3/4 lb Softened butter
  2. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Sugar
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 cup Cornstarch
  4. Make ready 2 cup Sifted flour
  5. Take 1/2 lb Finely grated almonds (1cup)
  6. Make ready 1 as needed Food color
  7. Take 1 Egg
  8. Make ready 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Steps to make Sharon Press Cookies (cookie press):
  1. Cream butter and sugar
  2. Add egg and vanilla
  3. Add flour and cornstarch
  4. Add almonds and finish mixing with hands
  5. Divide dough and add food color as desired
  6. Chill 1 hour
  7. Run thru cookie press onto un-greased sheet about 1 inch apart
  8. Bake around 400?F for 7-10 minutes (don't let bottom get too brown)
  9. Rest cookies on sheet for a couple minutes after baking
  10. Remove cookies from sheet with spatula carefully to finish cooling on wire rack (be careful the cookies are fragile)
