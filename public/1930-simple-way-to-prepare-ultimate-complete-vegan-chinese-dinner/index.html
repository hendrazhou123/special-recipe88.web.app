The ingredients needed to make Complete Vegan Chinese Dinner:
  1. Make ready 350 grams boiled rice
  2. Make ready 2 cups onions sliced thin
  3. Prepare 1 cup cabbage sliced thin
  4. Make ready 1 cup bell pepper sliced thin
  5. Make ready 1 cup Ginger garlic and chilli paste
  6. Take Oil for frying
  7. Get 1 1/2 cup spring onions
  8. Get 5 tbsp soy sauce
  9. Prepare 3 tbsp vinegar
  10. Take 1 pack chinese spice mix
  11. Get 1 cup tofu dusted in corn flour ready to fry
  12. Get Salt as per taste
  13. Take Pepper as per taste
  14. Prepare 1 cup shredded carrots, cabbages, onion and strained through muslin cloth
