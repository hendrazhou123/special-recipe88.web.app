The ingredients needed to make Best almond flour pancakes:
  1. Prepare 2 eggs
  2. Make ready 6 tablespoons almond milk
  3. Make ready 2 tablespoons olive oil
  4. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla
  5. Make ready 1 1/4 cups almond flour
  6. Take 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  7. Get 1 teaspoon baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/8 teaspoon salt

Almond flour pancakes are naturally gluten free and are full of protein. Delicious, light and fluffy, a wonderful pancake breakfast. Grain-free, gluten-free refined sugar-free almond flour pancakes. This easy and delicious paleo pancake recipe is sure to please the whole family!

Steps to make Best almond flour pancakes:
  1. In a blender combine the eggs, almond milk, olive oil, and vanilla. Blend for 30 seconds.
  2. Add the almond flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt. Blend until smooth. Let sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Heat a nonstick pan over medium-low heat and lightly grease (I sprayed with avocado spray). Pour less than a 1/4 cup (or 3 tablespoons) onto the pan to form small pancakes. Let cook for around 2 minutes, then flip and let cook for 1-2 more minutes.
  4. Serve hot with butter and syrup, or any other desired toppings.
