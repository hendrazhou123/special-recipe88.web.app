The ingredients needed to make Cake:
  1. Make ready 100-150 gr sugar
  2. Make ready 3 Egg
  3. Make ready 1 pk vanilla sugar
  4. Take 1 pk baking powder
  5. Make ready 1-2 dl milk
  6. Prepare 250 gr wheat flour

Steps to make Cake:
  1. Use a big bowl.
  2. Make 3 eggs inside
  3. Make the butter warm, until flow. not hot!
  4. Squirl the egg
  5. Add the rest of ingredients into the bowl. At first add only a small amount of the milk inside
  6. Mix all. Add additional milk until you have a strong flowing cream
  7. Coat the baking form inside with little butter or cover with baking paper
  8. Fill the form with the cake cream. make only 3/4 full.
  9. Heat the Oven to 180°. Upper and lower heat. When hot, move the cake form into the middle of the Oven
  10. Bake the cake for about 45min. check color.
  11. Eat and enjoy
  12. Suggestions; add some dry fruits inside. you can already put the dry fruits into rum or other alcoholics for taste
