The ingredients needed to make Microwave Peanut Butter cookie:
  1. Prepare 4 Tbs flour
  2. Take 1/4 tsp baking powder
  3. Prepare 1 Tbs sugar
  4. Prepare 4 Tbs milk (I use almond)
  5. Take 3 Tbs natural peanut butter
  6. Prepare 1/4 tsp vanilla
  7. Make ready Pinch cinnamon
  8. Make ready 2 Tbs mini chocolate chips

Steps to make Microwave Peanut Butter cookie:
