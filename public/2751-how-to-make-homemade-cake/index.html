Instructions to make Cake:
  1. Open both the biscuit in blending jar crushed them. By hands also. Add 2-3 tsp. Sugar
  2. Grind them till fine powder. Stain this paste to. Get fine powder.
  3. In a large bowl. Add water to start boil. Take Another pan and make it double boiler. Add chocolate to melt. After melting add 1 tsp of butter to Glaze.
  4. Take 3 tsp of this paste add to the powder mix them. Add few spoon of little warm Milk to make it semi liquid. Mix them very well. Add crushed dry fruit.
  5. Add 1/4 tsp baking powder mix them. Grease the baking pan transfer that mixture in baking pan set the temperature for 40 - 50 minutes in pre heat oven or otg. I use otg so it takes time
