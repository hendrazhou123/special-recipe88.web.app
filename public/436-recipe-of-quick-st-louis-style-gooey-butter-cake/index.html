The ingredients needed to make St. Louis Style Gooey Butter Cake:
  1. Prepare 36 oz yellow cake mix (2 boxes) your favorite brand
  2. Prepare 1 lb butter, melted
  3. Take 1 lb Cream cheese
  4. Take 6 eggs
  5. Prepare 8 cups powder sugar (approx 2lb bag)
  6. Take 2 tsp Vanilla extract
  7. Get 1 tbsp Cinnamon

Instructions to make St. Louis Style Gooey Butter Cake:
  1. In a bowl add your cake mix and melted butter, mix well and slowly add 2 eggs until well incorporated.
  2. In a separate bowl add your cream cheese, 4 eggs and powder sugar (slowly add sugar in stages to reduce mess) and beat until smoothe, no lumps
  3. In a greased pan pour your yellow cake mix and evenly spread with a spatula
  4. Then pour your cream cheese mixture on top of the cake mix and sprinkle with cinnamon.
  5. In a 350° preheated oven bake for 45-50 mins until the top is golden brown. There will be some wiggle in the center, that's normal.
  6. Let chill for a few hours, overnight is even better. Cut and enjoy!
