The ingredients needed to make Keto Peanut Butter Bread:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Peanut Flour
  2. Make ready 8 large egg yolks or 4 eggs
  3. Prepare 12 oz cream cheese
  4. Get 1 TBSP vanilla
  5. Get 8 TBSP Kerrygold Butter
  6. Take 2 tsp baking powder
  7. Make ready 2-4 TBSP Brown Sugar Truvia depending on taste
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
