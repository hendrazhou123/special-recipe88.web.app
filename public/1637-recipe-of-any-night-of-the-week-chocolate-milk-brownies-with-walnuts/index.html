The ingredients needed to make chocolate milk brownies with walnuts:
  1. Prepare 1 cup granulated sugar
  2. Get 3/4 cup melted butter
  3. Get 2 tbsp water
  4. Get 2 large eggs
  5. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 1 1/3 cups all purpose flour
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 cup nestle nesquik chocolate milk powder
  8. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Prepare 1/4 tsp salt
  10. Get 3/4 cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Steps to make chocolate milk brownies with walnuts:
  1. Preheat oven to 350° and grease a 13x9 bakeing pan . Mix sugar water and vanilla and melted butter into a large bowl.
  2. Combine flour, Nesquik, baking powder and salt in medium bowl; stir into sugar mixture
  3. . Stir in nuts. Spread into prepared baking pan.
  4. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes or until wooden pick inserted in center comes out slightly sticky. Cool in pan on wire rack. Cut into bars.
