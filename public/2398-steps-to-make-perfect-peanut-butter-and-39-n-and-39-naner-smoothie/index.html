The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter 'n 'Naner Smoothie:
  1. Take 1 oz. peanut butter
  2. Prepare 3 medium sized frozen bananas (peeled and sliced)
  3. Prepare 1/3 cup rolled oats (uncooked)
  4. Make ready 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  6. Get 1 1/2 cups milk (soy or almond is okay)
