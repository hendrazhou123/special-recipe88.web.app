The ingredients needed to make Chocolate muffins:
  1. Make ready 250 g self raising flour
  2. Take 25 g coco powder
  3. Make ready 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Prepare 175 g caster sugar
  5. Make ready 175 ml milk
  6. Take 2 large eggs beaten
  7. Get 75 g chocolate chunks
  8. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Make ready 100 ml sunflower oil

Instructions to make Chocolate muffins:
  1. Preheat oven to gas mark 5
  2. Sift together the flour, coco and baking powder
  3. Stir in the sugar note: I had to use a bit of brown sugar bc I didn’t have enough
  4. Add in the remaining ingredients and mix together
  5. Spoon in to cases
  6. Cook for 20 minutes
  7. Leave to cool for 10 minutes
  8. Done ✅
