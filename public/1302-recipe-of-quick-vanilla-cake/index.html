The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cake:
  1. Take 180 grams butter
  2. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 2 each eggs
  4. Take 1 1/2 cup allpourpose flour
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla essence
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup milk
  7. Get 1 tsp baking powder

This vanilla cake is like, the best cake I have ever made. It's not too bland, and it's not too sweet. This is great for all occasions and easy to do in your spare time. This perfectly Moist Vanilla Cake Recipe is a slightly denser than average cake with a tight crumb.

Instructions to make Vanilla cake:
  1. Preheat the oven between 170-180 deg.
  2. Take a mixing bowl and whip together butter and sugar til the mixture is creamy. Add in vanilla and mix well
  3. Add one egg at a time and beat well.add in the milk
  4. Seive flour and baking powder into the mixture and fold this micture with a wooden spoon. Do not use blender… Just fold it again and again.
  5. Put it in a broad baking dish and put it in oven for 15 to 20 mins or til a toothpick comes out clean when poked at center.
