The ingredients needed to make Cookie Cold Cake:
  1. Get 2 eggs
  2. Get 1 Cup Sugar
  3. Make ready 1 Cup Ghee/Butter
  4. Prepare 2 Rolls Marie Biscuits
  5. Take 2 tbsp Cocoa Powder
  6. Get Melted Cooking Chocolate

Steps to make Cookie Cold Cake:
  1. Mix all the ingredients together except biscuits in a non-stick pan and turn on the flame.
  2. Cook with constant stirring it until ghee starts separating. Now add the biscuits and mix well.
  3. Cook for further 2 minutes and transfer it into already greased foil paper lined in a pan.
  4. And let it cool down and spread melted cooking Chocolate. And freeze it for 4 hours and then take it out cut slices and serve.
