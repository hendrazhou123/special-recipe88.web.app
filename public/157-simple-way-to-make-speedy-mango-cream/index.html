Steps to make Mango Cream:
  1. Take the mango and peel it and dice the mango to the nice small pieces and chill them in fridge for 10 min.
  2. Now Take an attractive transparent bowl and layer half of the mango pieces and spread evenly.
  3. Then take 1 and 1/2 tbsp of chilled whip cream and spread it evenly on the above mango pieces.(l used ReadyMade whipped cream)
  4. Repeat steps 2 and 3 again respectively.
  5. Then place 1 scoop of mango ice cream over it. (you can increase Mango ice-cream scoops as per your taste)
  6. Then place remaining whipped cream over ice cream scoop. Finally, place some mango pieces over the cream. Your mango cream is ready.
