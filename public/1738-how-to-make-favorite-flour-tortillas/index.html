The ingredients needed to make Flour Tortillas:
  1. Get 4 cups flour
  2. Take 1/2 cup lard
  3. Prepare 1 tablespoon salt
  4. Prepare 1/4 tablespoon baking powder
  5. Get 1-1/2 cups warm water (depends on humidity)

Steps to make Flour Tortillas:
  1. Take flour, salt, lard, baking powder in a big bowl and combine to course crumbles
  2. Work Till it holds its shape when squeezed.
  3. Slowly add warm water and knead till a smooth dough forms.
  4. Make golf size balls
  5. Flaten each ball to a thin disk with a rolling pin. On a lightly floured surface.
  6. Cook on med high heat on a comal 1-2 min on each side till golden bubble like patches form.
  7. Remove from heat.
  8. Cool on a cotton cloth. store in a plastic bag.
