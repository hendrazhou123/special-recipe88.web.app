The ingredients needed to make Vanilla (simple eggless cake recipe):
  1. Make ready 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  4. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Get 1 tsp white vinegar
  6. Get 5 Tbsp vegetable oil
  7. Take 1 cup water
  8. Get 1 1/2 tsp pure vanilla extract
