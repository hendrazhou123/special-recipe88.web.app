  2. Get 1/4 grated fresh lime zest fom about 8 limes

  4. Get 2 tsp coarse ground pepper

Instructions to make Chipotle Lime seasoning salt:
  1. Preheat oven to 350. Line a baking sheet with parchment paper
  2. Combine all ingredients in a bowl, spread evenly on parchment lined baking sheet, bake for 5 minute until lime zest is dry, cool
  3. When cool, transfer to spice grinder or blender and grind until ground, stoe in a tight fitted container
  4. This seasoned salt is great on grilled meats. roasts, and vegetables!
