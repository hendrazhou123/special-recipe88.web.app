The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Almond Milk:
  1. Get 1 cup Raw almonds
  2. Take 4 1/2 cup Water, divided
  3. Prepare 4 tbsp Pure maple syrup
  4. Make ready 2 tsp Vanilla extract

Instructions to make Vanilla Almond Milk:
  1. Soak almonds in 2 cups water for 12-24 hours.
  2. Drain almonds and place in blender with maple syrup, vanilla and 1 1/2 cups water. Cover and blend for 3 minutes at high speed. Scrape down sides add 1 cup water and blend for one minute more.
  3. Strain through cheesecloth or thin cotton towel covering a strainer set on a bowl. Press all juices out.
  4. Will keep in fridge 3-4 days. Recipe adapted from food 52 website.
