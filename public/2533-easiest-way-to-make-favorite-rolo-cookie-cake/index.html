The ingredients needed to make Rolo cookie cake:
  1. Get 500 g self raising flour
  2. Make ready 250 g butter (unsalted)
  3. Take 200 g caster sugar
  4. Make ready 10 g bicarbonate soda
  5. Make ready 1 egg
  6. Get 200 g chocolate chips
  7. Take Oven setting
  8. Take 170 *c
  9. Take 20-25 mins cooking time

Instructions to make Rolo cookie cake:
  1. Put the flour, caster sugar, butter & egg in to a bowl.
  2. Then add the bicarbonate soda, then the chocolate chips.
  3. Mix together and form a flat cookie dough in the mixing bowl.Then using a ice cream scoop, equally put in to the Yorkshire pudding tin.
  4. Then press with your fingers to mould the cookie dough in the tin.
  5. Then you will need 6 packets of rolos, place 3 in to the cookie dough moulds.
  6. Then with the remaining cookie dough, get yourself a round cutter (I used a drink glass) and make the tops and place them on.
  8. Put them in a pre heated oven at 170•C for 20mins.
  9. Then once cooled after 3-4 mins get them out. You can have them with ice cream & caramel sauce or just on their own.
