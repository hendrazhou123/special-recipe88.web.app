The ingredients needed to make Buffalo-Garlic Butter Sub:
  1. Get 2 large boneless, skinless chicken breast
  2. Prepare 4 C chicken stock
  3. Take 1 C buffalo sauce
  4. Take 2 T garlic butter
  5. Make ready 1 baguette
  6. Take 1/2 yellow bell pepper; julienne
  7. Get 1/2 red onion; julienne

Steps to make Buffalo-Garlic Butter Sub:
  1. Cover chicken breasts with stock in a saucepot. Add cold water to cover if necessary.
  2. Simmer until fully cooked, about 20 minutes.
  3. Remove chicken from pot and transfer to a mixing bowl. When cool enough to handle, shred with your hands or forks. Reserve stock for alternative use if desired.
  4. Add bell pepper and onions to chicken. Mix. Add buffalo sauce. Mix.
  5. Spread garlic butter inside the baguette. Toast at 400¬∫ for approximately 1-2 minutes.
  6. Add chicken mixture to baguette, and cut in half. Serve.
  7. Alternatively, layer chicken mixture on the baguette after the garlic butter, top with cheese, and melt while toasting.
