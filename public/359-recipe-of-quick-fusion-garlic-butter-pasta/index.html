The ingredients needed to make Fusion Garlic Butter Pasta:
  1. Prepare 1/2 lb thin spaghetti
  2. Take 4 cloves garlic, sliced thin
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  4. Take 1-2 tsp red chilli flakes
  5. Get 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  6. Prepare 2 1/2 tbsp tamari or shoyu
  7. Prepare 2 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  8. Make ready 4-5 sprigs Italian parsley, washed, leaves removed, chopped
  9. Get 1/2-1 cup pasta water
  10. Prepare 3-4 tbsp grated parmesan cheese
