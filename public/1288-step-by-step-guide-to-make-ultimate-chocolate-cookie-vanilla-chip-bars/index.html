The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Cookie Vanilla Chip Bars:
  1. Take bars
  2. Get 1/2 cup salted butter plus 2 tablespoons, softened
  3. Get 1 1/2 cup packed dark brown sugar
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Take 3 large eggs
  6. Get 3 tsp vanilla
  7. Prepare 2 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Prepare 2 cup all purpose flour
  9. Get 3/4 cup dutch process cocoa powder
  10. Take 2 cup vanilla chips equivalent to 1, 12 ounce bag
  11. Take 1 cup chopped almonds

Steps to make Chocolate Cookie Vanilla Chip Bars:
  1. Preheat oven to 350°F.
  2. Grease a 9"" by 13" baking pan.
  3. In mixing bowl, cream butter and sugar until light and fluffy in texture.
  4. Beat in one egg at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  5. Add in vanilla then salt.
  6. In another bowl, combine flour, cocoa and baking powder. Mix together well.
  7. Slowly add to butter mixture. Add in nuts and chips.
  8. Batter is thick and on the dryer side. This is fine.
  9. Dampen your hands to pat batter into baking tray. This prevents batter from sticking to your hands.
  10. Bake 20-25 minutes. Cool for 10-15 minutes before cutting and serving.
  11. Store in airtight container for several days.
