The ingredients needed to make EASY banana bread (nut free):
  1. Get 4 extra ripe bananas
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup milk
  3. Prepare 2/3 cup sugar (you can use brown sugar for a richer darker banana bread)
  4. Prepare 3 eggs
  5. Make ready 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  6. Make ready 2 and 2/3 cup bisquick
  7. Prepare 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
