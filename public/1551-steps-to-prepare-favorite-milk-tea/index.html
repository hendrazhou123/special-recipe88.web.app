The ingredients needed to make Milk Tea:
  1. Prepare 1 1/2 glass milk
  2. Make ready 1/2 glass water
  3. Get 1 peel of Cardamom
  4. Take 2 teaspoon sugar (as per your taste)
  5. Get 1 1/2 teaspoon Tea powder (available in Indian grocery store)
  6. Get Coffee/tea strainer

Milk tea combines the smooth, semi-bitter taste of strong tea with the creamy richness of milk. You can prepare both hot and iced versions of milk tea.

Instructions to make Milk Tea:
  1. Take a small deep pan add milk and water together bring it to boil then add sugar,tea powder and peeling of cardamom to it
  2. Let it boil for 3-4 minutes on medium flame in between stir the tea with a help of steel spoon/fork so that boiling tea will not spill over
  3. After 3-4 minutes tea will be ready
  4. Then with the help of strainer, strain the tea to a glass and serve hot with some cookies/biscuits
