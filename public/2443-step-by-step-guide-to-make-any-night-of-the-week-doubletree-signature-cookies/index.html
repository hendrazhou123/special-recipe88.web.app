The ingredients needed to make DoubleTree Signature Cookies:
  1. Get 1/2 pound butter, softened (2 sticks)
  2. Get 3/4 cup + 1 tablespoon of sugar
  3. Take ¾ cup brown sugar
  4. Take 2 large eggs
  5. Get 1 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
  6. Get 1/4 lemon juice
  7. Get 2 1/4 cups flour
  8. Get 1/2 cup rolled oats
  9. Get 1 teaspoon baking soda
  10. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  11. Take Pinch cinnamon
  12. Prepare 2 2/3 cups semi sweet chocolate chips
  13. Prepare 1 3/4 cups walnuts or pecans

Steps to make DoubleTree Signature Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 300°F
  2. Cream butter, sugar and brown sugar
  3. Add eggs, vanilla and lemon juice until light and fluffy, then add flour, oats, baking soda, salt and cinnamon. Don't overmix.
  4. Stir in chocolate chips and nuts
  5. Portion onto baking sheet (parchment paper) leave 2" apart. Bake 20-23 minutes until edges are golden brown. Let cool.
