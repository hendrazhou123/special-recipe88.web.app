The ingredients needed to make Pound cake:
  1. Get 1 cup room temperature butter
  2. Prepare 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 1/2 cup Yogurt/sour cream
  4. Get 11/2 cup Flour
  5. Take 2 tablespoon vanilla extract
  6. Make ready 4 eggs
  7. Make ready 1 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions to make Pound cake:
  1. Put the butter in a bowl and use electric mixture to mix until fluffy then put sugar and mix
  2. Then put 4 eggs in the batter one by one then put the yogurt/sour cream in the mixture and mix
  3. Then in another bowl put 11/2 cup of flour and baking powder then slowly put the flour mixture in the batter and mix all the ingredients well
  4. Put it in a pan and bake it for 40 mins
