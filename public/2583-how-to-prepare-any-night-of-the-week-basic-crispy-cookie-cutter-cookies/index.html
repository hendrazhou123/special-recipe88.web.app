The ingredients needed to make Basic Crispy Cookie Cutter Cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 tbsp Milk or beaten eggs (Milk makes the cookies white, and milky!)
  2. Make ready 35 grams Unsalted margarine or unsalted butter
  3. Get 3 tbsp Sugar
  4. Prepare 120 grams White flour
  5. Prepare 2 drops Vanilla extract (optional)

Steps to make Basic Crispy Cookie Cutter Cookies:
  1. Microwave the butter for about 10 seconds, and mix with spatula by mashing. If you bring the butter to room temperature, you don't need to microwave.
  2. Add sugar and vanilla extract, and mix well.
  3. Add a beaten eggs or milk, and mix well until the mixture is smooth and no longer lumpy as illustrated in the picture. I recommend using a whisk.
  4. Add flour, mix, and roll into a ball. Using a spatula works better.
  5. Place the dough on top of plastic wrap or on the work surface with flour. Roll out with a rolling pin into 2 ~ 3 mm.
  6. Cut out shapes with your favorite cookie cutters.
  7. Lay on parchment paper over a baking sheet. You only see one baking sheet in the picture, but you will make 2 baking sheets worth of cookies.
