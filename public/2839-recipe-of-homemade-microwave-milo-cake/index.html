The ingredients needed to make Microwave Milo Cake:
  1. Get 1 Egg
  2. Get 2 tbsp Flour
  3. Take 2 tbsp Milo Powder (or Choc Powder)
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp Sugar
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp Oil/Melted Butter
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp Vanilla Essence
  8. Take Icing Sugar (optional)

Steps to make Microwave Milo Cake:
  1. Add all the ingredients except icing sugar.
  2. Mix well
  3. Put in a microwavable container and microwave for 3 minutes
  4. Add icing sugar or your preferred topping!
