Steps to make Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Prep: Leave butter out to soften to room temperature
  2. Prep: Heat oven to 350°
  3. Mix: Whisk together dry ingredients in a medium bowl.
  4. Mix: Use an Electric Mixer to beat together butter & sugars. Add in eggs, vanilla and peanut butter.
  5. Combine: Slowly add the dry ingredients. Careful not to over-mix.
  6. Form small cookie balls in a 3x4 pattern on a parchment paper lined pan. Cook for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Move to Cooling Rack 2 minutes after removing from the oven.
