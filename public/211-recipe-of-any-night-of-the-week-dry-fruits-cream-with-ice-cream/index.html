The ingredients needed to make Dry Fruits cream with Ice Cream:
  1. Get 2 cups Fresh cream
  2. Make ready 2 tbsp Cashews
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Raisins
  4. Prepare 2 tbsp Almonds
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Pistachio
  6. Get 3-4 tbsp Anjeer paste
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp Vanilla essence
  8. Get 3-4 tbsp Condensed Milk

Instructions to make Dry Fruits cream with Ice Cream:
  1. For making the ice cream take a bowl and add full fat cream (1cup)to it.
  2. Whisk it with the help of whisker to make it Creamy.
  3. Add condensed milk and vanilla essence to it.
  4. Now mix it all well and pour it into an air tight container.
  5. Refrigerate it for 5-6hrs and you will see the ice cream is made.
  6. Now next is to make the dry fruits cream, so for it again take a fresh and clean bowl and add (1cup) freshcream to it.
  7. Whisk it for 1minute and add the anjeer paste to it.
  8. Mix it well and add the chopped dry fruits over it.
  9. Put a scoop of ice cream onto the top of the cream.
  10. Serve it chill.
