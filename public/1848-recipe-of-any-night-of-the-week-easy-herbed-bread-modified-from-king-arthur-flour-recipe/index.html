The ingredients needed to make Easy herbed bread (modified from King Arthur Flour recipe):
  1. Make ready 4 cups bread flour + extra for dusting
  2. Get 3 tsp herbs de Provence
  3. Take 2 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1.5 tsp sugar
  5. Take 2 tsp instant dry yeast
  6. Get 1.5 cups warm water
  7. Prepare 1 egg for eggwash, lightly beaten

Instructions to make Easy herbed bread (modified from King Arthur Flour recipe):
  1. Mix all dry ingredients together in a bowl and form a well in the center
  2. Add 1.5 cups of warm water gradually and stir
  3. Knead dough with hands, dusting with extra flour if dough is sticky until a soft smooth dough is formed
  4. Rest the dough in a well greased bowl, covered with an oiled cling wrap for 1 hour in a warm place. Dough should be more than doubled in size by now.
  5. Remove dough and place on a flourboard/work surface lightly dusted with flour. Gently punch out air pockets and shape dough into a circle by folding and pinching dough.
  6. Place dough on a greased baking sheet and rest for another 30-45 minute for 2nd rise. Preheat oven to 190 degrees Celsius in the meantime.
  7. Slice the top of the bread with decorative patterns using a sharp knife and brush surface of dough with eggwash. Bake in the oven for 30-35min until bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped. Leave to cool for 30min before slicing.
  8. Serve warm with soups/olive oil or use it to make sandwiches. Voila! All done!
  9. Note: dough will be a little sticky and soft. Dust with extra flour as needed.
