The ingredients needed to make Chicken "Piqué macho":
  1. Take 8 pieces chicken thighs or 1whole chicken breast, chopped
  2. Get 6-8 Hot dog sausages, sliced diagonally into short pieces
  3. Prepare to taste Salt and pepper
  4. Take to taste Cayenne pepper
  5. Get 2-4 tbsp Margarine or oil for browning the chicken
  6. Make ready 1 large onion, diced
  7. Get 1 bell pepper, diced or sliced
  8. Get 2-4 tbsp Soya sauce
  9. Get 1 tomato, diced
  10. Prepare 1 cup tomato puree, juice or paste
  11. Get 2 carrots, sliced
  12. Take as needed Water
  13. Make ready 4 hard boiled eggs, for serving
  14. Make ready French fries for serving
Instructions to make Chicken "Piqué macho":
  1. Add the margarine or oil to a non stick skillet, when hot add the chicken and season with salt, pepper and Cayenne pepper.
  2. Add the sausage pieces to the pan with the chicken.
  3. Add the diced onions and bell pepper to the skillet and continue sautéing/browning everything.
  4. When onions are translucent and the chicken is brown add about 1/4cup water and all the soya sauce and tomato puree, simmer.
  5. Add the tomato and carrots, adjust salt to taste. Simmer. If more water is need add a little at a time.
  6. When the carrots are soft serve over a bed of French fries with a sliced boiled egg on top. Enjoy.
