The ingredients needed to make Dark Chocolates:
  1. Get 1 cup cocoa,
  2. Take 80 gm unsalted butter,
  3. Prepare 1 cup icing sugar,
  4. Prepare 1/2 cup milk powder
  5. Prepare as required Chopped dryfruit

Steps to make Dark Chocolates:
  1. In a double boiler melt unsalted butter,then add seived cocoa powder and icing sugar to this along with seived milk powder
  2. Now mix everything properly on double boiler,let it come to room temperature
  3. Grease the chocolate mould,fill in this chocolate mixture stuff it with dryfruit coarsely grinded to this
  4. Freeze it in freezer for six hours,unmould it
