  6. Prepare 1 banana
  7. Get peanut butter
  8. Make ready syrup
  9. Make ready powdered sugar

Steps to make Peanut Butter & Banana Stuffed French Toast:
  1. Mix egg, milk, cinnamon, and vanilla together
  2. Spread peanut butter on bread
  3. Slice banana and arrange on bread,only half of the bread. And then fold into sandwich
  4. Dip and coat both sides of the sandwich with the egg bath mixture
  5. Put in heated non stick skillet (or use cooking spray) and brown both sides and stand the sandwich on the smaller sides to cook the still wet parts.
  6. Chop the rest of the banana and put on top of sandwich, then powdered sugar, and then syrup. And enjoy!!! Make sure to post pictures of your work (:
