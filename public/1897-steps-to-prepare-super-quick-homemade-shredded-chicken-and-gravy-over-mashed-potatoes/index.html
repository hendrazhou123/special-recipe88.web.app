Steps to make Shredded chicken and gravy over mashed potatoes:
  1. Boil chicken with cajun seasoning until done. Let cool and shred. (DO NOT THROW OUT WATER!!)
  2. Sauté onion with 2 tlbs butter add oil and flour to make roux.
  3. Add stock from boiled chicken. Salt and pepper to taste Add water if to thick.
  4. Add chicken and heat throughout.
  5. Pour over mashed potatoes
