The ingredients needed to make Salted Brown Butter Butterscotch Bars:
  1. Make ready 250 g APF
  2. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 230 g unsalted butter, browned
  5. Get 280 g brown sugar
  6. Take 1 whole egg plus 1 yolk
  7. Make ready 1/2 cup butterscotch morsels, mine melted in the sun
  8. Make ready 2 tsp vanilla extract
  9. Make ready Salt, for sprinkling on top
  10. Get 95 g Walnuts, chopped (optional)

Steps to make Salted Brown Butter Butterscotch Bars:
  1. Mix wet and dry ingredients separately, thrn combine.
  2. Fold in butterscotch chips and walnuts if using.
  3. Evenly spread in prepared 8x8 inch pan. Sprinkle salt.
  4. Bake in preheated 170 degree Celsius oven for 35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out mostly clean.
  5. Let cool around 10 minutes before cutting. It tastes super delicious but a bit too buttery when still warm.
