The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake #Authormarathon #chocolatebakingcontest:
  1. Take 400 g all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa
  3. Get 11/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Make ready 200 g sugar
  6. Take 200 g margarine
  7. Get 4 eggs
  8. Prepare 2 drops vanilla essence
  9. Get 1 cup milk

  1. Heat oven 180 degrees
