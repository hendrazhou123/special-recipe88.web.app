Instructions to make Sugar cookies:
  1. Mix butter and sugar, add egg mixed well.
  2. Pour your flavor mix and add flour and milk. Do not over mix, make a soft dough and refrigate for 20mins
  3. Bring your dough out and make round balls. Place in a bake tray and press each ball with fork or any other shape u want. Bake for 15mins. Enjoy
