  2. Make ready 1 Cup Light Corn Syrup

  5. Take sheet Baking

Instructions to make 'No Bake' Peanut Butter Lovers Dream Squares:
  1. Put your corn squares into large bowl. Set Aside.
  2. Add Corn Syrup and Sugar into pan. Simmer on med/low, stirring occasionally until Sugar is completely Dissolved.
  3. Add Peanut Butter and continue to Stir until melted. Immediately pour over your corn squares. Carefully mix together until completely mixed.
  4. Pour onto your baking sheet lined with parchment paper. Let cool. Cut into desired pieces, eat and enjoy
