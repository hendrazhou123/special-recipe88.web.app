The ingredients needed to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Get 1 c Butter
  2. Prepare 1 1/2 c Sugar
  3. Prepare 2 Eggs
  4. Get 1 tsp Soda
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Salt
  6. Get 1 tsp Vanilla
  7. Get 1 tsp Cream of Tartar
  8. Get 3 1/2 c Flour

Instructions to make Sugar Cookies:
  1. Cream together butter and sugar for at least 2 minutes.
  2. Add in eggs, vanilla, soda, salt, and cream of tartar until combined in the batter.
  3. Slowly add in flour 1 cup at a time.
  4. Roll out to 1/2"to 1/4". Cut dough out with cookie cutters. Chill for 3-4 hours.
  5. Bake at 375 degrees for 6-8 minutes. DO NOT OVERBAKE!
  6. Notes: The best results that I have had baking this cookie is when they are a bit under-baked. The color will be a light brown and the center of the cookies drop down a little bit from the edges.
