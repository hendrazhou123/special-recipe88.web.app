The ingredients needed to make #cakedecoration:
  1. Take 4 cups Flour
  2. Make ready 10 Egg
  3. Prepare tbspn Baking powder
  4. Get 2 Simas
  5. Take 2 cup Sugar
  6. Get For the butter icing
  7. Make ready 1 Simas
  8. Get 500 g Icing sugar
  9. Take Milk 2tnspn
  10. Prepare Colour of your choice
  11. Make ready For the drip
  12. Prepare Cocoa powder
  13. Get Icing sugar
  14. Prepare Little warm water
Steps to make #cakedecoration:
  1. I started by mixing my sugar and butter till its fluffy I then add my egg and turn then my flour bit by bit followed by baking powder n flavour and turn it well then bake allow it to cool before decoration
  2. And for the butter icing i put my simas in a bowl then icing sugar and turn it then my powdered milk and flavour till it soft and fluffy then is now ready for decoration
  3. And for the drip I use cocaa powder icing sugar and little warm water and turn it together I then put it in a nylon back and decorate that's all.
