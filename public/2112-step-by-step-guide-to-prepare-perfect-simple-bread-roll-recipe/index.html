The ingredients needed to make Simple Bread/Roll Recipe:
  1. Make ready 1 Cup AP Flour
  2. Make ready 2 TBSP Mayo
  3. Prepare 1 TSP Salt
  4. Get 1 TSP Baking Powder
  5. Prepare 1/2 Cup Milk
  6. Take optional
  7. Take 3 TBSP Melted Butter
  8. Make ready 1 TSP Parmesan Cheese
  9. Take 1 TSP Garlic Powder
  10. Take 1 TSP Parsley

Steps to make Simple Bread/Roll Recipe:
  1. Preheat oven 350
  2. Mix all ingredients besides optional ingredients. Mix lightly to avoid tough bread!
  3. You can put the dough in muffin tins or on baking sheet as I did.
  4. In small bowl mix optional ingredients and brush tops of dough.
  5. Bake until lightly brown. Mine baked around 10-13 minutes. Once out the oven I sprinkled more parmesan on top. This recipe makes 5 rolls, my son decided to eat one before I took a pic! Enjoy!!!!
