The ingredients needed to make Butter Chicken:
  1. Get 1/4 cup ghee
  2. Get 2 lbs boneless chicken thigh, cut into chunks
  3. Make ready 1 lemon juiced
  4. Make ready 3 tbsp yogurt
  5. Get 1 box Shan butter chicken seasoning
  6. Get 1 onion diced
  7. Make ready 8 oz tomato sauce
  8. Make ready 1 cup water
  9. Make ready 2 bouillon cubes
  10. Take 1 cup cream
  11. Prepare 1/2 unsalted butter
  12. Take 2 Tbsp cilantro or parsley, chopped
  13. Prepare basmati rice, cooked

Steps to make Butter Chicken:
  1. Combine the lemon juice, yogurt, and seasoning in a bowl
  2. Add the chicken and mix. Set aside while you prep everything else.
  3. Heat the ghee in a large pot over medium heat
  4. Add the onions and cook until slightly browned.
  5. Add the chicken and all the yogurt mix. Cook until the chicken begins to stick.
  6. Add the water, bouillon, tomato sauce and simmer for 30 min.
  7. Add the cream, followed by the butter and the cilantro. Stir to combine. And serve over basmati rice
