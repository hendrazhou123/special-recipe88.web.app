Instructions to make Homemade Pure Vanilla Extract:
  1. Split vanilla beans lengthwise to expose the caviar (seeds).
  2. Drop beans into bottle of vodka. Place lid on and give a good shake.
  3. Place in dry dark place, Like a closet and give it a shake everyday.
  4. Let infuse for a minimum of 4-6 weeks. Best to let sit for six months. The longer you let the two infuse, the stronger the flavor/taste will develop.
