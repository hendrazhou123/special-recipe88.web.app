The ingredients needed to make Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  2. Make ready 3/4 cup butter softened
  3. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  4. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  5. Get 2 eggs
  6. Get 2 tsp vanilla
  7. Prepare 1 tsp baking soda
  8. Get 1 tsp salt
  9. Take 2 1/2 cups flour
  10. Get 12 oz. bag mini chocolate chips

Steps to make Peanut Butter Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Cream together butter, sugar, brown sugar, and peanut butter.
  2. Add in eggs and vanilla and beat until smooth.
  3. Slowly add in salt, baking soda and flour. Once combined fold in chocolate chips.
  4. Scoop into balls and press down to slightly flatten on a baking pan covered with parchment paper.
  5. Bake 350 for 10 minutes in the middle rack of the oven.
