The ingredients needed to make Mango cream:
  1. Prepare 400 gm hung curd
  2. Prepare 2 cups mango puree
  3. Get 1 cup mango pieces
  4. Prepare 2 tbs powder sugar
  5. Get Almond for garnish
  6. Take 3 tbs milk powder
  7. Take 1 tbs milk

Instructions to make Mango cream:
  1. Take curd in mixing bowl
  2. Add milk in milk powder make thick paste
  3. Add milk mixture and mango puree half sugar in curd
  4. Mix it properly to get silky texture
  5. Now in short glasses layer of puree fill curd in piping bag and ensemble second layer of it
  6. Now add mango pieces and cover it with curd top with almonds and mango keep in fridge to chill serve chilled you serve it dessert in summer it's best healthy dessert
