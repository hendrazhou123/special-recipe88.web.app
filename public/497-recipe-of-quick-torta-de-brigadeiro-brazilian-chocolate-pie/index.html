The ingredients needed to make Torta de Brigadeiro / Brazilian Chocolate Pie:
  1. Take Pie Crust
  2. Make ready 1 cup flour
  3. Take 1 egg yolk
  4. Take 100 g margarine
  5. Get 2 tbsp sugar
  6. Take 1 tbsp baking powder
  7. Get Brigadeiro / Chocolate Filling
  8. Take 1 can condensed milk
  9. Take 3 tbsp cocoa powder
  10. Take 1 tbsp butter
  11. Make ready 300 ml milk
  12. Prepare 3 tbsp cornstarch

Place them on a baking dish. The brigadeiro (Portuguese pronunciation: [bɾiga'dejɾu]) is a traditional Brazilian dessert, created by a confectioner from Rio de Janeiro, Heloisa Nabuco de Oliveira. It is made of condensed milk, cocoa powder, butter, and chocolate sprinkles covering the outside layer. It is a popular confection throughout the country, especially for festive events.

Steps to make Torta de Brigadeiro / Brazilian Chocolate Pie:
  1. Mix all ingredients to make the crust and place it in the pie dish
  2. Make holes in the bottom of the dough with a fork and bake it at 400F for 15-20 min
  3. Make the filling by combining the condensed milk, cocoa powder, milk, and butter in a saucepan
  4. Bring to a boil, then add the cornstarch (dissolved in milk)
  5. Mix it constantly until thick
  6. Add the filling into the pie crust (sift it if there are lumps), then add any toppings you’d like (I have added coconut wafers)
  7. Refrigerate for a few hours and enjoy!
