Instructions to make Sugar free microwave cake:
  1. Collect your ingredients.
  2. In a microwaveable bowl mix the melted butter and the eggs properly.
  3. Add in the dry ingredients and beat together.
  4. Put in microwave for 5 minutes. Do the toothpick readiness check. Remove then let cool for a few minutes.
  5. Serve and enjoy with your yogurt as cream. If the sugar is too far use natural honey to sweeten.
