Instructions to make Apricot Cookie Cake:
  1. Cook dried organic apricot until softened. And remove seeds.
  2. Mix all wet ingredients in food processor, and add flour.
  3. Divide small and big dough. And wrap smaller one and keep in refrigerator or freezer for a while. Put big dough in mold.
  4. Today I have white chocolate leftover I put it on dough and then put cooked apricot on it. You can put any dried fruits, jam or chocolates.
  5. Slice small dough with slicer. Put it on top.
  6. Bake in 180℃ preheated oven for 30 minutes.
