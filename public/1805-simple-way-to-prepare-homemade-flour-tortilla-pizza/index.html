Steps to make Flour tortilla pizza:
  1. Preheat oven to 375°
  2. brush one side of tortilla, lay tortilla on baking sheet oil side down
  3. top with sauce, cheese, pepperoni or toppings of your choice
  4. bake for 10-13 minutes until tortilla is crisp and cheese is melted
