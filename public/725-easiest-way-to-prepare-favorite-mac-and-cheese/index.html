Instructions to make Mac and Cheese:
  1. Bring the water to a boil and add salt and pasta. Cook until al dente. Drain.
  2. Use a cast iron pan to toast the panko and take out.
  3. In a saucepan, add half & half. Add havarti cheese, melt. Mascarpone, parmigiana reggiano, and white cheddar.
  4. In a bowl, put pasta in the bowl and add the cheese sauce. Coat.
  5. Use the butter to brush the cast iron pan. Add the mac. Top will the toasted panko
  6. Bake at 350 for 30 minutes.
