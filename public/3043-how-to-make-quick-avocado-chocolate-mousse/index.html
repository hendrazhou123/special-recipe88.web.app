The ingredients needed to make Avocado chocolate mousse:
  1. Take 2 ripe avocados
  2. Prepare 2-3 tbsp agave syrup
  3. Take 1-3 tbsp good-quality unsweetened cocoa powder
  4. Take 1/2 cup coconut milk
  5. Take Coconut chips (optional)

Instructions to make Avocado chocolate mousse:
  1. In a food processor or high-speed blender, combine all ingredients.
  2. Bled until creamy and smooth.
  3. Serve mousse cold with coconut chips.
  4. Enjoy!
