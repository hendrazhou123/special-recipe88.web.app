The ingredients needed to make Mandarin & Almond cake:
  1. Take Cake
  2. Take 75 g Butter, soften
  3. Take 75 g Caster sugar
  4. Prepare 125 g Mandarin jam
  5. Get 3 eggs
  6. Make ready 125 g Greek yoghurt
  7. Prepare 50 g Almond meal
  8. Get 125 g Plain flour
  9. Get 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  10. Make ready Syrup
  11. Take 75 ml Lemon juice
  12. Make ready 150 ml Mandarin juice
  13. Make ready 150 g White sugar

Steps to make Mandarin & Almond cake:
  1. Cream sugar and butter
  2. Mix in one egg at a time to mixture
  3. Mix in yoghurt and jam to mixture
  4. Add almond meal, plain flour and baking powder. Stir til combined.
  5. Pour mixture in choice of baking style. Cake, muffin or loaf and bake for 15mins or when done.
  6. To make syrup combine lemon, mandarin and sugar to a pot and bring to boil. Then simmer down to thin syrup.
  7. Pour syrup over cake and serve.
