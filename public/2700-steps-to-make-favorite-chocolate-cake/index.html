The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake:
  1. Prepare 2 cups - All purpose flour
  2. Take 3/4 cup - coco powder
  3. Take 1 tsp - salt

  5. Prepare 2 cups - Sugar
  6. Get 2 tsp - baking powder
  7. Prepare 1.5 cups - Milk
  8. Prepare 1 cup - yogurt
  9. Get 1/2 cup - oil
