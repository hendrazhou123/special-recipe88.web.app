The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Fudge:
  1. Take 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  2. Prepare 2 tbsp butter or margarine
  3. Get 2/3 cup evaporated milk
  4. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  5. Make ready 2 cups mini marshmallows
  6. Take 1 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  7. Get 1 cup chopped walnuts
  8. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Chocolate Fudge:
  1. Put marshmallows, walnuts, and chocolate chips in a bowl together and set aside. Line a 8x8 pan with foil.
  2. In a largeish pot bring sugar, evaporated milk, butter, and salt to a rolling boil. Boil for 5 min stirring constantly.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in marshmallows, chocolate, walnuts, and vanilla extract. Combine untill marshmallows are melted.
  4. Pour into pan and refrigerate for two hours.
