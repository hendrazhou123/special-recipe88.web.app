The ingredients needed to make Butter prawn:
  1. Get 15 prawns
  2. Make ready 4 tbspn butter
  3. Get 1/4 lemon
  4. Make ready 3 cloves garlic
  5. Prepare salt
  6. Get black pepper
  7. Make ready italian herbs (optional)

Instructions to make Butter prawn:
  1. Peel and wash the prawns
  2. Squeeze the lemon and set aside the lemon juice
  3. Mince the garlic and set it aside
  4. Put the butter into a pan and melt it on medium heat
  5. Cook prawns until it turns pink and add minced garlic
  6. Season with a pinch of salt, black pepper and italian herb
  7. Mix evenly
  8. Pour lemon juice on prawns when done
  9. The prawns are ready to be served!!
