The ingredients needed to make Lemon Mustard Chicken:
  1. Take 4 whole Chicken breasts
  2. Get 1/2 cup Lemon juice
  3. Take 2 tbsp mustrad seeds
  4. Make ready 1.5 cups chicken stock
  5. Take 3 tbsp dry white wine
  6. Get 1 cup heavy cream
  7. Take 1 tbsp onion minced
  8. Make ready 1/2 tbsp garlic minced
  9. Prepare Fresh rosemary
  10. Make ready Salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes

Instructions to make Lemon Mustard Chicken:
  1. Slice chicken breasts lengthwise so that you have a consistent thickness for easier cooking
  2. Pan fry in olive oil until they are well cooked and set aside
  3. In the same pan, add more olive oil if needed and fry the onions and then the garlic untill soft
  4. Lower the heat and add half the mustard seeds and cook for less than a minute. Be careful they start the sizzle if you do not lower the heat to the minimum
  5. Add the white wine and leave untill evaporated and then and then chicken stock
  6. Season the mixture with the salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes and leave to simmer
  7. Add the fresh cream while stirring, fresh rosemary and the rest of the mustard seeds. Leave the mixture to heat but not boil.
  8. Add the chicken and scoop some of the sauce over it and serve with sauteed baby potatoes and mushrooms, rice or pasta
