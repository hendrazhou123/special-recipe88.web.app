Steps to make Chocolicious Cookie Sandwich:
  1. Take maida in a bowl,add besan,sooji,baking powder and sieve all the ingredients.
  2. Now add a pinch of salt,melted butter and mix everything well and make a dough.If dough is dry then ads some milk and mix.
  3. Now take a small portion of dough,roll it on your palm and make a peda like cookies.Preapre all cookies like this.
  4. Now bake this cookies in oven at 180℃ for about 15 mins.Cookies are ready.
  5. Now take whipped cream apply it on cookies and place chocolate at the centre.
  6. Now put other cookie on this cookie and pour chocolate syrup over it.Chocolicious cookie sandwich is ready.Enjoy with your loved ones.
