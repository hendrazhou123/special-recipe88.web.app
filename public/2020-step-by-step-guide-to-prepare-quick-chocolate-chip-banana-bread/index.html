The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Get 1/2 cup (8 tpsb) Unsalted Butter, softened
  2. Take 2/3 cup Granulated Sugar
  3. Make ready 2 large Eggs
  4. Prepare 3 Bananas (very ripe)
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp Vanilla Extract
  6. Make ready 1 1/2 cups All-purpose Flour
  7. Get 1 tsp Baking Soda
  8. Make ready 1/2 tsp Salt
  9. Make ready 2/3 cup Chocolate Chips (semisweet)

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Banana Bread:
  1. Preheat the oven to 350deg F. Butter and flour a bread loaf pan
  2. In a mixing bowl using paddle attachment, cream together softened butter and sugar. Add the 2 lightly beaten eggs.
  3. Mash bananas with a fork until consistency of chunky sauce and add them to the mixing bowl along with vanilla Mix until well blended.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together dry ingredients : Flour, Baking soda and Salt. Add to the mixing bowl and mix until well incorporated.
  5. Fold in most of the chocolate chips then transfer to the prepared bread pan. Sprinkle the remaining chocolate over the top and bake at 350 deg F for 55-65 mins or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. Let the banana bread rest for 10 mins before transferring to a wire rack to cool.
