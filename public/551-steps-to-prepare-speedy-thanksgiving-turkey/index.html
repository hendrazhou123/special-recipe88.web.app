  4. Take Sazon with Achiote

  8. Get 1 stick margarine

Instructions to make Thanksgiving 🍁Turkey 🦃:
  1. Rinse turkey and pat dry……..
  2. Place turkey in a large baking pan…..
  3. Season all sides of turkey with above seasonings, I just eyeball all of the seasonings…….make sure you rub in the seasonings onto your turkey 🦃!
  4. Place stick of margarine, cut up, all around turkey……
  5. Cover and marinate overnight……
  6. When cooking your turkey cover breast area with aluminum foil, forming a tent over the breast area…….
  7. Cook according to package directions, I usually go by this chart…..Baste once every hour…..
  8. Remove tent from breast area when turkey is almost done and cook until breast area turns golden………
  9. Let turkey rest for 30 - 40 minutes, serve and enjoy 😉!!! Your turkey 🦃 will have a beautiful, golden color ! 😋 Yummy and moist ! 😍
