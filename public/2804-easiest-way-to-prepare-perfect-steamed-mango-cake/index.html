The ingredients needed to make Steamed mango cake:
  1. Take 1 bowl mango pulp fresh
  2. Take 1 bowl idli rava
  3. Prepare 1/2 bowl Jaggery
  4. Prepare As per your choice Cardamom powder or nutmeg powder
  5. Get 1 cup milk
  6. Take 1 small bowl coconut grated
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking powder
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp Baking soda
  9. Make ready As per taste salt

Steps to make Steamed mango cake:
  1. Take mango pulp in a bowl add idli rava and mix keep aside.
  2. Now add grated coconut and Jaggery to it mix well and wait for 5 mins.
  3. Now add milk and pinch of salt and cardamom powder, mix well.
  4. Now grease one pot and pour the mixture to it,prepare steamer, here i used idli steamer and steam for 15 mins check with toothpick.
  5. Let it cool drizzle 1 tspoon desi ghee and decorate with grated coconut. Yummy and healthy dish.
