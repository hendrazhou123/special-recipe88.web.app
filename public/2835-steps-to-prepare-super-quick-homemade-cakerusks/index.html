The ingredients needed to make Cakerusks:
  1. Prepare 100 gm Butter
  2. Take 100 gm Maida
  3. Make ready 100 gm Sugar
  4. Take 3 Eggs
  5. Take 1 & ½ TSP Baking Powder
  6. Prepare 1 TSP Vanilla Essence
  7. Prepare 1 tsp yellow colour

Steps to make Cakerusks:
  1. First of all, add Butter in a mixer, then add sugar - - Mix it well, until it became soft
  2. Now add eggs one by one, keep mixing - - After this, add vanilla Essence & mix it again
  3. Now pour this mixture into a bowl. Add Maida, & mix it well - Now mix it again by adding baking powder - It’s time for Molding. Take a mold, apply cooking oil at this mold
  4. Fix the butter paper in this mold - - Keep the material (which we have prepared above) in this Mold & oven bake it for 30 minutes at 180-200°C
  5. After oven bake, let it cool. And keep it aside for 4-5 hours - - It’s time for making cakes/Rusks - - Cut this material into cake rusks shape & oven bake it for 20-25 minutes at 160-180°C
  6. Your yummy cakeRusks are ready to serve❤
