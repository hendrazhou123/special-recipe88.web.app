The ingredients needed to make Star Cookie:
  1. Prepare 250 g Butter
  2. Prepare 3,1 /2 cups of flour
  3. Take 1 cup Sugar
  4. Prepare 1 Egg
  5. Take 1 tsp Vanilla flavor
  6. Get Icing sugar(enough to sprinkle)
  7. Make ready Condensed milk(1/2 cup)

Instructions to make Star Cookie:
  1. Mix all ingredients in a bowl except with the icing sugar and condensed milk,deflate the dough and cut out the star shape using cookie cutter.
  2. Arrange on a baking tray and bake for 18mints.
  3. Allow to cool down.
  4. Spread condensed milk all over
  5. And sprinkle icing sugar as the sugar will stick to the cookie.
  6. Your star cookies are ready to serve.
