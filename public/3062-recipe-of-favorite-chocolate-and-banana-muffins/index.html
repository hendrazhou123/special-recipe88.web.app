The ingredients needed to make Chocolate and banana muffins!:
  1. Make ready 3/4 cups sugar
  2. Take 2 eggs
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup melted butter or margarine plus 3 tablespoons for brushing the muffins when done
  4. Prepare 1 1/2 cups flour
  5. Get 1 ripe smashed banana
  6. Take 1 teaspoon baking powder
  7. Get 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  8. Take 1 cup chocolate chips
  9. Get 1/2 cup milk

Instructions to make Chocolate and banana muffins!:
  1. Beat the butter,eggs and sugar on high for about a minute.
  2. Then you add the banana and mix again for another minute.
  3. In a medium bowl add flour,baking powder and baking soda and mix well then add it to the wet ingredients and mix again for 1 minute then add the milk and mix again for about a minute more when done stir in the chocolate chips and put in paper lined muffin tins and bake for 1/2 an hour at 350.
  4. When done and still hot brush the 3 tablespoons of melted butter on each muffin.
