The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Mousse with Berries.:
  1. Get 1 box vanilla instant pudding
  2. Take 2 1/2 cup milk. can use low fat and just as good.
  3. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  4. Make ready 1 8oz container of coolwhip-thawed-can use fat free but the taste is better with original.
  5. Take 1/2 tsp chambord or other raspberry liquor.
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup blueberries, strawberries, & blackberries

Fill the filo pastries with the mousse. Garnish it with fresh berries and sprinkle it with powdered sugar. This creamy, fruity pudding is great to make a day ahead. To serve, grate a little white chocolate over each glass and decorate with raspberries.

Steps to make Vanilla Mousse with Berries.:
  1. Whisk pudding, milk, vanilla until smooth. Refrigerate for 1 hour or until firm
  2. Add container of cool whip and use rubber spatula to fold into pudding. Do not mix.
  3. In separate bowl add berries and chambord .refrigerate until chilled.
  4. Chill 6 martini glasses and layer berries and mousse. Top with a dollop of whipped cream.
