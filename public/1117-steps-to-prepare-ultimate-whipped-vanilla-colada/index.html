The ingredients needed to make Whipped Vanilla Colada:
  1. Make ready 8-10 Large Ice Cubes
  2. Get 1 1/2 Cups Whipped Cream (Frozen)
  3. Take 3/4 Cup French Vanilla Coffee Creamer
  4. Make ready 1/4 Cup Milk
  5. Take 1 Tablespoon Sugar (optional)

Steps to make Whipped Vanilla Colada:
  1. In a blender with a milkshake or frappe setting put half the ice cubes, then the whipped cream, and the other half of the one cubes.
  2. Then add vanilla creamer and milk. (Liquid should not be higher than ice cubes and whipped cream)
  3. Blend on high until smooth. Approximately 20-30 seconds.
  4. Take sugar and put it on a flat plate or dish.
  5. Take serving glasses and dip the rim in water or milk the dip in sugar to coat the rim in sugar.
  6. Pour into serving glasses and enjoy.
