The ingredients needed to make Fried Tofu Wrapped in Flour Batter (TAHU GORENG TEPUNG):
  1. Get 200 gr tofu ( the Chinese, Sumedang or another kind, about 15 pcs), cut in squares according to taste
  2. Get Oil for frying
  3. Prepare BATTER:
  4. Take 125 gr flour low protein/moderate
  5. Prepare 1 1/2 tbsp sago flour/cornstarch
  6. Make ready 1 scallions, thinly sliced
  7. Prepare 2 tsp coriander powder
  8. Take 1/4 tsp chicken bouillon powder
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking powder
  10. Take 1/4 tsp pepper ground
  11. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  12. Take 100 ml ice water
  13. Prepare COATING (mix together):
  14. Get 50 gr flour
  15. Take 1/2 tsp salt

Instructions to make Fried Tofu Wrapped in Flour Batter (TAHU GORENG TEPUNG):
  1. Steamed tofu a few minutes, then drain. Cut box/cube to taste then roll out the tofu in the coating material, set aside.
  2. BATTER : mix flour, sago flour/cornstarch, coriander powder, chicken bouillon powder, baking powder, pepper powder and salt. Add water and scallions and stir well.
  3. Dip tofu into batter, and fry in oil heated over medium heat until cooked. Drain.
  4. Serve warm with chili sauce or green chili.
