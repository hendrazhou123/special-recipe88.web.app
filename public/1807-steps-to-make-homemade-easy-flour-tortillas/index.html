The ingredients needed to make EASY flour tortillas:
  1. Make ready 3 cup flour
  2. Get 1 tsp salt
  3. Get 1/2 tsp baking powder
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup canola oil
  5. Get 1 cup hot water

Steps to make EASY flour tortillas:
  1. Mix the flour, salt, and baking powder together thoroughly.
  2. Add the oil and mix with your hands until the flour mixture resembles fine crumbs
  3. Add hot water and mix until a ball is formed
  4. Cover and let the dough rest for at least 30 minutes
  5. Divide dough into 12 even sized balls and roll out one at a time on a floured surface. Roll out thin!
  6. Brush off excess flour and cook on a hot, ungreased griddle over medium high heat. Turn the tortilla when brown blisters form. Serve warm!
