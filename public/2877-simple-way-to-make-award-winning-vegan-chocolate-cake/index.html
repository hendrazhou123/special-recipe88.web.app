Steps to make Vegan Chocolate Cake:
  1. Combine all dry ingredients. Set aside. Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F.
  2. Combine milk and vinegar. Set aside for 5 min until the mixture bubbles.
  3. Pour all wet ingredients into dry. Mix until just combined.
  4. Pour in a parchment-lined round cake pan and bake for around 30 min. Wait until cool to serve or frost. Enjoy :)
