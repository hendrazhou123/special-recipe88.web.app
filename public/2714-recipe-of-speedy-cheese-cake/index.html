The ingredients needed to make Cheese Cake:
  1. Take 1 Cup flour
  2. Prepare 1 Cup pecans
  3. Take 1/2 Cup butter
  4. Get Topping
  5. Prepare 1 Cup cream
  6. Prepare 1 pack cream cheese
  7. Prepare 1 Cup icing sugar
  8. Make ready 1 box vanilla pudding
  9. Get 1 1/2 Cup milk
