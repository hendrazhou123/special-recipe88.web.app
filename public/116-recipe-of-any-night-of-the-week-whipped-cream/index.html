The ingredients needed to make Whipped Cream:
  1. Make ready 2 cups heavy whipping cream
  2. Get 8 oz cream cheese
  3. Make ready 1 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  4. Get 1 teaspoon almond extract - optional
  5. Get 1/2 cup white sugar

Steps to make Whipped Cream:
  1. Combine the cream cheese, sugar, vanilla and almond extract in a medium bowl.
  2. Using an electric mixer on medium to high speed, mix the cream cheese mixture until well blended.
  3. Gradually add the heavy whipping cream into mixture while mixing on high speed.
  4. Mix until a firm peak is formed.
