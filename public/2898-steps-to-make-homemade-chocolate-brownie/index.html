The ingredients needed to make Chocolate brownie:
  1. Get 100 g flour
  2. Prepare 80 g sugar
  3. Get 20 g cocoa
  4. Make ready 1 piece chocolate
  5. Prepare 2 eggs
  6. Prepare 1 tea spoon Baking powder
  7. Make ready 100 g butter

Steps to make Chocolate brownie:
  1. Mix the butter until it becomes soft.
  2. Heat the chocolate in microwave for 1 minutes.
  3. Add sugar and cocoa to the butter and mix well.
  4. Add eggs to the mixture and mix well.
  5. Add flour and baking powder to the mixture and mix well.
  6. Add chocolate to the mixture and mix well.
  7. Put mixture into a baking box.
  8. Heat the oven 180C and bake the mixture for about 35 minutes.
