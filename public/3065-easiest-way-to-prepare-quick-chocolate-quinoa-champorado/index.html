The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Quinoa Champorado:
  1. Get 1/4 c quinoa
  2. Prepare 3/4 c water
  3. Prepare Pinch salt
  4. Take 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  5. Prepare 1 tbsp sugar
  6. Prepare Evaporated Milk

Steps to make Chocolate Quinoa Champorado:
  1. Boil the quinoa, water, and salt on high, and when you see it come to a rapid boil, reduce the heat to low. Cover. Simmer for 10 mins.
  2. Add in your cocoa powder and sugar. Gradually add more or less of each as needed.
  3. Stir in a splash of evaporated milk and enjoy! Because it’s made with quinoa, it won’t be as thick as the traditional version. You could add a tsp of cornstarch slurry, but why? It’s good as is :)
