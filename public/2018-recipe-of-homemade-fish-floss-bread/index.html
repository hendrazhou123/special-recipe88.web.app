The ingredients needed to make Fish Floss Bread:
  1. Get 1 cup flour
  2. Prepare a little melted butter
  3. Take 1 tsp sugar
  4. Get 1 little bit salt
  5. Make ready as needed fresh milk
  6. Make ready 1 egg
  7. Take 1 tsp yeast, soak in water

Instructions to make Fish Floss Bread:
  1. Mix all the dry ingredients then next are the wet ingredients. Mix all well. Then form a dough and begin kneading. Knead at least 5 mins
  2. Then put in a deep bowl and cover it for an hour to let the dough rise.
  3. After an hour punch it to let the air comes out. Knead again and cut into desired amount of dough for your bread.
  4. Roll it in rolling pin then form a circle, take 1 tbsp of fish Floss and place it in the middle of dough and form a ball shape.
  5. Beat egg and brush the surface. Heat oven at 180c and bake for 15 mins. After remove from the oven brush bread surface with melted butter
