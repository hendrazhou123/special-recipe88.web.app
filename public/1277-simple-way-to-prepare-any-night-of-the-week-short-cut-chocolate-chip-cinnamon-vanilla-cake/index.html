The ingredients needed to make Short Cut Chocolate Chip Cinnamon Vanilla Cake:
  1. Prepare 1 box vanilla cake mix
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Get 1 cup water
  4. Make ready 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup mini chocolate chips
  6. Get 2 tsp ground cinnamon

Steps to make Short Cut Chocolate Chip Cinnamon Vanilla Cake:
  1. Preheat oven at 350
  2. In a large bowl add the cake mix, cinnamon, water, eggs, and oil. Beat well.
  3. Grease a round baking pan and pour the batter top it off with the chocolate chips and bake for 35 min.
  4. Let it cool and enjoy!!!
