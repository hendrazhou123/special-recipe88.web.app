The ingredients needed to make Garlic Bread:
  1. Get 1 head garlic,grated
  2. Prepare Spring onion
  3. Take tbsp Melted butter,3
  4. Take 2 eggs
  5. Take 1 tsp yeast
  6. Prepare 1 cup flour
  7. Prepare 1 little salt
  8. Get 1 tsp sugar
  9. Prepare Fresh milk

Instructions to make Garlic Bread:
  1. Melt the butter then add spring onion and garlic.Set aside
  2. Mix flour yeast salt and sugar.mix properly.Then add melted butter egg,melted butter and milk.Then mix properly then start to knead for 5 mins.Then set aside for an hour to rise.
  3. After an hour,take out the dough and let it flatten using rolling pin.Then spread the butter garlic mixture.Then cut it.After that brush it with egg.
  4. Preheat oven at 200c and bake for 20 mins
