The ingredients needed to make Peppermint Ginger Hot Cocoa with Almond milk, honey, cacao powder and Semi sweet chocolate Chips:
  1. Make ready Almond milk - half gallon
  2. Take Peppermint medley tea bags - 2 bags
  3. Get Ginger - be generous (50 grams)
  4. Get 5 table spoons Unsweetened Cacao powder
  5. Prepare 6 table spoons of honey
  6. Prepare Half cup of Semi sweet chocolate Chips
  7. Prepare 1/4 cup water
