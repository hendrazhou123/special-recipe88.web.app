The ingredients needed to make Brussel sprouts and fenugreek in butter:
  1. Prepare 1 tablespoons fenugreek
  2. Get 2 pounds Brussel sprouts
  3. Take 1 stick butter
  4. Make ready 1 tablespoons grated parmesan cheese
  5. Prepare 1/4 teaspoon granulated garlic powder
  6. Get 3/4 teaspoon salt

  3. Add spouts salt and garlic
  4. Cover and cook till tender. Add cheese and cover remove from heat. Let sit 7 minutes. Serve I hope you enjoy!
