The ingredients needed to make Mushroom butter masala:
  1. Get 1 cup diced mushrooms
  2. Make ready 1 big onion sliced finely
  3. Make ready 1 tomato sliced
  4. Prepare 1 green chilli chopped optional
  5. Take 1 tbsp ginger garlic grated
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp hung curd or whipped cream
  7. Get 1 tbsp salt
  8. Make ready 1 tsp red chili powder optional
  9. Make ready 1 tsp turmeric
  10. Prepare Half tsp black pepper
  11. Prepare 1 tsp garam masala
  12. Prepare 1 tsp coriander powder
  13. Make ready 1 tsp cumin seeds
  14. Prepare 1 tsp cumin powder
  15. Get 1 tsp curry leaves
  16. Make ready Half cup peas

Steps to make Mushroom butter masala:
  1. Add half of mentioned butter in pan and roast cumin seeds ginger+garlic+green+onion.make them golden
  2. Add all the spices together and stir at low flame for 5 minute and add sliced tomatoes add stir to make smooth thick masala
