The ingredients needed to make Instant Pot Peanut Butter Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 tbsp olive oil
  2. Make ready 1 tbsp sesame oil
  3. Make ready 2 lbs chicken breast cubed
  4. Make ready 1 cup chicken broth
  5. Prepare 1/4 cup unsweetened peanut butter
  6. Make ready 1/4 cup soy sauce
  7. Get 1 tbsp vinegar
  8. Take 1 tbsp sriracha sauce
  9. Take 2-3 tbsp honey
  10. Prepare 2 tsp garlic powder
  11. Take 2 tsp ginger powder
  12. Take 1/2 tsp red chili flakes (optional)
  13. Prepare to taste Salt
  14. Take to taste Pepper
  15. Take 2 tbsp finely chopped green onion for garnish
  16. Take 1-2 tbsp roasted chopped peanuts

Steps to make Instant Pot Peanut Butter Chicken:
  1. Heat oil on saute mode
  2. Add chicken, broth, peanut butter, soy sauce, vinegar, sriracha sauce, honey, garlic powder, ginger powder, red chili flakes, salt and pepper. Mix together.
  3. Select manual for 9 minutes set to sealing.
  4. Vent after cooking, remove lid stir together.
  5. Cook on saute with no lid to reduce liquid
  6. Garnish with green onions and chopped peanuts
