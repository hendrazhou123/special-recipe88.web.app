The ingredients needed to make Iced vanilla latte:
  1. Prepare 1 cup Expresso or strong brewed coffee chilled
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup vanilla almond milk
  3. Take 2 1/2 tbsp liquid vanilla coffee creamer
  4. Prepare 1/3 cup Ice or coffee flavored Ice cubes is what I like to use
  5. Make ready 1 whip cream
  6. Make ready 1 Chocolate syrup
