The ingredients needed to make Vanilla cake!!:
  1. Prepare 21/4 cups flour
  2. Get 31/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 stick butter room temperature
  5. Prepare 1/8 cup oil
  6. Take 3 eggs room temperature
  7. Get 11/4 cups milk
  8. Take 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  9. Get 11/2 cups sugar
