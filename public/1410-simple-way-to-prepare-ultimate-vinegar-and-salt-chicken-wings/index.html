The ingredients needed to make vinegar and salt chicken wings:
  1. Prepare 1 cup apple cider vinegar
  2. Make ready 1/3 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup cornstarch
  4. Make ready 1/3 cup of chicken broth you make with wing tips
  5. Get 1 garlic powder to taste
  6. Prepare 1 salt to taste
  7. Make ready 1 tsp white pepper
  8. Make ready 1 oil to fry
  9. Prepare 1/3 cup water
  10. Prepare 2 tsp kosher salt
  11. Take 10 whole wings separated use wing tips for broth
  12. Get 1 stick butter
