The ingredients needed to make Sour cream:
  1. Take 1 cup Cooking cream
  2. Get 1/2 cup Curd (amul)
  3. Make ready 2-3 tsp Lemon juice
  4. Take Pinch Black pepper powder
  5. Take To taste Salt
  6. Make ready 1/2 tsp Paraley finely chopped

Instructions to make Sour cream:
  1. In a large bowl, whisk together cooking cream and curd until you get nice smooth texture.
  2. Add lemon juice to the bowl, whisk again.
  3. Add black pepper, salt, parsley and mix well.
  4. Serve chilled.
