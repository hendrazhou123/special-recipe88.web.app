The ingredients needed to make Chewy chocolate cookies:
  1. Get Ingredients A
  2. Prepare 65 g caster sugar
  3. Take 75 g raw granulated sugar
  4. Get 60 g brown sugar
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 100 g melted unsalt butter
  7. Prepare Ingredients B
  8. Get 160 g all-purpose flour
  9. Take 0.5 tsp baking soda
  10. Take Other ingredients
  11. Take 1 egg
  12. Get 1-2 tsp vanilla essence
  13. Take 60 g semisweet chocolate buttons

Instructions to make Chewy chocolate cookies:
  1. Mix Ingredients A in a mixer till it become a paste form.
  2. Add egg and vanilla essence and mix well.
  3. Mix and sift Ingredients B
  4. Fold in Ingredients B into - the above mixture(do not over mix), - then mix in Chocolate Buttons till no visible flour
  5. Chill the dough for at least 30 min.
  6. Preheat oven 180C, - baked for 12-15 min or - till the edge of the cookie turn darker
