The ingredients needed to make Low-carb / sugar-free Cheese Cake:
  1. Get 2 1/2 lbs cream cheese
  2. Get 1 cup stevia
  3. Prepare 5 eggs
  4. Make ready 2 egg yolks
  5. Make ready 2 Tbs almond flour
  6. Take 1/2 tsp baking powder
  7. Prepare 1 Tbs vanilla
  8. Take 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps to make Low-carb / sugar-free Cheese Cake:
  1. Mix cream cheese, stevia until blended then add eggs one at a time. Then vanilla, almond flour and baking powder. Then whip in heavy cream
  2. Bake for 10 min at 350 degrees.
  3. Bring down to 200 degrees for 60 min.
  4. Turn off oven and leave cake in for 60 min
  5. I used a 10" spring form pan.
