The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Prepare 2 1/2 cups: flour
  2. Take 2/3 cups: brown sugar
  3. Get 2/3 cups: white sugar
  4. Get 1/2 cup: milk
  5. Make ready 2/3 cup: vegetable oil
  6. Get 1 tsp: baking powder
  7. Make ready 1 tsp: baking soda
  8. Get 2 tsps: vanilla extract
  9. Get chocolate chips

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookies:
  1. Turn on oven at 180° C
  2. Add oil, brown sugar and white sugar - combine
  3. Add in vanilla extract - combine
  4. Add milk - combine
  5. In a separate bowl combine flour, baking soda and baking powder
  6. Combine flour mixture and all liquids
  7. Add chocolate chips
  8. Put dough in the fridge for 30 minutes
  9. Put cookies in oven for 7-10 minutes
