The ingredients needed to make Cheeseburger Mac N Cheese:
  1. Prepare 1 lb ground beef
  2. Get 1 small onion minced
  3. Prepare 5 cloves garlic minced
  4. Take 1/3 cup Worcestershire sauce
  5. Prepare 1/3 cup hoisin sauce
  6. Prepare 1 tbsp soy sauce
  7. Prepare 1 tsp Garlic pepper
  8. Make ready 1 tsp organic salt
  9. Take 1/3 cup oyster sauce
  10. Make ready 8 tbsp margarine butter
  11. Get 1/2 cup milk (optional)
  12. Prepare 14.5 oz elbow noodles
  13. Get 2 cups shredded cheese
  14. Make ready 2 tbsp oil

Instructions to make Cheeseburger Mac N Cheese:
  1. Boil noodles until it’s a little soft but not too soft. Drain water when it’s cooked. But do not rinse.
  2. Add grated cheese and butter and mix until all noodles are covered
  3. Take a wok and add oil then when hot sauté garlic and onion
  4. After 2 minutes add ground beef and cooked until meet is fully brown
  5. Add hoisin sauce, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and Worcestershire sauce and cook for 5 minutes then add garlic pepper and organic salt then cook for another 3 minutes
  6. Add the noodles that you mixed with cheese and butter to the ground beef and mix and cook for another 5 minutes
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
