The ingredients needed to make Vegan thai peanut tofu stir fry:
  1. Take 2 tbsp virgin coconut oil
  2. Get 2 green onions, sliced thin
  3. Make ready 4 cloves garlic, minced
  4. Get 2 mushrooms, sliced
  5. Prepare 4 sundried tomatoes, chopped
  6. Make ready 1 cup fresh basil, chopped
  7. Take 1/4 cup coconut flakes
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup cashews
  9. Make ready 1 package firm tofu, cubed
  10. Get Mr Spice Organic Thai Peanut Sauce
  11. Take 2 tbsp red or yellow curry
  12. Make ready water

Steps to make Vegan thai peanut tofu stir fry:
  1. Heat coconut oil in a large pan, and sautée the green onion, garlic, mushroom, and sundried tomatoes.
  2. After a few minutes, add basil, coconut flakes and cashews
  3. Add tofu cubes, sauce, curry, add a bit of water. Let simmer until flavours are fully absorbed by the tofu
