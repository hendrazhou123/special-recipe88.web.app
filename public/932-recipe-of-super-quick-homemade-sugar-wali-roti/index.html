Steps to make Sugar wali roti:
  1. Prepare balls from dough
  2. Stuff sugar and roll it with rolling pin
  3. Heat pan,once heated nicely put roti on tawa and cook on one side
  4. Put ghee and flip it to cook other side
  5. Put ghee on both sides and cook again
  6. Serve hot
  7. Keep a note while you cook keep flame on high as slow flame will melt the sugar fast and will end up burning
