The ingredients needed to make Chocolate brownies #chocolatebakingcontest:
  1. Get 1 cup all purpose flour
  2. Make ready 1 cup sugar
  3. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  4. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  5. Get 1/2 cocoa powder
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup butter
  7. Take 3/4 cup yorghut
  8. Make ready 1 tbp milk
  9. Get 2 tbspn vanilla essence
  10. Get 50 gms cashew nuts (crushed)
  11. Prepare 50 gms compound chocolate (grated)

Instructions to make Chocolate brownies #chocolatebakingcontest:
  1. Measure all your ingredients accurately. Sift flour, salt, cocoa and baking powder into a large bowl.
  2. Melt the grated compound chocolate and the butter in a sauce pan on low heat.
  3. In another bowl, using a spatula, mix the melted butter and chocolate, yorghut, vanilla essence and milk and combine. Add the sifted dry ingredients and mix. Be careful not to overmix.
  4. Add the cashew nuts and fold them in gently. The batter should be abit thick.
  5. Put in a greased baking tin and bake at 180degrees for 20-30minutes till done.
