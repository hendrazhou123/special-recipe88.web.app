The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Get 4/5 cup brown sugar
  2. Get 4/5 cup white sugar
  3. Prepare 1 sticks butter
  4. Make ready 2 eggs
  5. Make ready 2 cups all-purpose flour
  6. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  7. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Make ready 1 cup dark chocolate chips
  9. Prepare 1 cup milk chocolate chips
  10. Prepare 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Steps to make Chocolate chip cookies:
  1. Melt the butter on the stove
  2. In a bowl, combine the sifted flour, the salt and the baking powder (dry ingredients).
  3. In a bowl (or ideally stand mixer) mix in this order: both kinds of sugarar, the melted butter and the eggs and the vanilla. Then add the dry ingredients on step 2. When everything is incorporated, add both kinds of chocolate chips. Mix well but stop the mixer as soon as the chips are well distributed.
  4. Refrigerate the cookie dough for 30 minutes.
  5. Make balls (with an ice cream scoop) and put them on a baking sheet, separating them at least 5 cm from each other.
  6. OPTIONAL (but delicious): sprinkle a bit of additional kosher salt on top of each cookie.
  7. Bake them at 350°F for ~14 to 17 minutes.
