Steps to make Cheesee witches fingers:
  1. Firstly we make dough. Combine maida, rawa, salt, curd and salt. Mix well with your hands. Take little water make a medium soft dough.. Keep aside for 15 minutes.
  2. Now for stuffing take mashed potatoes, salt black pepper powder, red chilli powder, coriander, onion, green chillies, grated cheese in a bowl and mix well.
  3. Now Take a small ball of dough and chapati, and put stuffing in shape of finger into it and roll nicely with using your hands and give it shape of a finger..
  4. Break almonds in two pieces. Adjust half piece of almond as nails with help of water.
  5. Make remain fingers by same process.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and deep fry all fingers at low flame. And bring out in a tissue paper.
  7. For plating i used tomato ketchup.. Decorate with tomato ketchup. It's a scary starter or snack for Halloween party..
