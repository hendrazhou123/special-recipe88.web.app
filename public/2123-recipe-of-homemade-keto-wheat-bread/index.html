The ingredients needed to make Keto “Wheat Bread”:
  1. Get 1/2 teaspoon instant yeast
  2. Prepare 1 tbs warm water
  3. Get 1 3/4 cup almond flour
  4. Make ready 4 tbsp psyllium h us k powder
  5. Prepare 2 tsp baking powder
  6. Make ready 2 tbsp raw sunflower seeds/ chopped
  7. Make ready 4 tsp sesame seeds
  8. Prepare 2 tsp chia seeds
  9. Prepare 1 tsp salt
  10. Get 2 eggs
  11. Take 4 egg whites
  12. Make ready 2 tbsp Apple cedar vinegar
  13. Take 10 drops liquid stevia
  14. Make ready 6 tbsp unsalted butter/ melted/ cooled
  15. Get 1/2 cup boiling water

Steps to make Keto “Wheat Bread”:
  1. Let’s put all the ingredients aside. Preheat the oven to 350F. Line the inside of a 9” x 5 “ loaf pan with parchment paper
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together the almond flour, psyllium husk powder, baking powder, sunflower seeds, sesame seeds, chia seeds and salt
  3. In a medium bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg whites, vinegar, liquid stevia, butter and yeast mixture.
  4. Stir the egg mixture into the dry ingredients and then stir in 1/2 cup of boiling water.transfer the batter to the prepared loaf pan, spread out evenly.
  5. Bake until loaf is golden brown on the top and the bottom about 75 minutes. If the loaf is to dark in the top before it is baked, cover the top with aluminum foil during the last 15 min ya of baking
  6. Turn off the oven, leave the foil on top if you used, and let the loaf cook for 30 minutes in the oven. Remove and let it cook to room temp. Before munching !
