The ingredients needed to make vanilla slice:
  1. Take 250 grams morning coffee biscuits
  2. Take 2 cup milk
  3. Make ready 1 cup cream
  4. Make ready 85 grams packet instant vanilla pudding mix
  5. Take 1 1/2 cup sifted icing sugar
  6. Take 2 tbsp passionfruit pulp
  7. Get 60 grams melted butter

Steps to make vanilla slice:
  1. line a deep 19cm square cake tin with foil
  2. arrange a row of biscuits over the bottom
  3. put milk and cream in a small mixing bow. sprinkle pudding mix on top
  4. beat for five minutes on medium speed. pour over biscuits
  5. top with another layer of biscuits (wrong side up)
  6. beat icing sugar passionfruit pulp and butter in a small bowl until smooth
  7. spread over biscuits
  8. put in a fridge overnight before cutting into nine slices to serve
