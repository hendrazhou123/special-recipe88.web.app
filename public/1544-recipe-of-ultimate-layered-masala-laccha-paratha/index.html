The ingredients needed to make Layered Masala Laccha Paratha:
  1. Take 250 gms all purpose flour
  2. Prepare 100 ml water
  3. Prepare to taste Salt
  4. Take Dry Masalas - chat masala, aamchur powder, red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander leaves and carrom seeds
  5. Prepare 2 tbsp Oil for kneading dough and Oil for roasting

Warqi paratha exactly means layered paratha. Warq is used for layers here. Laccha partha and warqi paratha are prepared similarly. Lachha paratha is a popular North Indian flat-bread.

Instructions to make Layered Masala Laccha Paratha:
  1. For dough: take flour, add oil, salt to taste, carrom seeds and oil. Add water slowly as needed and make a fine dough (not too hard).
  2. Now roll the dough ball and make a big round flat bread (roti). On the top side of roti sprinkle all dry Masalas and coriander leaves.
  3. Now fold the roti alternatively (top & bottom) and then once a pipe like design is done further roll it inside like a round ball.
  4. Now make a medium sized paratha and roast it slowly on a medium flame. Do not press the paratha. Once it is roasted nicely and a bit cold. With the help of fork try to separate its layers and serve it with butter on top.
