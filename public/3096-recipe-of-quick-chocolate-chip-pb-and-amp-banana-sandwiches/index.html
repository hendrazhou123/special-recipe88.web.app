The ingredients needed to make Chocolate chip,pb & banana sandwiches:
  1. Take 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter
  2. Make ready 2 tablespoons honey
  3. Take 1/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  4. Take 2 tablespoons miniature chocolate chips
  5. Prepare 4 slices whole wheat bread
  6. Take 1 medium banana
