The ingredients needed to make Cakey brownies:
  1. Prepare 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  2. Take 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 5 tbsp cocoa powder
  4. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  5. Get 113 gr unsalted butter (room temp.)
  6. Make ready 2 large eggs
  7. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  8. Prepare White chocolate chips

Steps to make Cakey brownies:
  1. Preheat oven 200 degree celcius
  2. Mix butter with sugar until the coulour turns pale
  3. Add the rest of the ingredients, then mix just until it blends enough
  4. Transfer batter to a greased baking dish. Then add the white chocolate chips
  5. Bake for 10 mins with 200 degree celcius just to make the surface crisp. Lower the temperature to 175 degree celcius and bake for another 15-20 minutes
  6. Cool to room temperature then slice!
