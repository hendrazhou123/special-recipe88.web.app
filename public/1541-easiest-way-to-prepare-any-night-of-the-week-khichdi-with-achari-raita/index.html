The ingredients needed to make Khichdi with Achari Raita:
  1. Make ready 1 cup gobindbhog rice or any aromatic rice
  2. Take 1 cup split Moong dal
  3. Take 1 tsp ginger paste
  4. Make ready 1 tsp cumin powder
  5. Make ready 1/2 tsp red chilli powder
  6. Take 1 tsp turmeric powder
  7. Take 2 green chillies
  8. Prepare 1/2 cup mixed chopped veggies
  9. Prepare 1/2 cup cauliflower florets
  10. Get 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  11. Prepare 1/4 tsp sugar

Steps to make Khichdi with Achari Raita:
  1. Heat a oil in a Kadhai add rice sauté for few minutes transfer it and then washed it, now add dal to the same kadhai sauté it for few minutes then transfer it to another bowl
  2. Now fry the cauliflower florets once done keep it aside then sauté the veggies or few minutes then add the ginger paste and other spices cook it until the raw smell goes out
  3. Now add dal and rice and salt and cook it for few minutes then add water cover it and cook it until the rice and dal gets soft
  4. Now add the fry cauliflowers, tomatoes and little sugar cook it for few minutes then add ghee and turn off the flame
