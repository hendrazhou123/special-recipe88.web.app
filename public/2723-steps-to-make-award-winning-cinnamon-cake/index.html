The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon Cake:
  1. Take topping
  2. Get 3/4 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp cinnamon
  4. Take 3/4 chopped walnuts
  5. Get batter
  6. Take 3 cups flour
  7. Prepare 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Take 1 1/2 tsp baking soda
  9. Get 1 tsp salt
  10. Make ready 3/4 cup butter
  11. Take 1 1/4 cups sugar
  12. Take 3 eggs
  13. Prepare 1 1/2 cups greek yogurt
  14. Take 1 1/2 tsp vanilla

Steps to make Cinnamon Cake:
  1. Pre heat oven to 350 F (180 C)
  2. In a small bowl combine topping ingredients and set aside
  3. In a medium bowl with a whisk combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt
  4. Cream butter and sugar at medium speed until fluffy, add eggs one at a time, beating until light and creamy
  5. Add dry ingredients alternately with greek yogurt and vanilla to the creamed mixture with a wooden spoon
  6. Spread half the batter in a tube pan, sppon 3/4 of the topping over batter. cover with remaining topping
  7. Bake for approximately 60 - 65 minutes. if it is not yet done nd its becoming too brown cover with foil until baked
