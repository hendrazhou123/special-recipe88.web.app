The ingredients needed to make Bacon and three cheese carbonara with strozzapreti pasta:
  1. Prepare 450 g strozzapreti pasta
  2. Prepare 350 ml 2%milk
  3. Prepare 250 ml 18%cream
  4. Prepare 4-5 slices bacon sliced
  5. Make ready 1 tbs margarine or butter
  6. Take 1 tbs parsley chopped
  7. Get 1/2 cup cheddar shredded
  8. Make ready 1/4 cup parmigiana shredded
  9. Get 1/2 cup motsarella shredded
  10. Prepare to taste Salt
  11. Take to taste Ground black pepper
  12. Take 1/2 tsp corn flour

Steps to make Bacon and three cheese carbonara with strozzapreti pasta:
  1. On a pot boil the water and add the pasta to cook to your likeness at the same time in a skillet fry the sliced bacon to your likeness
  2. When the pasta is ready strain pasta and put a side
  3. Now in a smaller pot add the milk and cream and bring to boil at medium heat so you don’t burn the bottom of the pot
  4. Now add all the rest of the ingredients in to the pot with milk and cook till it starts to get a bit thick
  5. Now tip the sauce in to the pasta and stir well
  6. Enjoy (καλή όρεξη)
