The ingredients needed to make Spicy Cream Sauce:
  1. Prepare Pasta (any type and quantity desired)
  2. Get Meat for topping of your choice (I used frozen linguiça)
  3. Make ready 1-1.5 cup milk
  4. Make ready 4-6 tablespoons cream cheese (however creamy you like it)
  5. Get Half an onion, finely chopped
  6. Take 2 cloves garlic, chopped finely
  7. Get 2 jalapeños, chopped finely, without seeds
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
  9. Get to taste Pepper
  10. Take 1 tablespoon oil

Instructions to make Spicy Cream Sauce:
  1. Boil water for pasta with pinch of salt, add pasta when boiling. At the same time, prepare meat of your choice. (I just threw frozen Linguiça in a pan until hot)
  2. While cooking other two ingredients, chop onions, garlic and jalapeños. Add these three to a non-stick pan with the oil and a pinch of salt, and sweat on low heat. Keep moving for 8-10 minutes, until vegetables are at desired softness.
  3. Add milk, cream cheese (used flavored cream cheese for an extra kick, EX: jalapeño cream cheese), a pinch of salt and a dash of pepper to the pan with the vegetables. Stir often until cream cheese melts and sauce thickens. Add more milk or cream cheese if sauce is too thick or is not thick enough.
  4. When hot, serve over pasta with the cooked meat. (Sauce becomes even more flavorful when served the next day after the flavors have marinated, so leftovers are encouraged!)
