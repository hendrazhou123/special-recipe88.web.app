The ingredients needed to make Vanilla Crema Café:
  1. Get 1 liter vanilla Ice cream
  2. Take 5 grams Coffee Powder
  3. Take 300 ml Cold Milk
  4. Take 70 ml heavy cream
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp Chocolate Syrup
