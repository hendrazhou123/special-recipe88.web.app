The ingredients needed to make Chocolate milk microwave mug cake:
  1. Get 4 tbsp flour
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp nesquick
  3. Take 1 tbsp sugar
  4. Make ready 1 dash of salt
  5. Prepare 1/8 tsp baking powder
  6. Take 3 tbsp oil ,non flavored preferably canola oil
  7. Make ready 3 tbsp milk
  8. Prepare 1 optional : nuts, marshmellow, chocolate chips
  9. Take 1 few drops of vanilla
