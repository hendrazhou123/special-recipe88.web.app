Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Cookie Delight:
  1. Spread cookie dough in bottom of 9x13 pan and bake at 350 for 14-15 minutes
  2. Mix confectioners sugar, cream cheese and 1 3/4 cup whipped topping. Spread on top of cookie dough.
  3. Mix milk and pudding mixes, spread over last layer
  4. Top with remaining whipped topping and chocolate chips.
  5. Cover and refrigerate 8 hours.
