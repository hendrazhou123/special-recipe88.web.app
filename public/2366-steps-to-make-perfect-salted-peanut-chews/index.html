The ingredients needed to make Salted Peanut Chews:
  1. Get Crust
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup flour
  3. Get 2/3 cup brown sugar
  4. Take 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  5. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup butter, melted
  7. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla
  8. Get 2 egg yolks
  9. Make ready Topping
  10. Get 3 cups miniature marshmallows
  11. Make ready 2/3 cup karo syrup
  12. Take 1/4 cup butter, melted
  13. Make ready 2 teaspoon vanilla
  14. Prepare 12 oz package peanut butter chips
  15. Take 2 cups Rice Krispies
  16. Get 2 cups peanuts

Steps to make Salted Peanut Chews:
  1. Whisk the dry crust ingredients in medium bowl.
  2. Whisk the wet crust ingredients together, then add them to dry ingredients. Mix until combined.
  3. Press the crust evenly into a greased 9x13 pan and bake on 350 degrees for 12-15 minutes.
  4. When crust is done, add an even layer of marshmallows to the top and return to the oven for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Remove pan from the oven and let cool. Do not skip this step!
  6. For the topping, melt the peanut butter chips, butter, karo syrup, and vanilla in a large saucepan on medium low heat.
  7. When the mixture is smooth, remove from heat and quickly mix in the Rice Krispies and peanuts. Press the topping mixture into an even layer on top of the marshmallows.
  8. Refrigeration is not required, but I like to put the pan in the refrigerator for an hour or so to make it easier to cut through the gooey topping and marshmallows.
