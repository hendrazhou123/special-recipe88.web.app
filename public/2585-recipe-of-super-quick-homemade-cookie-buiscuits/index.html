The ingredients needed to make Cookie Buiscuits:
  1. Prepare 3 cups flour
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Get 250 g butter
  4. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla flavour
  5. Get 2 tsp milk powder

Instructions to make Cookie Buiscuits:
  1. Mix butter, sugar, milk and flavour with wooden spatulla until sugar is desolved
  2. Slowly add flour mixing continuously to a smooth dough.
  3. Place on cling film or nylon and roll about half inch thick.
  4. Use cookie cutter and place on baking tray, be sure to put baking paper, i used note book paper
  5. Preheat oven at 250° bake on low heat for exactly 10 to 12 minutes or until golden brown
