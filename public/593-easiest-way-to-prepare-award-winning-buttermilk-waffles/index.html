The ingredients needed to make Buttermilk Waffles:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Make ready 2 Tbs sugar
  3. Get 2 tsp baking powder
  4. Take 1 tsp baking soda
  5. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  6. Prepare 2 cups low-fat buttermilk
  7. Get 1/3 cup melter butter (not margarine)
  8. Get 2 eggs
  9. Make ready 1 tsp vanilla

Steps to make Buttermilk Waffles:
  1. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix in buttermilk, melted butter, eggs and vanilla.
  3. Heat up your waffle iron and waffle make away!
  4. Heat up some extra butter and drizzle on top of finished waffles. Top with maple syrup and enjoy!!
