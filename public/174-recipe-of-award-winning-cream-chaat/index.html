The ingredients needed to make Cream Chaat:
  1. Take 2 cups Apples cubed
  2. Take 2 cups bananas
  3. Take 1 cup strawberries
  4. Get 1 cup boiled Macaroni
  5. Get 1 cup yogurt
  6. Make ready 1/2 cup Cream
  7. Take 1/2 cup Mayonnaise
  8. Get 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Prepare I tsp black pepper
  10. Take 4 tbls castor sugar

Instructions to make Cream Chaat:
  1. Mix sugar, salt and black pepper in Yogurt very well.
  2. Then add Mayonnaise and cream in it and mix well till combine.
  3. Now add apple, bananas, strawberries and macaroni in Yogurt mixture and mix well.
  4. Keep it in fridge and serve it chill.
