The ingredients needed to make Chickpea Flour Choc Chip Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1/2 cup coconut oil melted
  2. Make ready 1/4 cup natural honey (warmed)
  3. Get 1 egg
  4. Prepare 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Take 1 & 1/2 cup chickpea flour (garbanzo flour)
  6. Get 3/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp salt
  8. Make ready 1/2-3/4 cup Ghirardelli 60% cacao chocolate chips
  9. Get (Optional) 1/4 c chopped nuts
  10. Take (Optional) brown sugar

Steps to make Chickpea Flour Choc Chip Cookies:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Line a large sheet with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, mix tougher the chickpea flour, baking soda, and salt
  4. In a separate bowl mix all other ingredients except the chocolate chips and nuts. Be sure to have the coconut oil warm and the honey warm.
  5. Using a wooden spoon, add dry ingredients to the wet until well mixed.
  6. Fold in chocolate chips /nuts
  7. Spoon onto baking sheet, sprinkle a little brown sugar on top.
  8. Bake for 9-12 min or until top starts to brown.
  9. Cool after baking. Makes 12 cookies !
