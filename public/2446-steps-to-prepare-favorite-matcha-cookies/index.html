The ingredients needed to make Matcha cookies:
  1. Take 140 g flour
  2. Take 1 tbsp matcha powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Take 1/2 tsp baking soda or 1.5 tsp baking powder
  5. Take 170 g melted butter (cold)
  6. Prepare 200 g brown sugar
  7. Take 50 g white sugar
  8. Take 1 egg
  9. Get 1 egg yolk
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp vanilla essence
  11. Get 255 g semi sweet chocolate chip

Instructions to make Matcha cookies:
  1. DRY INGREDIENT : Sift flour with matcha powder, baking soda and 1/2 tsp salt
  2. WET INGREDIENT : Melted butter, brown sugar and white sugar
  3. Then Mix with mixer until smooth
  4. Then add in vanilla essence and egg yolk and egg
  5. Fold with spatula to not overmix
  6. Add dry ingredients gradually once wet ingredients mixed properly
  7. As add in more dry ingredients, colour of mixture slowly change to brown to green
  8. When dough peel away from side of bowl, stop mixing immediately
  9. Put chocolate chips with spatula
  10. Scoop about one tablespoon of dough and roll into ball
  11. Bake at 325F for 5 minutes at second lowest tray then 25 minutes at second top tray
