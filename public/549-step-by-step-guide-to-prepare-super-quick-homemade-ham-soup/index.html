The ingredients needed to make Ham 🐷Soup 🥣:
  1. Prepare Ham (leftover from Thanksgiving), cut up
  2. Prepare 1 tablespoon margarine
  3. Prepare 1 onion, chopped
  4. Take 5 cloves garlic, minced
  5. Prepare 2 cans beef broth (14 ounce)
  6. Make ready 1 can white beans (15.5 ounce), undrained
  7. Make ready 1 tablespoon oregano
  8. Take 1/8 teaspoon black pepper
  9. Make ready 1/4 teaspoon Sazon complete
  10. Make ready 1/2 cup egg noodles
  11. Prepare 1 cup green beans (I’m using frozen)

Instructions to make Ham 🐷Soup 🥣:
  1. In a large pot heat up your margarine…….
  2. Sauté onion until tender, then add minced garlic……
  3. Next add to pot : beef broth, white beans, oregano, black pepper and Sazon, heat until mixture boils…..
  4. Now add your cut up ham, stir well…..
  5. Next add the egg noodles and green beans, heat for several minutes until egg noodles are tender….
  6. Serve and enjoy 😉!
