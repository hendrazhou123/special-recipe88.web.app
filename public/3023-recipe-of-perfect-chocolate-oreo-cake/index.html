The ingredients needed to make Chocolate oreo cake:
  1. Make ready 1 cup flour
  2. Get 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  3. Make ready 1/2 cup sugar
  4. Take 1 tblsp baking powder
  5. Prepare 1 tblsp baking soda
  6. Get 1 cup butter milk
  7. Prepare 1 tblsp vanilla flavor
  8. Make ready 2 tblsp chocolate syrup
  9. Get Oreo biscuits
  10. Take 1 tblsp milk powder
  11. Make ready 2 eggs
