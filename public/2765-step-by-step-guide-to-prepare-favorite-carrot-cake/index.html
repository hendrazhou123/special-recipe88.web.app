The ingredients needed to make Carrot Cake:
  1. Prepare 4 large eggs
  2. Make ready 2 cups sugar
  3. Get 1 cup canola oil
  4. Prepare 2 cups all purpose flour
  5. Prepare 2-3 tsp ground cinnamon
  6. Get 3/4 tsp baking soda
  7. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  8. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  9. Take 1/4 tsp nutmeg
  10. Make ready 3 cups graded carrots
  11. Make ready frosting
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup butter-softened
  13. Get 3 oz. Cream cheese-softened
  14. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  15. Prepare 3-3/4 cups sugar
  16. Prepare 2-3 tbsp 2% milk
  17. Take 1 cup chopped nuts. (Optional)

Steps to make Carrot Cake:
  1. In a large bowl combine eggs, sugar, and oil. Combine the flour, cinnamon, baking soda, baking powder, salt,and nutmeg. Beat into egg mixture. Stir in the carrots.
  2. Pour into 2 greased and floured 9in.cakes pans. Bake at 350° for 35-40 min or until a toothpick comes out clean. Cool 10mi before removingfrom the pans to sit on wire racks for cooling.
