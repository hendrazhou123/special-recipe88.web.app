The ingredients needed to make Creamy Ice-Cream:
  1. Prepare 2 cups whip cream
  2. Get 1 cup milkmaid
  3. Prepare 1 Cup crushed Oreo for flavouring

Steps to make Creamy Ice-Cream:
  1. Take cold whip cream in a deep bowl.
  2. Whip cream for 3 mins then add Milkmaid and whip untill little thick (10 mins).
  3. Then add crushed Oreo to the cream mix well.
  4. Put in air tight container.put container into zip lock bag or in carrybag and keep in freezer for overnight or for at least 10 hours.
  5. Garnish with chocolate syrup and some Oreo biscuit.Serve chilled..
  6. NOTE=You can also add of your choice chocolate or gems for flavouring..
