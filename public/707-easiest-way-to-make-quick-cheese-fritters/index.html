The ingredients needed to make Cheese fritters:
  1. Prepare cheese grated
  2. Prepare maida
  3. Get small onion chopped
  4. Take small capsicum chopped
  5. Prepare green chillies chopped
  6. Get garlic cloves chopped
  7. Prepare small carrot grated
  8. Make ready baking powder
  9. Get red chilli powder

Steps to make Cheese fritters:
  1. In a bowl mix all the ingredients except oil.
  2. Bind them nicely to make a smooth mixture. Take a small portion and using two spoons make the shape. If you want you can directly use your hands to make the fritters in oval or round or whatever shape you like.
  3. Deep fry them in oil and take out on a kitchen paper towel to remove the excess oil
  4. Serve with chilli sauce.
