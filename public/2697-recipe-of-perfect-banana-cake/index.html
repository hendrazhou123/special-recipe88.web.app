The ingredients needed to make Banana cake:
  1. Take 1/2 cup magarine/butter
  2. Take 1 cup sugar
  3. Take 2 eggs
  4. Get 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1/2 tsp banana extract
  6. Get 1/4 cup milk
  7. Make ready 3 medium sized bananas
  8. Get 2 cups flour
  9. Make ready 1 tsp baking powder
  10. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt
  11. Get 1 tsp nutmeg
  12. Make ready 1/2 cup groundnuts(optional)

Steps to make Banana cake:
  1. Measure ingredients, prep your baking pan and preheat oven to 360Â°.
  2. Cream sugar and magarine together until it's fluffy.
  3. Add in eggs one at a time until thoroughly mixed.
  4. Mash the bananas, add it to the mixture and mix together.
  5. Add in the extracts.
  6. Mix dry ingredients together and add half of it the mixture.
  7. Add in the milk and pour the rest of the flour into the mixture. Make sure not to over mix.
  8. Pour your cake mixture into your baking pan and sprinkle the ground nuts on top.
  9. Bake till your skewer or toothpick comes out clean
