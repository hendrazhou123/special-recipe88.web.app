Steps to make Chocolate milkshake Oreo:
  1. Bring a chocolate popsicle
  2. Put the popsicle in the cup and add in the cup 1 cup of hot milk
  3. Stir the chocolate popsicle and the milk together till the chocolate is melted
  4. Design the cup with caramel syrup
  5. Make 1/2 cup of hot coffee
  6. Pour the hot coffee in the cup
  7. Design the top with cream
  8. Put a cookie of oreo
  9. Done, enjoy your drink 😊
