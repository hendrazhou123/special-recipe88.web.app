Instructions to make Chocolate filled sugar cookies:
  1. Mix together all dry ingredients, with butter in large mixing bowl
  2. Add wet ingredients, mix well.
  3. Roll into 1-2 inc balls
  4. Press 3-5 chocolate chips into each cookie ball center.
  5. Cook in preheated oven at 350° for 13 minutes or until golden brown. Let sit 2 minutes before removing from pan. Note: line pan with wax paper to avoid burnt bottoms or sticking.
