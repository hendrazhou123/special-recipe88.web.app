The ingredients needed to make Easiest Butter Cookies!!!:
  1. Get 2 stick (113 g) each or 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  2. Get 1 tsp vanilla extract
  3. Make ready 3/4 cup powdered sugar (sifted)
  4. Get 2 cup all-purpose flour (sifted)
  5. Get strawberry preserves

Steps to make Easiest Butter Cookies!!!:
  1. Cream butter and vanilla extract until fluffy.
  2. Add powder sugar and mix until well combined.
  3. Add the flour cup by cup until combined.
  4. Refrigerate for 15 min. Then make little balls of dough and press on the center with the thumb, put a little bit of strawberry preserves. Bake for 12-14 min at 350° (on the highest rack).
