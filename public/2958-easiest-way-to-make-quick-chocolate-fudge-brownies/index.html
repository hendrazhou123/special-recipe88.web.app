The ingredients needed to make Chocolate fudge brownies:
  1. Make ready 100 g melted butter
  2. Get 1 cup vegetables oil
  3. Take 3 cups sugar
  4. Take 4 lagre egg's
  5. Make ready 1 cup plain flour
  6. Get 1 cup Coco powder
  7. Get 1 spoon baking powder
  8. Take Chopped chocolate (I used dairy milk)
  9. Make ready Chopped almonds for garnish
  10. Make ready Graded coconut for garnish

Instructions to make Chocolate fudge brownies:
  1. Mix all ingredients mentioned above
  2. Add plain flour and coco powder mix well
  3. Add chopped pieces of chocolate in it
  4. Greace a baking tray and pour batter in it
  5. Pre heat oven at 250° for 10 mints and keep tray in it
  6. Bake for 45 mints after finishing let it cook at room temperature
  7. Remove from the tray and garnish with graded coconut and chopped alomnds
  8. Cut into small pieces
