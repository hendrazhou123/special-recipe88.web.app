The ingredients needed to make Lemon sugar cookie:
  1. Make ready 1 cup cold butter cut into cubes
  2. Take 2 cups sugar
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Take 1 tsp vanilla extract
  5. Get 1 1/2 tbsp fresh lemon juice
  6. Take 1 tbsp lemon zest, may put more for flavor
  7. Prepare 3 cups flour
  8. Make ready 1 1/4 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp baking soda
  10. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  11. Take as required use yellow food coloring for colour(optional)

Instructions to make Lemon sugar cookie:
  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
  2. In a large bowl, cream butter and sugar for 4 minutes until light and fluffy. Add eggs, vanilla, lemon juice, and lemon zest and stir to combine.
  3. Stir in flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt and mix just until combined.
  4. Drop dough onto light-colored baking sheet.
  5. Bake for 9-11 minutes. Sprinkle with sugar or white sparkling sugar.
