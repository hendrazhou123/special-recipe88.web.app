The ingredients needed to make Asian Inspired Peanut Sauce:
  1. Take 6 tbsp Creamy Peanut Butter
  2. Make ready 4 tbsp Brown Sugar
  3. Prepare 2 tbsp Liquid Aminos/Soy Sauce
  4. Make ready 1/4-1/2 tsp Ground Ginger
  5. Make ready 1/2 cup Warm Water, or more or less
