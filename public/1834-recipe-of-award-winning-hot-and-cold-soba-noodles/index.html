The ingredients needed to make Hot and cold soba noodles:
  1. Take 8 oz soba noodles
  2. Make ready 1/2 cup chopped fresh cilantro leaves
  3. Get 2 tbsp low sodium soy sauce
  4. Make ready 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  5. Make ready 2 tsp chili oil
  6. Prepare 1/2 tsp crushed red pepper, or to taste
  7. Get 1 dash pepper
  8. Get 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Instructions to make Hot and cold soba noodles:
  1. Cook noodles according to package directions.
  2. Drain noodles and rinse well. Try to shake off as much water as possible.
  3. Meanwhile, whisk together soy sauce, rice vinegar, chili oil, and pepper.
  4. Toss noodles with sauce and cilantro. Chill 2 hours.
  5. Sprinkle with sesame seeds and serve.
