The ingredients needed to make Sweet Milk Pancakes:
  1. Get 1 cup flour
  2. Take 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  3. Take 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Get 1 tbsp sugar
  5. Get 1 egg beaten
  6. Take 1 cup milk
  7. Prepare 2 tsp melted shortening

Steps to make Sweet Milk Pancakes:
  1. Measure dry ingredients into a deep bowl.
  2. Make a well in the dry ingredients. Pour in the beaten egg, milk and melted shortening.
  3. Use a wire whisk to incorporate ingredients.
  4. Now you can design your own favorite pancake. Sliced apples and cinnamon can be mixed into the batter or perhaps fresh blueberries or maybe even chocolate chips.
  5. Use a ladle to pour pancake batter on a hot grill or skillet. Watch for bubbles before flipping pancake.
  6. Drizzle with maple syrup or powdered sugar. Enjoy!
