The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Prepare 1 cup packed brown sugar
  2. Get 1/2 cup peanut butter
  3. Prepare 1/2 cup butter or margarine softened
  4. Get 1 egg
  5. Take 1 tsp vanilla
  6. Make ready 1 1/4 cup flour
  7. Take 3/4 tsp baking soda
  8. Prepare 1/2 tsp baking powder
  9. Make ready 1/4 tsp salt if not using salted butter
  10. Get 1 cup peanut butter chips OR chocolate chips
  11. Make ready Granulated sugar for dipping

Instructions to make Chocolate Chip Peanut Butter Cookies:
  1. Heat oven to 375°F. Cream sugar, butter, peanut butter and egg in a large bowl.
  2. Stir in flour, baking soda, baking powder and your choice of "chips" I only had chocolate chips on hand so that's what I used.
  3. Roll dough into small balls, flatten slightly if you don't want really thick cookies, then dip into granulated sugar and place sugar side up on cookie sheet
  4. Bake between 10-15 minutes and let cool slightly on cookie sheet. Remove and serve.
