The ingredients needed to make Raisin cake:
  1. Take 4 eggs
  2. Get 1 glass sugar (1,1,2)
  3. Get 1 small spoonful of baking powder
  4. Get 1 glass milk
  5. Take Half a glass of oil or 200g butter
  6. Prepare 3 glasses flour
  7. Prepare Raisins in the required amount

Steps to make Raisin cake:
  1. Pour the eggs and sugar into a bowl And mix it to make it stiff
  2. Add baking powder, milk and oil
  3. Add flour and mix in one direction
  4. Grease the pan and Pour liquid into pan
  5. Pour the raisins on the liquid
  6. Heat for about fifty minutes
