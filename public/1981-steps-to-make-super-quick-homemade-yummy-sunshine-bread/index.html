Steps to make Yummy Sunshine bread:
  1. Put all the ingredients in the mixer instead of the liquid mixture if you want
  2. Mix it and wait for the dough to be like any kind of proper dough
  3. When you're done cut it into multiple big platforms
  4. Next put it on a tray then put In front of the sun aka on the outside! Also, it cannot be night or afternoon when you put it in front of the sun
  5. It must stay there for 1 hour and 30 min when done put it in the oven for 30 min should be as hot as 180'F
  6. After 30 min, it is done. Get it out of the oven and enjoy your sunny bread! Also you can eat it outside by itself or inside by itself or both together
