The ingredients needed to make Cookie House:
  1. Take 560 grams Cake flour
  2. Take 280 grams Butter
  3. Get 180 grams Sugar
  4. Take 2 Eggs
  5. Get 1 dash Vanilla extract
  6. Get 1 dash Food colouring (red/green/yellow)
  7. Prepare 1 tsp plus Cocoa
  8. Prepare 300 grams [For icing] Powdered sugar
  9. Prepare 1/2 plus [For icing] Egg white
  10. Take 1 dash [For icing] Food colouring

Instructions to make Cookie House:
  1. Design the house by drawing a roof, chimney and 4 walls in any design you like and make paper stencils.
  2. Bring butter to room temperature and soften. When it has softened, mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla extract.
  3. Add sifted flour and mix together. When it has come together as a dough, divide into separate parts and colour with the food colouring and cocoa. Wrap and let sit in the fridge.
  4. After letting it rest for 30 minutes, roll out to 0.5 mm, and then cut the parts out around paper stencils with a knife.
  5. Line the parts up on a baking tray and bake at 190℃ for 20 minutes. When baked, put the parts on a rack and let cool.
  6. When cooled, secure the parts with icing and it's done. Do this carefully one part at a time, making sure each part is hard and secured before moving on to the next.
