Steps to make Yummy Peanut 🥜 Butter & Jelly Stuffed Pancakes 🥞:
  1. Prepare pancake mix according to package directions or use your favorite pancake recipe…..😁
  2. Make several pancakes and set them off to the side or place on warm heat in the oven….I put wax paper between each of my pancakes so they won’t stick together !! 😉😊WATCH THEM CAREFULLY IF YOU PLACE THEM IN THE OVEN !!! You don’t want them to get burned !!! 😧😧😧
  3. Now lay one pancake 🥞 on a plate, take your peanut butter and just spread it on half of your pancake…..
  4. Use your jelly to spread on the other half of your pancake 🥞…..
  5. Now take another pancake and gently place it on top of your peanut butter/ jelly half of the pancake that is on your plate……
  6. Top your wonderful stuffed pancake 🥞 with syrup, cinnamon powder, chocolate syrup, whipped topping,sliced banana 🍌 and enjoy 😉!!!
