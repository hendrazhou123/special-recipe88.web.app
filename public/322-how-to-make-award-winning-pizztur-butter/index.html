The ingredients needed to make Pizztur Butter:
  1. Prepare 2 cups Malai
  2. Take 1/4 tspn turmeric powder
  3. Get 1/2 tspn salt
  4. Get 1/2 tspn chilli flakes
  5. Get 1/2 tspn oregano
  6. Prepare 1/2 tspn pizza spice mix

Instructions to make Pizztur Butter:
  1. Take Chilled Malai and blend with blender, it starts become thick.
  2. Blend some more time until leaves buttermilk. and blend little bit more.
  3. It become some curdle and thick. Now collect all butter with hands. And separate buttermilk.
  4. Now transfer buttermilk into another bowl to further use for paneer or in any gravy.
  5. Now wash butter with chilled water..
  6. Add turmeric powder, salt, chilli flakes, oregano and spice mix all into butter and mix well.
  7. Our butter is ready shift into any shaped box and kept into refrigerator to set.
  8. After setting demould the butter into plate and cut in slices.
  9. Ready our yummy tasty Masala butter to use.
