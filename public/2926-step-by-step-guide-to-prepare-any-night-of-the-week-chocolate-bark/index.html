  2. Get 10 oz. milk chocolate baking chips

Steps to make Chocolate Bark:
  1. Line a large pan or dish with ridges with parchment paper.
  2. Melt chocolate chips in separate pots using double boiler method.
  3. Add almonds to both batches of chocolate. Stir well.
  4. Pour milk chocolate into prepared pan. Pour white chocolate on top. Swirl with a toothpick or fork.
  5. Refrigerate at least 3 hours before breaking into large chunks.
