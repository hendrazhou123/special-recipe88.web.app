The ingredients needed to make Butter paneer:
  1. Take 4 tsp butter
  2. Get 200 gm paneer cut into pieces
  3. Make ready 2 tsp ghee
  4. Make ready to taste salt and red chilli powder
  5. Take 1/4 tsp black pepper Powder
  6. Take Pinch Turmeric powder
  7. Get 1 onion sliced
  8. Prepare 1/4 cup cream
  9. Take 3 tsp milk

Instructions to make Butter paneer:
  1. In a pan add butter and paneer.saute for two minutes and keep aside.
  2. In the same pan add ghee and onion.saute well.
  3. Then add all Spices and cream.cook for a minute.
  4. Now add paneer and milk.mix it.cook for two minutes.
  5. Paneer is ready to serve hot.
  6. Enjoy.
