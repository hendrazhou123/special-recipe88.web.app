The ingredients needed to make Matar Mushroom and paneer:
  1. Take 100 g matar or peas
  2. Take 100 g paneer
  3. Get 1 pkt mushroom
  4. Take 2 onions
  5. Get 2 tomatoes
  6. Prepare 5-6 piece garlic
  7. Prepare 1 inch ginger
  8. Prepare to taste Salt
  9. Get Red chilli powder
  10. Prepare Turmeric powder
  11. Take 1 glass water

Steps to make Matar Mushroom and paneer:
  1. First put onion,tomatoes,ginger,garlic in a mixture then make fine paste of it.
  2. Take a pressure cooker then add oil in it then put peas in it. cook for 5 minute
  3. Then add mixture of onions and tomatoes in it cook another 5 min
  4. Add 1 glass water and boil it for 5 minutes
  5. Put lid of pressure cooker cook till 2-3 whistle on medium flame
  6. After open the lid add paneer in it.cook in a steam not open gas.
  7. After 5 min ur matar,mushroom,paneer sabji is ready.
