The ingredients needed to make Cinnamon sugar chocolate sandwich:
  1. Take 2 slice Multigrain bread
  2. Prepare 1 tbsp ground sugar
  3. Prepare 1 TSP cinnamon powder
  4. Get 2 tbsp butter
  5. Make ready 1 slice Chocolate cheese
  6. Prepare 2 tbsp as per choice shredded choc. / Nutella / any choc. spread

Instructions to make Cinnamon sugar chocolate sandwich:
  1. In a bowl combine sugar and cinnamon powder and keep it aside.
  2. Now take 2 slice of Multigrain bread and spread butter generously.
  3. Now sprinkle cinnamon sugar powder on both the bread.
  4. Now add cheese slice easily available at stores. If not then add simple cheese slice.
  5. Now add any Chocolate spread or chocolate shreddings on top and put another bread on top.
  6. Apply butter on both the side and toast it in any toaster. I have used gas toaster.and toast it untill crispy, brown and untill chocolate melts.
  7. Now apply butter on top and sprinkle cinnamon sugar powder on top. Cut it in two and enjoy it when it's hot.
