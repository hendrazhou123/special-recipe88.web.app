The ingredients needed to make Sugar Glazed Doughnuts:
  1. Get For the dough
  2. Make ready 1 1/3 cups warm milk
  3. Get 2 tbs melted butter
  4. Make ready 1 egg, at room temperature
  5. Get 1 tsp salt
  6. Get 2 tbs sugar
  7. Prepare 1 tbs instant dry yeast
  8. Prepare 3 1/2 – 4 cups all-purpose flour
  9. Make ready For sugar glaze:
  10. Get 1 1/2 cups powdered sugar
  11. Take 2 tbs melted butter
  12. Prepare 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  13. Prepare 4-6 tbs hot water, may need more
  14. Get 2 cups vegetable oil for frying!

Steps to make Sugar Glazed Doughnuts:
  1. In a large bowl beat the egg, then add the milk,butter, vanilla extract and quickly stir.
  2. In another bowl combine : flour, yeast, sugar and salt, quickly mix and start adding to liquids.
  3. Add the flour slowly, allowing the liquids to absorb it.
  4. When the dough starts to thicken, transfer to your work surface and knead until smooth non sticky dough is formed.
  5. Place the dough into a greased bowl, cover with plastic or towel and allow to rise, 45-60 minutes.
