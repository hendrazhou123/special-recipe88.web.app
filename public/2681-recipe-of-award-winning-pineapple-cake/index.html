The ingredients needed to make Pineapple cake:
  1. Make ready 1 cup milk
  2. Prepare 1&1/2 tsp lemon juice
  3. Prepare 1&1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  4. Prepare 1 cup powdered sugar
  5. Prepare 4 tsp Pineapple crush
  6. Prepare As per taste Sugar syrup
  7. Get 1 tsp baking powder
  8. Get 1/4 tsp baking soda
  9. Prepare 2 cups Whipped cream
  10. Take As required Chocolate decoratives
  11. Make ready 2 tsp milk powder
  12. Prepare 1/2 cup Oil

Steps to make Pineapple cake:
  1. Take bowl take 1 cup milk add lemon juice and keep aside for 10 mins.
  2. Sieve all-purpose cream, milk powder, baking powder, baking soda in a bowl.
  3. Now in milk mixture add sugar and oil whisk nicely.
  4. Gradually add dry contents to wet, mix well.pour mixture in greased mould and bake for 30 mins in preheated oven at 180 degrees.
  5. Let cool the cake, cut into three parts moist with syurp and pineapple crush apply whipped cream same do all three parts and decorate.
