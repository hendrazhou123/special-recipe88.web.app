The ingredients needed to make Peanut-Butter Cookie bliss balls:
  1. Get 4 Tbsp Peanut-Butter
  2. Make ready 3 Tbsp honey
  3. Make ready 1 pinch salt
  4. Get 1 tsp vanilla essence
  5. Prepare 1 1/4 cups ground almond
  6. Prepare 40 grams chocolate (chips or chopped)

Steps to make Peanut-Butter Cookie bliss balls:
  1. Mix together the P-B, salt, honey and vanilla until combined
  2. Put in the almond and knead with your fingers until it forms a dough. Add chocolate and mix until incorparated.
  3. Shape the mixture into balls and put in the fridge to set. Keep the bliss balls in the fridge.
