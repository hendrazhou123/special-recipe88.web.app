The ingredients needed to make Korean Tteokbokki (Hot and Spicy Rice Cake):
  1. Take 120 gr rice flour
  2. Take 1 tbsp tapioca flour
  3. Prepare 1 tbsp corn stratch flour solution
  4. Take 50 ml boiled water (approxinately)
  5. Make ready 2 cayenne pepper (chopped)
  6. Prepare 1 tsp garlic (chopped)
  7. Make ready 1 spring onion (chopped)
  8. Prepare 4 tbsp tomato sauce
  9. Make ready 1 tsp salt
  10. Prepare 1 tsp buillon powder
  11. Prepare Sesame (i don't use it)
  12. Make ready Water
  13. Make ready 1 tbsp frying oil
