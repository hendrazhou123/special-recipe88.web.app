The ingredients needed to make Buttery delicious sugar cookies:
  1. Make ready 3 c flour
  2. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  3. Get 1/2 tsp salt
  4. Make ready 1 c butter, melted
  5. Make ready 1-1/2 c white sugar
  6. Take 2 eggs
  7. Make ready 4 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions to make Buttery delicious sugar cookies:
  1. Sift flour, baking powder and salt together, set aside. In large mixing bowl cream together butter and sugar until light and fluffy. Beat in eggs one at a time. Beat in vanilla. Gradually beat in flour mixture until combined.
  2. Cover dough and chill for 2 hours.
  3. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Grease cookie sheets. On a clean, floured surface roll out small portions of chilled dough til 1/4” thick. Cut out shapes using cookie cutters. Bake 8-10 minutes (you know they’re ready when the center doesn’t look “wet” anymore). Cool on wire racks.
  4. All done!
