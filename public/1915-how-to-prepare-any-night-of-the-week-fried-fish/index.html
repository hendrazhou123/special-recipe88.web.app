The ingredients needed to make Fried Fish:
  1. Take 1 cup corn meal
  2. Make ready 1 cup all purpose flour
  3. Get 2 eggs
  4. Get 4 tbsp hot sauce
  5. Get 2 tbsp yellow mustard
  6. Get 1 tbsp paprika
  7. Take 1 tbsp blk pepper
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp garlic powder
  9. Prepare 1 tbsp onion powder
  10. Prepare 1 tbsp Old Bay season
  11. Make ready 1 tbsp lemon juice

Steps to make Fried Fish:
  1. Mix all seasonings & in a separate ziplock bag mix flour & corn meal. Take a tbsp or so & add it to flour mix.
  2. Add eggs, mustard, lemon juice, & hot sauce to the remaining seasoning mix.
  3. Cover dry filets with seasoning/egg mix
  4. Place each filet in flour bag, coat well & set to the side unto ready to deep fry
  5. Fry until golden brown, remove from oil & place on paper towels. Enjoy with cold slaw, potato salad, or whatever sides you like.
