The ingredients needed to make Chocolate cake🍰:
  1. Get 1 3/4 cup flour
  2. Get 1 1/2 cup sugar
  3. Prepare 3/4 cup cacao powder
  4. Get 1 tbs baking soda
  5. Prepare 1 tbs baking powder
  6. Prepare 1/2 tbs salt
  7. Get 2-3 eggs
  8. Get 1 cup milk
  9. Take 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  10. Make ready 2 tbs vanilla extract
  11. Take 3/4 cup boiling water
  12. Get 500 grams cottage cheese or cream cheese
  13. Make ready 200 grams sour cream
  14. Take 100 grams powdered sugar
  15. Take Berries or chocolate for decorations

Instructions to make Chocolate cake🍰:
  1. Mix flour, cacao powder, baking soda, baking powder, salt🧂 and sugar🥣 1. Add eggs🥚, milk🥛, vanilla extract, vegetable oil. Use hand mixer or stand mixer 1. Add little by little boiling water💦 1. Pour the mass into a form 1. Bake for 30-35⏱ minutes, 180 degrees celsius
  2. Cream/cottage cheese mix with sour cream🍶, vanilla extract and powdered sugar. Use hand mixer 1. Grease the cakes with cream🎂 1. Decorate the cake with berries🍒,chocolate🍫 or fruits🥝 as you wish. Bon appetit!!!🎂
