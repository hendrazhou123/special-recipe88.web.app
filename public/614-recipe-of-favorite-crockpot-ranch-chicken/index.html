The ingredients needed to make Crockpot Ranch Chicken:
  1. Make ready 1 pack boneless skinless chicken breast
  2. Get 1 (8 oz) bar cream cheese(softened)
  3. Prepare 1 envelope Ranch Dressing mix (or 2 tablespoons dry mix)
  4. Take 2 onions, sliced
  5. Take Fresh garlic (about 6 cloves)
  6. Prepare Sprinkle Black Pepper
  7. Take Sprinkle Parsley Flakes
  8. Take 3 heaping tablespoons of butter (or 1 stick margarine)

Steps to make Crockpot Ranch Chicken:
  1. Rinse out your chicken, cut into strips and place into greased crockpot…..
  2. Take your softened cream cheese and add it to your chicken in the crockpot, cutting up the cream cheese into smaller blocks…
  3. Add your dry ranch dressing mix……
  4. Add your sliced onions……
  5. Add your fresh garlic, black pepper and parsley flakes….
  6. Next add your butter or margarine……
  7. Mix all well and cook on low for 6 hours…..
  8. Stir occasionally so that your ingredients all melt into your chicken……
