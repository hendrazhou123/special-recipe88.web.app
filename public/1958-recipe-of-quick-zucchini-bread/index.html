The ingredients needed to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Prepare 2 grated zucchinis
  2. Make ready 3 cups all purpose flour
  3. Get 1 cup brown sugar
  4. Prepare 1 cup white sugar
  5. Take 1 tsp baking powder
  6. Get 1 tsp baking soda
  7. Get 3 large eggs
  8. Make ready 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Take 3 tsp ground cinnamon
  10. Prepare 3 tsp vanilla extract
  11. Make ready 1 tsp ground kosher salt
  12. Get 1 cup vege oil
  13. Take Optional
  14. Take 1 cup chopped walnuts

Combine sugars, oil, and eggs and mix. In another bowl, mix flour, baking powder, soda, salt, and cinnamon. My zucchini bread is filled with great cinnamon flavor. It makes two loaves—have one now.

Steps to make Zucchini Bread:
  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Grate zucchini and mix ingredients in bowl.
  3. Grease and flour two bread baking pans. Pour mixture in pans.
  4. Add to oven bake for 50 mins. Or until done in center.
  5. Let cool. 15 minutes
  6. With a lil sweet cream butter, ENJOY friends!
