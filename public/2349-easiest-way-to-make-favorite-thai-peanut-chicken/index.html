The ingredients needed to make Thai Peanut Chicken:
  1. Get 2 Boneless Chicken Breasts, diced and cooked
  2. Make ready 1 Red Onion
  3. Make ready 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  4. Get 1 tbsp Lemon Grass
  5. Take 1 tsp Asian Fish Sauce
  6. Make ready 1 Can Coconut Milk
  7. Get 3/4 Cup Peanut Butter
  8. Take 2 tbsp Brown Sugar
  9. Take 2 tbsp freshly grated Ginger

Steps to make Thai Peanut Chicken:
  1. Saute red onion in butter until browned. Add soy sauce, sugar, fish sauce, lemon grass and ginger. Saute over low heat for 5-10 minutes.
  2. Add coconut milk, cook over medium low 5 minutes.
  3. Add peanut butter, use barmixer to blend.
  4. Serve over chicken with rice or pasta and vegetables.
