The ingredients needed to make Bourbon treacle and vanilla granola:
  1. Get bourbon canola
  2. Get 1 cup Bourbon
  3. Take 1/2 cup real maple syrup
  4. Prepare 4 tbsp real vanilla extract
  5. Prepare 3 tbsp of treacle
  6. Take 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  7. Take 1 tsp ground clove
  8. Make ready 1 tsp ground nutmeg
  9. Get 3 tbsp oil
  10. Prepare 2 tbsp honey
  11. Take 1 cup light brown sugar
  12. Make ready 1 kg rolled oats
  13. Get 250 grams mix fruit and nuts
  14. Prepare 50 grams seeds any
  15. Make ready 200 grams pecans
  16. Take 1 cup honey crusted cashews
  17. Take 1 pinch sea salt

Steps to make Bourbon treacle and vanilla granola:
  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  2. Mix all the ingredients together in a large bowl liquid first then brown sugar followed by others
  3. Pour mixed ingredients onto a lined baking tray
  4. Back in top of oven for 30/35 minutes or until golden brown
  5. Take out and cool completely on bench
  6. Store in an airtight container in pantry
