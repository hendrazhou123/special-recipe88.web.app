The ingredients needed to make Cookie batter oatmeal:
  1. Get 1/2 cup old fashioned oats
  2. Take 3/4 cup milk (any)
  3. Get 1 tbsp cocoa powder
  4. Take 4 tsp sugar
  5. Take 2/3 mashed banana
  6. Make ready 1 chopped nuts (as much as you want)
  7. Take 1 chocolate syrup (as much as you want)
  8. Prepare 1 tbsp peanut butter
  9. Get pinch salt

Instructions to make Cookie batter oatmeal:
  1. In a small boiling pan put in oats, milk, cocoa powder, and salt and bring to a boil. Lower heat and let sit 10 minutes stirring occasionally.
  2. Remove from heat add in peanut butter, sugar, and mashed banana.
  3. Drizzle chocolate syrup and nuts over top. Enjoy :)
