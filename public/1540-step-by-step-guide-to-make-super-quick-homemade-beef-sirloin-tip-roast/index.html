Instructions to make Beef Sirloin Tip Roast:
  1. Rub sirloin tip with pepper, salt and Italian herbs
  2. Roast uncovered at 450 degrees for 15 min
  3. Cover, reduce heat to 350 degrees and continue roasting for 45min (medium rare) to 1h (medium well)
  4. Let rest 10min before serving
