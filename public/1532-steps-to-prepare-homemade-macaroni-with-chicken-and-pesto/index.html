The ingredients needed to make Macaroni with chicken and pesto:
  1. Prepare 2 cups macaroni
  2. Make ready 500 ml water
  3. Prepare 1 table spoon salt
  4. Get 2 teaspoon pepper
  5. Make ready 5 table spoon pesto
  6. Prepare 200 gram chicken breast
  7. Get 100 gram pork belly
  8. Get 100 ml oil

Steps to make Macaroni with chicken and pesto:
  1. Put 500 ml of water into a boil
  2. Add in 2 cups of macaroni and 1 table spoon of salt, 2 teaspoon of pepper and stir and leave it until its cook
  3. While waiting for the macaroni to cook.cut the chicken into cube and the pork belly into 2 cm cubes
  4. Marinate the chicken with salt and pepper and stir until its even and leave it aside for 10 minutes
  5. While waiting for the chicken and pork belly to marinate drain the macaroni and set it aside
  6. After chicken and pork belly is marinate cook it in a pan with oil until its cook all the way through
  7. Drain the oil from the chicken.
  8. In a new pan add in the cook chicken pork belly macaroni and pesto and stir until its warmed up and mixed well
