The ingredients needed to make Peanut butter Milky Way cookies:
  1. Make ready 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  2. Prepare 1 cup creamy peanut butter
  3. Prepare 2 eggs
  4. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla
  5. Prepare 2 teaspoons baking soda
  6. Make ready 1 teaspoon salt
  7. Make ready 1 1/2 cups all purpose flour
  8. Take 1 cup quick cooking oats
  9. Get 1 package Milky Way unwrapped bites, roughly chopped

Instructions to make Peanut butter Milky Way cookies:
  1. Cream butter, peanut butter, and sugar in a stand mixer until light and fluffy
  2. Add vanilla. Then add eggs one at a time making sure the first egg is well incorporated before adding the next.
  3. Stir flour, salt, and baking soda in a separate bowl. Then add gradually to the butter and egg mixture until everything is well incorporated.
  4. Add the oats and Milky Way pieces and stir until combined
  5. Chill dough for at least an hour, can be chilled overnight.
  6. Roll dough into two inch balls then flatten them into disks with your palms. Place on an ungreased baking sheet
  7. Bake in an oven preheated to 325 degrees for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown.
  8. Enjoy!
