The ingredients needed to make Banana walnut cake:
  1. Get Dry ingredients
  2. Get 120 g Flour(cake)
  3. Make ready 3/4 tsp Baking powder
  4. Prepare 1/4 tsp Baking soda
  5. Take 60 g Brown sugar
  6. Take 1/2 tsp Salt
  7. Make ready 1 pinch Chopped walnut
  8. Take Wet ingredients
  9. Prepare 3 pcs Babana
  10. Get 2 pcs Egg
  11. Make ready 40 g Butter

Instructions to make Banana walnut cake:
  1. Sift the flour, add all dry ingredients (except the chopped walnut) and mix well. (leave a pinch of brown sugar for later)
  2. Melt the butter, mash the bananas and stir all wet ingredients well.(leave some melted butter for later)
  3. Mix both the dry and wet ingredients together.
  4. Put parchment paper inside a loaf tray, then pour the ingredient into it. Evenly spread the brown sugar on the top (left from step 1) and squirt a line of melted butter (left from step 2) before baking it.
  5. 150 degree for 30 mins, 175 degree for 5 mins. Remove the loaf tray and leave the cake cool.
