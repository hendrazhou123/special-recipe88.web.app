Instructions to make Roasted Red Potatoes:
  1. Cut the potatoes and boil them with a touch of salt about 15 minutes and preheat oven to 375°
  2. While they're cooking in a bowl mix all other ingredients
  3. When potatoes are done drain them and put them in with ingredients bowl and gently mix them all together so potatoes are coated
  4. Pour all of the ingredients into a baking pan and bake about 10 minutes or til they start to get the edges browned
