The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Porridge:
  1. Take 6 dates
  2. Make ready 100 g oats
  3. Get 450 g water or milk
  4. Make ready 1 ripe bannana
  5. Take 1 tablespoon cocoa powder
  6. Prepare Yoghurt, fruit and granola to serve

Steps to make Chocolate Porridge:
  1. In the thermocooker blitz the dates, banana and cocoa for 10 - 15 seconds, speed 8.
  2. Add water or milk and oats. Cook speed 2, reverse, 10 minutes.
  3. Serve with toppings of choice. Its sweet enough as it is but you can drizzle on maple syrup or honey if needed.
