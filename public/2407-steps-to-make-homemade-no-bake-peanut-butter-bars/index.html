Instructions to make no bake peanut butter bars:
  1. Mixed together the butter graham cracker crumbs and confectionery sugar one cup of peanut butter.
  2. Press evenly in a 9 by 13 ungreased pan. Chill for 15 to 20 minutes before spreading the topping over the base
  3. For the topping, in the microwave melt the chocolate chips and peanut butter. Stir well until smooth
  4. Spread over the crust and refrigerate for 20 minutes. Cut into squares so topping won't crack and continue to refrigerate.
