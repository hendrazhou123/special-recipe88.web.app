The ingredients needed to make Orlando Pub Cheese:
  1. Take Extra sharp cheddar, cut into cubes
  2. Get Monterey Jack, cut into cubes
  3. Make ready fresh garlic, minced
  4. Prepare Worcestershire sauce
  5. Make ready Red wine vinegar
  6. Prepare Dark beer or stout
  7. Make ready Dijon mustard
  8. Make ready sriracha

Steps to make Orlando Pub Cheese:
  1. In a food processor with chopping blade, blend all ingredients until creamy smooth.
  2. If you like it spicier, add more sriracha, a little at a time.
  3. Refrigerate, covered.
