The ingredients needed to make Easy Custard Cream:
  1. Take 3 pieces Yolk
  2. Take 1 tbsp Flour
  3. Make ready 3 tbsp Sugar
  4. Get 150 ml Milk
  5. Make ready As needed Vanilla essence

Steps to make Easy Custard Cream:
  1. Put the all ingredients in a heat resistance bowl. Mix it well. (Be careful not to coagulate the flour.)
  2. Covered the bowl with wrap and heat it for 1 minuet with 500W microwave.
  3. Take it out of the microwave and stir it for 30 seconds.
  4. Repeat step 3 & 4, total four times. Finish!
