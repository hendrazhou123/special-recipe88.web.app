The ingredients needed to make Salt and Vinegar French Fries:
  1. Make ready 5 lb Russet Potatoes
  2. Make ready 3 cup White Vinegar
  3. Take 3 cup Water
  4. Get 1 Course Sea Salt (to your liking)
  5. Take 1 Black Pepper (to your liking)
  6. Take 3 tbsp Olive Oil

I fill a tiny spray bottle with vinegar so we can spray additional on our fries. This is an old recipe from Home Cooking. Sprinkle them with the nutritional yeast and salt (if using) to taste. Cooking Channel serves up this Vinegar French Fries recipe plus many other recipes at CookingChannelTV.com.

Steps to make Salt and Vinegar French Fries:
  1. Cut potatoes into french fries. I used a french fry cutter, so easy!
  2. In a large pot, combine vinegar and water.
  3. Add fries and bring to a boil.
  4. Let cook until tender.
  5. Pre heat oven to 400.
  6. Let cool in vinegar and water. About 1/2 hour.
  7. Drain and toss in olive oil.
  8. Place fries on lightly greased cookie sheet.
  9. Add salt and pepper, bake until browned.
  10. Enjoy!

Dress the rustic cut potatoes in plenty of vinegar and salt to enhance the already-tangy flavor. Apparently putting vinegar on fries brings out the potato flavor more.
