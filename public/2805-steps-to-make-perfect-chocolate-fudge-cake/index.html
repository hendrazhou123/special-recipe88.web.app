The ingredients needed to make Chocolate Fudge Cake:
  1. Take 1 Cup Sugar
  2. Prepare 1 Cup Brown Sugar
  3. Prepare 1 Cup Butter/Margarine
  4. Get 2 Cups Sifted All Purpose Flour
  5. Prepare 2 Eggs
  6. Take 1 teaspoon vanilla
  7. Prepare 1 Cup Sour Cream (I used maziwa Mala)
  8. Take 1 teaspoon salt
  9. Get 2 teaspoon baking soda
  10. Get 1 teaspoon baking powder
  11. Get 100 g Chopped Dark Chocolate
  12. Get 3/4 Cup powder cocoa
  13. Make ready 1 Cup coffee mix with water

Steps to make Chocolate Fudge Cake:
  1. Preheat the oven. In a bowl mix butter and the sugars using low hand mixer. Once creamy add Vanilla and the eggs.
  2. In a separate bowl shift the flour, cocoa, baking soda and baking powder. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix with a mixer. Add the coffee water Add the sour cream and the chopped chocolate.
  3. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes at 175 degrees.
