The ingredients needed to make Edible Cookie Dough:
  1. Get 2 cups flour
  2. Prepare Half a cup of milk
  3. Take 3 tablespoons powdered
  4. Get 1 pinch salt
  5. Make ready Chocolate melted (a piece of chocolate a small piece)
  6. Take 1 bag chocolate chips

Steps to make Edible Cookie Dough:
  1. Put the flour in a pan and preheat oven at 250 and put the flour in the pan inside (To kill bacteria)
  2. Next take out the flour and add the sugar and the salt and mix then add your milk
  3. Now melt a piece of chocolate and add it to the mix of flour sugar and salt and milk
  4. Lastly add the chocolate chips mix good
  5. Eat and enjoy it's that simple!!!!!
