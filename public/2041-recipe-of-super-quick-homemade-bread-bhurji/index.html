The ingredients needed to make Bread Bhurji:
  1. Take 8 bread slices
  2. Make ready 3 eggs
  3. Get 1 onion chopped
  4. Make ready 1 tomato chopped
  5. Prepare Butter (optional)
  6. Get 1 tbsp oil
  7. Take 1/4 tsp mustard seeds
  8. Prepare 1 tsp red chilly powder
  9. Make ready 1 tsp coriander powder
  10. Get 1/4 tsp cumin powder
  11. Take 1 pinch turmeric powder
  12. Get Salt as required

Instructions to make Bread Bhurji:
  1. Toast the bread slices with or without butter.
  2. Cut them into pieces
  3. Heat a pan with oil. add mustard seeds and let it splutter. Add onion and sauté until it becomes glassy. Add tomato and sauté until it becomes soft and mushy.
  4. Add all spice powder (turmeric, coriander, cumin and red chilly powder) and salt. Sauté for few mins.
  5. Break and pour the eggs. Stir constantly and scramble the eggs.
  6. When it is almost cooked (80-90%), add toasted bread pieces. Stir it for few mins until it gets mixed well. By this time eggs will get completely cooked.
  7. Serve hot with tomato ketchup.
