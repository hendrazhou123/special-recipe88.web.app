The ingredients needed to make Snow Ice Cream:
  1. Prepare 1 can evaporated milk (12 ounces)
  2. Get 1 cup granulated sugar
  3. Prepare 1 teaspoon vanilla
  4. Take 8-10 cups snow in separate bowl

Instructions to make Snow Ice Cream:
  1. In a small bowl, mix the milk, sugar, and vanilla together until well combined.
  2. In a large bowl, add about 8-10 cups of snow. Pour the milk mixture over the top and stir well to combine.
  3. Serve right away.
  4. Depending on snow, (wet or dry) add until you get the consistency you want. It will be a thinner than regular ice cream, however it taste good.
